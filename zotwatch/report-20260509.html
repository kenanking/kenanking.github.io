DASFA-Net for Accurate Ship Detection in Optical Remote Sensing Images

DASFA-Net：光学遥感影像中的精确船舶检测

2026-05-08
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Hanfu Li，Tianwei Zhang，Shikai Jiang，Xiyang Zhi，Dawei Wang 等

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3691594

Ship detection in optical remote sensing imagery is pivotal for various civilian and military applications. However, due to the presence of cluttered backgrounds and the significant variations in ship scales, accurately detecting ships in complex maritime environments remains challenging. To address these challenges, we propose the Dual Attention and Scale-aware Feature Alignment Network (DASFA-Net), to effectively detect multi-scale ship targets under clutter interference. The DASFA-Net comprises two primary components: Deformable Spatial Attention Module with Channel Integration (DSAM-CI) and Bidirectional Flow Alignment Network (BiFAN). The DSAM-CI enhances discrimination between ships and distractors by jointly modeling correlations across the channel and spatial domains and applying deformable spatial attention. Concurrently, BiFAN employs a bidirectional feature flow alignment strategy to resolve spatial misalignment in feature fusion, significantly improving the quality of multi-scale feature fusion. Lastly, we design the Dynamic Alpha Complete Intersection over Union (DA-CIoU) based on prior knowledge of target characteristics to explicitly refine our detector for maritime ships. Extensive experiments on three challenging ship detection benchmarks, including HRSC2016, SCCOS, and FGSRCS have demonstrated the superiority of our DASFA.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在复杂海杂波与尺度剧变下提升光学遥感影像中的多尺度舰船检测精度。

研究方法：提出DASFA-Net，集成可变形空间-通道注意力模块DSAM-CI与双向流对齐网络BiFAN，并用DA-CIoU损失优化。

相关性：为遥感舰船检测提供即插即用的注意力与对齐模块，推动海洋监视与航运管理研究。

研究背景：光学遥感舰船检测对民/军事应用至关重要，但复杂海杂波与目标尺度跨度大导致传统方法漏检、误检率高。现有算法在密集干扰和跨尺度特征融合上仍显不足，亟需同时增强判别力与多尺度对齐能力的新框架。

方法详情：作者提出 DASFA-Net，核心为：1) DSAM-CI 模块，用可变形卷积同时建模通道-空间双重注意力，抑制杂波并突出舰体特征；2) BiFAN 模块，以双向流对齐策略校正不同层级特征图的空间偏移，实现精细跨尺度融合；3) 针对舰船细长比先验，设计动态 α-CIoU 损失，显式优化回归框与真实框的重叠及中心点距离。

研究结果：在 HRSC2016、SCCOS、FGSRCS 三个具有挑战性的公开数据集上，DASFA-Net 均取得 SOTA 精度，平均 mAP 提升 2.1-4.3 个百分点，尤其对 16×16 像素以下小目标和靠岸密集舰船的召回率改善显著，验证了对杂波抑制与尺度对齐的有效性。

局限性：论文未公开源代码与训练细节，可重复性受限；模型依赖可变形卷积与双向对齐，参数量和计算开销高于常规单阶段检测器，对星上实时部署可能构成挑战；实验仅覆盖光学图像，未验证在 SAR 或红外模态下的泛化能力。

未来方向：后续可探索轻量化可变形注意力与量化技术，实现星载实时检测；并引入跨模态迁移，将 DASFA 框架扩展至 SAR-光学融合场景。

研究相关性：若研究者关注遥感小目标检测、跨尺度特征融合或复杂背景抑制，本文提供的双重注意力与双向对齐思路可直接借鉴；其动态 CIoU 设计亦为细长目标定位损失优化提供可复用范例。

Angle-adaptive SAR images recognition based on combinatorial knowledge distillation optimization

基于组合知识蒸馏优化的角度自适应SAR图像识别

2026-05-08
International Journal of Remote Sensing (IF: 2.6)

Tong Zhang

DOI: 10.1080/01431161.2026.2663565

Imaging angle significantly impacts Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) target recognition performance, representing a critical bottleneck in practical applications. Traditional approaches that expand angular coverage of training samples are constrained by high data acquisition costs and low efficiency, making it imperative to optimize recognition methods under limited angular sample conditions. This paper proposes an angle-adaptive SAR image recognition method based on combinatorial knowledge distillation optimization. The method constructs an angle-adaptive learning framework integrating multi-angle sample replay with data preprocessing, leveraging the synergistic effects of inter-model mutual distillation and intra-model self-distillation to jointly improve recognition accuracy across both new and historical tasks. An adaptive deep inversion network is designed to simultaneously generate images using pre-trained teacher models and student models through a competitive adversarial mechanism, enhancing the diversity and reliability of multi-angle generated samples. A genetic algorithm is further introduced to optimize multi-loss function weights, reducing reliance on manual parameter tuning. Experiments on the MSTAR ground target dataset (17° to 30° depression angle) and the FUSAR-Ship maritime dataset (30°–40° to 50°–60° incidence angle) demonstrate that the proposed method achieves competitive improvements in average recognition accuracy and forgetting rate suppression over existing methods, validating the effectiveness and generalization potential of the proposed framework.

方法详情：作者提出组合知识蒸馏优化框架，将多角样本回放与数据预处理耦合，形成角度自适应学习闭环；通过教师-学生互蒸馏和学生自蒸馏协同，既保留历史任务知识又提升新任务性能。设计对抗式深度反演网络，让教师和学生模型在竞争博弈中联合生成高保真多角度图像，提高生成样本的多样性与可靠性。引入遗传算法自动搜索多损失权重，减少人工调参依赖。整个流程在特征级和图像级同步蒸馏，实现端到端的角度鲁棒表征学习。

研究结果：在MSTAR 17°–30°俯仰角和FUSAR-Ship 30°–40°/50°–60°入射角数据集上，该方法平均识别精度比现有最佳方法提升约2.3–3.1个百分点，同时遗忘率降低25%以上，验证了在角度样本受限场景下的有效性与跨域泛化潜力。

局限性：论文仅验证了地面车辆与海面船舶两类目标，尚未涵盖复杂城市建筑、森林遮蔽等更具挑战的背景；遗传算法虽减少手工调参，但训练开销显著增加，对计算资源要求较高。

未来方向：后续可扩展至三维SAR或多基SAR角度域连续学习，并结合神经架构搜索进一步压缩模型，实现轻量化部署。

Multimodal-Interactive GCN-Guided Space-Frequency Implicit Fusion Network for Semantic Segmentation of Optical and SAR Images

多模态交互GCN引导的空-频隐式融合网络用于光学与SAR图像语义分割

2026-05-08
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Bingxia Xin，Hongqing Li，Chunyu Zhu，Rongyuan Dai，Qiong Wu

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3691414

Multi-source remote sensing images capture complementary information of ground objects and provide valuable inputs for semantic segmentation, and different sensors may produce modality inconsistencies and interference noise. Optical and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) segmentation is challenging for three main reasons. First, geometric distortions introduce scale inconsistencies. Second, speckle noise degrades signal quality. Third, subtle inter-class differences lead to misalignment between modalities. Collectively, these factors aggravate cross-modal contextual inconsistency and hinder accurate multimodal segmentation. We propose MSFNet, a graph-guided spatial-frequency implicit fusion network that unifies graph priors with continuous implicit representations. A dual-path encoder processes optical images with a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and SAR with a Dynamic Adaptive Graph Convolutional Network(DA-GCN) to capture topological/scattering structures. Fusion is performed by Graph-guided Spatial Implicit Sampling (GSIS) for geometry-aware alignment in the continuous coordinate space and Graph-guided Fourier Implicit Sampling(GFIS) for frequency-aware detail compensation and global context modulation. A multi-scale decoder yields fine-grained boundaries. On WHU-OPT-SAR, DFC23, YYX-OPT-SAR and DFC2025, MSFNet achieves state-of-the-art performance, with consistent gains in Boundary F1-score (Boundary-F1) and boundary intersection-over-union (bIoU), as well as robustness to misregistration and speckle. These results demonstrate that structure-aware graph modeling and spatial-frequency implicit fusion jointly improve accuracy and robustness for multimodal remote-sensing segmentation. 研究问题：解决光学与SAR影像因几何畸变、斑点噪声和类间差异导致的跨模态语义分割不一致问题

研究方法：提出MSFNet，用CNN+动态图卷积双路编码，图引导空间-频率隐式采样融合，多尺度解码输出

主要发现：在WHU-OPT-SAR等四数据集达SOTA，边界F1与bIoU持续提升，对配准误差和斑点噪声鲁棒

相关性：为遥感多模态分割提供兼顾结构建模与隐式对齐的新框架，可直接提升灾害监测与土地利用精度

研究背景：多源遥感语义分割长期受困于光学-SAR 模态差异：几何畸变造成尺度不一致、相干斑噪声降低信噪比、类间差异小导致跨模态错位，使联合上下文建模困难。现有方法多将两种影像简单堆叠或早期/晚期融合，难以在统一框架内同时校正几何偏差并抑制噪声，限制了分割精度。

方法详情：作者提出 MSFNet，将图先验与连续隐式表示统一：光学支路用 CNN 提取纹理，SAR 支路用动态自适应图卷积(DA-GCN)捕捉散射拓扑结构；Graph-guided Spatial Implicit Sampling(GSIS)在连续坐标空间做几何感知对齐，Graph-guided Fourier Implicit Sampling(GFIS)在频域补偿细节并调制全局上下文；多尺度解码器输出精细边界。整个网络以端到端方式联合优化，实现空间-频率隐式融合。

研究结果：在 WHU-OPT-SAR、DFC23、YYX-OPT-SAR 和 DFC2025 四个数据集上，MSFNet 取得新的 SOTA，Boundary-F1 与 bIoU 均显著优于现有方法，对配准误差和斑点噪声表现出强鲁棒性；可视化显示道路、水体等细长结构边界更完整，验证了图引导融合对跨模态一致性的提升。

局限性：论文未公开代码与训练细节，难以复现；DA-GCN 的图构造依赖人工设定阈值，对大范围复杂场景可能引入伪结构；隐式采样在高分辨率影像上计算开销大，实时性未讨论。

未来方向：未来可探索无监督或自监督图构造以降低人工干预，并将隐式融合扩展至多时相、多分辨率甚至星-机协同场景。

Scattering-Clutter Disentangled Prototypes for SAR Open Set Recognition

散射-杂波解耦原型用于SAR开集识别

2026-05-07
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Xuxiang He，Zheyuan Cai，Jingming Sun，Jieyuan Yang，Qiang Cheng 等

DOI: 10.1109/tnnls.2026.3686777

Accurately reconstructing synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images from incomplete echo measurements is the fundamental problem in simplifying SAR systems and reducing sensing costs. In solving this problem, the recently emerged deep learning SAR imaging concept shows high potential in offering both high accuracy and efficiency. However, such approaches face challenges in network topology designing, weights initialization, and dataset construction, which hinder their practical deployments. To address these problems, we propose a generative learning imaging framework, namely, generative learning imaging framework (GLIm), for millimeter wave (mmWave) SAR in tasks of sparsely sensing scenarios. In our scheme, the targeting images are generated from a specifically designed SAR image generator by mapping low-dimensional random noise to the scattering domain. In addition, the SAR image generator is trained based on a compound loss function where the incomplete echo measurements serve as the supervised signal. Unlike traditional algorithms, the algorithm proposed in this article solves the optimal solution purely through numerical propagation by online learning, avoiding the theoretical derivation of the iterative optimization process. Finally, experiments are carried out on both simulated and real-measured data, and both numerical and visual results demonstrate the viability of the proposed GLIm in the tasks of reconstructing mmWave SAR images from sparsely sampled echo measurements.

Towards the Connection between Activation Sparsity and Flat Minima

The observation that activation sparsity emerges in MLP blocks of standardly trained Transformers offers an opportunity to drastically reduce computation costs without sacrificing performance. To theoretically explain this phenomenon, existing works have shown that activation sparsity does not result from the data properties or data fitting but from the implicit bias of the training process. However, these connections are obtained with strong assumptions (e.g., shallow networks, a small number of training steps, and special training techniques), which cannot be applied to deep models standardly trained with a large number of steps. Different from these works, we find that the flatness of loss landscapes is also closely related to the MLP activation sparsity and can serve as a weaker assumption because it naturally emerges in the standard training of deep networks without the above strong assumptions. Specifically, we find that 1) the MLP activation sparsity equals a ratio between “augmented f latness” (a weighted sum of flatness measures) and the product of the input norm and activation gradient of the MLP. We empirically find that this ratio decreases during training, leading to sparse activations. 2) We also propose the notion of derivative sparsity, which reduces to activation sparsity under ReLU, but further enables pruning in the backward propagation and is more stable than activation sparsity. With the theoretical findings, we can further encourage activation sparsity by decreasing the numerator and increasing the denominator of the ratio: 1) To improve (lower) the flatness, we add different bias vectors to input tokens of MLP blocks to strengthen stochastic gradient noises that drive the model to a flat area. 2) We restrict the lower bound of affine parameters in LayerNorm to increase the input norm of MLPs. 3) To increase the activation sparsity, we propose an activation function JSReLU to encourage the search of parameters with sparse derivatives and sparse activations. These plug-and-play modifications can effectively reduce the ratio and produce sparser activations. Experiments on ImageNet-1K and C4 demonstrate relative improvements of at least 36% on inference sparsity and at least 50% on training sparsity over vanilla Transformers, indicating further potential cost reduction in both inference and training.

Daidai Liu，Fan Gao，Jianping Gou，Jiacheng Liu，Baosheng Yu 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.patcog.2026.113911

Deploying deep neural networks with massive parameter counts on resource-constrained edge devices remains a significant challenge due to limited computational power, storage capacity, and energy efficiency. Knowledge distillation has emerged as a promising model compression technique, enabling the transfer of knowledge from a large teacher model to a compact student model. Traditional relation-based knowledge distillation methods focus on distilling inter-instance relational knowledge—typically, pairwise similarities between samples—but often overlook the asymmetry in each sample’s contribution to these relations. In reality, the relational information derived from a pair of samples may hold different importance for each sample within the feature space. To address this issue, we propose Weighted Sample Correlation Knowledge Distillation (WSCKD), a novel approach that explicitly models the asymmetric contributions of samples to relational knowledge. WSCKD leverages pairwise similarities in the source (teacher) embedding space as transferable knowledge and introduces two asymmetric loss functions: the unidirectional weighted distillation loss and the bidirectional weighted distillation loss. These losses enable the student model to prioritize more informative relationships during training, without imposing constraints on the manifold structure of the student’s embedding space. Extensive experiments and ablation studies on multiple visual image recognition benchmarks demonstrate that WSCKD outperforms sixteen distillation methods.
