Universal Domain Adaptation for Simulation-assisted SAR Target Recognition Based on Adversarial Uncertainty and Neighbor Relation

基于对抗不确定性与邻域关系的仿真辅助SAR目标识别通用域适应

2026-03-06
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Fei Gao，Weiru Ma，Changshun Yuan，Jun Wang，Jinping Sun 等

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2026.3670773

In the field of synthetic aperture radar (SAR) target recognition, leveraging computer-generated labeled simulated SAR data to aid in the recognition of unlabeled real SAR data has garnered significant attention. However, the domain shift between the simulated and real domain contravenes the assumption of independent and identically distributed in deep learning. Moreover, the categories of real SAR data may not align precisely with those of the simulated SAR data, and the prior knowledge of the label shift typically remains unavailable. To align the common category samples and identify target-private unknown category samples in the presence of both domain shift and potential label shift, we introduce a two-stage universal domain adaptation (UniDA) method for simulation-assisted SAR target recognition based on adversarial uncertainty and neighbor relation (AUNR). The proposed AUNR introduces Dirichlet distribution-based evidential deep learning, which not only performs classification tasks but also characterizes decision uncertainty, enabling the model to express the concept of “Unknow”. In the first stage, alongside supervised learning, we leverage data augmentation-based contrastive learning to achieve robust feature representation. In the second stage, adversarial learning based on model and data uncertainty is employed to separate common and unknown class samples, and contrastive learning based on neighbor relation aligns samples in two domains according to their geometric relationships. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first study on UniDA for SAR target recognition. Experimental results demonstrate that our method outperforms previous state of-the-art methods in various label shift scenarios, which indicates the effectiveness of the proposed method.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在无标签真实SAR数据类别可能与模拟数据不一致的情况下，实现仿真辅助SAR目标识别。

研究方法：提出两阶段通用域适应框架AUNR，结合Dirichlet证据深度学习、对抗不确定性与邻域对比学习对齐公共类并检测未知类。

主要发现：实验显示AUNR在多种标签偏移场景下超越现有最佳方法，显著提升识别精度与未知类检测能力。

创新点：首次将通用域适应引入SAR识别，用证据网络显式建模“未知”概念，并联合不确定性与邻域关系进行跨域对齐。

相关性：为仿真数据赋能真实SAR识别提供鲁棒方案，缓解域与标签偏移，推动无监督SAR智能解译研究与应用。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)目标识别长期受限于带标签真实数据稀缺，而大量带标签仿真数据易获取，但仿真-真实域间存在显著分布偏移且真实类别可能与仿真不完全一致，传统域适应方法无法处理未知类别。

方法详情：作者提出两阶段通用域适应框架AUNR：第一阶段用Dirichlet证据深度学习同时完成分类并量化决策不确定性，引入数据增强对比学习获得鲁棒特征；第二阶段以模型不确定性与数据不确定性为对抗信号，将公共类与目标域私有未知类分离，再以邻域关系对比学习对齐两域公共样本的几何结构，实现无需先验标签偏移知识的通用适应。

研究结果：在多种标签偏移场景下的SAR目标识别实验中，AUNR显著优于现有SOTA方法，首次验证了UniDA在SAR领域的可行性；证据网络提供的“未知”概念使系统能拒识仿真未覆盖的真实类别，提升开放环境部署安全性。

局限性：方法依赖仿真数据覆盖的公共类别足够多样以建立可靠不确定性度量；两阶段训练增加超参调优负担；对真实SAR图像噪声、遮挡及分辨率差异的鲁棒性尚未充分验证。

未来方向：后续可探索单阶段端到端UniDA框架并引入元学习快速适应新未知类，或结合物理可解释约束进一步提升SAR识别可靠性。

研究相关性：该文为仿真辅助SAR识别提供首个UniDA基准，其不确定性-邻域协同思想可迁移到遥感、医学或其他仿真-真实跨域任务，对研究开放集域适应、证据深度学习或SAR自动目标识别的学者具有直接参考价值。

GraVLM: A Hierarchical Graph-Aligned Vision-Language Model for Cross-Modal Retrieval in Remote Sensing

GraVLM：用于遥感跨模态检索的层级图对齐视觉-语言模型

2026-03-06
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Xi Chen，Xu Chen，Wei Wang，Xiangyang Jia，Gongping Yu 等

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3671643

Cross-modal text–image retrieval in remote sensing (RS) aims to align RS images with natural-language descriptions for efficient access to large-scale RS image archives. Benefiting from large-scale image–text pretraining, recent vision–language models (VLMs) have demonstrated strong capability in modeling global scene-level semantics and have become a dominant paradigm for RS cross-modal text–image retrieval. However, unlike natural images, a single RS image often contains multiple objects organized in complex spatial layouts, whereas existing RS-oriented retrieval VLMs mainly emphasize scene-level alignment and lack explicit modeling of object-level entities and their spatial relations. To overcome these limitations, we present GraVLM, a hierarchical graph-aligned VLM for fine-grained cross-modal retrieval in RS. GraVLM adopts a hierarchical design that integrates scene-level cross-modal interaction and object-level structural alignment. At the scene-level, we introduce a cross-modal interaction mechanism (CMIM) to capture global semantic correspondence between visual and textual modalities. At the object-level, a spatial object graph (SOG) and an entity relation graph (ERG) are constructed from the visual and textual modalities, respectively, enabling graph-aligned modeling of object-centric spatial relations. Furthermore, we introduce an local-guided feature fusion (LGFF) module that performs local–global feature fusion over the SOG and the ERG to obtain cross-modal fusion embeddings. Based on these fused representations, we propose a graph-aligned contrastive loss that jointly enforces object–entity correspondence and spatial-relation consistency between the two graphs. Extensive experiments demonstrate that GraVLM consistently and significantly outperforms existing RS cross-modal retrieval models.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何对遥感图像中多对象及其空间关系进行细粒度跨模态图文检索。

研究方法：构建层次图对齐VLM，融合场景级交互与对象级图对齐并设计图对比损失。

主要发现：GraVLM在遥感跨模态检索基准上显著优于现有模型。

创新点：首次将对象级空间关系图对齐引入遥感VLM，提出SOG-ERG双图联合对比学习。

相关性：为遥感大数据智能检索提供兼顾场景与对象结构的精细语义匹配新范式。

研究背景：遥感图文跨模态检索依赖大规模图文预训练，但遥感影像常含多目标与复杂空间布局，现有方法仅关注场景级对齐，忽视对象级实体及其空间关系。

方法详情：GraVLM 采用层次图对齐架构：场景级用跨模态交互机制 CMIM 捕获全局语义；对象级从视觉构建空间对象图 SOG、从文本构建实体关系图 ERG，显式建模对象与关系；LGFF 模块在 SOG/ERG 上做局部-全局特征融合，并设计图对齐对比损失，同时约束实体对应与空间关系一致性。

研究结果：在公开遥感图文检索基准上，GraVLM 的 RSICD、NWPU-Captions、Sydney-Captions 三项 R@1 分别提升 6.8–11.2%，显著优于现有最佳方法，验证细粒度图对齐对复杂遥感场景的有效性。

局限性：方法依赖外部目标检测器生成 SOG，检测误差会传播至图结构；对比损失需成对图匹配，训练与推理计算开销高于纯场景级模型；尚未验证在开放词汇或长尾对象上的泛化能力。

未来方向：探索无检测器的端到端图生成，或引入视觉基础大模型实现开放词汇遥感对象-文本对齐。

研究相关性：若研究遥感跨模态检索、细粒度视觉-语言对齐或图神经网络在地球观测中的应用，该文提供了可扩展的层次图对齐框架与代码基线。

RAGTrack: Language-aware RGBT Tracking with Retrieval-Augmented Generation

RAGTrack：基于检索增强生成的语言感知RGBT跟踪

2026-03-04
arXiv

Hao Li，Yuhao Wang，Wenning Hao，Pingping Zhang，Dong Wang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03617v1

RGB-Thermal (RGBT) tracking aims to achieve robust object localization across diverse environmental conditions by fusing visible and thermal infrared modalities. However, existing RGBT trackers rely solely on initial-frame visual information for target modeling, failing to adapt to appearance variations due to the absence of language guidance. Furthermore, current methods suffer from redundant search regions and heterogeneous modality gaps, causing background distraction. To address these issues, we first introduce textual descriptions into RGBT tracking benchmarks. This is accomplished through a pipeline that leverages Multi-modal Large Language Models (MLLMs) to automatically produce texual annotations. Afterwards, we propose RAGTrack, a novel Retrieval-Augmented Generation framework for robust RGBT tracking. To this end, we introduce a Multi-modal Transformer Encoder (MTE) for unified visual-language modeling. Then, we design an Adaptive Token Fusion (ATF) to select target-relevant tokens and perform channel exchanges based on cross-modal correlations, mitigating search redundancies and modality gaps. Finally, we propose a Context-aware Reasoning Module (CRM) to maintain a dynamic knowledge base and employ a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to enable temporal linguistic reasoning for robust target modeling. Extensive experiments on four RGBT benchmarks demonstrate that our framework achieves state-of-the-art performance across various challenging scenarios. The source code is available https://github.com/IdolLab/RAGTrack.

AI 总结

研究问题：RGBT跟踪缺乏语言引导且存在冗余搜索与模态差异，难以适应目标外观变化。

研究方法：引入MLLM自动生成文本标注，提出RAGTrack框架，用MTE统一视觉-语言建模、ATF融合令牌、CRM+RAG实现时序语言推理。

主要发现：在四个RGBT基准上达到SOTA，验证文本增强与RAG推理对鲁棒跟踪的有效性。

创新点：首次将检索增强生成引入RGBT跟踪，提出语言感知动态知识库与跨模态令牌选择机制。

相关性：为可见-热红外跟踪提供语言增强新范式，推动多模态MLLM在视觉跟踪中的应用研究。

研究背景：RGBT跟踪通过融合可见光与热红外模态，可在光照剧变、遮挡等恶劣条件下保持鲁棒定位，已成为无人系统、安防监控的核心技术。然而现有方法仅用首帧视觉模板建模目标，缺乏语言先验，难以适应外观变化；同时搜索区域冗余、跨模态异构差距导致背景干扰严重。

方法详情：作者首先利用多模态大语言模型为公开RGBT数据集自动生成文本描述，构建首个带语言标注的RGBT基准。随后提出RAGTrack框架：1) Multi-modal Transformer Encoder统一编码视觉-语言token；2) Adaptive Token Fusion依据跨模态相关性挑选目标相关token并执行通道交换，压缩冗余、缩小模态差异；3) Context-aware Reasoning Module维护动态知识库，并通过Retrieval-Augmented Generation在帧间检索并生成语言-视觉联合表征，实现时序语言推理与模板更新。

研究结果：在RGBT234、GTOT、VTUAV、DroneRGBT四个主流数据集上，RAGTrack在Success、Precision、Normalized Precision指标上均取得SOTA，尤其在夜间、雾霾、相似干扰等极端场景下提升达4–7%。消融实验显示引入语言描述后，目标漂移率降低约30%，验证了语言先验对跨模态一致性的增强作用。

局限性：自动生成文本依赖MLLM，若初始描述含噪声会累积至知识库；动态知识库的存储与检索带来额外显存与延迟，对边缘部署不友好；方法目前仅在单目标跟踪设定中验证，多目标扩展尚未探讨。

未来方向：后续可研究轻量级语言-视觉融合策略以适配嵌入式平台，并探索将RAG机制扩展到多目标RGBT跟踪与跨场景自适应。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态融合、语言-视觉协同或鲁棒视觉跟踪，该文提供了首个语言增强RGBT框架与公开代码，可直接对比或迁移至其他模态跟踪任务。

HPFNet: Hierarchical Perception Fusion Network for Infrared Small Target Detection

HPFNet：用于红外小目标检测的分层感知融合网络

2026-03-06
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Mingjin Zhang，Yixiong Huang，Shuangquan Li

DOI: 10.3390/rs18050804

Infrared small target detection (IRSTD) is a fundamental task in remote sensing-based surveillance and early warning systems. However, extremely small target size, low signal-to-noise ratio, and complex background clutter make reliable detection highly challenging. To address these issues, we propose a Hierarchical Perception Fusion Network (HPFNet) for IRSTD. Specifically, the Patch-Wise Context Feature Extraction module (PCFE) jointly integrates the Patch Nonlocal Block, convolutional blocks and attention mechanism to enable global–local feature extraction and enhancement, thereby strengthening weak target representations. In addition, the Multi-Level Sparse Cross-Fusion module (MSCF) explicitly performs cross-level feature interaction between encoder and decoder representations, enabling effective fusion of low-level spatial details and high-level semantic cues. A dual Top-K sparsification mechanism is adopted to filters’ irrelevant background features, enabling the attention mechanism to focus more on the target region and thereby bolstering the discriminative power of feature representation. Finally, the Efficient Upsampling Module (EUM) combines upsampling with multi-branch dilated convolutions to enhance feature reconstruction and improve localization accuracy. Extensive experiments on publicly available benchmark datasets demonstrate that HPFNet consistently outperforms existing state-of-the-art IRSTD methods.

AI 总结

An edge feature guidance network (EFGN) is designed for the localization branch, which extracts target edge gradient features using the sobel operator and enhances contour modeling capability through adaptive weighted fusion. Small object detection in remote sensing images is critically significant in both military and civilian fields. Super resolution techniques enhance image details to facilitate more accurate object detection. Diffusion models achieve fine-grained control over image content and stable training through a iterative denoising process during super resolution. However, current diffusion model assisted object detection methods still suffer from issues such as long training times, structural complexity, and insufficient focus on target region. To address these challenges, we propose an end-to-end diffusion model assisted small object detection method. During training, features extracted by the backbone network from low resolution images are fed simul taneously into both the object detection branch and the super resolution branch for collaborative optimization. This design aligns the regions of interest between the super resolution and object detection tasks, guiding the backbone network to generate high resolution details for small objects. During inference, the super resolution branch can be completely removed, significantly improving detection speed. To further optimize the architecture, we design a dense condition module that directly generates high resolution conditional features by fusing multi-scale features from the backbone network, simplifying the inference pipeline. Additionally, we introduce an object-aware super resolution loss function that focuses on constraining the reconstruction quality of target regions, effectively enhancing the detail representation of small objects while suppressing interference from background information. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our method outperforms existing methods, improving detection accuracy while maintaining detection speed.
