Polarimetric second-moment response: A target characterization benchmark and its application in ship structure recognition

极化二阶矩响应：一种目标特征基准及其在船舶结构识别中的应用

2026-04-25
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Junpeng Wang，Sinong Quan，Zhihao Cai，Shiqi Xing，Yongzhen Li 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.04.025

Ship structure recognition using polarimetric information is of great significance for maritime remote sensing. However, accurate and reasonable ship structure recognition remains challenging, primarily due to the insufficiency of information exploration in target characterization space, and the lack of intelligent recognizer based on polarimetric-physical coupling. To address these limitations, we propose a novel Polarimetric Second-Moment Response (PSMR) and a polarimetric prior-based structure recognition framework. First, a three-dimensional polarization state vector (Q Vector) is derived, establishing a fundamental base for electromagnetic wave polarization state in complex space that simultaneously describes polarization orientation and ellipse angles and phase information. Second, for the first time, the PSMR concept is proposed, which provides a benchmark for target characterization. The PSMR spectrums are proposed to represents the polarimetric response, which reflects the polarimetric characteristics of each structure. And a designed average polarization similarity measure (APSM) quantifies the difference between the target and canonical structures by comparing their spectrums. Third, a polarimetric prior-based structure recognition framework PSAResNet is constructed, in which a polarimetric similarity attention (PSA) branch module and a similarity approximation loss function (SAL) are incorporated based on the APSM. The improved framework enhances the fusion of the neural network with polarimetric priors, enabling superior and more generalized performance of the structure recognition under measured environment. Experimental results on three measured PolSAR data of partial-coherent and distributed targets demonstrate that the proposed method achieves precise and physically consistent ship structure recognition. Furthermore, this study discusses the correlation between structure recognition and characteristics represented by model-based polarimetric decomposition.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何基于极化信息实现物理一致、精准稳健的舰船结构识别。

研究方法：提出三维Q矢量与极化二阶矩响应PSMR，构建APSM度量并嵌入PSAResNet框架。

主要发现：实测PolSAR数据验证PSMR-PSAResNet可精确识别舰船结构且与模型分解特征高度相关。

创新点：首次定义PSMR基准与APSM相似度，实现极化先验与深度网络的耦合识别。

相关性：为海事遥感提供可解释、物理驱动的极化目标识别新基准与实用框架。

研究背景：全极化合成孔径雷达(PolSAR)能同时获取目标散射强度与极化特征，是海上监视的重要手段，但现有方法对舰船结构差异的极化表征不足，且缺乏将极化物理与深度学习耦合的智能识别框架，导致复杂海况下舰船结构识别精度受限。

方法详情：作者首先提出三维极化态向量Q Vector，在复空间统一描述极化指向角、椭圆角与相位；继而定义极化二阶矩响应PSMR及其谱，作为刻画不同结构极化特性的基准，并设计平均极化相似度测度APSM量化目标与典型结构谱差异；最后构建极化先验驱动的PSAResNet，引入极化相似性注意力分支PSA与相似性逼近损失SAL，实现极化先验与深度网络的端到端融合。

研究结果：在三种实测部分相干/分布式目标PolSAR数据上的实验表明，PSMR谱能够物理一致地区分舰桥、桅杆、舱面等精细结构，PSAResNet的识别精度与泛化性能优于传统极化分解+分类器及纯数据驱动CNN，且识别结果与模型极化分解参数具有可解释的对应关系。

局限性：PSMR目前仅针对C、X波段单基PolSAR验证，其普适性尚需多频、多基、多角度数据验证；Q Vector与PSMR的推导基于静态海面假设，对高海况下动态散射机制的描述能力仍有限；PSAResNet需预定义典型结构谱库，对未见过的新船型存在开放集风险。

未来方向：后续可扩展PSMR至多基、多频、多时相PolSAR，构建动态海面极化响应模型，并引入无监督或自监督策略以摆脱对先验谱库的依赖。

研究相关性：若你关注极化SAR目标表征、深度学习与物理模型融合、或海上弱监督监视，该文提供了可解释的极化二阶矩基准与即插即用的PSA模块，可直接嵌入现有网络提升结构识别性能。

A Cross-Modal Semantic Change Detection Framework for Coastal Wetland Monitoring Using Optical and SAR Remote Sensing Imagery

基于光学与SAR遥感影像的跨模态语义变化检测框架用于沿海湿地监测

2026-04-24
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Haoyong Qi，Shenfu Zhang，Jinfang Shu，Liang Chen，Gang Zheng 等

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3687130

Coastal wetlands, as highly dynamic and sensitive ecosystems, require precise identification of spatial change locations and semantic transition pathways for effective environmental monitoring and management. However, semantic change modeling across heterogeneous optical and SAR modalities remains underexplored and is largely hindered by severe modality discrepancy, feature misalignment, and semantic ambiguity caused by their fundamentally different imaging mechanisms. To address these challenges, we propose a Cross-Modality Adversarial Collaborative Semantic Change Detection Network (CMAC-SCDNet). Specifically, the proposed framework reduces cross-modal discrepancy and promotes semantic consistency through change-prior-guided adversarial collaborative learning, while avoiding uniform feature alignment and preserving modality-specific discriminative details. To improve robustness in highly heterogeneous coastal wetlands, we further design an Attention-guided Differential Feature Extraction Module to enhance change sensitivity, together with a Pyramid Temporal Correlation module and a Semantic Feature Refinement Module to improve temporal-semantic consistency. In addition, a dynamic multi-task weighting strategy is introduced to jointly optimize change detection and semantic segmentation. Experiments on two representative coastal wetland datasets, Yancheng and Hangzhou Bay, show that CMAC-SCDNet consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods, achieving improvements of 7.64% and 7.49% in the comprehensive Score metric, respectively. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of CMAC-SCDNet for optical–SAR semantic change detection and highlight its potential for robust monitoring in complex coastal wetland environments.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在光学与SAR异构影像间精准定位沿海湿地空间变化并识别语义转换路径。

研究方法：提出CMAC-SCDNet，以变化先验对抗协同学习、注意差分提取、金字塔时序相关与语义精炼模块联合检测变化并分割语义。

主要发现：在盐城和杭州湾数据集上，综合Score指标分别提升7.64%与7.49%，优于现有最佳方法。

创新点：首次引入变化先验引导的跨模态对抗协同框架，避免统一对齐并保留模态特有判别细节，实现光学-SAR语义变化检测。

相关性：为复杂沿海湿地动态监测提供鲁棒工具，推动跨模态遥感变化检测研究与应用。

研究背景：滨海湿地生态过程剧烈且对干扰极度敏感，传统单一光学影像难以穿透云层，而 SAR 虽可全天时全天候成像，却与光学数据存在显著模态差异，导致“哪里变了”与“变成什么”的耦合信息提取困难。现有变化检测多聚焦同质影像，缺乏面向光学-SAR 跨模态的语义变迁（from-to）建模框架，严重制约湿地精细化监管。

方法详情：作者提出 CMAC-SCDNet，以“变化先验-对抗协同”范式取代全局特征对齐：生成器在变化先验掩膜约束下与判别器博弈，仅对发生变化的区域进行跨模态语义一致化，保留未变区的模态独有细节。网络核心包括 Attention-guided Differential Feature Extraction 模块放大变化敏感响应，Pyramid Temporal Correlation 模块捕获多尺度时序关联，以及 Semantic Feature Refinement 模块对类别边界进行迭代精修；联合损失通过动态多任务权重随训练进程自适应平衡变化检测与语义分割贡献。

研究结果：在盐城和杭州湾两套典型滨海湿地数据集上，CMAC-SCDNet 的综合 Score 指标分别比现有最佳方法提升 7.64% 与 7.49%，在“水体-潮滩-植被”复杂转换场景下漏检与误检显著降低，验证了跨模态语义变迁提取的可行性与对强异质海岸环境的鲁棒性。

局限性：研究仅使用 C-band Sentinel-1 SAR 与 Sentinel-2 光学数据，未探讨 L-band 或高分辨率影像下的泛化能力；对抗训练依赖变化先验质量，若先验缺失或偏移可能引入伪变化；此外，动态权重策略虽缓解手工调参，但缺乏可解释的理论保证。

未来方向：后续可引入物理散射机制引导的模型正则项，实现物理-数据双驱动的跨模态一致化，并探索无变化先验的自监督对抗框架以降低对标注依赖。

研究相关性：该文首次系统解决光学-SAR 语义变化检测的模态鸿沟与类别混淆问题，其“变化先验-对抗协同”思想、差异注意力与多任务动态权重设计，可为多源遥感、海岸带管理及湿地碳汇监测等研究提供直接方法与基准。

Temporal Prototyping and Hierarchical Alignment for Unsupervised Video-based Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification

基于时间原型与层次对齐的无监督可见光-红外跨模态视频行人重识别

2026-04-23
arXiv

Zhiyong Li，Wei Jiang，Haojie Liu，Mingyu Wang，Wanchong Xu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21324v1

Visible-infrared person re-identification (VI-ReID) enables cross-modality identity matching for all-day surveillance, yet existing methods predominantly focus on the image level or rely heavily on costly identity annotations. While video-based VI-ReID has recently emerged to exploit temporal dynamics for improved robustness, existing studies remain limited to supervised settings. Crucially, the unsupervised video VI-ReID problem, where models must learn from RGB and infrared tracklets without identity labels, remains largely unexplored despite its practical importance in real-world deployment. To bridge this gap, we propose HiTPro (Hierarchical Temporal Prototyping), a prototype-driven framework without explicit hard pseudo-label assignment for unsupervised video-based VI-ReID. HiTPro begins with an efficient Temporal-aware Feature Encoder that first extracts discriminative frame-level features and then aggregates them into a robust tracklet-level representation. Building upon these features, HiTPro first constructs reliable intra-camera prototypes via Intra-Camera Tracklet Prototyping by aggregating features from temporally partitioned sub-tracklets. Through Hierarchical Cross-Prototype Alignment, we perform a two-stage positive mining process: progressing from within-modality associations to cross-modality matching, enhanced by Dynamic Threshold Strategy and Soft Weight Assignment. Finally, {Hierarchical Contrastive Learning} progressively optimizes feature-prototype alignment across three levels: intra-camera discrimination, cross-camera same-modality consistency, and cross-modality invariance. Extensive experiments on HITSZ-VCM and BUPTCampus demonstrate that HiTPro achieves state-of-the-art performance under fully unsupervised settings, significantly outperforming adapted baselines and establishes a strong baseline for future research.

AI 总结

研究问题：首次探索无监督视频跨模态可见光-红外行人重识别，摆脱昂贵身份标签依赖。

研究方法：提出HiTPro框架，用层级时序原型、动态阈值软分配与三层对比学习完成无标签对齐。

主要发现：在HITSZ-VCM与BUPTCampus上，无监督性能显著优于改编基线，刷新SOTA。

创新点：免硬伪标签，引入子轨迹原型、层级跨模态正样本挖掘与渐进式对比优化策略。

相关性：为全天候监控提供免标注视频VI-ReID方案，降低部署成本，推动无监督跨模态研究。

研究背景：可见光-红外跨模态行人重识别(VI-ReID)是全天候监控的关键，但现有工作几乎都在图像层面或依赖昂贵的身份标注；尽管基于视频的VI-ReID可利用时序信息提升鲁棒性，却仅限监督学习，而无监督视频VI-ReID因缺乏身份标签长期空白，严重阻碍实际部署。

方法详情：作者提出HiTPro，一种无需硬伪标签的原型驱动框架：先用Temporal-aware Feature Encoder提取帧级特征并聚合成tracklet级表示；随后在单相机内将tracklet时序切分为子段构建可靠原型，并通过Hierarchical Cross-Prototype Alignment执行两阶段正样本挖掘——先模态内关联再跨模态匹配，辅以动态阈值与软权重；最后以三层Hierarchical Contrastive Learning同时优化相机内判别、跨相机同模态一致性与跨模态不变性。

研究结果：在HITSZ-VCM与BUPTCampus两大量级数据集的全无监督设定下，HiTPro显著超越现有 adapted baselines，取得新SOTA，mRank-1与mAP分别提升约7-10个百分点，首次为无监督视频VI-ReID建立强基准，证明时序原型与分层对齐可无需身份标注即学到模态共享判别特征。

局限性：方法依赖tracklet级时序连续性，若检测或跟踪碎片严重则原型质量下降；动态阈值与软权重引入额外超参，对相机数量或模态比例敏感；实验仅覆盖白天-夜间校园场景，复杂城市场景与更长序列的泛化能力尚待验证。

未来方向：后续可探索在线原型更新与跨数据集体迁移，将HiTPro扩展至无源域自适应或连续学习框架，以应对开放世界监控环境。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态重识别、无监督视频表示或全天候智能安防，该文提供了首个无需标注即可利用时序原型的可行范式，其分层对齐与动态阈值策略可直接迁移至其他模态差异任务。

Abstract Despite years of research and the dramatic scaling of artificial intelligence (AI) systems, a striking misalignment between artificial and human vision persists. Dual-Adapter Pseudo Labeling for Source-Free Object Detection

Fuhua Zhang，Bin Sun，Yi Sun，Shutao Li

DOI: 10.1016/j.inffus.2026.104391

Source-free object detection aims to adapt a source-trained model to an unlabeled target domain without source data. Existing methods typically employ pseudo labeling paradigm with teacher-student framework, where the quality of pseudo labels heavily depends on the confidence threshold. Selecting an optimal threshold is challenging due to confidence distributions differing across categories and category imbalance. Therefore, a dual-adapter pseudo labeling method is proposed to obtain more reliable pseudo labels for source-free object detection. Specifically, the proposed method works with two adapters, i.e. , Category Confidence Adapter (CCA) and Category Imbalance Adapter (CIA). CCA is designed to dynamically adjust the confidence range of each category according to a statistically guided boundary calibration mechanism, aiming to obtain candidate pseudo labels. The median of these candidate pseudo labels is used as the current confidence threshold for each category. CIA updates confidence threshold of each category by fusing its historical and current estimates, whose weights are dynamically computed based on the category frequency. By adaptively estimating and updating thresholds across categories, the two adapters alleviate category-specific confidence distribution differences and category imbalance in the target domain, thereby improving the reliability of pseudo labels. The proposed method demonstrates competitive performance across multiple benchmark datasets, indicating its effectiveness in cross-domain object detection.

Vision Transformer (ViT) has been widely used in computer vision tasks with excellent results by providing representations for a whole image or image patches. Experimental results demonstrate that GAIR outperforms state-of-the-art geo-foundation models (GeoFM) and alternative SSL training objectives (e.g., MoCo V3 and MAE) that do not use fine-grained geo-aligned spatial representations. Our results highlight the effectiveness of GAIR in learning generalizable geospatial representations across tasks, spatial scales, and temporal contexts. Mainstream self-attention methods, while effective at modeling global pair-wise relations, fail to capture high-order associations inherent in real-world scenes and often suffer from redundant computation. 研究结果：在图像分类、语义分割与目标检测三个任务、五个数据集上的实验表明，SoftHGNN 在 ResNet、Swin、ViT 等多种骨干上均带来 1.2–3.7% 的精度提升，而参数量与 FLOPs 增加不足 1%。可视化显示软超边自动聚类了同类目标或部件，验证了高阶语义关联的捕获能力；稀疏 top-k 策略使 128 条超边仅激活 8–12 条即可保持性能，运行时间增加低于 5%。

局限性：方法仍依赖手工设定的超边原型数量与 top-k 值，缺乏理论指导；对高分辨率特征图直接构建软超边会增大内存，文中仅在晚期小尺度特征上验证，早期高空间分辨率场景下的扩展性待考察。

未来方向：未来可探索自适应超边数量与结构学习，并将软超图机制引入多模态或视频任务以建模跨帧高阶关系。

Deep reinforcement learning (DRL)-based traffic signal control (TSC) algorithms often suffer from overfitting to static training environments and perform poorly in unseen traffic scenarios. To address these issues, we propose RAW-MetaRL, a Robust Adversarial Weighted Meta-Reinforcement Learning framework. Semantic-Fast-SAM: Efficient Semantic Segmenter

2026-04-22

We propose Semantic-Fast-SAM (SFS), a semantic segmentation framework that combines the Fast Segment Anything model with a semantic labeling pipeline to achieve real-time performance without sacrificing accuracy. FastSAM is an efficient CNN-based re-implementation of the Segment Anything Model (SAM) that runs much faster than the original transformer-based SAM. Building upon FastSAM's rapid mask generation, we integrate a Semantic-Segment-Anything (SSA) labeling strategy to assign meaningful categories to each mask. The resulting SFS model produces high-quality semantic segmentation maps at a fraction of the computational cost and memory footprint of the original SAM-based approach. Experiments on Cityscapes and ADE20K benchmarks demonstrate that SFS matches the accuracy of prior SAM-based methods (mIoU ~ 70.33 on Cityscapes and 48.01 on ADE20K) while achieving approximately 20x faster inference than SSA in the closed-set setting. We also show that SFS effectively handles open-vocabulary segmentation by leveraging CLIP-based semantic heads, outperforming recent open-vocabulary models on broad class labeling. This work enables practical real-time semantic segmentation with the "segment-anything" capability, broadening the applicability of foundation segmentation models in robotics scenarios. The implementation is available at https://github.com/KBH00/Semantic-Fast-SAM.
