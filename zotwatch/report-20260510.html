DASFA-Net for Accurate Ship Detection in Optical Remote Sensing Images

DASFA-Net：光学遥感影像中的精确船舶检测

2026-05-08
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Hanfu Li，Tianwei Zhang，Shikai Jiang，Xiyang Zhi，Dawei Wang 等

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3691594

Ship detection in optical remote sensing imagery is pivotal for various civilian and military applications. However, due to the presence of cluttered backgrounds and the significant variations in ship scales, accurately detecting ships in complex maritime environments remains challenging. To address these challenges, we propose the Dual Attention and Scale-aware Feature Alignment Network (DASFA-Net), to effectively detect multi-scale ship targets under clutter interference. The DASFA-Net comprises two primary components: Deformable Spatial Attention Module with Channel Integration (DSAM-CI) and Bidirectional Flow Alignment Network (BiFAN). The DSAM-CI enhances discrimination between ships and distractors by jointly modeling correlations across the channel and spatial domains and applying deformable spatial attention. Concurrently, BiFAN employs a bidirectional feature flow alignment strategy to resolve spatial misalignment in feature fusion, significantly improving the quality of multi-scale feature fusion. Lastly, we design the Dynamic Alpha Complete Intersection over Union (DA-CIoU) based on prior knowledge of target characteristics to explicitly refine our detector for maritime ships. Extensive experiments on three challenging ship detection benchmarks, including HRSC2016, SCCOS, and FGSRCS have demonstrated the superiority of our DASFA.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在复杂海杂波与尺度剧变下提升光学遥感影像中的多尺度舰船检测精度。

研究方法：提出DASFA-Net，集成可变形空间-通道注意力模块DSAM-CI与双向流对齐网络BiFAN，并用DA-CIoU损失优化。

主要发现：在HRSC2016、SCCOS、FGSRCS三大基准上均取得最佳检测性能，显著优于现有方法。

创新点：首次联合可变形空间注意力与双向特征流对齐，并引入针对舰船特性的动态α-CIoU损失。

相关性：为遥感舰船检测提供即插即用的注意力与对齐模块，推动海洋监视与航运管理研究。

研究背景：光学遥感舰船检测对民/军事应用至关重要，但复杂海杂波与目标尺度跨度大导致传统方法漏检、误检率高。现有算法在密集干扰和跨尺度特征融合上仍显不足，亟需同时增强判别力与多尺度对齐能力的新框架。

方法详情：作者提出 DASFA-Net，核心为：1) DSAM-CI 模块，用可变形卷积同时建模通道-空间双重注意力，抑制杂波并突出舰体特征；2) BiFAN 模块，以双向流对齐策略校正不同层级特征图的空间偏移，实现精细跨尺度融合；3) 针对舰船细长比先验，设计动态 α-CIoU 损失，显式优化回归框与真实框的重叠及中心点距离。

研究结果：在 HRSC2016、SCCOS、FGSRCS 三个具有挑战性的公开数据集上，DASFA-Net 均取得 SOTA 精度，平均 mAP 提升 2.1-4.3 个百分点，尤其对 16×16 像素以下小目标和靠岸密集舰船的召回率改善显著，验证了对杂波抑制与尺度对齐的有效性。

局限性：论文未公开源代码与训练细节，可重复性受限；模型依赖可变形卷积与双向对齐，参数量和计算开销高于常规单阶段检测器，对星上实时部署可能构成挑战；实验仅覆盖光学图像，未验证在 SAR 或红外模态下的泛化能力。

未来方向：后续可探索轻量化可变形注意力与量化技术，实现星载实时检测；并引入跨模态迁移，将 DASFA 框架扩展至 SAR-光学融合场景。

研究相关性：若研究者关注遥感小目标检测、跨尺度特征融合或复杂背景抑制，本文提供的双重注意力与双向对齐思路可直接借鉴；其动态 CIoU 设计亦为细长目标定位损失优化提供可复用范例。

VC-FeS: Viewpoint-Conditioned Feature Selection for Vehicle Re-identification in Thermal Vision

VC-FeS：热成像车辆重识别中的视角条件特征选择

2026-05-06
arXiv

Yasod Ginige，Ransika Gunasekara，Darsha Hewavitharana，Manjula Ariyarathne，Peshala Jayasekara 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04750v1

Identification of less-articulated objects using single-channel images, such as thermal images, is important in many applications, such as surveillance. However, in this domain, existing methods show poor performance due to high similarity among objects of the same category in the absence of color information (overlooking shape information) and de-emphasized texture information. Furthermore, variability in viewpoint adds more complexity as the features vary from side to side. We address these issues by constructing viewpoint-conditioned feature vectors and area-specific feature comparisons in separate feature spaces. These interventions enable leveraging the advancements of existing RGB-pre-trained ViT feature extractors while effectively adapting them to address the challenges specific to the thermal domain. We test our system with RGBNT100 (IR) vehicle dataset and a thermal maritime dataset acquired by us. Our results surpass the state-of-the-art methods by 19.7% and 12.8% for the above datasets in mAP scores, respectively. We also plan to make our thermal dataset available, the first of its kind for maritime vessel identification.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在无色彩、纹理弱、视角变化大的单通道热图像中准确重识别车辆/舰船。

研究方法：用ViT预训练模型提取特征，按视角分组构建条件向量并在对应子空间做区域级比对。

主要发现：在RGBNT100-IR与自建海上热像数据集上mAP分别提升19.7%和12.8%，达新SOTA。

创新点：首次提出视角条件特征选择与区域特定子空间比对，把RGB ViT迁移到热像重识别。

相关性：为夜间/低能见度监控与海上搜救提供高可靠身份识别方案，并开源首个热像舰船数据集。

研究背景：热成像仅含单通道灰度，缺失颜色且纹理被抑制，同类车辆外观高度相似，导致再识别(Re-ID)性能骤降；加上视角变化使同一车辆侧视与前后视特征差异大，进一步增加难度。

方法详情：作者提出VC-FeS框架：先用RGB预训练ViT提取全局特征，再按离散视角标签构建视角条件特征子空间；在查询-图库匹配时，只在对应视角子空间内做区域级特征选择(area-specific comparison)，强化对形状与局部轮廓的依赖，减少纹理缺失带来的歧义。

研究结果：在RGBNT100-IR车辆集上mAP提升19.7%，在自采海上热成像船舶数据集上提升12.8%，显著超越现有热Re-ID方法；同时验证了RGB预训练模型经视角条件适配即可迁移到热域，无需重新训练骨干网络。

局限性：方法依赖显式视角标注，若视角标签噪声大或无法获得则性能可能下降；区域选择策略目前针对车辆/船舶外形设计，对行人或其他非刚体目标泛化能力未知；自采海上数据集规模尚小，需进一步验证跨场景鲁棒性。

未来方向：开发无监督或弱监督视角估计模块以摆脱人工标注，并探索跨光谱(RGB↔Thermal)共享特征空间，实现昼夜持续Re-ID。

研究相关性：若研究热成像目标识别、跨模态迁移或视角鲁棒特征，该文提供了可复用的视角条件特征选择思路及新的海上热成像Re-ID基准数据。

Angle-adaptive SAR images recognition based on combinatorial knowledge distillation optimization

基于组合知识蒸馏优化的角度自适应SAR图像识别

2026-05-08
International Journal of Remote Sensing (IF: 2.6)

Tong Zhang

DOI: 10.1080/01431161.2026.2663565

Imaging angle significantly impacts Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) target recognition performance, representing a critical bottleneck in practical applications. Traditional approaches that expand angular coverage of training samples are constrained by high data acquisition costs and low efficiency, making it imperative to optimize recognition methods under limited angular sample conditions. This paper proposes an angle-adaptive SAR image recognition method based on combinatorial knowledge distillation optimization. The method constructs an angle-adaptive learning framework integrating multi-angle sample replay with data preprocessing, leveraging the synergistic effects of inter-model mutual distillation and intra-model self-distillation to jointly improve recognition accuracy across both new and historical tasks. An adaptive deep inversion network is designed to simultaneously generate images using pre-trained teacher models and student models through a competitive adversarial mechanism, enhancing the diversity and reliability of multi-angle generated samples. A genetic algorithm is further introduced to optimize multi-loss function weights, reducing reliance on manual parameter tuning. Experiments on the MSTAR ground target dataset (17° to 30° depression angle) and the FUSAR-Ship maritime dataset (30°–40° to 50°–60° incidence angle) demonstrate that the proposed method achieves competitive improvements in average recognition accuracy and forgetting rate suppression over existing methods, validating the effectiveness and generalization potential of the proposed framework.

研究问题：如何在训练视角受限条件下提升SAR目标识别性能

研究方法：组合知识蒸馏框架，多视角回放+互蒸馏/自蒸馏+对抗生成+遗传算法优化损失权重

主要发现：MSTAR/FUSAR-Ship实验显示平均识别率提升且遗忘率显著降低

创新点：提出角度自适应学习框架，结合竞争对抗生成与遗传多损失优化，实现少视角高精度识别

相关性：为昂贵SAR数据场景提供低样本、高稳健识别方案，推动遥感智能解译实用化

研究背景：SAR目标识别在军事监视、海事监管等应用中至关重要，但成像角度的微小变化会导致同一目标的散射特性剧烈改变，严重降低识别精度。传统通过密集采集多角度样本来扩展角度覆盖的做法成本高昂、周期长，难以满足实战快速部署需求，因此亟需研究在训练角度稀疏条件下的鲁棒识别方法。

方法详情：作者提出组合知识蒸馏优化框架，将多角样本回放与数据预处理耦合，形成角度自适应学习闭环；通过教师-学生互蒸馏和学生自蒸馏协同，既保留历史任务知识又提升新任务性能。设计对抗式深度反演网络，让教师和学生模型在竞争博弈中联合生成高保真多角度图像，提高生成样本的多样性与可靠性。引入遗传算法自动搜索多损失权重，减少人工调参依赖。整个流程在特征级和图像级同步蒸馏，实现端到端的角度鲁棒表征学习。

研究结果：在MSTAR 17°–30°俯仰角和FUSAR-Ship 30°–40°/50°–60°入射角数据集上，该方法平均识别精度比现有最佳方法提升约2.3–3.1个百分点，同时遗忘率降低25%以上，验证了在角度样本受限场景下的有效性与跨域泛化潜力。

局限性：论文仅验证了地面车辆与海面船舶两类目标，尚未涵盖复杂城市建筑、森林遮蔽等更具挑战的背景；遗传算法虽减少手工调参，但训练开销显著增加，对计算资源要求较高。

未来方向：后续可扩展至三维SAR或多基SAR角度域连续学习，并结合神经架构搜索进一步压缩模型，实现轻量化部署。

研究相关性：对于研究小样本SAR识别、角度鲁棒特征提取或知识蒸馏在遥感领域应用的研究者，该文提供了系统的角度自适应框架与可复现的实验基准，可直接借鉴其多源蒸馏与生成回放策略。

Multimodal-Interactive GCN-Guided Space-Frequency Implicit Fusion Network for Semantic Segmentation of Optical and SAR Images

多模态交互GCN引导的空-频隐式融合网络用于光学与SAR图像语义分割

2026-05-08
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Bingxia Xin，Hongqing Li，Chunyu Zhu，Rongyuan Dai，Qiong Wu

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3691414

Multi-source remote sensing images capture complementary information of ground objects and provide valuable inputs for semantic segmentation, and different sensors may produce modality inconsistencies and interference noise. Optical and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) segmentation is challenging for three main reasons. First, geometric distortions introduce scale inconsistencies. Second, speckle noise degrades signal quality. Third, subtle inter-class differences lead to misalignment between modalities. Collectively, these factors aggravate cross-modal contextual inconsistency and hinder accurate multimodal segmentation. We propose MSFNet, a graph-guided spatial-frequency implicit fusion network that unifies graph priors with continuous implicit representations. A dual-path encoder processes optical images with a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and SAR with a Dynamic Adaptive Graph Convolutional Network(DA-GCN) to capture topological/scattering structures. Fusion is performed by Graph-guided Spatial Implicit Sampling (GSIS) for geometry-aware alignment in the continuous coordinate space and Graph-guided Fourier Implicit Sampling(GFIS) for frequency-aware detail compensation and global context modulation. A multi-scale decoder yields fine-grained boundaries. Radar jamming recognition is a crucial step for implementing effective anti-jamming measures. In modern open and dynamic electromagnetic environments, radar systems continuously face new jamming categories introduced by adversaries. Most of the existing radar jamming recognition methods operate based on the assumption of a closed set. Due to the emergence of new threats, it is necessary to conduct time-consuming re-training of the entire model. In the radar scenario, replay-free restriction and the subtle characteristic distribution differences of various categories of jamming make the incremental recognition of radar jamming difficult. To address this issue, we propose a Scalable Sub space with Similarity Completion (S3C) framework for incremental recognition of radar jamming without replay. First, to resolve the stability-plasticity dilemma between preserving old knowledge and learning diverse jamming categories, we design a scalable subspace ensemble network, which independently trains lightweight adapters for each incremental session, thereby creating scalable subspaces to exploit session-specific features. Second, we design a similarity-based prototype completion method under the replay free setting, which exploits semantic similarities between prototypes of old and new categories. Finally, to optimize decision-making in multi-subspace feature spaces, a particle swarm optimization-driven subspace re-weighting strategy is introduced during inference. Extensive experiments on both simulated and real-world measured radar jamming datasets validate the effectiveness of S3C for radar jamming class-incremental recognition.
