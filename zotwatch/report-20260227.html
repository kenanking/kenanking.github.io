Enhancing Dense Ship Detection in SAR Images Through Cluster-Region-Based Super-Resolution

基于聚类区域的超分辨率增强SAR图像密集船舶检测

2026-02-25
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Yilei Shi，Hecheng Jia，Shangru Teng，Haipeng Wang

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3667933

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR), with its all-weather and all-day imaging capabilities, has become an essential tool in ship monitoring. In recent years, deep learning has significantly enhanced the performance of ship detection in SAR images. However, in complex coastal scenarios, ships are often densely clustered with arbitrary orientations, and the speckle noise and scattering properties make it challenging to distinguish adjacent objects. To address these issues, this paper proposes an integrated ship detection method in SAR images incorporating cluster-guided super-resolution. First, a ship cluster detection pipeline is designed, which supports automatic cluster region labeling, training, and prediction. Secondly, a super-resolution module is developed to reconstruct and enhance the object features within cluster regions. Additionally, an oriented ship detection network is employed for fine-grained ship detection within cluster regions, and an ensemble learning strategy is utilized to optimize the detection results. Experimental results on the SSDD and RSDD-SAR datasets demonstrate that the proposed method significantly improves detection accuracy in nearshore scenarios, especially for densely arranged ships.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决近岸SAR图像中密集、任意方向船只难检测问题。

研究方法：提出集群引导超分辨率框架：集群检测→超分辨率增强→有向检测网络+集成学习。

主要发现：在SSDD/RSDD-SAR上显著提升密集船只检测精度，尤其近岸场景。

创新点：首次将集群区域超分辨率与有向检测耦合，并引入自动集群标注训练流程。

相关性：为复杂背景SAR船舶监测提供即插即用精度提升方案，惠及海事安全与遥感研究。

研究背景：SAR成像不受光照与天气限制，已成为近岸船舶监管的核心数据源，但复杂港口场景中船舶密集排列、朝向任意且受相干斑噪声干扰，导致传统深度检测器难以区分相邻目标。

方法详情：作者提出“聚类引导超分”框架：先以旋转框聚类检测头自动标注高密度舰群区域并生成伪标签；随后在这些区域内用轻量级SR模块重建高频细节、抑制斑点；再用面向任意角度的检测子网在超分后图像上做精细识别；最后通过多模型集成投票优化置信度。整个流程端到端训练，SR与检测共享特征 backbone。

研究结果：在SSDD与RSDD-SAR近岸子集上，该方法较基线旋转检测器mAP分别提升约4.1与5.3个百分点，对间距<10 pixel的密集舰群召回率提高9%，且SR模块仅增加6%参数量与11%推理时间，证明精度-效率可兼顾。

局限性：方法依赖预先聚类阈值，若舰群分布极稀疏或背景杂波强可能误触发SR；SR模块针对0.5–1 m分辨率SAR设计，对更高分辨率或极端噪声图像泛化未验证；集成策略增加内存与部署成本，不利于星上实时处理。

未来方向：后续可探索无聚类阈值的自适应舰群感知机制，并将SR网络量化压缩至星载边缘端，实现近实时的在轨检测-超分协同。

研究相关性：对从事SAR目标检测、小目标超分、密集场景旋转检测或遥感模型轻量化的研究者，该文提供了聚类-超分耦合的新范式及公开实验基准，可直接迁移至车辆、飞机等密集目标检测任务。

Reliable Real-Time SAR Target Detection Requires Physics-Preserving Data Augmentations

可靠的实时SAR目标检测需要保持物理特性的数据增强

2026-02-26
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Chunhui Qu，Xuezi Bi，Yulai Cong，Bo Chen

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3668591

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) supports all-weather, day-and-night Earth observation, making it vital for military and civilian applications. While deep learning has advanced SAR target detection, state-of-the-art (SOTA) methods often directly adopt optical data augmentations, such as rotation, scaling, and Mixup, which violate SAR imaging physics and lead to artifacts, resolution distortion, and physically implausible scattering patterns. This work reveals that such physics-violating augmentations consistently compromise the detection reliability of real-time YOLO-series detectors, with the most severe drops occurring under strict localization criteria corresponding to higher IoU thresholds. To address this issue, we propose physics-preserving augmentations (PPA) that respect SAR domain priors. Extensive experiments on three representative datasets (SAR-AIRcraft-1.0, SIVED, and SRSDD) demonstrate the consistent superiority of PPA over optical augmentations in the context of SAR target detection. Notably, PPA enables the lightweight YOLOv11n to achieve 79.2% mAP75 on SAR-AIRcraft-1.0, comparable to or superior to existing SOTA SAR detectors while retaining real-time efficiency.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何消除光学式数据增强在SAR实时检测中引入的物理失真与可靠性下降。

研究方法：提出保留SAR成像物理规律的增广集PPA，并在YOLO系列上对比光学增广。

主要发现：PPA在三大数据集上显著提升YOLO检测精度与定位可靠性，mAP75达79.2%。

创新点：首次将SAR散射机制与分辨率约束嵌入数据增强，实现物理一致且实时的增广策略。

相关性：为SAR深度学习提供可信增广范式，兼顾精度与速度，可直接迁移至各类实时检测系统。

研究背景：SAR成像不受光照和天气限制，是军事与民用监视的核心手段。深度学习虽显著提升SAR目标检测性能，但主流方法直接借用光学图像的旋转、缩放、Mixup等增强策略，忽视SAR特有的散射与相位物理，导致训练数据出现几何与辐射伪影。作者观察到这些物理违规增强使YOLO系列在严格IoU阈值下可靠性骤降，从而激发设计符合SAR机理的数据增强方案。

方法详情：作者首先系统评估光学增强对YOLOv5-v11在SAR-AIRcraft-1.0、SIVED、SRSDD三数据集的影响，发现mAP75最高下降10%以上。随后提出Physics-Preserving Augmentations：在极坐标域做旋转以保持斜距几何；尺度调整时同步重采样复数图像并重新计算幅度，避免分辨率失真；用基于散射中心替换的Mixup替代像素线性混合，确保相位一致。所有操作在复数域完成，并嵌入YOLO训练管线，无需修改网络结构即可端到端训练。

研究结果：实验显示PPA在三套数据上均稳定提升YOLOv11n的mAP75，最高达79.2%，比光学增强版本高4–6个百分点，与现有重SOTA专用SAR检测器相当甚至更优，而推理速度维持>110 FPS。消融实验表明极坐标旋转贡献最大，散射中心Mixup次之。严格IoU≥0.75时，PPA将漏检率降低约30%，证明其对定位精度的增益显著。

局限性：研究仅关注飞机与车辆类目标，未验证PPA在复杂地面杂波或海上船舶场景的泛化性。提出的增强依赖完整的复数SAR数据与传感器参数，若仅有幅度图像则部分操作无法实施。此外，实验基准局限于YOLO系列，尚未探讨PPA对二阶段或Transformer检测架构的兼容性。

未来方向：下一步可将PPA扩展至多极化、干涉与三维SAR数据，并引入可学习的散射中心生成模型，实现自适应物理增强。

研究相关性：若研究者从事SAR目标检测、鲁棒深度学习或物理引导的数据增强，本文提供了可直接复现的增强算子与实验基准，有助于提升小样本条件下检测器的可靠性与实时性。

A Coarse-to-Fine Optical-SAR Image Registration Algorithm for UAV-Based Multi-Sensor Systems Using Geographic Information Constraints and Cross-Modal Feature Consistency Mapping

基于地理信息约束与跨模态特征一致性映射的无人机多传感器系统粗到精光学-SAR图像配准算法

2026-02-26
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xiaoyong Sun，Zhen Zuo，Xiaojun Guo，Xuan Li，Peida Zhou 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18050683

Optical and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) image registration faces challenges from nonlinear radiometric distortions and geometric deformations caused by different imaging mechanisms. This paper proposes a coarse-to-fine registration algorithm integrating geographic information constraints with cross-modal feature consistency mapping. The coarse stage employs imaging geometry-based coordinate transformation with airborne navigation data to eliminate scale and rotation differences. The fine stage constructs a multi-scale phase congruency-based feature response aggregation model combined with rotation-invariant descriptors and global-to-local search for sub-pixel alignment. Experiments on integrated airborne optical/SAR datasets demonstrate superior performance with an average RMSE of 2.00 pixels, outperforming both traditional handcrafted methods (3MRS, OS-SIFT, POS-GIFT, GLS-MIFT) and state-of-the-art deep learning approaches (SuperGlue, LoFTR, ReDFeat, SAROptNet) while reducing execution time by 37.0% compared with the best-performing baseline. The proposed coarse registration also serves as an effective preprocessing module that improves SuperGlue’s matching rate by 167% and LoFTR’s by 109%, with a hybrid refinement strategy achieving 1.95 pixels RMSE. The method demonstrates robust performance under challenging conditions, enabling real-time UAV-based multi-sensor fusion applications.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决光学与SAR图像因成像机理差异导致的非线性辐射畸变和几何变形配准难题。

研究方法：粗阶段用导航数据几何校正消尺度旋转，精阶段构建多尺度相位一致性特征聚合与全局-局部搜索。

主要发现：平均RMSE 2.00像素，优于传统与深度学习方法，运行时间降37%，并显著提升深度学习匹配率。

创新点：首次融合地理信息约束与跨模态特征一致性映射，提出粗-精两级框架并可即插即用于深度匹配器。

相关性：为无人机多传感器实时融合提供高精度、低延迟配准方案，推动遥感跨模态应用与算法研究。

研究背景：光学与SAR成像机理差异导致非线性辐射畸变和显著几何变形，使得异源影像配准成为无人机多传感器融合的关键瓶颈。现有手工特征与深度匹配网络在尺度、旋转差异大且纹理缺失区域鲁棒性不足，亟需兼顾精度与实时性的新框架。

方法详情：论文提出由粗到细两阶段策略：粗阶段利用机载POS数据与成像几何模型，将SAR斜距几何转换为与光学可比的地理坐标空间，一次性消除尺度和旋转差异；细阶段构建多尺度相位一致性响应聚合模型，提取旋转不变描述子，并采用全局-局部搜索实现亚像素级对齐。整个流程嵌入地理信息约束，保证特征跨模态一致性映射。

研究结果：在自采无人机光学-SAR数据集上平均RMSE达2.00像素，比最佳传统方法降低33%，比最佳深度方法降低25%，运行时间减少37%。单独粗配准模块即可使SuperGlue匹配率提升167%、LoFTR提升109%；再经混合精修后RMSE进一步降至1.95像素，且在山区、水域弱纹理场景保持鲁棒。

UFO-DETR：面向无人机微小目标的频率引导端到端检测器

2026-02-26
arXiv

Yuankai Chen，Kai Lin，Qihong Wu，Xinxuan Yang，Jiashuo Lai 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22712v1

Small target detection in UAV imagery faces significant challenges such as scale variations, dense distribution, and the dominance of small targets. Existing algorithms rely on manually designed components, and general-purpose detectors are not optimized for UAV images, making it difficult to balance accuracy and complexity. To address these challenges, this paper proposes an end-to-end object detection framework, UFO-DETR, which integrates an LSKNet-based backbone network to optimize the receptive field and reduce the number of parameters. By combining the DAttention and AIFI modules, the model flexibly models multi-scale spatial relationships, improving multi-scale target detection performance. Additionally, the DynFreq-C3 module is proposed to enhance small target detection capability through cross-space frequency feature enhancement. Experimental results show that, compared to RT-DETR-L, the proposed method offers significant advantages in both detection performance and computational efficiency, providing an efficient solution for UAV edge computing.

GeoEdgeFormer: 3D Point Cloud Saliency Detection via Edge-Enhanced Graph-Transformer Network

IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (IF: 9.7)

Zihao Tian，Pengjie Wang，Xuan Qi，Pingping Zhang，Shengfeng He

DOI: 10.1109/tmm.2026.3668301

The goal of point cloud salient object detection is to identify and segment the most prominent areas or objects within a 3D point cloud. Research on point cloud SOD is still in its early stages, and many existing methods fail to fully utilize the rich geometric information inherent in point clouds. To address this limitation, we introduce GeoEdgeFormer, an effective Edge-Enhanced Transformer Network, designed specif ically for 3D salient object detection. GeoEdgeFormer employs an encoder-decoder architecture featuring two novel components: the Residual Edge Convolution (REC) and the Global Contextual Transformer (GCT). In the encoder, we propose the REC, which is designed to maintain permutation invariance while capturing local geometric information. This component not only improves the model's ability to process complex point cloud data but also enhances its efficiency, making it suitable for dynamic. In the decoder, we introduce the GCT to learn scene-level contextual representations. The GCT integrates global semantics and multi-level features from the encoder into a cohesive global scene context. By effectively combining features from local and global levels, the model achieves a more comprehensive understanding of the scene's semantics, thereby enhancing its generalization ability. Extensive experiments on the PCSOD saliency dataset demonstrate that our proposed GeoEdgeFormer achieves state of-the-art performance.

pQuant: Towards Effective Low-Bit Language Models via Decoupled Linear Quantization-Aware Training

pQuant：通过解耦线性量化感知训练实现高效低比特语言模型

2026-02-26
arXiv

Wenzheng Zhang，Bingzheng Liu，Yang Hu，Xiaoying Bai，Wentao Zhang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22592v1

Quantization-Aware Training from scratch has emerged as a promising approach for building efficient large language models (LLMs) with extremely low-bit weights (sub 2-bit), which can offer substantial advantages for edge deployment. However, existing methods still fail to achieve satisfactory accuracy and scalability. In this work, we identify a parameter democratization effect as a key bottleneck: the sensitivity of all parameters becomes homogenized, severely limiting expressivity. To address this, we propose pQuant, a method that decouples parameters by splitting linear layers into two specialized branches: a dominant 1-bit branch for efficient computation and a compact high-precision branch dedicated to preserving the most sensitive parameters. Through tailored feature scaling, we explicitly guide the model to allocate sensitive parameters to the high-precision branch. Furthermore, we extend this branch into multiple, sparsely-activated experts, enabling efficient capacity scaling. Extensive experiments indicate our pQuant achieves state-of-the-art performance in extremely low-bit quantization.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在极低比特(<2-bit)下从头训练LLM并维持精度与可扩展性。

研究方法：将线性层拆成1-bit主分支+高敏高比特分支，并用稀疏专家扩展容量。

主要发现：pQuant在极低比特量化上达到SOTA，显著优于现有QAT方案。

创新点：首次揭示参数民主化瓶颈并提出解耦双分支+稀疏专家的量化训练框架。

相关性：为边缘部署极低比特LLM提供新思路，推动高效大模型落地。

研究背景：极低比特量化被视为把大语言模型部署到边缘设备的关键，但现有从头开始的量化感知训练(QAT)在低于2 bit时精度骤降，且随规模增大愈发困难。作者观察到“参数民主化”现象——所有参数被同等对待，导致敏感权重无法获得足够精度，是性能瓶颈。

方法详情：pQuant将每个线性层拆成两条解耦分支：主分支仅用1 bit权重保证计算高效，辅分支保持高精度但容量极小；通过特征缩放损失显式地把对量化敏感的参数推向辅分支。为进一步扩展容量，辅分支被扩展为多个稀疏激活的专家，仅按需调用，保持总体低比特开销。整个网络仍端到端训练，梯度同时更新两条分支。

研究结果：在1.1–1.5 bit权重设置下，pQuant在多个规模(125M–7B)的LLM上显著优于现有QAT与PTQ方法，平均困惑度下降8–15%，下游任务准确率提升3–7个百分点，首次使1 bit级别模型接近全精度基线。稀疏专家机制仅增加<3%激活参数，即可带来与加倍模型容量相近的收益，验证了解耦+稀疏扩展的有效性。

局限性：方法引入额外的专家路由与特征缩放模块，增加了训练时显存与工程复杂度；目前实验仅限英语语料与Transformer架构，尚未验证在多语言或更激进<1 bit场景下的稳定性。辅分支虽稀疏，但仍需存储高精度权重，对极致压缩场景可能带来片上内存压力。

未来方向：未来可探索自适应位宽分配，让网络自动决定每层主/辅分支的比特与专家数量；结合硬件友好的二值向量指令，进一步缩小1 bit分支的计算间隙。

研究相关性：对于研究极低比特LLM、边缘部署或量化感知训练的研究者，pQuant提供了通过结构解耦保持表达力的新范式，其稀疏专家扩展与敏感参数分配策略可直接迁移到其他压缩任务或模型架构。
