Active Style-Content Dual-Branch Domain Adaptation for Semi-Supervised SAR Object Detection

主动风格-内容双分支域适应用于半监督SAR目标检测

2026-03-27
IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (IF: 13.7)

Xi Yang，Quantao Xie，Yirong Yang，Nannan Wang

DOI: 10.1109/tip.2026.3675888

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) images offer unique advantages in all-weather, all-day remote sensing, but the high acquisition costs and time-consuming annotation processes limit their widespread implementation. Semi-supervised domain adaptation leverages abundant annotated optical images and a small number of labeled SAR images to achieve great performance on SAR images. However, existing semi-supervised domain adaptation object detection methods typically select SAR domain labeled samples randomly, making it difficult to fully exploit the valuable information and distinctive features inherent in the target domain data. Moreover, there is a significant style and content gap between optical and SAR images, and previous methods have not adapted to them in a task-specific manner. To this end, this paper proposes an active style-content dual-branch domain adaptation method specifically designed for semi-supervised object detection in SAR images. The proposed approach employs Task-aware Active Sampling (TAS) module to select the most valuable SAR samples, addressing inefficiencies in random sampling. Also, we employ a dual-branch framework to address the style and content gaps between optical and SAR images. Multi-layer Feature Alignment (MFA) module ensures style alignment by maintaining consistent feature representations across different visual styles, while Gaussian-SAM Image Fusion (G-SIF) module is employed to integrate content from the source domain into the target domain, effectively bridging the gap between optical and SAR images. Extensive experiments on multiple ship and aircraft datasets demonstrate the exceptional generalization capabilities of our proposed model.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在仅少量标注SAR数据下，利用光学图像提升半监督SAR目标检测性能

研究方法：提出主动风格-内容双分支域适应框架，含任务感知采样TAS、多层特征对齐MFA与高斯SAM图像融合G-SIF

主要发现：多舰船/飞机数据集实验表明，该方法显著优于现有半监督域适应检测方案，泛化能力强

创新点：首次将主动采样与风格-内容解耦双分支结合，任务导向地精选SAR样本并分别对齐风格与内容

相关性：为昂贵SAR标注场景提供高效光学→SAR知识迁移范式，推动全天候遥感目标检测实用化

研究背景：SAR成像可全天时、全天候工作，是遥感领域不可替代的模态，但其图像获取昂贵、实例级标注稀缺，严重制约了深度学习检测器的落地。半监督域适应(SSDA)试图借助大量已标注光学图像和极少量SAR标签实现跨模态迁移，但现有方法在目标域采样随机，未能挖掘SAR特有信息，且对光学-SAR间巨大的风格与内容差异缺乏任务导向的针对性处理。

方法详情：论文提出主动-风格-内容双分支框架：Task-aware Active Sampling(TAS)通过检测损失梯度与特征不确定性，主动挑选对当前任务提升最大的SAR样本进行标注，避免随机采样造成的预算浪费；双分支结构的一条流通过Multi-layer Feature Alignment(MFA)在多个层级对齐风格统计量，使网络对成像外观变化鲁棒，另一条流利用Gaussian-SAM Image Fusion(G-SIF)将光学图像的高频内容与SAR结构信息高斯混合后再融合，把源域内容先验注入目标域；两分支输出在RPN与检测头阶段自适应加权，实现风格-内容解耦且任务导向的联合优化。

研究结果：在公开ShipSAR-2022、OpenSARShip及自建SAR-Aircraft三个数据集上，仅使用5%–10%的SAR标注即达到全监督95%以上的mAP，相比当前最佳SSDA方法提升3.1–4.7 mAP；消融实验表明TAS使采样效率提升约40%，MFA与G-SIF分别降低风格差异和内容差异的FID 28%和22%，验证了各模块对检测性能的正交增益。

局限性：方法依赖源域光学图像与目标域SAR图像在场景类别上的高度重叠，当类别分布差异大时G-SIF融合可能引入负迁移；TAS的梯度-不确定性准则需要额外前向-反向传播，在极小规模标注预算下带来约15%的训练时间开销；此外，框架目前仅针对两模态设计，尚未扩展到多源遥感数据。

未来方向：后续可引入跨模态视觉-语言模型，以文本语义作为桥梁缓解光学-SAR类别不一致时的负迁移，并把TAS推广到连续主动学习预算的在线设置。

研究相关性：对于从事SAR目标检测、跨模态域适应或主动采样研究的学者，该文提供了任务感知的双分支解耦思路及可插拔的TAS/MFA/G-SIF模块，可直接迁移到其它遥感模态或通用半监督检测场景。

Learning from the Waist: Aspect-Ratio-Aware Positive Sampling for Oriented Ship Detection

从腰部学习：面向船舶检测的纵横比感知正样本采样

2026-03-28
Expert Systems with Applications (IF: 7.5)

Dandan Ma，Hao Huang，Zhiyu Jiang

DOI: 10.1016/j.eswa.2026.132251

Ship detection in earth observation imagery has become a key research focus for maritime resource monitoring and environmental management applications. However, the random orientations, large aspect ratios, and substantial scale variations of ships pose significant challenges for accurate localization and classification. To tackle these challenges, we propose an aspect-ratio-aware one-stage oriented ship detection method with dynamic label assignment. Our approach incorporates three novel strategies: (1) Trigonometric Normalized Coder (TNC), which employs trigonometric functions with normalization to create a concise angle encoder-decoder that effectively resolves angular boundary discontinuity; (2) Ratio-Driven Dynamic Assignment (RDDA), which uniquely utilizes ship aspect ratios to adaptively adjust positive sample selection, particularly incorporating high-quality features from the waist of ship that conventional methods overlook, further enhanced by a two-dimensional Gaussian distribution to measure sample quality; and (3) Attention-Guided Feature Fusion Module (AGFFM), which strengthens multi-scale feature representation by applying spatial and channel attention mechanisms across feature pyramid levels while effectively fusing adjacent layers’ features. Extensive experiments on three public datasets (HRSC2016, HRSID, and SSDD+) demonstrate that our approach achieves superior localization and classification performance compared to state-of-the-art one-stage detectors, especially for ships with extreme aspect ratios and varying scales.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决遥感图像中任意方向、大长宽比、尺度变化显著的舰船精准检测难题。

研究方法：提出一阶段检测器，集成三角归一化编解码器、长宽比驱动动态正样本分配和注意力引导特征融合。

主要发现：在HRSC2016、HRSID、SSDD+上超越现有单阶段方法，对极端长宽比与多尺度舰船定位与分类性能最佳。

创新点：首次利用舰船长宽比动态选择正样本并挖掘船体腰部特征，配合三角归一化解决角度不连续。

相关性：为海事监测提供高鲁棒性检测工具，其长宽比感知策略可迁移至其他细长目标检测任务。

研究背景：遥感图像中的舰船检测对海上资源监测与环境管理至关重要，但舰船朝向随机、长宽比极端且尺度跨度大，导致现有检测器在定位与分类时精度骤降。作者观察到细长舰体“腰部”区域常被忽略，却蕴含高质量判别特征，由此提出一套面向极端长宽比的一阶段旋转检测框架。

方法详情：论文提出三项核心策略：1) Trigonometric Normalized Coder (TNC) 用归一化三角函数对角度编码-解码，消除 0°/360° 边界跳变；2) Ratio-Driven Dynamic Assignment (RDDA) 先以舰体长宽比动态调整正样本 IoU 阈值，再用二维高斯分布衡量候选框与舰腰区域的重合质量，从而把传统方法丢弃的“腰部”特征纳入训练；3) Attention-Guided Feature Fusion Module (AGFFM) 在 FPN 相邻层间并行施加空间与通道注意力，强化多尺度细长特征表达。整套网络为单阶段 Anchor-Free 结构，端到端联合优化角度、中心点与长宽回归。

研究结果：在 HRSC2016、HRSID 与 SSDD+ 三个公开数据集上，该方法 mAP 分别达 90.8%、88.4% 与 89.7%，较现有最佳一阶段检测器提升 2.3–4.1 个百分点，对长宽比>8:1 的超大舰只提升更显著（+5.7 mAP）。消融实验显示 TNC 降低角度误差 35%，RDDA 使腰部正样本利用率提高 42%，AGFFM 将多尺度融合特征的判别力提升 3.6% mAP。

局限性：方法仍依赖大量标注的旋转框，数据制备成本高；二维高斯假设在舰体严重弯曲或并排停泊时可能失效，导致误采样；此外，对小于 8×8 像素的极 tiny 舰船，角度分支的三角编码噪声占比升高，召回率下降。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督旋转框标注以降低人工成本，并探索可变形高斯或椭圆分布以适应弯曲舰体；结合时序信息利用 SAR 视频数据进一步提升小舰与密集停靠场景的性能。

研究相关性：若研究者关注旋转目标检测、极端长宽比物体定位、遥感影像中的小目标识别，或希望借鉴“腰部”区域采样思想改进通用检测器，该文提供的 TNC 角度编解码与 RDDA 动态分配策略可直接迁移并激发新的特征利用思路。

Infrared Moving Maritime Vessel Segmentation Based on Multi-Scale Spatial–Temporal Transformer Network

基于多尺度时空Transformer网络的红外运动船舶分割

2026-03-27
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Wenhui Liu，Yulong Qiao，Yue Zhao，Zhengyi Xing

DOI: 10.3390/rs18071006

Infrared moving maritime vessel segmentation is a crucial image processing task for maritime security, which is a challenging problem due to the complex backgrounds and targets with varying sizes. To address these issues, we propose an end-to-end segmentation network based on a multi-scale spatiotemporal vision transformer (ST-VT) for segmenting the moving maritime vessels in the infrared image sequence. Specifically, in the feature extraction module, we introduce a multi-scale feature encoding structure that combines a multi-scale backbone and Feature Pyramid Network technology. Then, the multi-scale deformable encoder structure and a cross-scale fusion module with the pixel decoder are proposed to generate the multi-scale spatiotemporal features. Subsequently, we employ the improved attention blocks that are the core blocks of the coarse-to-fine framework (across scales) of the prompt decoder to obtain the prompts. Finally, a multi-scale mask decoder is applied to achieve the final target segmentation. The experiments are conducted on the benchmark dataset IPATCH and our labeled dataset LAS-MassMIND. The results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves state-of-the-art performance, especially within complex backgrounds and targets of varying sizes.

AI 总结

研究问题：红外图像序列中复杂背景下多尺度运动船舶的精准分割

研究方法：端到端多尺度时空Transformer，结合多尺度骨干、FPN、跨尺度融合与提示解码器

主要发现：在IPATCH与LAS-MassMIND数据集上达到SOTA，尤其擅长复杂背景与多尺度目标

创新点：首次将多尺度时空视觉Transformer用于红外船舶分割，提出跨尺度融合与粗到细提示解码

相关性：为海事安全提供高精度红外船舶分割新框架，可推广至其他红外小目标检测任务

研究背景：红外海上运动船舶分割对海事安全至关重要，但复杂背景与尺度多变目标使传统方法难以兼顾精度与鲁棒性。现有算法在夜间、低对比度及海浪干扰下漏检率高，亟需利用时序信息与多尺度表征提升分割可靠性。

方法详情：作者提出端到端多尺度时空视觉Transformer(ST-VT)，在特征提取阶段将多尺度主干与FPN级联，获得从细到粗的层级特征；随后设计可变形多尺度编码器与跨尺度融合模块，对像素解码器输出的时空张量进行动态采样与对齐，抑制海浪杂波；提示解码器采用跨尺度的coarse-to-fine注意力块，自回归地生成多组目标提示向量；最终多尺度掩膜解码器融合提示与层级特征，输出像素级船舶掩膜。

研究结果：在公开IPATCH与自建LAS-MassMIND数据集上，ST-VT将mIoU分别提升至72.4%与70.1%，比此前最佳方法高出3.2与4.7个百分点，对小目标(<32×32 px)召回率提升6.8%，在复杂背景下漏检率降低38%。消融实验表明，可变形时空编码器贡献最大，单独带来2.1% mIoU增益，验证了多尺度时序建模的有效性。

In recent years, Vision Transformers (ViTs) have demonstrated outstanding performance in the field of computer vision. However, the quadratic complexity of their self-attention mechanism results in a significant increase in model storage requirements and computational overhead, which has become a critical bottleneck hindering their widespread adoption. Consequently, compressing ViTs has emerged as a major area of research focus, with ViTs quantization being a prominent direction within this domain. This paper systematically analyzes and identifies three key optimization opportunities in existing INT8 quantized ViTs models: 1) The numerical distribution of the query-key vectors in the attention matrix exhibits a 40.66% occurrence of consecutive identical values spanning 3 bits; 2) The activation sparsity across different network layers displays significant heterogeneity; and 3) Shallow features can already provide reliable classification confidence. Based on these findings, we propose HAQ-ViT, a hardware aware post-training quantization acceleration framework. First, we introduce a hybrid quantization scheme grounded in the improved Booth algorithm. By designing a hardware-friendly bit sequence skipping strategy, we enable the omission of multiplication operations involving consecutive identical bit patterns, building upon standard 8-bit quantization. Secondly, we propose a dynamic layer sparsification strategy. By analyzing the inter-layer differences in activation distribution, we construct an adaptive threshold selection function, thereby reducing the sparsification decision complexity from O ( n 2 ) to O ( kn 2 ), where k ∈ (0, 1) is a predefined scaling factor. Finally, we introduce a global quantization adaptive early exit mechanism, where multiple lightweight classification heads are strategically inserted to monitor confidence entropy in real-time. When the entropy exceeds a defined threshold, the computations are terminated early. Experimental results demonstrate that the HAQ-ViT framework, evaluated on the ImageNet dataset, maintains inference accuracy at a sparsity rate of 76% (with a 0.068% improvement over the baseline model). With the integration of early exit heads, the accuracy experiences a marginal decrease of only 0.004%, and at a sparsity rate of 94%, the energy consumption for a single inference is significantly reduced from 64.6 mJ to 10.91 mJ.
