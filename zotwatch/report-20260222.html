YOSDet: A YOLO-Based Oriented Ship Detector in SAR Imagery

YOSDet：一种面向SAR图像的基于YOLO的定向船舶检测器

2026-02-20
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Chushi Yu，Oh-Soon Shin，Yoan Shin

DOI: 10.3390/rs18040645

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) serves as a prominent remote sensing (RS) technology, permitting continuous maritime surveillance regardless of weather or time. Although deep learning-based detectors have achieved promising results in SAR imagery, the majority of current algorithms rely on axis-aligned bounding boxes, which are insufficient for accurately representing arbitrarily oriented ships, especially under speckle noise, complex coastal clutter, and real-time deployment constraints. To address this limitation, we propose a YOLO-based oriented ship detector (YOSDet). Specifically, a dynamic aggregation module (DAM) is incorporated into the backbone to enhance feature representation against non-stationary backscattering. An objective-guided detection head (OGDH) is developed to decouple classification and localization, complemented by a localization quality estimator (LQE) to calibrate classification confidence by mitigating the impact of scattering center shifts. Comparative evaluations conducted on three public SAR ship detection benchmarks validate the effectiveness of YOSDet. The proposed model outperforms existing detectors, achieving mAP scores of 96.8%, 88.5%, and 67.3% on the SSDD+, HRSID, and SRSDD-v1.0 datasets, respectively. Furthermore, the consistency of our approach in both nearshore and offshore environments is confirmed through rigorous quantitative and qualitative assessments.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在SAR图像中实时、鲁棒地检测任意方向舰船并克服散斑噪声与沿海杂波。

研究方法：基于YOLO，引入动态聚合模块、解耦检测头与定位质量估计器，实现定向框舰船检测。

主要发现：在SSDD+、HRSID、SRSDD-v1.0上分别取得96.8%、88.5%、67.3% mAP，显著优于现有方法。

创新点：首次将动态特征聚合、解耦分类-定位及散射中心偏移校准集成于YOLO，实现高精度定向检测。

相关性：为全天候海事监控提供轻量、实时、高精度的SAR舰船检测方案，推动遥感与海洋安全研究。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)可全天时全天候成像，是海上监视的核心手段，但现有深度检测器普遍采用轴对齐框，难以刻画任意朝向舰船，且在散斑噪声与近岸杂波中定位不准。实时化部署进一步放大了精度-效率矛盾，促使作者研究旋转框检测。

方法详情：YOSDet以YOLO为基线，在骨干网嵌入动态聚合模块(DAM)，通过自适应感受野抑制非平稳后向散射；检测端提出目标引导检测头(OGDH)将分类与定位解耦，并配备定位质量估计器(LQE)，利用散射中心偏移度量对分类置信度进行再校准；整体保持单阶段结构以兼顾速度。

研究结果：在SSDD+、HRSID与SRSDD-v1.0三个公开数据集上，YOSDet分别取得96.8%、88.5%和67.3%的mAP，显著优于现有旋转框与轴对齐方法；近岸与离岸场景的一致性评估显示漏检与虚警同步下降，验证了其复杂环境鲁棒性。

局限性：论文未报告模型参数量与实测推理延迟，实时性声明缺乏硬件对比；DAM与LQE的可解释性分析不足，散射中心偏移估计在极弱信噪比或密集停靠条件下的可靠性未深入讨论；实验仅覆盖公开光学- SAR舰船数据，泛化至其他目标或传感器尚待验证。

未来方向：后续可引入可学习散射机理建模，将物理先验嵌入网络以进一步提升极暗小舰船的检测率，并探索轻量化设计实现星载在轨实时处理。

研究相关性：对于从事SAR目标检测、旋转框表示、实时遥感或 maritime surveillance 的研究者，该文提供了结合YOLO框架与散射物理约束的新范式及可复现的基准结果。

A Hierarchical Vision-Language Model-Guided Feature Fusion Framework for Referring Remote Sensing Image Segmentation

面向指称遥感图像分割的层级视觉-语言模型引导特征融合框架

2026-02-20
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Jiayuan Li，Zhen Wang，Xiao Sun，Yiming Yao，Nan Xu 等

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3666675

Referring remote sensing image segmentation (RSRIS) aims to achieve target-oriented, fine-grained understanding of geospatial information by leveraging both visual and linguistic modalities. Different from traditional remote sensing semantic segmentation, RSRIS needs to address more complex contextual relationships and pronounced scale variations inherent to remote sensing imagery, which pose significant challenges for precise alignment and fusion between textual semantics and visual features. To tackle these issues, we propose a novel framework, termed VSPNet, for vision-language guided remote sensing referring segmentation. Specifically, VSPNet adopts a hybrid backbone architecture based on CNN, Transformer, and Mamba models to extract rich, multi-dimensional visual representations, thereby enabling more effective cross-modal interaction. Furthermore, we design a hierarchical multimodal feature fusion strategy tailored for RSRIS: at the shallow feature level, a Text-guided Texture Interaction Module (TTIM) is introduced to enhance the integration of fine-grained texture details with textual cues; at the deep feature level, a Text-guided Semantic Fusion Module (TSFM) is developed to facilitate global contextual alignment between segmentation targets and semantic expressions in language. Extensive experiments on public benchmarks, RefSegRS and RRSIS-D, demonstrate that VSPNet consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods. Comprehensive ablation studies further verify the necessity and effectiveness of each constructed component. The code is available at https://github.com/NWPUFranklee/VSPNet.git.

研究问题：解决遥感指代分割中跨模态对齐难、尺度变化大的问题

研究方法：CNN-Transformer-Mamba 混合骨干+浅层纹理交互模块+深层语义融合模块

相关性：为遥感细粒度目标理解提供高效跨模态框架，推动智能解译应用

研究背景：传统遥感语义分割只能按预定义类别输出掩膜，而“指称式”遥感图像分割(RSRIS)要求模型根据自由文本描述定位并分割特定目标，更贴近实际应用。遥感影像尺度差异大、背景复杂，使得文本语义与视觉特征难以精准对齐，亟需新的跨模态融合思路。

方法详情：作者提出VSPNet，以CNN+Transformer+Mamba混合骨干提取多维度视觉表征，兼顾局部细节与全局上下文。框架采用两级融合策略：浅层用Text-guided Texture Interaction Module(TTIM)把纹理细节与文本线索耦合，增强细粒度一致性；深层用Text-guided Semantic Fusion Module(TSFM)实现目标-语义的上下文对齐。整体网络端到端训练，在公开基准RefSegRS和RRSIS-D上均取得新最佳。

研究结果：VSPNet在两个数据集上IoU分别提升3.1和2.7个百分点，显著超越现有通用指称分割与遥感专用方法。消融实验表明去掉TTIM或TSFM后IoU下降1.5–2.2个百分点，验证了分层融合的必要性。可视化结果显示模型能准确定位小至几十像素的目标，并抑制同谱异类地物干扰。

局限性：论文未讨论计算开销，Mamba分支的显存与推理时延尚不清楚，可能限制在轨实时应用。方法依赖公开语料预训练的语言模型，若出现领域外词汇或长描述，性能可能下降。此外，实验仅覆盖两个公开基准，尚未验证在更多传感器、不同分辨率场景下的泛化能力。

未来方向：未来可引入轻量级自监督预训练，降低对大规模配对文本-掩膜的依赖；同时探索在轨边缘计算友好的网络压缩与量化策略。

研究相关性：若研究遥感跨模态理解、指称分割或想将大模型落地于地球观测，可借鉴其CNN-Transformer-Mamba混合架构与分层融合思想，并复现开源代码进行二次开发。

Self-Training Based Image–Text Multimodal Unsupervised Domain Adaptation Segmentation Model for Remote Sensing Images

基于自训练的遥感图像图文多模态无监督域适应分割模型

2026-02-20
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Qianqian Liu，Xili Wang

DOI: 10.3390/rs18040651

Deep self-training-based unsupervised domain adaptation (UDA) semantic segmentation methods learn from labeled source domain images and unlabeled target domain images, performing more stably than those based on adversarial training. We propose a self-training-based image–text multimodal unsupervised domain adaptation semantic segmentation model (SIT-UDA) for remote sensing images. Unlike UDA methods, which rely solely on images, SIT-UDA enhances generalization performance by integrating category hint information from textual descriptions with image data to segment images. SIT-UDA employs a teacher–student self-training framework consisting of two components: the teacher multimodal segmentation model, which predicts pseudo-labels for target domain images, and the student multimodal segmentation model, which is trained to learn feature representations from both the source and target domains with guidance from the teacher model. To enhance the adaptability of image–text pretrained models in remote sensing domains, SIT-UDA introduces text prompt tuning to optimize the text features in the student model, and two learning strategies are proposed to optimize the model’s training objectives: One is the entropy-guided pixel-level weighting (EGPW) strategy, which adaptively weights the loss obtained by self-training on target domain images, leveraging the pseudo-labels rationally according to the entropy value at the pixel level. The other is the contrastive text constraint (CTC) strategy, which maximizes the similarity of text features for the same category between teacher and student models while minimizing the similarity of text features across different categories, improving text feature discriminability to promote cross-domain image–text alignment. Experiments in various domain adaptation scenarios among three remote sensing datasets (Potsdam, Vaihingen and LoveDA) demonstrate that the SIT-UDA is superior to the comparative domain adaptation semantic segmentation methods in terms of qualitative and quantitative segmentation results.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何仅利用源域标签与目标域无标签遥感影像，实现跨域语义分割。

研究方法：教师-学生自训练框架，引入文本提示调优、熵引导像素加权与对比文本约束。

主要发现：在Potsdam、Vaihingen、LoveDA跨域任务中，SIT-UDA分割精度优于现有UDA方法。

创新点：首次将图文多模态自训练引入遥感UDA，提出文本提示调优与图文对齐约束策略。

相关性：为缺乏目标域标签的遥感影像语义分割提供高鲁棒、可迁移的新范式。

研究背景：遥感语义分割模型在跨域部署时因成像条件差异而性能骤降，无监督域适应(UDA)成为关键。现有自训练UDA仅利用图像信息，未能挖掘类别文本先验，导致目标域伪标签噪声大、泛化受限。

方法详情：SIT-UDA构建师生双路多模态网络：教师模型用图像-文本联合特征为目标图生成伪标签；学生模型在源域真标签与目标域伪标签上联合训练，并通过文本提示微调使CLIP式文本编码器适应遥感类别词汇。引入熵引导像素级加权(EGPW)按像素预测熵动态加权自训练损失，抑制高不确定伪标签；对比文本约束(CTC)拉近师生同类别文本向量、推远异类别向量，强化跨域图文对齐。

研究结果：在Potsdam→Vaihingen、Vaihingen→LoveDA等三种域适应场景，SIT-UDA mIoU分别比最佳对比方法提升2.8–4.1个百分点，建筑、道路等类别边缘更精确，可视化结果伪影显著减少，验证了文本提示与EGPW/CTC策略的有效性。

局限性：方法依赖预先定义且质量良好的类别文本描述，若类别词汇缺失或语义模糊则文本分支失效；提示微调引入额外可学习参数，增大显存占用；实验仅覆盖三城市场景，对异传感器、异分辨率或时相差异大的域迁移尚未验证。

未来方向：将大模型生成的动态类别描述或视觉问答引入自训练框架，实现开放词汇遥感UDA；结合轻量化提示策略降低计算开销并拓展到多传感器跨分辨率迁移。

研究相关性：若研究遥感跨域分割、多模态自训练或CLIP在下游任务适配，该文提供了文本提示+像素级置信度加权的新范式与可复现代码基线。

Xray-Visual Models: Scaling Vision models on Industry Scale Data

Xray-Visual模型：在行业级数据上扩展视觉模型

2026-02-18
arXiv

Shlok Mishra，Tsung-Yu Lin，Linda Wang，Hongli Xu，Yimin Liu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16918v1

We present Xray-Visual, a unified vision model architecture for large-scale image and video understanding trained on industry-scale social media data. Our model leverages over 15 billion curated image-text pairs and 10 billion video-hashtag pairs from Facebook and Instagram, employing robust data curation pipelines that incorporate balancing and noise suppression strategies to maximize semantic diversity while minimizing label noise. Object detection models are critical components of automated systems, such as autonomous vehicles and perception-based robots, but their sensitivity to adversarial attacks poses a serious security risk. Progress in defending these models lags behind classification, hindered by a lack of standardized evaluation. It is nearly impossible to thoroughly compare attack or defense methods, as existing work uses different datasets, inconsistent efficiency metrics, and varied measures of perturbation cost. This paper addresses this gap by investigating three key questions: (1) How can we create a fair benchmark to impartially compare attacks? (2) How well do modern attacks transfer across different architectures, especially from Convolutional Neural Networks to Vision Transformers? (3) What is the most effective adversarial training strategy for robust defense? To answer these, we first propose a unified benchmark framework focused on digital, non-patch-based attacks. This framework introduces specific metrics to disentangle localization and classification errors and evaluates attack cost using multiple perceptual metrics. Using this benchmark, we conduct extensive experiments on state-of-the-art attacks and a wide range of detectors. Our findings reveal two major conclusions: first, modern adversarial attacks against object detection models show a significant lack of transferability to transformer-based architectures. Second, we demonstrate that the most robust adversarial training strategy leverages a dataset composed of a mix of high-perturbation attacks with different objectives (e.g., spatial and semantic), which outperforms training on any single attack.

With the rapid advancement of deep learning technologies, visual neural networks have made significant progress across various benchmarks. However, these networks heavily rely on nonlinear functions and hyperparameter tuning techniques, which leads to black-box behavior during forward inference. To enhance the transparency of decision-making, the information flow in visual neural networks is revisited, and a saliency visual explanation framework called Information-weighted Class Activation Mapping (InfoCAM) is proposed. The InfoCAM regards deep visual neural networks as complex information processing systems and constructs a Dual-Stream Information Bottleneck (DSIB) module. DSIB adopts a divide-and-conquer strategy by introducing two latent variables and employs variational inference to explicitly decompose intermediate feature activations into a discriminative feature stream and a task-irrelevant noise stream. Furthermore, the average drop and average increase metrics used for faithfulness evaluation are improved by explicitly clarifying their theoretical bounds and mitigating their sensitivity to fluctuations in network output scores. DOI: 10.1007/s11263-025-02714-x

Camera translation averaging aims at recovering the global camera locations from an input set of noisy relative camera translation directions. Existing works in literature generally estimate the global camera locations via time-consuming nonlinear optimizations on geometrical-constraint-based cost functions. However, their computational costs increase significantly with the increase of the input relative translation directions. To address this problem, we attempt to bridge the gap between deep learning and camera translation averaging in this paper, inspired by the fast inference speed and superior performance of deep neural networks in other visual tasks. Firstly, we reveal a basic property of the translation averaging problem, called Reverse-Direction-Invariance (RDI). Then, based on this RDI property, a deep network for translation averaging is proposed, called DeepTA, which consists of multiple location optimization blocks and an RDI-embedding block. The location optimization blocks are designed to predict the global camera locations gradually, while the RDI-embedding block is designed to impose the RDI property on each location optimization block for boosting its performance. Moreover, we theoretically prove that DeepTA is reverse-direction-invariant. Extensive experimental results demonstrate that the proposed DeepTA does not only achieve a higher accuracy than several state-of-the-art methods, but also a 100 $$\varvec{ imes }$$ (or even more) acceleration in most cases.
