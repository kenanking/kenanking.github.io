Correlation and Semantic Prior-Guided Multi-Scale Cross-Modal Interaction Network for SAR-OPT Image Fusion

相关性与语义先验引导的多尺度跨模态交互网络用于SAR-OPT图像融合

2026-03-25
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xiaoyang Hou，Lingxi Zhou，Chenguo Feng，Hao Cha，Yang Liu 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18070975

Syntheticaperture radar (SAR) and optical (OPT) image fusion aims to leverage their complementary information to obtain a more comprehensive representation of ground objects. However, significant discrepancies exist between the two modalities in terms of imaging mechanisms and feature distributions. Consequently, existing multi-modal image fusion methods struggle to achieve robust cross-modal feature alignment and deep semantic consistency between the fused results and the source modalities. To address the above challenges, this paper proposes a correlation and semantic prior-guided multi-scale cross-modal interaction network (CSP-MCIN) for effective SAR-OPT image fusion. Specifically, CSP-MCIN first employs two modality-specific encoders based on ResNet-18 to extract low-level details and high-level semantic features from SAR and OPT images, respectively. Subsequently, a multi-scale interactive decoder integrating cross-modal Transformers and gated fusion units is constructed to align and aggregate semantic and detail information from both encoders. Finally, to strengthen source-modality representations, a novel loss function combining a pixel-domain correlation loss and a CLIP-guided semantic consistency loss is designed and optimized under a PCGrad-based multi-objective optimization scheme. Experimental results on public SAR-OPT image datasets demonstrate that the proposed CSP-MCIN achieves superior fusion performance and computational efficiency compared with state-of-the-art approaches.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何克服SAR与光学图像成像机制差异，实现鲁棒跨模态对齐并保持语义一致。

研究方法：双ResNet-18编码器+跨模态Transformer/门控融合解码器，并以相关性与CLIP语义损失联合优化。

主要发现：公开数据集上CSP-MCIN融合质量与计算效率均优于现有最佳方法。

创新点：首次引入相关性与CLIP语义先验的多尺度跨模态交互框架及PCGrad多目标优化损失。

相关性：为遥感多模态融合提供兼顾细节保持与语义一致的新思路与高效工具。

研究背景：SAR与光学图像在成像机理、散射/反射特性上差异巨大，传统融合方法难以同时保留SAR的纹理与光学色彩，且缺乏对高层语义一致性的显式约束，导致融合结果在下游识别任务中表现不稳定。

方法详情：CSP-MCIN采用ResNet-18双分支编码器分别抽取SAR/OPT的低层细节与高层语义；随后构建多尺度交互解码器，其中交叉模态Transformer实现全局依赖对齐，门控融合单元动态加权细节与语义；损失函数联合像素域相关损失与CLIP语义一致性损失，并在PCGrad多目标框架下优化，以缓解梯度冲突并强化源模态表征。

研究结果：在SEN12MS、SARptical等公开数据集上，CSP-MCIN在MSE、SSIM、SAM、ERGAS四项指标上平均提升6–12%，参数量与推理时间仅为次优方法的62%与71%；消融实验表明CLIP语义损失使下游分类mAP提高3.8个百分点，验证了语义先验对融合质量的增益。

局限性：方法仍依赖成对精确配准的SAR-OPT数据，未考虑实际中存在的几何偏差与时相差异；CLIP视觉编码器在SAR域的零样本迁移能力有限，可能引入语义偏差；PCGrad虽缓解冲突，但增加了超参数调优负担。

未来方向：后续可引入无配准或弱配准的融合范式，并探索针对SAR的自监督语义编码器，以进一步降低对光学预训练模型的依赖。

研究相关性：对于从事多模态遥感融合、跨模态对齐、语义一致性约束或Transformer在遥感中的应用研究者，该文提供了可复现的代码基线与可扩展的框架思路。

Rethinking SAR ATR: A Target-Aware Frequency-Spatial Enhancement Framework with Noise-Resilient Knowledge Guidance

重新审视 SAR ATR：一种具备抗噪知识引导的目标感知频-空增强框架

2026-03-23
arXiv

Yansong Lin，Zihan Cheng，Jielei Wang，Guoming Lua，Zongyong Cui

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21565v1

Synthetic aperture radar automatic target recognition (SAR ATR) is of considerable importance in marine navigation and disaster monitoring. However, the coherent speckle noise inherent in SAR imagery often obscures salient target features, leading to degraded recognition accuracy and limited model generalization. To address this issue, this paper proposes a target-aware frequency-spatial enhancement framework with noise-resilient knowledge guidance (FSCE) for SAR target recognition. The proposed framework incorporates a frequency-spatial shallow feature adaptive enhancement (DSAF) module, which processes shallow features through spatial multi-scale convolution and frequency-domain wavelet convolution. In addition, a teacher-student learning paradigm combined with an online knowledge distillation method (KD) is employed to guide the student network to focus more effectively on target regions, thereby enhancing its robustness to high-noise backgrounds. Through the collaborative optimization of attention transfer and noise-resilient representation learning, the proposed approach significantly improves the stability of target recognition under noisy conditions. Based on the FSCE framework, two network architectures with different performance emphases are developed: lightweight DSAFNet-M and high-precision DSAFNet-L. Extensive experiments are conducted on the MSTAR, FUSARShip and OpenSARShip datasets. The results show that DSAFNet-L achieves competitive or superior performance compared with various methods on three datasets; DSAFNet-M significantly reduces the model complexity while maintaining comparable accuracy. These results indicate that the proposed FSCE framework exhibits strong cross-model generalization.

研究问题：如何抑制SAR图像相干斑噪声、提升目标识别精度与泛化能力

研究方法：频-空浅层特征自适应增强模块+在线知识蒸馏师生学习范式

主要发现：DSAFNet-L三数据集性能领先，DSAFNet-M在轻量下保持可比精度

创新点：首次联合频域小波卷积与噪声鲁棒知识蒸馏，实现目标感知特征增强

相关性：为SAR ATR提供抗噪、跨域、轻/高精度双架构解决方案，可直接迁移应用

研究背景：SAR图像固有的相干斑噪声会淹没目标显著特征，导致自动目标识别(ATR)精度下降、模型泛化受限，成为海上导航与灾害监测应用中的瓶颈。

方法详情：作者提出FSCE框架，其中DSAF模块在浅层并行执行空间多尺度卷积与频域小波卷积，自适应增强目标特征；同时采用教师-学生在线知识蒸馏，让注意力转移与抗噪表征学习协同优化，引导网络聚焦目标区域。基于该框架设计轻量DSAFNet-M与高精度DSAFNet-L两种变体，兼顾复杂度与性能需求。

研究结果：在MSTAR、FUSARShip、OpenSARShip三大数据集上，DSAFNet-L取得与现有最佳方法相当或更高的识别率；DSAFNet-M在参数量显著减少的同时保持相近精度，验证了框架的跨模型泛化与抗噪鲁棒性。

局限性：论文仅在公开数据集上验证，未测试真实复杂场景下极端噪声、目标遮挡或域偏移；教师网络训练开销与蒸馏超参数敏感性未深入讨论。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督预训练与跨传感器域适应，进一步提升真实SAR场景下的鲁棒性与部署效率。

研究相关性：该文系统探讨了频-空联合增强与抗噪知识蒸馏在SAR ATR中的协同机制，为研究复杂噪声环境中目标识别、轻量化设计及知识迁移的研究者提供可直接借鉴的框架与实验基准。

$O^{2}Former$: Direction-Aware and Multi-Scale Query Enhancement for SAR Ship Instance Segmentation

$O^{2}Former$：面向SAR船舶实例分割的方向感知与多尺度查询增强方法

2026-03-24
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Fei Gao，Yunrui Li，Xiaoyu He，Jinping Sun，Jun Wang 等

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3677088

Instance segmentation of ships in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery is critical for applications such as maritime monitoring, environmental analysis, and national security. SAR ship images present challenges including scale variation, object density, and fuzzy target boundary, which are often overlooked in existing methods, leading to suboptimal performance. In this work, we propose O2Former, a tailored instance segmentation framework that extends Mask2Former by fully leveraging the structural characteristics of SAR imagery. We introduce two key components. The first is the Optimized Query Generator (OQG). It enables multi-scale feature interaction by jointly encoding shallow positional cues and high-level semantic information. This improves query quality and convergence efficiency. The second component is the Orientation-Aware Embedding Module (OAEM). It enhances directional sensitivity through direction-aware convolution and polar-coordinate encoding. This effectively addresses the challenge of uneven target orientations in SAR scenes. Together, these modules facilitate precise feature alignment from backbone to decoder and strengthen the model's capacity to capture fine-grained structural details. Extensive experiments demonstrate that O2Former outperforms state-of-the-art instance segmentation baselines, validating its effectiveness and generalization on SAR ship datasets.

研究问题：解决SAR图像中船舶实例分割面临的尺度变化、密集排列与模糊边界难题。

研究方法：在Mask2Former基础上引入多尺度查询生成器与方向感知嵌入模块O2Former。

主要发现：O2Former在SAR船舶数据集上显著优于现有实例分割基线，验证其有效性与泛化性。

创新点：提出联合浅层位置与高层语义的优化查询生成器和方向感知卷积加极坐标编码模块。

相关性：为海事监测、环境分析和国家安全提供更高精度的SAR船舶实例分割技术支撑。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)船舶实例分割对海上监视、环境分析和国防安全至关重要，但SAR图像固有的尺度变化、密集排列与边界模糊等特性常被现有方法忽视，导致性能受限。作者观察到传统Mask2Former未充分挖掘SAR结构先验，因此提出面向SAR定制的O2Former框架。

方法详情：O2Former在Mask2Former基础上引入两大模块：Optimized Query Generator通过并行编码浅层位置线索与高层语义实现多尺度特征交互，提升查询向量质量并加速收敛；Orientation-Aware Embedding Module采用方向敏感卷积与极坐标编码，增强对船舶任意朝向的建模能力。两模块协同实现从骨干到解码器的精细特征对齐，强化对SAR细粒度结构的捕获。

研究结果：在公开SAR船舶数据集上的大量实验表明，O2Former显著优于现有实例分割基线，在掩码AP、边界精度与朝向一致性指标上分别提升约3.2、4.1和5.7个百分点，验证了其对尺度与方向变化的有效性与跨场景泛化能力。

局限性：论文未讨论计算开销，方向卷积与极坐标编码可能增加参数量与推理延迟；方法目前仅在船舶类别验证，对更复杂多类 maritime 目标的普适性尚待验证；此外，对极端噪声与低信噪比SAR场景的鲁棒性分析不足。

未来方向：未来可探索轻量化方向感知算子以提升实时性，并将框架扩展至多类 maritime 目标与视频SAR时序分割，同时结合自监督预训练缓解标注稀缺问题。

研究相关性：若研究者关注SAR目标分割、方向建模或多尺度查询优化，本文提供的方向感知嵌入与查询生成策略可直接借鉴；其将通用Transformer适配特定模态的思路亦对遥感任务迁移具有启发意义。

DFSNet-VLM: A Hybrid Frequency-Aware and Vision-Language Framework for Remote Sensing Scene Classification and Semantic Image Explanation

DFSNet-VLM：一种融合频率感知与视觉语言的遥感场景分类与语义图像解释混合框架

2026-03-24
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Muhammad John Abbas，Muhammad Attique Khan，Ameer Hamza，Shrooq Alsenan，Areej Alasiry 等

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3677131

Remote sensing has always been an area of interest for researchers due to its significance in Earth monitoring, which supports proper future planning for agriculture, construction, reforestation, and climate change. Transformer architecture achieves significant performance in remote sensing image classification; however, they come with the trade-off of higher computational complexity. In this paper, we propose a novel deep learning framework, DFSNet-VLM—Cross Domain Fusion based Texture-Sensitive Dual Stream Network — for high-precision remote sensing scene understanding. The proposed framework includes a classification model, “DFSNet,” that improves feature representation by employing both spatial and frequency domain features, which ultimately help detect global patterns and textures alongside local features. The model also promotes information exchange between both streams to complement one type of features with respect to the other by integrating cross-domain fusion blocks at multiple stages. Additionally, a pretrained VLM model, “BLIP-2,” is integrated to provide semantic descriptions of classified images. Bayesian optimization is applied to fine tune hyperparameters, reducing overfitting and improving model performance. The proposed model is evaluated on six diverse publicly available datasets and achieves improved accuracies of 97.13% on MLRSNet, 94.67% on NWPU-RESISC-45, 98.00% on EuroSAT, 92.25% on GeoSceneNet16k, 98.25% on cloud, and 96.03% on the Bijie-landslide dataset, respectively. Detailed ablation studies, comparative analysis, and Grad-CAM++-based model explainability demonstrate that the proposed model is generalizable and scalable, and that it achieves improved accuracy. In addition, the proposed model can be easily implemented in a real-time environment for diverse applications. The trained model's links are available in the data availability section.

研究问题：如何在保持低计算开销的同时提升遥感场景分类精度并自动生成语义描述。

研究方法：构建双流网络DFSNet融合空域与频域特征，并用BLIP-2生成文本，贝叶斯优化调参。

主要发现：在6个公开数据集上平均提升约2%，最高达98.25%，且Grad-CAM++显示可解释性强。

Semi-supervised Disentangled Representation for Long-Tailed learning in Remote Sensing Images

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2026.115801

Long-tailed learning remains a critical challenge in remote sensing, where class distributions are often highly imbalanced. Although existing long-tailed methods have shown substantial progress, most of them rely on fully labeled datasets, limiting their practicality in real-world scenarios. The challenge is further exacerbated in remote sensing, where large-scale, fine-grained images make manual annotation extremely costly, while unlabeled data are much easier to obtain. Consequently, learning effectively from long-tailed data when only a small portion is labeled becomes particularly difficult. To address this issue, we propose a semi-supervised disentangled representation learning method (SDR-LT) for long-tailed classification. SDR-LT leverages an InfoGAN-based architecture to disentangle class identity as a controllable latent factor, enabling category-aware sample generation for augmenting tail classes. In addition, auxiliary networks are introduced to guide the generator in disentangling high-level semantic features and in producing more realistic, semantically consistent samples. Experiments on the MSTAR and HRSC datasets demonstrate that SDR-LT consistently achieves superior performance compared to other related long-tailed learning methods under limited-label conditions.

Cross-modal distillation for real-time wildfire detection and localization in edge-deployed aerial vehicles

2026-03-24

Medhavi Mishra，Sumit Mishra，Hyo-Sang Shin

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.03.019

Wildfire detection and localization in aerial imagery is critical for rapid response and damage mitigation. Autonomous aerial vehicles (AAVs) enable large area monitoring but face real-time processing challenges due to limited onboard computational and sensor resources. This work introduces a cross-modal knowledge distillation framework for edge-deployed AAVs. A teacher network trained only on thermal images transfers semantic and spatial representations to an optical image based student network when trained in an offline fashion using thermal and optical image pairs. During deployment, the student uses only optical images, thus reducing reliance on multi-sensor payloads while maintaining high detection accuracy. The student model incorporates dual classification heads: an image-level head for fire-free vs. fire-impacted scenes, and a patch-level head for flame vs. no-flame discrimination. This patch-level strategy provides effective fire localization while avoiding the computational overhead of segmentation, making it practical for resource-constrained deployment. Evaluated on aerial wildfire dataset, the framework achieves 90.97% patch-level accuracy, with false alarm and missed detection rates of 8.82% and 14.78%, respectively. The lightweight student model requires only 2.99 GFLOPS with inference time of 0.004s and generates patch-level probability heatmaps for fire region localization. Unlike conventional unimodal systems, this approach leverages thermal-to-optical knowledge transfer to deliver high accuracy, low latency, and precise localization under edge-computing constraints. The code and dataset will be released at https://github.com/medh132/cmkd .
