DSF-DETR: End-to-End SAR Ship Detection via Spatial-Frequency State Space Modeling and Dynamic Attention

DSF-DETR：通过空间-频率状态空间建模与动态注意力机制的端到端SAR船舶检测

2026-05-27
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Yunqi Zhang，Lin Bai，Wenqing Zhou，Danni Xue，Wentao Liu 等

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3697755

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) ship detection sup ports maritime surveillance, but remains difficult due to multiplicative speckle, complex coastal clutter, and large-scale variation in ship size. CNN-based detectors often trade noise suppression for detail and typically rely on post-processing such as non-maximum suppression (NMS). To overcome these limitations, we propose DSF-DETR, an end-to-end Transformer detector that decouples spatial and frequency processing to explicitly disentangle noise-sensitive fluctuations from target structures. Its Decoupled Spatial-Frequency Backbone (DSF-Net) extracts local geometry in a spatial branch, while a frequency branch combines a Discrete Wavelet Transform (DWT) with State Space Models (SSMs) to aggregate global context and suppress noise sensitive components. To avoid redundant computation over homogeneous background, Content-Aware Dynamic Attention (CADA) sparsifies the encoder, and an Efficient Re-parameterized Feature Pyramid (ERFP) strengthens multi-scale fusion and is re-parameterized into a single-branch structure for inference. Experiments on SSDD, HRSID and the SAR-Ship-Dataset show that DSF-DETR improves AP50 over RT-DETR by 2.0, 2.3, and 1.4 points, respectively, while reducing the parameters by 44.5% in SSDD. On the NVIDIA Jetson Xavier NX, the re parameterized model delivers a 53.3% FPS gain. Our code will be available at https://github.com/CHD-IPAC/DSF-DETR.

相关性：为SAR舰船检测提供高效端到端方案，降低计算开销，便于边缘设备部署。

研究背景：SAR船检测面临乘性散斑噪声、复杂海岸杂波和船尺寸大尺度变化等挑战，传统CNN检测器常需在噪声抑制与细节保留间权衡，且依赖NMS后处理。为此，本文提出端到端Transformer检测器DSF-DETR，通过解耦空间与频率处理显式分离噪声敏感波动与目标结构。

局限性：虽然频率分支有效抑制噪声，但DWT+SSM的组合可能增加训练复杂度；内容感知注意力依赖于均匀背景假设，在极端杂乱场景下可能失效。此外，模型在极小型目标上的检测性能未详细分析。

D3S-Net: A Dense SAR Ship Detection Network for Dynamic Denoising and Structural Sparsity Deinterference

D3S-Net：用于动态去噪和结构稀疏性去干扰的密集SAR舰船检测网络

2026-05-28
Digital Signal Processing (IF: 3.0)

Shun He，Ruirui Yuan，Zhiwei Yang，Jiaxue Liu

DOI: 10.1016/j.dsp.2026.106295

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) is crucial for ship detection in marine monitoring, due to its all-weather, all-time, and wide-area imaging capabilities.However, coherent speckle noise, coastal scattering interference, and dense ship distributions reduce detection accuracy. To solve these problems, this paper proposes D3S-Net, a dense SAR ship detection network based on frequency-domain dynamic denoising and sparse structural interference suppression. First, a frequency-domain dynamic denoising module (F3D) is designed to suppress speckle noise. By converting features to the frequency domain and combining an attention mechanism to adaptively adjust thresholds, the module achieves differentiated noise reduction while preserving target features. Then, a Mamba-based sparse interference suppression module (MBSIS) is constructed using the sparse distribution prior of targets. After capturing global semantics via the Mamba state-space selection mechanism, redundant interference features are removed through sparse constraints and mask generation. Finally, an IoU-aware mechanism is introduced into the detection head to form a comprehensive ranking metric combining classification confidence and localization accuracy, optimizing the dense target filtering strategy. Experiments on the SSDD+ and R-SDD SAR datasets show that mAP is increased by 2.3% and 2% compared with the baseline, verifying the effectiveness and superiority of the proposed method in speckle noise and interference suppression.

研究问题：解决SAR图像中相干斑噪、海岸散射干扰和密集舰船分布导致的检测精度下降问题。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达（SAR）在海洋监测中具有全天候、全天时和大范围成像能力，对船舶检测至关重要。然而，相干斑噪声、海岸散射干扰以及密集船舶分布严重降低了检测精度。为此，本文提出D3S-Net，旨在通过频域动态去噪和结构稀疏干扰抑制来解决这些问题。

方法详情：首先，设计了频域动态去噪模块（F3D），通过将特征转换到频域并结合注意力机制自适应调整阈值，实现差异化降噪并保留目标特征。其次，构建了基于Mamba的稀疏干扰抑制模块（MBSIS），利用目标分布稀疏先验，通过Mamba状态空间选择机制捕获全局语义，再通过稀疏约束和掩码生成去除冗余干扰特征。最后，在检测头中引入IoU感知机制，形成结合分类置信度和定位精度的综合排序指标，优化密集目标筛选策略。

研究结果：在SSDD+和R-SDD两个SAR数据集上的实验表明，与基线相比，mAP分别提升了2.3%和2.0%，验证了该方法在斑噪和干扰抑制方面的有效性和优越性。该结果证明了频域动态去噪与稀疏干扰抑制结合能显著提升复杂场景下的SAR船舶检测性能。

局限性：该方法依赖于频域变换和Mamba模块的计算复杂度，可能对实时性要求高的应用存在挑战。此外，实验仅基于两个公开数据集，未在更多样化的SAR场景（如不同分辨率、极化模式）下验证泛化能力。

未来方向：未来可探索轻量化网络设计以降低计算开销，并针对多源SAR数据（如高分辨率、多极化）进行适应性改进，同时研究端到端联合优化去噪和检测的框架。

StarRoute-DBNet: A Novel Multi-Modal Framework for Advanced Target Detection in Dynamic Environments Using SAR and Optical Image Fusion with FocusGraph and PhaseRoute

StarRoute-DBNet：一种利用SAR与光学图像融合及FocusGraph和PhaseRoute的动态环境中高级目标检测的新型多模态框架

2026-05-27
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Lanfang Lei，Sheng Chang，Zhongzhen Sun，Jianxin Zou，Huazheng Yang 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18111731

Multimodal object detection based on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) and optical imagery is of great significance in remote sensing, particularly under adverse weather conditions, nighttime environments, and complex background scenarios. Although SAR imagery has unique advantages under all-weather conditions, its object detection performance still faces challenges in low-texture regions and cluttered scenes. Optical imagery provides rich spatial and texture information, but its applicability is limited in harsh environments. To overcome the limitations of unimodal SAR object detection, this paper proposes a novel multimodal object detection framework, termed StarRoute-DBNet, to improve detection accuracy and robustness through multimodal data fusion and efficient feature interaction. Specifically, a FocusGraph (Graph Convolution-Based Feature Relationship Modeling) module is first designed to adaptively model the spatial relationships between optical and SAR features via graph convolutional networks (GCNs), thereby capturing complex cross-modal spatial dependencies. This module enhances feature interaction across modalities, improves the localization accuracy of oriented targets, and shows clear advantages for small-object detection in complex backgrounds. Second, to alleviate the loss of critical information during downsampling, a PhaseRoute (Sparse Routing Polyphase Downsampling Module) is introduced, which combines multi-phase decomposition with a Top-2 sparse routing strategy to preserve informative spatial cues. By incorporating Gumbel noise into the routing process, the proposed module further improves routing flexibility, detection accuracy, and model robustness. In addition, a Multi-Scale Shuffle-Gated Fusion (MSSGF) module is proposed to address the multi-scale issue in multimodal feature fusion. This module integrates multi-scale convolutional branches, channel shuffles, and dual-attention mechanisms to enhance feature interaction across scales, while an adaptive weighted fusion strategy is employed to dynamically adjust the fusion weights of multimodal features. As a result, the proposed method significantly improves detection accuracy and robustness, especially in complex scenes. Extensive experiments conducted on the MVSDA dataset and the M4-SAR dataset demonstrate that the proposed StarRoute-DBNet consistently outperforms existing state-of-the-art methods under complex backgrounds and adverse conditions. In particular, it achieves clear advantages in oriented object detection and small-object detection, verifying its effectiveness and robustness for cross-modal remote sensing object detection.

研究问题：如何通过SAR与光学图像融合提升动态环境下目标检测的准确性和鲁棒性？

研究结果：在MVSDA和M4-SAR数据集上的大量实验表明，StarRoute-DBNet在复杂背景和恶劣条件下持续优于现有最先进方法。尤其在定向目标检测和小目标检测中表现突出，验证了其跨模态遥感目标检测的有效性和鲁棒性。

Deep learning is revolutionizing the way InSAR data are processed.
