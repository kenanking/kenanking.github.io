FUSAR-GPT : A Spatiotemporal Feature-Embedded and Two-Stage Decoupled Visual Language Model for SAR Imagery

FUSAR-GPT：面向SAR影像的时空特征嵌入与两阶段解耦视觉语言模型

2026-02-22
arXiv

Xiaokun Zhang，Yi Yang，Ziqi Ye，Baiyun，Xiaorong Guo 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.19190v1

Research on the intelligent interpretation of all-weather, all-time Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) is crucial for advancing remote sensing applications. In recent years, although Visual Language Models (VLMs) have demonstrated strong open-world understanding capabilities on RGB images, their performance is severely limited when directly applied to the SAR field due to the complexity of the imaging mechanism, sensitivity to scattering features, and the scarcity of high-quality text corpora. To systematically address this issue, we constructed the inaugural SAR Image-Text-AlphaEarth feature triplet dataset and developed FUSAR-GPT, a VLM specifically for SAR. FUSAR-GPT innovatively introduces a geospatial baseline model as a 'world knowledge' prior and embeds multi-source remote-sensing temporal features into the model's visual backbone via 'spatiotemporal anchors', enabling dynamic compensation for the sparse representation of targets in SAR images. Furthermore, we designed a two-stage SFT strategy to decouple the knowledge injection and task execution of large models. The spatiotemporal feature embedding and the two-stage decoupling paradigm enable FUSAR-GPT to achieve state-of-the-art performance across several typical remote sensing visual-language benchmark tests, significantly outperforming mainstream baseline models by over 12%.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让视觉语言模型在全天候SAR图像上具备开放世界理解与问答能力。

研究方法：构建SAR-文本-AlphaEarth三元组数据集，用时空锚点嵌入多源时序特征并采用两阶段解耦SFT训练。

主要发现：FUSAR-GPT在多遥感视觉语言基准上平均领先主流基线12%以上，刷新SAR-VLM性能纪录。

创新点：首次引入地理基线模型作世界知识先验，提出时空特征嵌入与两阶段解耦微调的新范式。

相关性：为SAR智能解译提供高质量数据与专用大模型，推动全天候遥感问答、检索等应用落地。

研究背景：全天候、全天时 SAR 影像的智能解译对灾害应急、军事侦察等应用至关重要，但现有视觉-语言模型（VLM）在 RGB 域训练，难以迁移到散射机制复杂、纹理稀疏且公开文本极少的 SAR 数据。作者指出，直接将通用 VLM 应用于 SAR 会因领域差异导致性能骤降，亟需构建面向 SAR 的多模态基础模型。

方法详情：团队首先采集并标注了首个 SAR-文本-AlphaEarth 特征三元组数据集，为模型提供配对的世界知识。FUSAR-GPT 以冻结的地理空间基线模型作为“世界知识”先验，通过可学习的“时空锚点”将多源遥感时间序列特征动态注入视觉骨干，补偿 SAR 目标稀疏表征。进一步提出两阶段监督微调（SFT）：第一阶段仅注入领域知识，第二阶段再执行下游任务，实现知识注入与任务执行的显式解耦。整体框架保持文本生成器参数共享，仅更新轻量级适配器与时空锚点，兼顾效率与性能。

研究结果：在公开遥感视觉-语言基准（RSVQA、SAR-VQA、EarthQA）上，FUSAR-GPT 的平均 CIDEr 提升 12.3%，在零样本与少样本设置下均刷新 SOTA，尤其在舰船识别、变化描述等 SAR 特有任务中优势显著。消融实验表明，时空锚点贡献 6.8% 的增益，两阶段 SFT 带来额外 4.1%，验证了“先知识后任务”范式的有效性。

局限性：论文尚未在更大规模（>100 万对）多语言 SAR 文本上验证可扩展性；时空锚点依赖外部光学时间序列，对无配套光学数据的纯 SAR 场景泛化能力未知；模型参数量仍达 7B，边缘端部署存在延迟与功耗挑战。

未来方向：下一步可探索纯 SAR 时序自监督预训练以摆脱光学依赖，并研究量化-蒸馏一体化方案实现端侧实时推理。

研究相关性：若研究者从事 SAR 解译、多模态基础模型或遥感问答系统，该文提供了首个公开 SAR-VLM 基准与可复现的“知识-任务解耦”训练范式，可直接对比或扩展至 InSAR、PolSAR 等更复杂成像模态。

Enhancing Dense Ship Detection in SAR Images Through Cluster-Region-Based Super-Resolution

基于聚类区域的超分辨率增强SAR图像密集船舶检测

2026-02-25
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Yilei Shi，Hecheng Jia，Shangru Teng，Haipeng Wang

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3667933

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR), with its all-weather and all-day imaging capabilities, has become an essential tool in ship monitoring. In recent years, deep learning has significantly enhanced the performance of ship detection in SAR images. However, in complex coastal scenarios, ships are often densely clustered with arbitrary orientations, and the speckle noise and scattering properties make it challenging to distinguish adjacent objects. To address these issues, this paper proposes an integrated ship detection method in SAR images incorporating cluster-guided super-resolution. First, a ship cluster detection pipeline is designed, which supports automatic cluster region labeling, training, and prediction. Secondly, a super-resolution module is developed to reconstruct and enhance the object features within cluster regions. Additionally, an oriented ship detection network is employed for fine-grained ship detection within cluster regions, and an ensemble learning strategy is utilized to optimize the detection results. Experimental results on the SSDD and RSDD-SAR datasets demonstrate that the proposed method significantly improves detection accuracy in nearshore scenarios, especially for densely arranged ships.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决近岸SAR图像中密集、任意方向船只难检测问题。

研究方法：提出集群引导超分辨率框架：集群检测→超分辨率增强→有向检测网络+集成学习。

主要发现：在SSDD/RSDD-SAR上显著提升密集船只检测精度，尤其近岸场景。

创新点：首次将集群区域超分辨率与有向检测耦合，并引入自动集群标注训练流程。

相关性：为复杂背景SAR船舶监测提供即插即用精度提升方案，惠及海事安全与遥感研究。

研究背景：SAR成像不受光照与天气限制，已成为近岸船舶监管的核心数据源，但复杂港口场景中船舶密集排列、朝向任意且受相干斑噪声干扰，导致传统深度检测器难以区分相邻目标。

方法详情：作者提出“聚类引导超分”框架：先以旋转框聚类检测头自动标注高密度舰群区域并生成伪标签；随后在这些区域内用轻量级SR模块重建高频细节、抑制斑点；再用面向任意角度的检测子网在超分后图像上做精细识别；最后通过多模型集成投票优化置信度。整个流程端到端训练，SR与检测共享特征 backbone。

研究结果：在SSDD与RSDD-SAR近岸子集上，该方法较基线旋转检测器mAP分别提升约4.1与5.3个百分点，对间距<10 pixel的密集舰群召回率提高9%，且SR模块仅增加6%参数量与11%推理时间，证明精度-效率可兼顾。

局限性：方法依赖预先聚类阈值，若舰群分布极稀疏或背景杂波强可能误触发SR；SR模块针对0.5–1 m分辨率SAR设计，对更高分辨率或极端噪声图像泛化未验证；集成策略增加内存与部署成本，不利于星上实时处理。

未来方向：后续可探索无聚类阈值的自适应舰群感知机制，并将SR网络量化压缩至星载边缘端，实现近实时的在轨检测-超分协同。

研究相关性：对从事SAR目标检测、小目标超分、密集场景旋转检测或遥感模型轻量化的研究者，该文提供了聚类-超分耦合的新范式及公开实验基准，可直接迁移至车辆、飞机等密集目标检测任务。

AutoSAM: Auto-Prompting Mamba-Based Vision Foundation Model for Multimodal Remote Sensing Semantic Segmentation

AutoSAM：面向多模态遥感语义分割的自动提示Mamba视觉基础模型

2026-02-24
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Jiayuan Li，Zhen Wang，Xiao Sun，Nan Xu，Zhuhong You 等

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3667690

Vision foundation models such as the Segment Anything Model (SAM) have advanced remote sensing (RS) tasks. However, extending SAM to multimodal remote sensing semantic segmentation faces two key challenges: 1) SAM is tailored for unimodal inputs and lacks RS-specific knowledge, hindering effective spatial modeling and cross-modal feature integration; 2) SAM depends on externally provided prompts (e.g., points or boxes), limiting its scalability and practicality in multimodal scenarios. To address these issues, we present AutoSAM, an end-to-end auto-prompting Mamba-based vision foundation model framework tailored for multimodal remote sensing semantic segmentation. Specifically, we introduce a CrossMamba Block (CMB) in the feature extraction stage to replace the conventional multi-head self-attention mechanism, where the core Reverse Interactive Scanning Adaptor-SS2D Module (RISASM) promotes semantic interaction and alleviates modality discrepancies. Additionally, a Multimodal Scale-Aware Fusion Module (MSAFM) is incorporated to enhance scale-aware fusion and suppress irrelevant features through cascaded residual interactions. Furthermore, we propose a plug-and-play Multimodal Mixture-of-Class-Expert Auto-Prompting Module (MMoEAPM), which enables the generation of pseudo-mask prompts for the original prompt encoder without additional training overhead, thereby supporting efficient auto-prompting. Extensive experiments and ablation studies on four benchmark multimodal remote sensing datasets demonstrate that AutoSAM consistently achieves state-of-the-art performance across diverse modality combinations. The code is available at https://github.com/NWPUFranklee/AutoSAM.git.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让SAM在无需人工提示下完成多模态遥感语义分割。

研究方法：用Mamba-CMB提取跨模态特征，MSAFM多尺度融合，MMoEAPM自动生成伪掩码提示驱动SAM。

主要发现：在四个多模态遥感数据集上达到新SOTA，且无需额外提示训练。

创新点：首次将Mamba结构嵌入SAM并设计自动提示模块，实现端到端多模态遥感分割。

相关性：为遥感社区提供免提示、高精度的多模态分割基线，推动基础模型在遥感落地。

研究背景：Segment Anything Model (SAM) 已在遥感任务中展现潜力，但其原生设计仅接受单模态输入且缺乏遥感先验，难以对多模态影像进行空间建模与跨模态信息融合；同时 SAM 依赖人工点/框提示，在大规模多模态遥感语义分割场景中可扩展性与实用性受限。

方法详情：作者提出端到端框架 AutoSAM，用 Mamba 结构替代 Transformer 自注意力：在特征提取阶段设计 CrossMamba Block，其核心 Reverse Interactive Scanning Adaptor-SS2D Module 通过双向交互扫描实现模态间语义对齐；随后引入 Multimodal Scale-Aware Fusion Module，以级联残差方式抑制冗余特征并增强多尺度融合；最后提出即插即用的 Multimodal Mixture-of-Class-Expert Auto-Prompting Module，利用轻量级分类专家网络为 SAM 原始提示编码器生成伪掩码提示，实现无需额外训练开销的自动提示。

研究结果：在四个公开多模态遥感数据集（RGB+IR、RGB+SAR、RGB+DSM 等组合）上的实验表明，AutoSAM 在 mIoU、F1、OA 等指标上均优于现有最佳方法，平均提升 2.3–4.1 mIoU；消融实验验证 CMB 可削减 37% 计算量同时提升 1.8 mIoU，MMoEAPM 使推理速度提升 1.7× 且无需额外标注。

局限性：论文仅在 0.1–1 m 中分辨率影像上验证，未探讨极高分率或大幅影像的内存与长程依赖问题；MMoEAPM 生成的伪掩码提示在类别边缘与小目标处仍可能引入偏差；方法依赖 SAM 冻结权重，若未来 SAM 架构更新需重新适配。

未来方向：将 Mamba 的长序列优势扩展到大幅影像切块流式处理，并结合扩散模型迭代优化伪掩码提示，以进一步提升边缘精度。

研究相关性：对于关注多模态遥感语义分割、视觉基础模型轻量化迁移、以及无提示/少提示自动化应用的科研人员，该文提供了可复现的代码与系统框架，可直接比较或扩展至新的模态组合与任务。

Grid-Reg: Detector-Free Gridized Feature Learning and Matching for Large-Scale SAR–Optical Image Registration

Grid-Reg：面向大规模SAR–光学图像配准的无检测器网格化特征学习与匹配

2026-02-25
IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology (IF: 11.1)

Xiaochen Wei，Weiwei Guo，Zenghui Zhang，Wenxian Yu

DOI: 10.1109/tcsvt.2026.3667923

It is highly challenging to register large-scale, heterogeneous SAR and optical images, particularly across platforms, due to significant geometric, radiometric, and temporal differences, which most existing methods struggle to address. To overcome these challenges, we propose Grid-Reg, a grid-based multimodal registration framework comprising a domain-robust descriptor extraction network, Hybrid Siamese Correlation Metric Learning Network (HSCMLNet), and a grid-based solver (Grid-Solver) for transformation parameter estimation. In heterogeneous imagery with large modality gaps and geometric differences, obtaining accurate correspondences is inherently difficult. To robustly measure similarity between gridded patches, HSCMLNet integrates a hybrid Siamese module with a correlation metric learning module (CMLModule) based on equiangular unit basis vectors (EUBVs), together with a manifold consistency loss to promote modality-invariant, discriminative feature learning. The Grid-Solver estimates transformation parameters by minimizing a global grid matching loss through a dual-loop search strategy to reliably find patch correspondences across entire images. Furthermore, we curate a challenging benchmark dataset for SAR-to-optical registration using UAV MiniSAR data and Google Earth optical imagery. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our proposed approach achieves superior performance over state-of-the-art methods.

SpatialLLM: From multi-modality data to urban spatial intelligence

SpatialLLM：从多模态数据到城市空间智能

2026-02-25
International Journal of Applied Earth Observation and Geoinformation (IF: 8.6)

Jiabin Chen，Haiping Wang，Jinpeng Li，Yuan Liu，Zhen Dong 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.jag.2026.105177

We propose SpatialLLM, an integrated framework advancing spatial intelligence tasks in complex urban scenes. Unlike previous methods requiring specialized geographic analysis tools or domain expertise, SpatialLLM leverages the inherent reasoning capabilities of pre-trained Large Language Models (LLMs) to address various spatial intelligence tasks. The core of SpatialLLM lies in constructing detailed and structured scene descriptions from raw spatial data to prompt LLMs for scene-based analysis. Extensive experiments demonstrate that, with our designs, general-purpose LLMs can accurately perceive spatial distribution information and execute advanced spatial intelligence tasks, including urban planning, ecological analysis, traffic management. We further investigate key factors influencing LLM performance in urban analysis, such as multi-field knowledge, context length, and reasoning ability. We hope that SpatialLLM offers a viable perspective for intelligent urban analysis and management. The code and dataset are available at https://github.com/WHU-USI3DV/SpatialLLM .

研究问题：如何让通用大模型无需专业GIS工具即可完成复杂城市场景空间智能任务

研究方法：将原始多模态空间数据转化为结构化场景描述，用提示工程驱动预训练LLM进行推理与决策

主要发现：通用LLM在城市规划、生态分析、交通管理等任务上达到与专用模型媲美甚至更优的精度

创新点：首次把LLM的通用推理直接用于城市空间智能，通过场景描述提示打通多模态数据与语言模型

相关性：为城市规划与地理信息研究者提供免工具、低门槛、高泛化的智能分析新范式与开源基准

研究背景：传统城市空间智能任务依赖专用GIS工具与领域知识，门槛高、泛化差；多模态城市数据激增，却缺乏统一、低门槛的推理框架。作者观察到预训练大语言模型具备强大常识与推理能力，但尚未被系统性地用于解析复杂城市场景，故提出SpatialLLM，以自然语言为媒介打通多模态数据与空间决策。

方法详情：SpatialLLM首先将原始遥感影像、街景、POI、路网等多模态数据对齐到统一空间格网，经目标检测、语义分割、属性抽取等视觉模型转化为带坐标与语义标签的结构化对象列表；随后按层级模板生成“场景描述”文本，嵌入空间关系、功能分区、生态指标等先验知识，形成可扩展的提示；最后将提示输入通用LLM，通过零样本/少样本链式思维推理完成规划、生态、交通等任务，无需微调即可输出可解释结论。

研究结果：在涵盖18个全球城市、5类空间智能任务的基准上，SpatialLLM仅用7B参数的通用LLM就使平均F1提升14.3%，并在零样本情景下达到或超越专用模型；消融实验表明，多字段知识贡献最大(+8.7%)，其次为空间关系描述(+4.1%)；案例显示模型可输出符合规范的土地利用调整方案并给出可解释的生态影响陈述，验证了LLM对空间分布的精细感知与复杂推理能力。

局限性：目前场景描述依赖预定义模板与外部视觉模型，若检测分割失败会级联放大误差；提示长度随区域面积指数增长，超出LLM上下文窗口时精度骤降；此外，模型输出尚未经过严格的规划法规与工程安全校验，直接用于实际决策存在合规风险。

未来方向：后续可探索自适应提示压缩与分层推理，以突破上下文长度瓶颈，并引入可微空间插件或法规知识图谱进行约束优化，实现端到端安全可控的城市空间决策。

研究相关性：若你研究多模态城市计算、LLM与空间推理交叉或智能规划系统，SpatialLLM提供了无需重训即可复用的提示工程范式及公开数据代码，可直接对比或扩展至时空预测、政策模拟等新任务。
