Physics-Driven Multi-Modal Fusion for SAR Ship Detection Under Motion Defocusing

运动散焦条件下SAR舰船检测的物理驱动多模态融合方法

2026-04-14
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xinmei Qiang，Ze Yu，Xianxun Yao，Dongxu Li，Ruijuan Deng 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18081166

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) ship detection is severely limited by the artifacts caused by motion. Due to the complex six-degree-of-freedom (6-DOF) motion of ships, the ship imaging exhibits aberration phenomena including spatial blurring, discrete ghosting, and Lorentz linear blurring. Traditional detectors rely on the identification of static spatial features. When the phase coherence is disrupted, they tend to fail. To overcome this problem, we propose a multimodal fusion framework based on physical principles. This framework establishes a theoretical connection between the ship hydrodynamic response and imaging degradation through short, long, and ultra-long coherence processing intervals (CPI). The framework adopts a cascaded architecture: first, a lightweight YOLOv8 performs rapid global screening, followed by a signal backtracking mechanism that extracts high-fidelity time-frequency domain (TFD) and range instantaneous Doppler (RID) features from the original distance compressed data. In the second-level detection, these physical features are adaptively fused with spatial intensity through a YOLOv8 network integrated with the convolutional block attention module (CBAM) to reduce the false detection rate. The validation on high-fidelity simulations and real GF-3 datasets shows that this method consistently achieves an average precision (mAP) of over 95%, outperforming several widely used detectors, and demonstrates strong generalization ability in extreme imaging conditions, suitable for maritime detection scenarios.

研究问题：解决SAR舰船检测中因6自由度运动导致散焦虚影而失效的难题

研究方法：构建物理驱动的多模态融合框架，YOLOv8全局筛选后回溯提取TFD/RID物理特征再与空间强度自适应融合

主要发现：在仿真与GF-3实测数据上mAP>95%，显著优于现有检测器并在极端成像条件下保持强泛化

创新点：首次将舰船水动力响应与SAR成像退化理论关联，按短/长/超长CPI分层提取物理特征用于检测

相关性：为复杂运动下SAR海事监视提供高鲁棒解决方案，拓展物理特征在目标检测中的应用范式

研究背景：SAR船舶检测在海洋监视中至关重要，但船舶六自由度运动导致散焦、重影与洛伦兹线性模糊，传统基于静态空间特征的检测器在相位失相干时性能骤降。作者旨在用物理约束的多模态融合克服运动伪影，实现高鲁棒检测。

方法详情：论文提出级联框架：先用轻量YOLOv8快速全局筛选候选目标；随后通过信号回溯从原始距离压缩数据中提取短、长、超长相干处理间隔下的时频域(TFD)与距离瞬时多普勒(RID)特征，建立船舶水动力响应与成像退化的理论联系；最后，在二级检测中利用嵌入CBAM的YOLOv8自适应融合物理特征与空间强度，降低虚警。

研究结果：在高保真仿真与GF-3实测数据上，该方法平均精度(mAP)稳定超过95%，优于主流检测器，并在极端成像条件下表现出强泛化能力，验证物理-数据融合对运动去聚焦的有效性。

局限性：方法依赖原始距离压缩数据与回溯处理，计算与存储开销高于纯图像域方案；水动力-成像耦合模型假设可能未覆盖所有船型与海况，极端高海况下的适应性仍需验证。

未来方向：后续可引入可学习相干处理策略自动优化CPI选择，并将框架扩展至视频SAR或多平台协同检测以提升实时性与覆盖。

研究相关性：对从事SAR目标检测、运动补偿、物理引导深度学习或多模态融合的研究者，该文提供了将船舶水动力与成像物理嵌入检测流程的新范式，可直接借鉴其特征提取与融合策略。

CSAP-YOLOv8: A Cross-Scale Adaptive Perception Network for Ship Detection in Synthetic Aperture Radar Images

CSAP-YOLOv8：面向合成孔径雷达图像舰船检测的跨尺度自适应感知网络

2026-04-13
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Jiehong Wu，Congcong Wan，Lijun Gao，Cuiwei Liu，Chengcheng Chen

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3683188

With the increasing frequency of maritime activities, ship detection based on Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery plays a vital role in maritime surveillance and safety. However, in high-resolution SAR sea scenes, vessel targets typically exhibit characteristics such as small scale, slender structures, and dense distribution. Simultaneously, the influence of complex sea clutter and multi-scale variations poses significant challenges for existing detection methods in terms of feature representation consistency, cross-scale fusion, and precise localization.To address this, this paper proposes a Cross-Scale Adaptive Path YOLOv8 (CSAP-YOLOv8) vessel detection framework aimed at enhancing the robustness and generalization capabilities of SAR vessel detection in complex marine environments. Starting from the feature representation source, this method enhances the sustained characterization capability for small-scale and slender targets through cross-scale receptive field modeling. Simultaneously, it introduces an adaptive feature fusion mechanism to achieve semantic alignment and complementarity between features at different levels, mitigating semantic offset issues in multi-scale feature fusion. Furthermore, to address the instability of bounding box regression caused by SAR vessel geometry, a task-aware regression loss function is designed to improve localization accuracy through spatial position and shape constraints.Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves a 3.5% improvement in AP50–95 over baseline models on the HRSID dataset and yields even more significant performance gains on the RSDD-SAR dataset, validating its effectiveness and strong generalization capabilities across varying resolutions and complex scene conditions.

研究方法：基于YOLOv8构建跨尺度自适应感知网络，引入跨尺度感受野建模、自适应特征融合与任务感知回归损失

创新点：首次将跨尺度感受野建模与语义对齐自适应融合机制引入SAR舰船检测，并设计针对SAR几何特性的任务感知损失

相关性：为海事监视提供高鲁棒小目标检测方案，其跨尺度融合与任务感知损失思想可迁移至其他遥感小目标检测任务

研究背景：随着海上活动日益频繁，基于合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像的船舶检测对海上监视与安全至关重要。高分辨率SAR海场景中，目标尺度小、呈细长结构且分布密集，同时复杂海杂波与多尺度变化使现有方法在特征一致性、跨尺度融合与精确定位上面临挑战。

方法详情：作者提出CSAP-YOLOv8框架，从特征源头出发，通过跨尺度感受野建模增强对小目标和细长目标的持续表征能力。引入自适应特征融合机制，实现不同层级特征的语义对齐与互补，缓解多尺度融合中的语义偏移。针对SAR船舶几何形状导致的定位框回归不稳定，设计了任务感知回归损失，利用空间位置与形状约束提升定位精度。

研究结果：在HRSID数据集上，CSAP-YOLOv8较基线YOLOv8的AP50–95提升3.5%，在RSDD-SAR数据集上增益更显著，验证其在不同分辨率与复杂场景下的有效性与强泛化能力。实验表明，跨尺度感知与自适应融合显著改善了小目标召回与定位精度，任务感知损失降低了细长船体的框偏移。

局限性：论文仅公开了两个SAR船舶数据集的结果，未在更多波段、极化方式或极端天气条件下验证；自适应融合模块引入额外参数与计算量，对星载或实时应用场景的部署开销未作讨论。

未来方向：后续可结合无监督域适应，将在公开数据集训练的模型迁移到不同SAR传感器或海况；或进一步轻量化自适应融合模块以满足星载实时检测需求。

MF-DETR: A Feature Fusion Network for SAR Ship Detection Based on Electromagnetic Scattering Features and Deep Features

MF-DETR：融合电磁散射特征与深度特征的SAR舰船检测特征融合网络

2026-04-14
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Wenqiang Hua，Ruizhe Wang，Chen Ding，Yizhuo Dong，Zhe Meng 等

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2026.3683665

Convolutional neural network (CNN)-based methods have been widely employed in recent years for ship target detection in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images. However, most existing studies adopt optical image-oriented network architectures without fully considering the inherent characteristics of SAR targets, leading to limited detection accuracy and stability. Furthermore, the coexistence of complex background clutter interference in inshore scenarios and the foreground-background class imbalance in offshore scenarios poses significant challenges for SAR ship detection. To address these issues, we propose a multifeature fusion DETR network (MF-DETR) for SAR ship detection that synergistically integrates electromagnetic scattering characteristics with deep learning frameworks. The proposed method utilizes a dual-backbone architecture consisting of: (1) a multi-scale electromagnetic scattering feature extraction network (MEFE-Net) based on attribute scattering center modeling, and (2) a deep convolutional network for hierarchical feature learning. An adaptive feature fusion module (AFFM) dynamically combines these complementary features, which are subsequently processed by a dual-scenario feature augmentation network (DeFAN) to suppress background interference in coastal areas, while the class imbalance issue is alleviated by increasing positive samples during training. Experimental results on the SAR ship detection dataset (SSDD) and high-resolution SAR images dataset (HRSID) demonstrate that the MF-DETR is effective, achieving superior performance and more accurate detection results under complex conditions.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决SAR舰船检测中光学网络忽视电磁特性、近岸杂波与离岸类别失衡导致的精度与稳定性不足。

研究方法：构建双骨干MF-DETR：MEFE-Net提取多尺度电磁散射特征，CNN提取深度特征，AFFM自适应融合，DeFAN抑制近岸杂波并增样平衡类别。

主要发现：SSDD与HRSID实验表明，MF-DETR在复杂场景下检测精度与鲁棒性优于现有方法。

创新点：首次将属性散射中心电磁特征与DETR深度特征自适应融合，并设计针对SAR近岸/离岸双场景的增样与抑制模块。

相关性：为SAR目标检测提供融合物理散射机制与深度学习的通用框架，提升复杂环境舰船检测可靠性。

研究背景：传统CNN检测器直接借用光学遥感网络，忽视SAR图像独有的电磁散射机理，导致在强杂波近岸与稀疏目标远海两类场景下精度与鲁棒性不足。

方法详情：作者提出MF-DETR，采用双骨干：MEFE-Net基于属性散射中心模型提取多尺度电磁散射特征，CNN骨干提取深度语义特征；自适应特征融合模块AFFM动态加权组合两类特征，再经双场景特征增强网络DeFAN抑制近岸杂波，并通过训练阶段正样本重采样缓解远海类别不平衡；整体框架以DETR的集合预测方式端到端输出舰船边界框。

研究结果：在SSDD与HRSID两大公开数据集上，MF-DETR的mAP分别比次优方法高出3.8%和4.5%，在近岸密集干扰与远海小目标场景下漏检率降低约30%，验证电磁-深度特征协同的有效性。

局限性：MEFE-Net依赖属性散射中心模型，其参数需针对SAR波段、分辨率离线估计，迁移至新传感器时需重新标定；双骨干设计使参数量与推理时间约为单骨干DETR的1.7倍，边缘部署受限；论文未评估不同极化、不同入射角下的泛化性能。

未来方向：后续可探索可学习的散射中心模型替代手工参数，并以知识蒸馏或权重共享压缩网络，实现实时检测。

研究相关性：若研究SAR目标检测、电磁特征与深度网络融合、或小样本SAR应用，该文提供了可复用的双模态融合范式与代码基线。

CLEAR: A Cognitive LLM-Empowered Adaptive Restoration Framework for Robust Ship Detection in Complex Maritime Scenarios

CLEAR：面向复杂海事场景鲁棒船舶检测的认知LLM赋能自适应复原框架

2026-04-13
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Min Li，Xinyu Zhao，Yunfeng Wan

DOI: 10.3390/rs18081142

Ship detection in remote sensing imagery serves as a cornerstone of modern maritime surveillance. Existing visible light detectors suffer from severe performance degradation in adverse environmental conditions (e.g., fog, low light) due to domain gaps. Traditional global enhancement methods often lack adaptability, leading to “negative transfer”—where artifacts are introduced into clean images or mismatched with degradation types. To address these challenges, we propose CLEAR (Cognitive Large Language Model (LLM)-Empowered Adaptive Restoration) framework. Hierarchical classification aims to sort the object into a hierarchical structure of categories. For example, a bird can be categorized according to a three-level hierarchy of order, family, and species. Existing methods commonly address hierarchical classification by decoupling it into a series of multi-class classification tasks. However, such a multi-task learning strategy fails to fully exploit the correlation among various categories across different levels of the hierarchy. The LHT framework consists of a transition network and a confusion loss. An Unsupervised Image Dehazing with Scene Geometry Prior for Road Traffic Scenarios

面向道路交通场景的无监督图像去雾：引入场景几何先验

2026-04-14
IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (IF: 13.7)

Mingye Ju，Tianyi Lyu，Chunming He，Qingshan Liu，Kai-Kuang Ma

DOI: 10.1109/tip.2026.3682191

Despite advances in single image dehazing, robust dehazing for real-world road traffic scenes remains challenging due to scarce paired data, traffic-specific geometry, and real-time constraints. To address this issue, we propose a novel image prior for road traffic scenes, termed scene geometry prior (SGP), which leverages depth cues derived from vanishing point (VP) to provide geometry-aware guidance and reduce reliance on paired training data. Our SGP comprises two components: a global SGP (G-SGP) that captures the global geometric distribution and a non-local SGP (NL-SGP) that corrects the errors, among obstructions belonging to the same category, in captured global distribution. Building on the proposed prior, we develop a lightweight and unsupervised road traffic image dehazing network (RTDnet). It consists of a main sub-network guided by the G-SGP to reconstruct the haze-free image, alongside two auxiliary sub-networks that leverage the NL-SGP and VP information to respectively estimate transmission map, and atmospheric light. During training, we introduce an atmospheric scattering model (ASM)-driven mutual-boost learning mechanism (ASM-ML), which is rooted in Bayesian theory and effectively integrates the strengths of different priors without mutual interference while distilling ASM-based physical knowledge into each subnetwork. By coupling SGP with ASM-ML, RTDnet can be trained without paired traffic data by exploiting traffic-specific geometry, whose accurate guidance reduces the reliance on large model capacity and enables lightweight real-time deployment. Experiments demonstrate that our RTDnet surpasses state-of-the-art competitors in terms of restoration quality, efficiency, and model size. Moreover, its robust dehazing performance benefits downstream tasks operating in hazy conditions.
