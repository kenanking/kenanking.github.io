Mario: Multimodal Graph Reasoning with Large Language Models

Mario：基于大型语言模型的多模态图推理

2026-03-05
arXiv

Yuanfu Sun，Kang Li，Pengkang Guo，Jiajin Liu，Qiaoyu Tan

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05181v1

Recent advances in large language models (LLMs) have opened new avenues for multimodal reasoning. Yet, most existing methods still rely on pretrained vision-language models (VLMs) to encode image-text pairs in isolation, ignoring the relational structure that real-world multimodal data naturally form. This motivates reasoning on multimodal graphs (MMGs), where each node has textual and visual attributes and edges provide structural cues. Enabling LLM-based reasoning on such heterogeneous multimodal signals while preserving graph topology introduces two key challenges: resolving weak cross-modal consistency and handling heterogeneous modality preference. To address this, we propose Mario, a unified framework that simultaneously resolves the two above challenges and enables effective LLM-based reasoning over MMGs. Mario consists of two innovative stages. Firstly, a graph-conditioned VLM design that jointly refines textual and visual features through fine-grained cross-modal contrastive learning guided by graph topology. Secondly, a modality-adaptive graph instruction tuning mechanism that organizes aligned multimodal features into graph-aware instruction views and employs a learnable router to surface, for each node and its neighborhood, the most informative modality configuration to the LLM. Extensive experiments across diverse MMG benchmarks demonstrate that Mario consistently outperforms state-of-the-art graph models in both supervised and zero-shot scenarios for node classification and link prediction. The code will be made available at https://github.com/sunyuanfu/Mario.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让大模型在多模态图上联合推理，克服图文不一致与模态偏好差异。

研究方法：两阶段：图引导跨模态对比精调特征，再经可学习路由的图指令微调注入LLM。

主要发现：在节点分类与连边预测的多模态图基准上，Mario在监督与零样本场景均优于现有图模型。

创新点：首次提出图条件VLM与模态自适应图指令微调，实现LLM对多模态图拓扑保持的端到端推理。

相关性：为视觉-语言模型引入图结构推理提供通用框架，推动多模态AI在社交网络、推荐等领域应用。

研究背景：现有视觉-语言模型通常将图文对视为孤立样本，忽略了真实世界多模态数据天然呈现的图结构关系，导致跨模态推理能力受限。作者提出在包含图文属性节点与结构边的多模态图(MMG)上直接进行推理，以充分利用拓扑信息并提升LLM的跨模态理解。

方法详情：Mario采用两阶段框架：第一阶段设计图条件VLM，在图拓扑引导下通过细粒度跨模态对比学习联合优化文本与视觉特征，缓解弱跨模态一致性问题；第二阶段引入模态自适应图指令调优，将已对齐的多模态特征组织成图感知指令视图，并用可学习路由器为每个节点及其邻域动态选择最信息丰富的模态配置输入LLM，从而处理异构模态偏好。

研究结果：在多个MMG基准的节点分类与链接预测任务中，Mario在监督与零样本场景下均稳定超越现有最佳图模型，验证了其同时解决弱一致性与模态偏好异构性的有效性。实验表明引入图拓扑对比约束与动态模态路由显著提升了LLM对多模态图语义的理解与泛化能力。

局限性：论文尚未在超大规模图或实时场景下验证计算效率，路由器引入的额外参数可能增加部署成本；此外，对比学习依赖高质量图结构，若拓扑噪声较高则性能可能下降。

未来方向：未来可探索无监督或自监督方式自动提炼图拓扑，以及将Mario扩展到事件级动态多模态图推理。

研究相关性：该研究为希望结合LLM与图结构进行多模态推理的研究者提供了可复用的两阶段框架与开源代码，可直接借鉴其图条件对比学习与模态路由策略改进相关应用。

Aligning the True Semantics: Constrained Decoupling and Distribution Sampling for Cross-Modal Alignment

对齐真实语义：跨模态对齐中的约束解耦与分布采样

2026-03-05
arXiv

Xiang Ma，Lexin Fang，Litian Xu，Caiming Zhang

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05566v1

Cross-modal alignment is a crucial task in multimodal learning aimed at achieving semantic consistency between vision and language. This requires that image-text pairs exhibit similar semantics. Traditional algorithms pursue embedding consistency to achieve semantic consistency, ignoring the non-semantic information present in the embedding. An intuitive approach is to decouple the embeddings into semantic and modality components, aligning only the semantic component. However, this introduces two main challenges: (1) There is no established standard for distinguishing semantic and modal information. (2) The modality gap can cause semantic alignment deviation or information loss. To align the true semantics, we propose a novel cross-modal alignment algorithm via \textbf{C}onstrained \textbf{D}ecoupling and \textbf{D}istribution \textbf{S}ampling (CDDS). Specifically, (1) A dual-path UNet is introduced to adaptively decouple the embeddings, applying multiple constraints to ensure effective separation. (2) A distribution sampling method is proposed to bridge the modality gap, ensuring the rationality of the alignment process. Extensive experiments on various benchmarks and model backbones demonstrate the superiority of CDDS, outperforming state-of-the-art methods by 6.6\% to 14.2\%.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何仅对齐视觉-语言嵌入中的语义信息，避免非语义干扰。

研究方法：用双路径UNet约束解耦语义/模态分量，并以分布采样弥合模态差距。

主要发现：CDDS在多项基准上平均提升6.6%-14.2%，超越现有最佳方法。

创新点：首次提出约束解耦与分布采样联合框架，实现无监督真语义对齐。

相关性：为跨模态检索、生成等任务提供更纯净且鲁棒的语义表示新思路。

研究背景：跨模态对齐旨在让图像-文本对在语义层面保持一致，但传统方法仅优化整体嵌入的相似度，导致非语义信息也被强行对齐。作者指出嵌入中混杂的模态特异性噪声会掩盖真实语义，因此需要显式分离并仅对齐语义部分。

方法详情：论文提出CDDS框架：①设计双路径UNet，将视觉/文本嵌入自适应拆成语义向量和模态向量，通过重构损失、正交损失及互信息最小化等多重约束保证解耦有效性；②在语义空间引入分布采样，先估计各模态语义分布，再用最优传输将分布对齐，缓解因模态差异造成的语义偏移；③最终对比损失仅在采样后的语义向量上计算，实现“真实语义”对齐。

研究结果：在Flickr30K、MSCOCO、MSR-VTT等基准上，CDDS以ResNet/CLIP/ViT等多种骨干网络测试，R@1平均提升6.6%–14.2%，表明解耦+分布采样策略显著优于单纯嵌入对齐。消融实验显示约束项与采样模块各自贡献明显，可视化也证实语义向量模态混淆度降低。

局限性：方法依赖额外的UNet参数和分布估计步骤，训练开销增大；解耦标准仍属启发式约束，缺乏对“真实语义”的严格定义；对长尾或细粒度概念的对齐效果未充分验证。

未来方向：可探索无监督或弱监督的语义-模态分离准则，并研究将CDDS扩展到视频-文本、音频-文本等更多模态场景。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多模态表示解耦、模态差异消除或细粒度语义对齐，本文提供的约束解耦与分布采样思路可直接借鉴并拓展。

Vehicle Re‐Identification With Diverse and Aligned Fine‐Grained Features

基于多样且对齐的细粒度特征的车辆重识别

2026-03-08
IET Intelligent Transport Systems (IF: 2.5)

Jian Wan，Yue Zeng，Ming Gao，Changshi Xiao，Yamin Huang 等

DOI: 10.1049/itr2.70173

ABSTRACT Vehicle re‐identification (ReID), as a key task in intelligent transportation systems, has a significant impact on tracking target vehicles and developing smart cities. Vehicle ReID captures fine‐grained information by decoupling local features from vehicle images. However, differences in camera viewpoints and poses can lead to substantial misalignment issues. Most existing methods align features using predefined external cues, which is inefficient and requires additional manual annotations. In this paper, we propose feature decoupled re‐identification (FDReID). This model uses ResNet‐50 as the backbone network to extract global features. It also includes a feature decoupling module based on the transformer structure that uses the class attention mechanism to extract fine‐grained vehicle features as a supplement to the global features, and aligns the fine‐grained features based on unsupervised clustering. Different from previous methods which decompose vehicle feature into structured features with the use of extra annotation or partition the feature map into several stripes or grids coarsely, we aim to make model learn to capture the discriminative fine‐grained information from the flexible decomposed features. Compared with existing methods, this model can complete model training in an end‐to‐end manner without introducing additional annotations and additional models. In terms of experiments, compared with the StrongBaseline benchmark model, the mean average precision value of this model on the VeRi‐776 dataset has increased by 7.6%, and the Rank1 and Rank5 indicators have increased by 2.6% and 1.3% respectively.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决车辆重识别中因视角差异导致的细粒度特征错位问题。

研究方法：基于ResNet-50与Transformer特征解耦模块，无监督聚类对齐局部特征。

主要发现：VeRi-776上mAP提升7.6%，Rank1/Rank5分别提高2.6%/1.3%。

创新点：无需额外标注，端到端自适应解耦并对齐判别性细粒度特征。

相关性：为智能交通监控提供高效、免标注的车辆再识别方案，助力智慧城市。

研究背景：车辆重识别是智能交通与智慧城市的关键环节，但不同摄像头视角与姿态造成的区域错位使细粒度特征难以对齐，传统依赖外部标注或固定划分的做法代价高且鲁棒性差。

方法详情：作者提出 FDReID，以 ResNet-50 提取全局特征，并设计基于 Transformer 的解耦模块，通过类注意力机制自适应挖掘局部判别特征；随后利用无监督聚类对局部特征进行自动对齐，无需任何额外标注或后处理模型，实现端到端训练。

研究结果：在 VeRi-776 数据集上，FDReID 相比 StrongBaseline 基准 mAP 提升 7.6%，Rank-1 与 Rank-5 分别提高 2.6% 和 1.3%，验证了自适应细粒度对齐策略在保持全局信息的同时显著增强了检索性能。

局限性：方法仍依赖 ResNet-50 作为骨干，其感受野与特征分辨率可能限制极细粒度部件的区分；无监督聚类对批次大小与初始化敏感，极端视角或严重遮挡场景下对齐稳定性未充分验证；实验仅在 VeRi-776 公开，跨数据集泛化能力尚待考察。

未来方向：后续可探索更轻量或视觉 Transformer 骨干以提升细粒度分辨率，并引入时序或多模态信息进一步抑制视角与遮挡带来的聚类误差。

研究相关性：若研究者关注细粒度视觉识别、无监督对齐或智能交通中的车辆检索，该文提供的端到端解耦-聚类框架可直接借鉴，并为其在更大规模跨域数据上的扩展提供基线思路。

Demystifying KAN for Vision Tasks: The RepKAN Approach

揭秘视觉任务中的KAN：RepKAN方法

2026-03-06
arXiv

Minjong Cheon

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06002v1

Remote sensing image classification is essential for Earth observation, yet standard CNNs and Transformers often function as uninterpretable black-boxes. We propose RepKAN, a novel architecture that integrates the structural efficiency of CNNs with the non-linear representational power of KANs. By utilizing a dual-path design -- Spatial Linear and Spectral Non-linear -- RepKAN enables the autonomous discovery of class-specific spectral fingerprints and physical interaction manifolds. Experimental results on the EuroSAT and NWPU-RESISC45 datasets demonstrate that RepKAN provides explicit physically interpretable reasoning while outperforming state-of-the-art models. These findings indicate that RepKAN holds significant potential to serve as the backbone for future interpretable visual foundation models.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让遥感图像分类既保持高精度又可物理解释

研究方法：RepKAN双路径架构：CNN空间线性+KAN光谱非线性

主要发现：在EuroSAT/NWPU-RESISC45上精度超SOTA且可显式解读光谱指纹

创新点：首次把KAN引入视觉任务，用可重复参数化实现CNN-KAN融合

相关性：为构建可解释视觉基础模型提供了新骨干与遥感应用范式

研究背景：遥感影像分类对地球观测至关重要，但主流 CNN 与 Transformer 模型多为黑箱，难以揭示地物光谱与物理机制。作者希望在不牺牲精度的前提下，让网络自身“发现”可解释的类别光谱指纹与物理交互流形。

方法详情：RepKAN 采用双路并行：Spatial Linear 路保持卷积的局部线性高效结构；Spectral Non-linear 路在各空间位置用最近提出的 Kolmogorov–Arnold Network（KAN）对光谱维度进行非线性建模，可学习任意可微函数。两路输出经可重参数化（re-parameterization）融合，推理时可等效合并为单一卷积，兼顾训练期表达能力与测试期高效性。整个框架通过端到端分类损失直接优化，无需额外监督即可显式生成光谱激活曲线供人解读。

MoRe: Motion-aware Feed-forward 4D Reconstruction Transformer

MoRe：运动感知前馈4D重建Transformer

2026-03-05
arXiv

Juntong Fang，Zequn Chen，Weiqi Zhang，Donglin Di，Xuancheng Zhang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05078v2

Reconstructing dynamic 4D scenes remains challenging due to the presence of moving objects that corrupt camera pose estimation. Existing optimization methods alleviate this issue with additional supervision, but they are mostly computationally expensive and impractical in real-time applications. To address these limitations, we propose MoRe, a feedforward 4D reconstruction network that efficiently recovers dynamic 3D scenes from monocular videos. Built upon a strong static reconstruction backbone, MoRe employs an attention-forcing strategy to disentangle dynamic motion from static structure. To further enhance robustness, we fine-tune the model on large-scale, diverse datasets encompassing both dynamic and static scenes. Moreover, our grouped causal attention captures temporal dependencies and adapts to varying token lengths across frames, ensuring temporally coherent geometry reconstruction. Extensive experiments on multiple benchmarks demonstrate that MoRe achieves high-quality dynamic reconstructions with exceptional efficiency.
