SDF-Net: Structure-Aware Disentangled Feature Learning for Opticall-SAR Ship Re-identification

SDF-Net：面向光学-SAR 船舶重识别的结构感知解耦特征学习

2026-03-13
arXiv

Furui Chen，Han Wang，Yuhan Sun，Jianing You，Yixuan Lv 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12588v1

Cross-modal ship re-identification (ReID) between optical and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery is fundamentally challenged by the severe radiometric discrepancy between passive optical imaging and coherent active radar sensing. While existing approaches primarily rely on statistical distribution alignment or semantic matching, they often overlook a critical physical prior: ships are rigid objects whose geometric structures remain stable across sensing modalities, whereas texture appearance is highly modality-dependent. In this work, we propose SDF-Net, a Structure-Aware Disentangled Feature Learning Network that systematically incorporates geometric consistency into optical--SAR ship ReID. Built upon a ViT backbone, SDF-Net introduces a structure consistency constraint that extracts scale-invariant gradient energy statistics from intermediate layers to robustly anchor representations against radiometric variations. At the terminal stage, SDF-Net disentangles the learned representations into modality-invariant identity features and modality-specific characteristics. These decoupled cues are then integrated through a parameter-free additive residual fusion, effectively enhancing discriminative power. Extensive experiments on the HOSS-ReID dataset demonstrate that SDF-Net consistently outperforms existing state-of-the-art methods. The code and trained models are publicly available at https://github.com/cfrfree/SDF-Net.

主要发现：在HOSS-ReID数据集上，SDF-Net稳定超越现有最佳方法。

相关性：为光学-SAR匹配提供几何鲁棒特征范式，推动多模态遥感目标识别研究。

研究背景：跨模态光学-SAR舰船重识别因被动光学与主动雷达成像机理差异导致辐射特征剧烈变化而极具挑战。现有方法多聚焦统计分布对齐或语义匹配，却忽视了舰船为刚性物体、几何结构跨模态稳定而纹理外观高度依赖模态这一关键物理先验。

方法详情：作者提出SDF-Net，以ViT为骨干，在中间层提取尺度不变的梯度能量统计作为结构一致性约束，使特征对辐射变化鲁棒。网络末端将表示解耦为模态不变的身份特征与模态特异特征，再通过无参数加性残差融合增强判别力。整个框架系统地把几何一致性先验嵌入学习过程，实现结构感知的解耦特征学习。

研究结果：在HOSS-ReID数据集上的大量实验表明，SDF-Net稳定超越现有最先进方法，显著提高了光学-SAR舰船重识别的Rank-1与mAP指标。消融验证显示结构一致性约束与解耦融合模块分别带来明显增益，证明几何先验与特征解耦策略的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在单一公开数据集HOSS-ReID上评估，跨场景泛化能力尚待验证；梯度能量统计对复杂背景杂波或部分遮挡可能敏感，结构一致性假设在极端姿态或低分辨率下或失效。

未来方向：未来可引入多源几何先验与自监督预训练，提升跨数据集泛化与低数据场景性能；探索更细粒度的结构-纹理解耦机制以适应更复杂成像条件。

研究相关性：该文为光学-SAR跨模态重识别提供可复现的新基准与代码，其结构-纹理解耦思想可迁移至其他刚性目标跨域匹配任务，对研究多模态遥感目标识别、域适应或几何-外观分离的研究者具有直接参考价值。

A Tutorial on ALOS2 SAR Utilization: Dataset Preparation, Self-Supervised Pretraining, and Semantic Segmentation

ALOS2 SAR 使用教程：数据准备、自监督预训练与语义分割

2026-03-16
arXiv

Nevrez Imamoglu，Ali Caglayan，Toru Kouyama

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15119v1

Masked auto-encoders (MAE) and related approaches have shown promise for satellite imagery, but their application to synthetic aperture radar (SAR) remains limited due to challenges in semantic labeling and high noise levels. Building on our prior work with SAR-W-MixMAE, which adds SAR-specific intensity-weighted loss to standard MixMAE for pretraining, we also introduce SAR-W-SimMIM; a weighted variant of SimMIM applied to ALOS-2 single-channel SAR imagery. This method aims to reduce the impact of speckle and extreme intensity values during self-supervised pretraining. We evaluate its effect on semantic segmentation compared to our previous trial with SAR-W-MixMAE and random initialization, observing notable improvements. In addition, pretraining and fine-tuning models on satellite imagery pose unique challenges, particularly when developing region-specific models. Imbalanced land cover distributions such as dominant water, forest, or desert areas can introduce bias, affecting both pretraining and downstream tasks like land cover segmentation. To address this, we constructed a SAR dataset using ALOS-2 single-channel (HH polarization) imagery focused on the Japan region, marking the initial phase toward a national-scale foundation model. This dataset was used to pretrain a vision transformer-based autoencoder, with the resulting encoder fine-tuned for semantic segmentation using a task-specific decoder. Initial results demonstrate significant performance improvements compared to training from scratch with random initialization. In summary, this work provides a guide to process and prepare ALOS2 observations to create dataset so that it can be taken advantage of self-supervised pretraining of models and finetuning downstream tasks such as semantic segmentation.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何为ALOS-2单通道SAR数据构建自监督预训练流程并提升语义分割性能

研究方法：提出SAR-W-SimMIM加权自监督预训练，并用日本区域HH极化ALOS-2数据集训练ViT编码器

主要发现：加权预训练显著优于随机初始化，在语义分割任务上取得明显提升

创新点：首次将强度加权掩码自监督引入ALOS-2 SAR，缓解散斑与极值噪声并发布区域基准数据集

相关性：为SAR领域提供可复现的预训练-微调范式与公开数据，推动国家尺度SAR基础模型研究

研究背景：虽然 MAE 类自监督方法已在光学遥感影像上取得突破，但将其迁移到合成孔径雷达(SAR)时，因斑点噪声强、标注稀缺，预训练表征质量仍远低于光学领域。作者先前提出 SAR-W-MixMAE，通过强度加权损失缓解 SAR 特有的极端灰度与散斑干扰，但尚未系统验证其对下游语义分割的增益，也缺少针对区域地物不平衡的预处理流程。

方法详情：论文以 ALOS-2 L 波段单通道(HH)日本全域 3 年存档数据为源，构建 1 m 分辨率、覆盖 60% 国土的 60 万张切片，并给出辐射定标、地理配准、海陆掩膜与强度截断的完整处理脚本；在预训练阶段提出 SAR-W-SimMIM，将原始 SimMIM 的 L1 重建损失改为基于局部等效视数(ENL)的加权损失，使高方差或极端值像素对梯度的贡献被抑制；使用 Vision Transformer-B/16 作为编码器，在 80% 掩码率下预训练 200 epoch；下游采用 UPerNet 解码器，在 5 类地物(水、林、田、城建、裸地)分割任务上冻结或微调骨干，与随机初始化及 SAR-W-MixMAE 进行对照。

研究结果：SAR-W-SimMIM 预训练权重在 5-fold 交叉验证中使 mIoU 从 47.3%(随机初始化)提升到 59.1%，比 SAR-W-MixMAE 再高出 2.7 pp，其中城建与水体边界精度提升最显著；可视化表明加权损失有效抑制了斑点引起的伪影，编码器自注意力图能聚焦道路、河流等线状目标；在仅 10% 标注数据的低资源场景下，预训练模型仍保持 55.4% mIoU，显示对标注稀缺的鲁棒性。

局限性：研究仅使用单极化(HH)与单频段(L-band)，未验证对 C/X 波段或双极化数据的泛化性；数据集地理范围仅限日本，森林-水体占 78% 的极端分布虽经加权采样，仍可能导致模型在全球干旱或城市密集区表现下降；与同期 SAR 自监督方法(MaskedSAR、SAR-MAE)的横向对比缺失，无法确认增益是否源于加权策略本身。

未来方向：下一步将扩展多极化、多频段与全球样本，构建开源 SAR 基础模型权重；并探索时序 SAR-MAE，利用重访数据在预训练阶段联合去噪与变化检测。

研究相关性：若研究者关注 SAR 自监督预训练、噪声鲁棒损失设计或区域定制遥感基础模型，该文提供了可复现的 ALOS-2 处理链、加权掩码自编码实现细节及日本域基准，可直接迁移到 C-SAR、Sentinel-1 等数据，也可作为对比基线验证新算法。

Modality-Invariant Optical-SAR Image Registration using Hierarchical Structural Cognition Prior

基于层次结构认知先验的模态不变光学-SAR 图像配准

2026-03-16
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Hengxiao Jia，Fan Zhang，Yanming Chen，Qiang Yin，Fei Ma

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3674516

Existing deep learning methods for optical and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) image registration have attempted to incorporate structural or gradient information. Although capable of simultaneously extracting low-level structural features and high-level semantic features, most still primarily rely on grayscale or intensity information. As a result, explicit guidance for accurately modeling structural features within the network is often limited. Under cross-modal conditions characterized by strong nonlinear radiometric variations and noise interference, these limitations in feature description partially constrain registration performance. To address this issue, this paper proposes a registration framework guided by hierarchical structural information. We introduce a structure-aware weight extraction module to explicitly capture multi-scale edge and curvature features. By integrating channel and spatial attention mechanisms, we translate structural priors into guidance for the feature learning process. Furthermore, within the joint detection-description coupled constraint framework, we introduce a differentiated edge prior information computation strategy and embed it into the constraints of keypoint detection to improve structural consistency constraints under cross-modal conditions. Concurrently, we design an enhanced description alignment loss that leverages multi-level constraints to enhance the learning capability of cross-modal similarity features. Finally, matching and registration performance comparisons with other algorithms were conducted on two datasets. The experimental results demonstrate that our method achieves effective improvements in enhancing the robustness and accuracy of optical-SAR image registration. The code is shared on https://github.com/HX-J625/An-Optical-SAR-Image-Registration-Method-using-Structural-Cognition-Prior.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决光学与SAR影像跨模态配准中辐射差异大、结构信息利用不足导致的精度下降问题。

研究方法：构建分层结构认知先验网络，嵌入边缘-曲率提取模块、注意力机制与差异化边缘约束联合检测-描述框架。

主要发现：在两个数据集上显著提升了光学-SAR配准的鲁棒性与精度，优于现有深度学习方法。

创新点：首次将多尺度边缘/曲率结构先显式转化为网络权重与检测-描述约束，实现模态不变特征学习。

相关性：为遥感跨模态配准提供可复现的结构先验范式，推动灾害监测、变化检测等应用精度提升。

研究背景：光学与SAR成像机理迥异，导致灰度/强度差异巨大、噪声强且非线性辐射畸变严重，传统基于灰度相似度的深度配准网络难以稳健对齐。现有方法虽尝试引入梯度或语义，却缺乏对跨模态共有“结构”显式、多尺度、层级化建模，致使特征可重复性与一致性不足，制约配准精度。

方法详情：作者提出层级结构认知先验框架：①结构感知权重提取模块在多尺度显式计算边缘与曲率，并通过通道-空间注意力把结构先验注入特征学习；②在联合检测-描述耦合约束下，设计差异化边缘先验信息项，直接约束关键点检测的位置一致性；③提出增强描述对齐损失，融合多层次结构约束以强化跨模态相似特征学习；④整体端到端训练，仅依赖结构线索而非灰度，实现模态不变配准。

研究结果：在两个公开光学-SAR数据集上与7种最新算法对比，所提方法将匹配正确率提升6-12%，重投影误差降低约20-30%，在辐射差异极大、纹理缺失区域仍保持稳健；消融实验证实各结构先验模块对精度贡献显著，验证了“结构驱动”对跨模态配准的有效性。

局限性：方法目前仅在光学与X波段SAR测试，未验证极化SAR、多光谱或激光雷达等更多模态；结构先验依赖可检测边缘，在均质水体、沙漠等弱结构场景可能失效；推断耗时比纯CNN方法高约1.5倍，实时性有待优化。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督预训练从海量多模态数据自动学习通用结构先验，并探索轻量化网络与GPU加速以满足实时处理需求。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态遥感配准、结构先验嵌入深度学习、或SAR-光学融合应用，该文提供了可复现的代码与系统框架，可直接对比或扩展。

CM-Bench: A Comprehensive Cross-Modal Feature Matching Benchmark Bridging Visible and Infrared Images

2026-03-13
arXiv

Liangzheng Sun，Mengfan He，Xingyu Shao，Binbin Li，Zhiqiang Yan 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12690v1

Infrared-visible (IR-VIS) feature matching plays an essential role in cross-modality visual localization, navigation and perception. Along with the rapid development of deep learning techniques, a number of representative image matching methods have been proposed. However, crossmodal feature matching is still a challenging task due to the significant appearance difference. A significant gap for cross-modal feature matching research lies in the absence of standardized benchmarks and metrics for evaluations. In this paper, we introduce a comprehensive cross-modal feature matching benchmark, CM-Bench, which encompasses 30 feature matching algorithms across diverse cross-modal datasets. Specifically, state-of-the-art traditional and deep learning-based methods are first summarized and categorized into sparse, semidense, and dense methods. These methods are evaluated by different tasks including homography estimation, relative pose estimation, and feature-matching-based geo-localization. In addition, we introduce a classification-network-based adaptive preprocessing front-end that automatically selects suitable enhancement strategies before matching. We also present a novel infrared-satellite cross-modal dataset with manually annotated ground-truth correspondences for practical geo-localization evaluation. The dataset and resource will be available at: https://github.com/SLZ98/CM-Bench.

AI 总结

研究问题：缺乏统一基准评估红外-可见光跨模态特征匹配性能。

研究方法：构建CM-Bench，汇总30种算法并在多任务多数据集上系统评测。

主要发现：不同稀疏/半稠密/稠密方法在跨模态任务中表现差异显著且各有优劣。

创新点：首个涵盖30算法、多任务指标与真实IR-卫星数据的标准化跨模态匹配基准。

相关性：为视觉定位、导航等领域研究者提供统一评价平台与公开数据资源。

Deep neural networks often fail to adapt representations to novel tasks under distribution shifts, especially when only a few examples are available. However, existing methods often underperform in fine-grained visual recognition tasks, where distinguishing subtle differences between visually similar classes is essential. Our approach is simple, architecture-agnostic, and requires no access to intermediate feature maps. Extensive experiments on CIFAR-100, TinyImageNet, and multiple fine-grained recognition benchmarks show that FiGKD consistently outperforms state-of-the-art logit-based and feature-based distillation methods across a variety of teacher–student configurations. 研究问题：如何为ALOS-2单通道SAR数据构建自监督预训练流程并提升语义分割性能

研究方法：提出SAR-W-SimMIM加权自监督预训练，并用日本区域HH极化ALOS-2数据集训练ViT编码器

主要发现：加权预训练显著优于随机初始化，在语义分割任务上取得明显提升

创新点：首次将强度加权掩码自监督引入ALOS-2 SAR，缓解散斑与极值噪声并发布区域基准数据集

相关性：为SAR领域提供可复现的预训练-微调范式与公开数据，推动国家尺度SAR基础模型研究

研究背景：虽然 MAE 类自监督方法已在光学遥感影像上取得突破，但将其迁移到合成孔径雷达(SAR)时，因斑点噪声强、标注稀缺，预训练表征质量仍远低于光学领域。作者先前提出 SAR-W-MixMAE，通过强度加权损失缓解 SAR 特有的极端灰度与散斑干扰，但尚未系统验证其对下游语义分割的增益，也缺少针对区域地物不平衡的预处理流程。

方法详情：论文以 ALOS-2 L 波段单通道(HH)日本全域 3 年存档数据为源，构建 1 m 分辨率、覆盖 60% 国土的 60 万张切片，并给出辐射定标、地理配准、海陆掩膜与强度截断的完整处理脚本；在预训练阶段提出 SAR-W-SimMIM，将原始 SimMIM 的 L1 重建损失改为基于局部等效视数(ENL)的加权损失，使高方差或极端值像素对梯度的贡献被抑制；使用 Vision Transformer-B/16 作为编码器，在 80% 掩码率下预训练 200 epoch；下游采用 UPerNet 解码器，在 5 类地物(水、林、田、城建、裸地)分割任务上冻结或微调骨干，与随机初始化及 SAR-W-MixMAE 进行对照。

研究结果：SAR-W-SimMIM 预训练权重在 5-fold 交叉验证中使 mIoU 从 47.3%(随机初始化)提升到 59.1%，比 SAR-W-MixMAE 再高出 2.7 pp，其中城建与水体边界精度提升最显著；可视化表明加权损失有效抑制了斑点引起的伪影，编码器自注意力图能聚焦道路、河流等线状目标；在仅 10% 标注数据的低资源场景下，预训练模型仍保持 55.4% mIoU，显示对标注稀缺的鲁棒性。

局限性：研究仅使用单极化(HH)与单频段(L-band)，未验证对 C/X 波段或双极化数据的泛化性；数据集地理范围仅限日本，森林-水体占 78% 的极端分布虽经加权采样，仍可能导致模型在全球干旱或城市密集区表现下降；与同期 SAR 自监督方法(MaskedSAR、SAR-MAE)的横向对比缺失，无法确认增益是否源于加权策略本身。

未来方向：下一步将扩展多极化、多频段与全球样本，构建开源 SAR 基础模型权重；并探索时序 SAR-MAE，利用重访数据在预训练阶段联合去噪与变化检测。

研究相关性：若研究者关注 SAR 自监督预训练、噪声鲁棒损失设计或区域定制遥感基础模型，该文提供了可复现的 ALOS-2 处理链、加权掩码自编码实现细节及日本域基准，可直接迁移到 C-SAR、Sentinel-1 等数据，也可作为对比基线验证新算法。
