DCT-ART: A Spatial–Frequency Fusion Network for Robust Ship Wake Detection

DCT-ART：一种用于鲁棒舰船尾流检测的空间-频率融合网络

2026-07-15
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Yaxiong Chen，Yang Ding，Yankai Liu，Shili Xiong

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3713616

Ship wake detection is critical in remote sensing tasks such as maritime target recognition and behavior analysis. However, wakes are often fragmented and distorted under complex sea conditions. In addition, strong sea clutter overlaps with wake signals, posing severe challenges for conventional feature-driven detection methods. To tackle this, we propose DCT-ART, a novel detection network that jointly exploits spatial and frequency-domain representations. It introduces two key modules: 1) a multi-branch Aggregated Residual Transformation (ART) module with grouped convolutions to extract multi-scale spatial features of wakes; 2) a Discrete Cosine Transform-based Channel Attention (DCT-CA) module in the frequency domain to highlight discriminative spectral features and suppress background clutter. Experimental results on the SWIM and CGWX datasets demonstrate that DCT-ART achieves superior mAP and Recall compared to state-of-the-art methods, while maintaining robustness under low-visibility and high-clutter conditions, thereby improving the accuracy and reliability of wake detection in maritime remote sensing imagery.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决复杂海况下船尾波检测中尾波断裂、失真及海杂波干扰的问题。

研究方法：提出DCT-ART网络，含多分支ART模块和频域DCT-CA模块进行空间-频率融合。

主要发现：在SWIM和CGWX数据集上mAP和召回率优于现有方法，低可见度高杂波下鲁棒性强。

创新点：首次将DCT频域通道注意力与分组卷积残差变换结合用于船尾波检测。

相关性：提升海事遥感中目标识别与行为分析的准确性和可靠性。

研究背景：船尾迹检测在海洋遥感任务（如海上目标识别和行为分析）中至关重要，但复杂海况下尾迹常出现碎片化、形变，且强海杂波与尾迹信号重叠，传统特征驱动方法面临严峻挑战。本文旨在通过融合空间和频域表示，提高尾迹检测的准确性和鲁棒性。

方法详情：提出DCT-ART网络，包含两个关键模块：一是多分支聚合残差变换（ART）模块，利用分组卷积提取尾迹的多尺度空间特征；二是基于离散余弦变换的通道注意力（DCT-CA）模块，在频域中突出判别性频谱特征并抑制背景杂波。网络联合利用空间和频域信息，实现端到端的鲁棒尾迹检测。

研究结果：在SWIM和CGWX数据集上的实验表明，DCT-ART在mAP和Recall指标上均优于现有最先进方法，尤其在低可见度和高杂波条件下保持强鲁棒性。这证明了空间-频域融合网络能有效提升船尾迹检测的准确性和可靠性，为海洋遥感应用提供有力支撑。

局限性：该方法可能对计算资源要求较高，因为引入了分组卷积和频域变换模块，实时性有待验证。此外，实验仅在两个公开数据集上评估，缺乏对不同海域、不同传感器类型的广泛验证，泛化能力仍需进一步测试。

未来方向：未来可探索轻量化设计以降低计算开销，并将频域注意力机制推广到其他海洋遥感任务（如目标分类、海冰检测）。同时，结合多时序数据或雷达/光学融合数据，进一步提升尾迹检测在复杂动态场景下的性能。

研究相关性：该论文针对船尾迹检测这一遥感领域难题，提出了创新的空间-频域融合架构，对从事海上目标检测、遥感图像分析、深度学习应用于地球观测的研究者具有重要参考价值，尤其对处理高噪声、低对比度图像场景的方法设计有启发意义。

A Structured Constraint-Based RPCA Algorithm for Ground Moving Target Detection and Parameter Estimation

基于结构化约束的RPCA算法用于地面运动目标检测与参数估计

2026-07-15
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Kun Liu，Xiongpeng He，Guisheng Liao，Jian Xue，Biao Yang

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3713514

Robust Principal Component Analysis (RPCA) exploits target characteristics in multi-channel SAR-GMTI to decompose the observed data matrix into a low-rank clutter matrix and a sparse target matrix. However, during the optimization process, conventional RPCA algorithms may distort the target phase information, resulting in the separation of target detection and parameter estimation. In addition, strong scatterers may lead to a high false alarm rate in target detection. To address these challenges, a structured constraint-based RPCA (SCRPCA) algorithm is proposed. On the one hand, the rank-one property of clutter is utilized to replace the conventional low-rank constraint; on the other hand, the sparse target component is reformulated as the product of amplitude and phase terms, enabling simultaneous target detection and parameter estimation. Furthermore, the ATI phase is incorporated as a weighting factor to suppress clutter energy leakage. The block coordinate descent algorithm is employed to solve the proposed optimization model. Experimental results using real measured data demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method.

研究问题：解决传统RPCA算法中目标相位失真、检测与参数估计分离及高虚警率的问题。

研究方法：提出结构化约束RPCA（SCRPCA），利用杂波秩一性并重构稀疏目标为幅度相位乘积，结合ATI相位加权抑制能量泄漏。

主要发现：SCRPCA能同时检测目标并估计参数，有效降低强散射体引起的虚警率。

创新点：用秩一约束替代低秩约束，稀疏目标分解为幅度相位项，实现检测与参数估计联合优化。

相关性：提升SAR-GMTI中运动目标检测的准确性和参数估计效率，适用于实际雷达系统。

研究背景：多通道SAR-GMTI中，传统RPCA算法在优化过程中可能扭曲目标相位信息，导致目标检测与参数估计分离；同时强散射体易引起高虚警率。为克服这些问题，提出一种基于结构化约束的RPCA算法。

方法详情：利用杂波的秩一特性替代传统低秩约束，同时将稀疏目标分量建模为幅度与相位项的乘积，实现目标检测与参数估计的联合处理；进一步引入ATI相位作为权重因子抑制杂波能量泄漏，并采用块坐标下降算法求解优化模型。

研究结果：实测数据实验表明所提方法有效解决了相位信息失真和高虚警率问题，能够在抑制杂波的同时准确检测运动目标并估计其参数，验证了算法的优越性。

局限性：该方法可能依赖于ATI相位的准确性，在复杂杂波环境或强干扰场景下性能可能下降；此外，块坐标下降算法的计算复杂度较高，实时应用存在挑战。

未来方向：未来可探索将深度学习与结构化约束相结合以提升鲁棒性，或扩展算法以处理多目标场景并降低计算负担。

研究相关性：该论文针对SAR-GMTI中运动目标检测与参数估计的瓶颈提出创新解法，对从事雷达信号处理、稳健主成分分析及地面动目标检测的研究者具有重要参考价值。

Spectral-Prior-Guided Swin TransUnet for Sparse-Aperture FMCW MIMO-SAR Imaging

光谱先验引导的Swin TransUnet用于稀疏孔径FMCW MIMO-SAR成像

2026-07-15
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Jiawei Wang，Xiaopeng Yan，Qin Zhao，Chengqi Chen，Yongqiang Wang 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18142350

In millimeter-wave frequency-modulated continuous-wave (FMCW) multiple-input multiple-output synthetic-aperture radar (MIMO-SAR) imaging, platform displacement beyond the spatial Nyquist limit during a slow-time sampling interval creates aperture gaps, causing azimuth aliasing and degraded resolution. This paper proposes a spectral-prior-guided Swin TransUnet (SSTU) method for suppressing azimuth ambiguity in sparse moving-array imaging. Gaussian soft labels derived from point-scatterer positions formulate localization as heatmap regression and guide mainlobe learning. A two-dimensional fast Fourier transform (2D-FFT) layer then constructs a range–azimuth spectrum that exposes main peaks, sidelobes, and periodic grating lobes. A convolutional encoder extracts local spectral features, Swin Transformer blocks model long-range ambiguity correlations, and a U-Net-style multiscale decoder reconstructs high-resolution range–azimuth images. Simulations show that SSTU reliably recovers multiple point targets from noise and grating lobes despite substantial aperture gaps. At 60% aperture sparsity and signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) above −6 dB, it achieves a root mean square error (RMSE) below 10−2 and an azimuth ambiguity suppression ratio better than −30 dB, outperforming conventional methods. Measurements using a 77 GHz radar platform further demonstrate high-quality outdoor imaging of randomly distributed strong scatterers at 60% moving-aperture sparsity.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决稀疏孔径FMCW MIMO-SAR成像的方位模糊和分辨率下降问题。

研究方法：提出谱先验引导Swin TransUnet，结合高斯软标签、2D-FFT、卷积编码器和Swin Transformer。

主要发现：60%稀疏度且SNR>-6dB时，RMSE<10^-2，模糊抑制比优于-30dB。

创新点：融合谱先验与Swin Transformer及U-Net，利用高斯软标签引导主瓣学习抑制模糊。

相关性：对毫米波雷达成像和稀疏阵列压缩感知研究有重要参考价值。

研究背景：在毫米波FMCW MIMO-SAR成像中，平台缓慢时间采样间隔内的位移若超过空间奈奎斯特极限，会形成孔径间隙，导致方位模糊和分辨率下降。传统方法如压缩感知或插值在高度稀疏孔径下抑制模糊效果有限，因此亟需开发新的深度学习方法来有效抑制栅瓣和噪声，实现高分辨率成像。

方法详情：本文提出频谱先验引导的Swin TransUnet（SSTU）方法：首先利用点散射体位置生成高斯软标签作为热图回归目标，引导主瓣学习；然后通过2D-FFT层将距离-方位信号转换至频谱域，突出主瓣、旁瓣和周期栅瓣；接着使用卷积编码器提取局部频谱特征，Swin Transformer模块建模长程模糊相关性，最后以U-Net风格的多尺度解码器重建高分辨率距离-方位图像。

研究结果：仿真实验表明，在60%孔径稀疏度且信噪比高于-6 dB时，SSTU的均方根误差低于10⁻²，方位模糊抑制比优于-30 dB，显著优于传统方法。77 GHz雷达实测进一步证实了该方法在60%移动孔径稀疏度下对随机分布强散射体的高质量室外成像能力。

局限性：该方法依赖点散射体位置生成高斯软标签作为监督信号，在实际复杂场景中获取精确标注困难；当信噪比低于-6 dB时性能下降明显；Swin Transformer模块计算复杂度较高，且模型泛化到非点目标或密集扩展场景的能力有待验证。

未来方向：后续研究可探索将SSTU扩展到扩展目标或非稀疏场景，结合自监督或半监督学习减少对标签的依赖，并通过网络轻量化或知识蒸馏降低计算成本，同时集成运动补偿模块以提升实际部署的鲁棒性。

研究相关性：对于从事稀疏孔径雷达成像、MIMO-SAR信号处理以及深度学习在雷达中应用的研究者，本文提供了一种融合频谱先验与Transformer的高效方位模糊抑制框架，展示了在极端稀疏采样下的优异性能，可启发车载毫米波雷达、近距离高分辨成像等领域的进一步研究。

The Emerging Paradigm of Geospatial Foundation Models: From Pre-Training to Agentic Reasoning

地理空间基础模型的新兴范式：从预训练到智能推理

2026-07-13
arXiv

Shelley Cazares

DOI: 10.1007/978-3-032-18474-0_1

The analysis of satellite and aerial imagery has entered a new era with the advent of foundation models. This paper describes the concept of Geospatial Foundation Models (GeoFMs), which are artificial intelligence/machine learning (AI/ML) models pre-trained on massive geospatial datasets through varied methodologies. We first articulate the core paradigm shift that GeoFMs enable: a separation of duties, where large-scale model providers perform the computationally intensive pretraining, allowing domain experts to rapidly fine-tune or prompt these models for specific, mission-critical tasks. This approach democratizes access to state-of-the-art AI/ML while maintaining the security and confidentiality of the downstream task. We then explore the novel capabilities unlocked by different types of GeoFMs, distinguishing between the finetunable vision models produced by self-supervised techniques like masked auto-encoding, and the vision-language models produced by contrastive learning which enable zero-shot tasks like open-vocabulary image analysis. Next, we discuss the practical considerations for operationalizing GeoFMs, from performance-cost analysis to the broader MLOps ecosystem. To that end, we introduce a taxonomy of model adaptation strategies and propose a framework for domain experts to select the most cost-effective adaptation approach for their particular mission set. Finally, we present a forward-looking vision of Agentic Geospatial Reasoning, where Large Language Models act as intelligent orchestrators, leveraging GeoFMs as tools to answer high-level user queries in natural language and automate complex analytical workflows, moving the field from perception to cognition.

研究问题：如何通过地理空间基础模型分离预训练与下游任务并实现智能推理？

Domain-Aware Scaling Laws Uncover Data Synergy

领域感知的缩放定律揭示数据协同效应

2026-07-13
arXiv

Kimia Hamidieh，Lester Mackey，David Alvarez-Melis

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11052v1

Machine learning progress is often attributed to scaling model size and dataset volume, yet the composition of data can be just as consequential. Empirical findings repeatedly show that combining datasets from different domains yields nontrivial interactions. For instance, adding code improves mathematical reasoning, while certain mixtures introduce interference that reduces model performance. We refer to these effects collectively as data synergy, where the contribution of multiple domains exceeds or falls short of the sum of their isolated contributions. In this work, we formalize and quantify data synergy in language model pretraining. Leveraging observational variation across open-weight LLMs with diverse pretraining mixtures, we estimate both direct domain-to-benchmark synergy (how one domain contributes to performance on another) and a second-order domain-domain synergy (capabilities that require co-occurrence of multiple domains). Our framework improves predictive accuracy over domain-agnostic scaling laws and recovers stable synergy estimates. We validate these estimates by training models on predicted optimal and predicted anti-optimal mixtures and confirm that our synergy estimates correctly predict performance rankings.
