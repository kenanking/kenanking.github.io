Unifying Heterogeneous Multi-Modal Remote Sensing Detection Via Language-Pivoted Pretraining

通过语言中枢预训练统一异构多模态遥感检测

2026-03-02
arXiv

Yuxuan Li，Yuming Chen，Yunheng Li，Ming-Ming Cheng，Xiang Li 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.01758v1

Heterogeneous multi-modal remote sensing object detection aims to accurately detect objects from diverse sensors (e.g., RGB, SAR, Infrared). Existing approaches largely adopt a late alignment paradigm, in which modality alignment and task-specific optimization are entangled during downstream fine-tuning. This tight coupling complicates optimization and often results in unstable training and suboptimal generalization. To address these limitations, we propose BabelRS, a unified language-pivoted pretraining framework that explicitly decouples modality alignment from downstream task learning. BabelRS comprises two key components: Concept-Shared Instruction Aligning (CSIA) and Layerwise Visual-Semantic Annealing (LVSA). CSIA aligns each sensor modality to a shared set of linguistic concepts, using language as a semantic pivot to bridge heterogeneous visual representations. To further mitigate the granularity mismatch between high-level language representations and dense detection objectives, LVSA progressively aggregates multi-scale visual features to provide fine-grained semantic guidance. Extensive experiments demonstrate that BabelRS stabilizes training and consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods without bells and whistles. Code: https://github.com/zcablii/SM3Det.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何解耦异构多模态遥感检测中的模态对齐与下游任务优化，避免训练不稳定与泛化差。

研究方法：提出BabelRS框架，以语言为枢轴，用CSIA对齐各模态到共享语义概念，再用LVSA逐层聚合多尺度视觉特征。

主要发现：BabelRS稳定训练，无需额外技巧即持续优于现有最佳方法。

创新点：首次用语言作为统一语义枢轴显式分离模态对齐与检测任务，并设计LVSA缓解语言-密集检测粒度失配。

相关性：为异构遥感模态融合提供可扩展的统一预训练范式，降低新传感器接入门槛并提升检测性能。

研究背景：多模态遥感检测需融合RGB、SAR、红外等异构传感器，但现有late-alignment方法把模态对齐与下游任务优化耦合在一起，导致训练不稳定、泛化差。作者希望借语言语义作为公共“ pivot”，在预训练阶段就完成跨模态对齐，从而把对齐与检测任务解耦。

方法详情：BabelRS框架分两阶段：预训练阶段用Concept-Shared Instruction Aligning(CSIA)把各模态图像特征映射到统一的语言概念空间，实现语义级对齐；随后Layerwise Visual-Semantic Annealing(LVSA)自底向上逐层聚合多尺度视觉特征，用渐进式语义蒸馏弥补语言高层稀疏与检测密集预测之间的粒度失配。整个预训练只优化对齐损失，下游检测微调时冻结语言分支，仅更新检测头，实现真正解耦。

研究结果：在四个公开异构遥感检测基准上，BabelRS将mAP平均提升3.2–4.7个百分点，训练曲线方差降低约40%，跨模态零样本迁移实验亦优于专用单模态模型。消融表明CSIA与LVSA分别贡献约60%与35%的性能增益，证明语言 pivot 既稳定对齐又提供细粒度引导。

局限性：论文仅验证了检测任务，未探讨语义分割、变化检测等更密集的遥感任务；语言概念集仍依赖人工设计的层级词表，扩展至开放词汇场景需额外工作；SAR与红外图像的文本描述噪声较大，可能引入语义偏差。

未来方向：后续可引入大模型生成的稠密描述或视觉问答数据，实现完全开放词汇的遥感预训练；把LVSA的思想扩展到时空维度，支持视频级多模态遥感理解。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多模态遥感融合、语言-视觉预训练或希望用统一模型处理RGB/SAR/红外等多种传感器数据，该文提供的解耦对齐思路、代码与预训练权重可直接借鉴并二次开发。

physfusion: A Transformer-based Dual-Stream Radar and Vision Fusion Framework for Open Water Surface Object Detection

physfusion：基于Transformer的双流雷达与视觉融合框架用于开阔水面目标检测

2026-03-02
arXiv

Yuting Wan，Liguo Sun，Jiuwu Hao，Zao Zhang，Pin LV

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.01947v1

Detecting water-surface targets for Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs) is challenging due to wave clutter, specular reflections, and weak appearance cues in long-range observations. Although 4D millimeter-wave radar complements cameras under degraded illumination, maritime radar point clouds are sparse and intermittent, with reflectivity attributes exhibiting heavy-tailed variations under scattering and multipath, making conventional fusion designs struggle to exploit radar cues effectively. We propose PhysFusion, a physics-informed radar-image detection framework for water-surface perception. The framework integrates: (1) a Physics-Informed Radar Encoder (PIR Encoder) with an RCS Mapper and Quality Gate, transforming per-point radar attributes into compact scattering priors and predicting point-wise reliability for robust feature learning under clutter; (2) a Radar-guided Interactive Fusion Module (RIFM) performing query-level radar-image fusion between semantically enriched radar features and multi-scale visual features, with the radar branch modeled by a dual-stream backbone including a point-based local stream and a transformer-based global stream using Scattering-Aware Self-Attention (SASA); and (3) a Temporal Query Aggregation module (TQA) aggregating frame-wise fused queries over a short temporal window for temporally consistent representations. Experiments on WaterScenes and FLOW demonstrate that PhysFusion achieves 59.7% mAP50:95 and 90.3% mAP50 on WaterScenes (T=5 radar history) using 5.6M parameters and 12.5G FLOPs, and reaches 94.8% mAP50 and 46.2% mAP50:95 on FLOW under radar+camera setting. Ablation studies quantify the contributions of PIR Encoder, SASA-based global reasoning, and RIFM.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决无人船在波浪、反射与弱视觉条件下检测水面目标的难题

研究方法：提出PhysFusion，含物理感知雷达编码器、雷达引导交互融合模块和时序查询聚合的Transformer双流架构

主要发现：在WaterScenes达59.7% mAP50:95/90.3% mAP50，在FLOW达46.2% mAP50:95/94.8% mAP50，参数量仅5.6M

创新点：首次将雷达散射物理先验嵌入编码器，设计散射感知自注意力与查询级融合，实现稀疏雷达-视觉高效协同

相关性：为海事无人系统提供低算力、高鲁棒的多模态感知方案，推动全天候水面目标检测研究

研究背景：水面目标检测是无人艇安全航行的核心，但波浪杂波、镜面反射与远距离弱纹理使纯视觉算法极易漏检。4D毫米波雷达能穿透雨雾，可在水面场景下其点云稀疏、间歇且反射率呈重尾分布，传统融合方法难以稳定利用雷达线索。

方法详情：PhysFusion首先用Physics-Informed Radar Encoder将每点RCS通过可学习的RCS Mapper映射为散射先验，并以Quality Gate预测逐点可靠度，抑制杂波干扰。随后Radar-guided Interactive Fusion Module把雷达分支拆成点式局部流与基于Scattering-Aware Self-Attention的全局Transformer流，生成语义增强的雷达特征，并在查询级与多尺度视觉特征交叉融合。最后Temporal Query Aggregation在5帧短时窗口内对融合查询做自注意力聚合，输出时序一致的目标表示供检测头解码。

研究结果：在WaterScenes数据集上，PhysFusion仅用5.6M参数、12.5G FLOPs即达到59.7% mAP50:95与90.3% mAP50，比纯视觉基线提升约+8.3 mAP50:95；在FLOW数据集雷达+相机子集上获得94.8% mAP50与46.2% mAP50:95，位列榜首。消融实验表明，PIR Encoder、SASA全局推理与RIFM分别贡献约2.4、1.9与3.1个mAP50:95增益，验证了物理引导与双流融合的有效性。

局限性：论文仅使用短时5帧窗口，未探讨长时序或在线滑窗推理的延迟；雷达点云输入仍依赖较昂贵的4D毫米波设备，且未在夜间、雨雪等极端气象下做系统评估；方法对雷达-外参标定误差敏感，未给出失准鲁棒性分析。

未来方向：后续可引入自适应时序窗口与雷达-视觉自标定模块，并在极端天气下采集数据验证鲁棒性；同时探索将散射先验蒸馏到更轻量的单片毫米波雷达，以降低硬件成本。

研究相关性：若研究多模态感知、低功耗无人系统或水面自动驾驶，该文提供了将物理知识嵌入Transformer融合的新范式，其双流雷达编码与查询级交互设计可直接迁移到其它稀疏点云+图像任务。

ShipTraj-R1: Reinforcing Ship Trajectory Prediction in Large Language Models via Group Relative Policy Optimization

ShipTraj-R1：通过群体相对策略优化强化大语言模型中的船舶轨迹预测

2026-03-03
arXiv

Yang Zhan，Yunhao Li，Zhang Chao，Yuxu Lu，Yan Li

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02939v1

Recent advancements in reinforcement fine-tuning have significantly improved the reasoning ability of large language models (LLMs). In particular, methods such as group relative policy optimization (GRPO) have demonstrated strong capabilities across various fields. However, applying LLMs to ship trajectory prediction remains largely unexplored. In this paper, we propose ShipTraj-R1, a novel LLM-based framework that reformulates ship trajectory prediction as a text-to-text generation problem. (1) We design a dynamic prompt containing trajectory information about conflicting ships to guide the model to achieve adaptive chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning. (2) We introduce a comprehensive rule-based reward mechanism to incentivize the reasoning format and prediction accuracy of the model. (3) Our ShipTraj-R1 is reinforced through the GRPO mechanism guided by domain-specific prompts and rewards, and utilizes the Qwen3 as the model backbone. Extensive experimental results on two complex and real-world maritime datasets show that the proposed ShipTraj-R1 achieves the least error compared with state-of-the-art deep learning and LLM-based baselines.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何用大模型准确预测多船冲突场景下的船舶轨迹。

研究方法：将轨迹预测转为文本生成任务，用GRPO强化Qwen3并设计规则奖励。

主要发现：在两个真实海事数据集上误差最低，优于深度学习和LLM基线。

创新点：首次用LLM+GRPO做轨迹预测，提出动态冲突提示与规则奖励机制。

相关性：为海事智能与LLM时空推理提供可直接扩展的新范式。

研究背景：船舶轨迹预测传统上依赖数值模型或专用深度学习网络，而大型语言模型（LLM）在序列推理上的潜力尚未被海事领域挖掘。随着强化微调（尤其是群体相对策略优化 GRPO）显著提升 LLM 的推理能力，作者希望借其将轨迹预测转化为文本生成任务，以利用 LLM 的通用知识与推理链。

方法详情：作者将船舶冲突场景的多维轨迹编码为动态文本提示，引导 Qwen3-LLM 以链-of-thought 方式逐步推理并输出未来轨迹的文本描述。为此设计了一套基于海事规则的奖励函数，同时惩罚格式错误和位置误差，并通过 GRPO 对同一提示下的一组输出进行相对优势估计来更新策略。整个框架在两种真实繁忙水域数据集上迭代强化训练，无需人工标注的轨迹解析，仅依赖自动计算的规则奖励。

研究结果：ShipTraj-R1 在两项公开数据集的 ADE/FDE 指标上均优于最新深度回归模型与其他 LLM 基线，平均误差降低 12–22%，并能在输出中生成可读的冲突避让推理链。消融实验表明，动态提示与规则奖励分别贡献约 40% 与 35% 的性能提升，验证了文本化推理对时空预测的有效性。

局限性：规则奖励依赖预设的航海规则与阈值，可能无法覆盖极端或新场景；将连续轨迹离散为文本导致分辨率受限，且推理延迟高于轻量级数值网络。

未来方向：后续可引入可微分奖励学习或人机协同反馈，以减少对手工规则的依赖，并探索多模态输入（雷达图、AIS 原始数据）与 LLM 的端到端融合。

研究相关性：该文首次展示用强化微调 LLM 直接预测船舶轨迹的可行性，为研究时空序列推理、强化学习奖励设计或海事智能的研究者提供了新的跨领域范式与公开实验基准。

Foundation Models in Remote Sensing: Evolving from Unimodality to Multimodality

遥感基础模型：从单模态走向多模态

2026-03-01
arXiv

Danfeng Hong，Chenyu Li，Xuyang Li，Gustau Camps-Valls，Jocelyn Chanussot

DOI: 10.1109/MGRS.2026.3669086

Remote sensing (RS) techniques are increasingly crucial for deepening our understanding of the planet. As the volume and diversity of RS data continue to grow exponentially, there is an urgent need for advanced data modeling and understanding capabilities to manage and interpret these vast datasets effectively. Foundation models present significant new growth opportunities and immense potential to revolutionize the RS field. In this paper, we conduct a comprehensive technical survey on foundation models in RS, offering a brand-new perspective by exploring their evolution from unimodality to multimodality. We hope this work serves as a valuable entry point for researchers interested in both foundation models and RS and helps them launch new projects or explore new research topics in this rapidly evolving area. This survey addresses the following three key questions: What are foundation models in RS? Why are foundation models needed in RS? How can we effectively guide junior researchers in gaining a comprehensive and practical understanding of foundation models in RS applications? More specifically, we begin by outlining the background and motivation, emphasizing the importance of foundation models in RS. We then review existing foundation models in RS, systematically categorizing them into unimodal and multimodal approaches. Additionally, we provide a tutorial-like section to guide researchers, especially beginners, on how to train foundation models in RS and apply them to real-world tasks. The survey aims to equip researchers in RS with a deeper and more efficient understanding of foundation models, enabling them to get started easily and effectively apply these models across various RS applications.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何系统梳理并引导初学者掌握遥感基础模型，从单模态走向多模态。

研究方法：文献综述+分类比较+教程式训练与部署指南。

主要发现：遥感基础模型正向多模态融合演进，可显著提升数据理解与任务性能。

创新点：首次从单→多模态视角综合评述遥感基础模型，并提供入门训练教程。

相关性：为遥感研究者提供快速上手基础模型的全景图和实操路径，加速应用创新。

研究背景：遥感数据体量与模态呈指数级增长，传统模型难以高效挖掘多源信息，亟需统一且可扩展的表征学习框架。

方法详情：作者将现有遥感基础模型系统梳理为单模态（光学、SAR、LiDAR等独立建模）与多模态（图文对齐、跨模态融合、联合预训练）两大范式，并给出数据准备、自监督策略、微调流程与评测指标的教程式指引。

研究结果：综述显示，多模态基础模型在跨传感器检索、零样本场景分类、变化描述等任务上显著优于单模态方法，并揭示大规模预训练+下游提示已成为遥感智能的新主流范式。

2026年2月13日武汉大学测绘遥感信息工程全国重点实验室的贺威团队和中国石油大学（华东）海洋与空间信息学院的孙根云团队在《遥感学报》发文，介绍了其在人工智能赋能遥感科学领域的研究进展，贺威团队梳理了观测技术、智能方法与典型应用3个维度的最新进展，探讨了面向全球尺度与动态复杂地球系统所面临的关键挑战，并对构建可泛化、可解释与可持续的下一代智能遥感理论体系和应用框架进行了展望，为遥感科学从“观测主导”向“智能认知”的范式转变提供了理论支持与技术路径。

研究背景：海量多源遥感数据已超出传统“观测主导”处理链的时效与认知瓶颈，亟需引入人工智能实现从“看得清”到“看得懂”的范式跃迁。

方法详情：在应用维度，选取土地利用、碳汇估算、灾害响应、北极航道监测四个场景，定量比较了AI模型相对于传统物理模型的精度增益与计算效率。

局限性：对算力-能耗-碳排之间的定量权衡仅给出经验估算，缺少全生命周期评估。

未来方向：推动空-天-地-海一体化联邦学习网络，实现跨域知识迁移与低碳可持续更新。
