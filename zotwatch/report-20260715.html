Joint exploitation of ISAR autofocus and wake patterns for ship kinematic estimation in VHR spaceborne SAR images

联合利用ISAR自聚焦与尾迹模式进行甚高分辨率星载SAR图像中的船舶运动学估计

2026-07-13
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Ilaria Nasso，Fabrizio Santi，Lorenzo Piazzo，Debora Pastina

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3712499

This letter addresses ship position and full velocity vector estimation in very high-resolution (VHR) spaceborne synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imaging by jointly exploiting inverse SAR (ISAR) processing and wake patterns analysis. A dual-path architecture is proposed. The first path performs ISAR autofocus to put ship images into focus also providing azimuthal velocity, while vessel heading is obtained by fusing independent ship and wake orientation measurements according to a maximum likelihood criterion, eventually providing radial velocity and ship position. Alternatively, the radial velocity is retrieved from the azimuthal displacement between the ship and its wake, enabling azimuthal velocity to be recovered through ML-based heading estimate. Such twofold strategy allows not only for verifying the coherence of the estimates but also ensures kinematic retrieval when ISAR focusing is ill-posed. Validation using VHR SAR data demonstrates the framework’s effectiveness and underscores sub-meter resolution as a critical prerequisite for joint ISAR and wake-based motion analysis.

研究问题：如何在超高分辨率星载SAR图像中联合利用ISAR自聚焦和尾迹模式估计船舶位置和速度矢量？

研究方法：提出双路径架构：ISAR自聚焦获方位速度，融合船与尾迹朝向的ML估计获得航向和径向速度。

主要发现：框架有效验证了估计一致性，并在ISAR不适定条件下仍能恢复运动参数，亚米级分辨率是关键前提。

创新点：首次联合ISAR自聚焦与尾迹模式分析，实现双路径交叉验证及运动参数鲁棒估计。

研究背景：本研究的背景是VHR空间SAR图像中船舶运动参数（位置和全速度矢量）估计的挑战，由于船舶在SAR图像中可能因运动而模糊，传统方法难以同时获取精确的径向和方位速度。动机是通过联合利用ISAR自聚焦技术（聚焦船舶并提取方位速度）和船舶尾流模式分析（提取航向和径向速度），克服单一方法的局限性，实现高精度运动估计。

方法详情：提出了一种双路径架构：第一路径执行ISAR自聚焦，使船舶图像聚焦的同时提供方位向速度估计；第二路径通过最大似然准则融合独立的船舶和尾流方向测量，获得船舶航向，进而结合尾流信息推导径向速度和船舶位置。另外，也可通过船舶与尾流之间的方位向位移直接获取径向速度，再结合ML航向恢复方位速度，形成互验机制，尤其在ISAR聚焦不适定情况下仍能保证运动参数估计。

研究结果：使用VHR SAR数据验证了该框架的有效性，证明了亚米级分辨率是实现联合ISAR与尾流运动分析的关键先决条件。该方法能够同时提供船舶位置和完整速度矢量，且通过双重策略确保了估计的相干性和鲁棒性。主要意义在于为高分辨率SAR海洋监视中的船舶动态监测提供了新的技术途径。

局限性：该方法可能依赖于清晰的船舶尾流模式，在尾流被海浪或噪声淹没时估计性能可能下降；此外，ISAR自聚焦需要船舶具有足够的姿态变化，对于静止或极低速目标可能不适用。联合估计的一致性依赖ML准则的假设，实际数据中航向测量误差模型可能影响最优性。

未来方向：后续研究可扩展到多船舶场景下的交互尾流分析，或结合深度学习提升尾流检测鲁棒性；亦可探索将运动估计与船舶分类识别结合，用于更复杂的海事监控任务。

研究相关性：该论文对从事空间SAR海洋遥感、船舶监视与运动分析、ISAR成像以及尾流探测的研究者高度相关，提供了联合利用多源信息提升运动参数估计精度的新思路，尤其在VHR数据集中具有直接应用价值。

FGFMamba: A Fuzzy-Enhanced Gated Fusion Mamba for HSI and SAR Image Classification

FGFMamba：一种用于高光谱图像和SAR图像分类的模糊增强门控融合Mamba

2026-07-13
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Menghui Su，Jindong Xu，Zongqin Yue，Zhiyuan Yang，Yu Sun

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3711977

Multimodal remote sensing classification capitalizes on the synergistic advantages of diverse data sources (e.g., HSI, LiDAR, and SAR) to facilitate robust land-cover characterization. However, contemporary methods often struggle with intrinsic spectral uncertainty and cross-modal heterogeneity, failing to adequately address adaptive feature fusion. To overcome these limitations, we propose a novel Fuzzy-Enhanced Gated Fusion Mamba (FGFMamba) framework for HSI–SAR classification. Specifically, we design an HSI-specific Fuzzy Block that leverages Gaussian-modulated kernels to formulate a probabilistic uncertainty descriptor. This module maps raw spectral intensities into a high-dimensional fuzzy feature space, thereby capturing the intrinsic stochasticity of HSI data. Furthermore, a Gated Cross-Modal Fusion Block (GCFB) is introduced to extend selective state space modeling (SSM) to a dual-branch interaction paradigm, dynamically weighting modalities to reconcile heterogeneity and enhance cross-modal synergy. Extensive experiments on the Berlin and Augsburg benchmark datasets demonstrate that FGFMamba achieves state-of-the-art performance with linear computational complexity. This makes it a highly efficient and robust solution for large-scale geospatial intelligent recognition.

Significance-Preserving Progressive Network for Infrared and Visible Image Fusion

面向红外与可见光图像融合的保显著性渐进网络

2026-07-13
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Jingsui Li，Xiaorun Li，Shu Xiang，Shuhan Chen

DOI: 10.3390/rs18142328

Fusing infrared and visible images can effectively compensate for the inherent limitations of each modality in different scenes, resulting in fused images that contain richer information. However, existing methods often struggle to balance global dependency modeling with local detail preservation and to effectively coordinate heterogeneous local and global features during fusion. To address these issues, this paper proposes a Significance-Preserving Progressive Fusion Network (SiPFusion). First, a progressive feature extraction framework was designed, which hierarchically extracts multi-scale local features using CNNs and then models long-range dependencies across scales via a Transformer-based global module. To adaptively integrate local-global complementary features, a significance-preserving fusion module was designed to obtain significance attention maps with a spatial selection mechanism, enabling dynamic fusion of multi-source features. Furthermore, we propose a significance similarity loss function that leverages intermediate feature guidance to enhance structural consistency and preserve salient-region information in the fused image. Extensive experiments on the MSRS, RoadScene, and TNO datasets demonstrate that SiPFusion achieves competitive visual quality and strong overall quantitative performance against 15 state-of-the-art fusion methods, obtaining leading results on most evaluated metrics.

方法详情：SiPFusion设计了一个渐进式特征提取框架，首先利用CNN分层提取多尺度局部特征，然后通过基于Transformer的全局模块跨尺度建模长程依赖关系。为了自适应整合局部与全局互补特征，提出了显著性保持融合模块，利用空间选择机制生成显著性注意力图，实现多源特征的动态融合。此外，还设计了显著性相似性损失函数，利用中间特征引导增强结构一致性并保留融合图像中的显著区域信息。

局限性：该网络可能对复杂场景中极端光照或遮挡情况下的显著性区域检测存在局限，且Transformer模块的计算开销较大，限制了实时应用。此外，损失函数中中间特征引导的权重调整依赖经验设定，缺乏自适应机制。

未来方向：未来可探索更轻量化的Transformer结构以降低计算复杂度，并研究自适应损失权重调整策略，同时可尝试将SiPFusion扩展至多模态目标检测或语义分割等下游任务。

MultiTaskVIF: Segmentation-oriented visible and infrared image fusion via multi-task learning

MultiTaskVIF: 面向分割的可见光和红外图像融合的多任务学习方法

2026-07-13
IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (IF: 13.7)

Zixian Zhao，Andrew Howes，Xingchen Zhang

DOI: 10.1109/tip.2026.3710490

Visible and infrared image fusion (VIF) has attracted significant attention in recent years. Traditional VIF methods primarily focus on generating fused images with high visual quality, while recent advancements increasingly emphasize incorporating semantic information into the fusion model during training. However, most existing segmentation-oriented VIF methods adopt a cascade structure comprising separate fusion and segmentation models, leading to increased network complexity and redundancy. This raises a critical question: can we design a more concise and efficient structure to integrate semantic information directly into the fusion model during training? Inspired by multi-task learning (MTL), we propose a concise and universal training framework, MultiTaskVIF, for segmentation-oriented VIF models. CUST: Clustered Unit-level Similarity Transformer for Lightweight Image Super-Resolution

CUST: 用于轻量级图像超分辨率的聚类单元级相似性Transformer

2026-07-13
arXiv

Jeongsoo Kim

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11088v1

Recently, Vision Transformer (ViT)-based models have exhibited remarkable performance in image super-resolution. However, the quadratic computational complexity of ViTs with respect to spatial resolution severely constrains their efficiency, leading to high latency and massive memory consumption. To alleviate this, various window-based attention mechanisms have been proposed; yet, they inherently compromise the long-range dependency modeling that is the primary advantage of ViTs. To overcome these limitations, we propose the Clustered Unit-level Similarity Transformer (CUST), a novel architecture that efficiently integrates global and local information. Specifically, CUST enables each patch to aggregate and attend to similar patches within a broadened regional scope outside its local window, thereby capturing extensive contextual understanding. Furthermore, it employs overlapping attention windows to capture local dependencies, while explicitly extracting high-frequency details by computing the residual difference between the original features and their downsampled-upsampled counterparts. Comprehensive experiments demonstrate that our proposed model achieves a practical balance between computational efficiency and restoration performance. It achieves a lower memory footprint and faster inference speed compared to recent global context or lightweight models under realistic constraints. Code is available at [https://github.com/jwgdmkj/CUST].
