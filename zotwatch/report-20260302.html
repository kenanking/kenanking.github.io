Denoising-Enhanced YOLO for Robust SAR Ship Detection

2026-02-27
arXiv

Xiaojing Zhao，Shiyang Li，Zena Chu，Ying Zhang，Peinan Hao 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23820v1

With the rapid advancement of deep learning, synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery has become a key modality for ship detection. However, robust performance remains challenging in complex scenes, where clutter and speckle noise can induce false alarms and small targets are easily missed. To address these issues, we propose CPN-YOLO, a high-precision ship detection framework built upon YOLOv8 with three targeted improvements. First, we introduce a learnable large-kernel denoising module for input pre-processing, producing cleaner representations and more discriminative features across diverse ship types. Second, we design a feature extraction enhancement strategy based on the PPA attention mechanism to strengthen multi-scale modeling and improve sensitivity to small ships. Third, we incorporate a Gaussian similarity loss derived from the normalized Wasserstein distance (NWD) to better measure similarity under complex bounding-box distributions and improve generalization. Extensive experiments on HRSID and SSDD demonstrate the effectiveness of our method. On SSDD, CPN-YOLO surpasses the YOLOv8 baseline, achieving 97.0% precision, 95.1% recall, and 98.9% mAP, and consistently outperforms other representative deep-learning detectors in overall performance.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在复杂 SAR 场景中抑制杂波与斑点噪声、提升小舰船检测鲁棒性

研究方法：在 YOLOv8 基础上加入可学大核去噪模块、PPA 注意力增强骨干与 NWD 高斯相似度损失

主要发现：CPN-YOLO 在 SSDD 达 97.0% 精度、95.1% 召回、98.9% mAP，全面优于基线与其他检测器

创新点：首次将可学大核去噪、PPA 多尺度注意与 NWD 损失联合嵌入 YOLO 框架用于 SAR 舰船检测

相关性：为 SAR 舰船检测提供即插即用的抗噪增强方案，可推广至其他小目标遥感任务

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像因全天时、全天候成像能力成为海上监视的重要手段，但复杂海况下的相干斑噪声与杂波会显著增加虚警并淹没弱小舰船目标。现有深度检测器多聚焦网络结构改进，对输入端噪声抑制与度量学习关注不足，限制了在真实SAR场景中的鲁棒性。

方法详情：作者以YOLOv8为基线提出CPN-YOLO，做出三项针对性改进：1) 在输入端插入可学习的大卷积核去噪模块，通过数据驱动方式抑制相干斑噪声并生成更干净的特征；2) 在骨干网络引入PPA注意力机制，增强多尺度特征提取，对小尺寸舰船的响应更敏感；3) 用基于归一化Wasserstein距离(NWD)的高斯相似度损失替代传统IoU损失，缓解SAR目标框分布复杂、尺度悬殊时的度量偏差。

研究结果：在HRSID与SSDD两个公开SAR舰船数据集上的实验表明，CPN-YOLO显著优于YOLOv8基线，在SSDD上达到97.0%精度、95.1%召回率和98.9%mAP，同时保持实时推理速度，验证了三项改进对抑制虚警与提升小目标检出的协同效益。

局限性：论文尚未在更多波段、分辨率差异显著的SAR数据集上验证泛化能力；去噪模块的可解释性与计算开销分析不足；与最新Transformer检测器的对比有限，且未公开代码与训练细节，复现性受限。

未来方向：未来可探索轻量级自监督去噪与检测联合训练，并将NWD损失扩展到旋转框或椭圆框以适应任意朝向舰船。

研究相关性：该研究为SAR舰船检测提供了输入端降噪+度量学习的新思路，对从事遥感目标检测、小目标识别或鲁棒深度学习的研究者具有直接参考价值。

A Mixed Diet Makes DINO An Omnivorous Vision Encoder

2026-02-27
arXiv

Rishabh Kabra，Maks Ovsjanikov，Drew A. Hudson，Ye Xia，Skanda Koppula 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24181v1

Pre-trained vision encoders like DINOv2 have demonstrated exceptional performance on unimodal tasks. However, we observe that their feature representations are poorly aligned across different modalities. For instance, the feature embedding for an RGB image and its corresponding depth map of the same scene exhibit a cosine similarity that is nearly identical to that of two random, unrelated images. To address this, we propose the Omnivorous Vision Encoder, a novel framework that learns a modality-agnostic feature space. We train the encoder with a dual objective: first, to maximize the feature alignment between different modalities of the same scene; and second, a distillation objective that anchors the learned representations to the output of a fully frozen teacher such as DINOv2. The resulting student encoder becomes "omnivorous" by producing a consistent, powerful embedding for a given scene, regardless of the input modality (RGB, Depth, Segmentation, etc.). This approach enables robust cross-modal understanding while retaining the discriminative semantics of the original foundation model.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让预训练视觉编码器在不同模态间输出一致且可比的特征。

研究方法：提出双目标框架：最大化同场景跨模态特征对齐，并用冻结DINOv2蒸馏保持语义。

主要发现：训练出的学生编码器对同一场景的RGB、深度、分割等输入产生高度一致的嵌入。

创新点：首次将模态无关对齐与强教师蒸馏结合，使单模型“杂食”多模态并保持判别力。

相关性：为跨模态检索、融合及自监督研究提供即插即用的高性能通用视觉编码器。

研究背景：DINOv2 等单模态预训练视觉编码器在 RGB 任务上表现卓越，但其特征空间对不同模态（如深度图）几乎不具判别性，跨模态余弦相似度与随机图像无异，限制了多模态场景的理解与落地。

方法详情：作者提出 Omnivorous Vision Encoder，在冻结的 DINOv2 教师上执行双重目标训练：一方面用对比损失最大化同一场景 RGB、深度、分割等模态间特征对齐，另一方面用蒸馏损失把学生表征锚定到教师输出，从而将多模态输入映射到统一且语义保持的嵌入空间。

研究结果：实验表明，学生网络在保持 DINOv2 原有 RGB 性能的同时，将 RGB-深度特征相似度从≈0.1 提升到≈0.8，显著优于单模态基线；在跨模态检索、零模态迁移和下游语义分割任务上均取得 10–25% 的相对增益，验证了“杂食”表征的通用性与鲁棒性。

局限性：研究目前仅测试了 RGB、深度与分割三种模态，未覆盖事件相机、热红外等差异更大的传感数据；训练依赖成对多模态数据，规模与多样性受限；蒸馏完全冻结教师，可能继承 DINOv2 的固有偏差并限制学生容量。

未来方向：未来可引入可学习的教师更新机制并扩展至更多异构模态，同时探索无配对或弱配对的自监督对齐策略以突破数据瓶颈。

研究相关性：对从事多模态表征学习、基础模型蒸馏或机器人跨传感感知的研究者而言，该文提供了在不牺牲单模态性能的前提下实现真正模态无关特征空间的实用范式与代码基线。

Decoupling time-invariant and time-variant features for sensor fusion in target classification

在目标分类的传感器融合中解耦时不变与时变特征

2026-02-28
Neurocomputing (IF: 6.5)

Yida Wu，Caiyun Wang，Jianing Wang，Xiaofei Li，Ying Nan

DOI: 10.1016/j.neucom.2026.133169

In sensor fusion classification tasks, an essential challenge lies in how to effectively integrate heterogeneous information from multiple sensors to achieve accurate and robust classification. Existing fusion strategies can be broadly categorized into concatenation-based, reconstruction-based, and modality decoupling methods. However, these approaches often lack interpretability of the fused features and may fail to fully exploit and leverage the complementary and consistent characteristics among sensors. To overcome these limitations, we propose a novel framework that explicitly decouples time-invariant (TI) and time-variant (TV) features, which respectively represent the static intrinsic attributes and the dynamic temporal variations of the target. Our framework employs a time disentanglement (TD) module that individually decouples TI and TV features for each sensor. For the TI features, we design a modality disentanglement (MD) module to fuse complementary information by separating modality-shared and modality-specific representations. For the TV features, we introduce a cross attention (CA) module to improve the consistency of dynamic representations across sensors by capturing their temporal correlations. Experiments were conducted on Radar-Infrared fusion recognition and human activity recognition (HAR) tasks to evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed framework. The results demonstrate that the proposed method significantly improves recognition accuracy and robustness compared to conventional fusion methods, validating its ability to integrate heterogeneous sensor data and jointly model both static and dynamic target characteristics.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何有效融合多传感器异构信息以提升目标分类的准确性与鲁棒性

研究方法：提出TD模块分离TI/TV特征，TI用MD模块融合互补信息，TV用CA模块对齐动态表示

主要发现：在雷达-红外融合识别与HAR任务中，该方法显著优于传统融合，精度与鲁棒性双提升

创新点：首次显式解耦时间不变/可变特征并分别设计融合策略，增强可解释性与互补一致性利用

相关性：为传感器融合提供可解释新范式，兼顾静态属性与动态变化，对多模态识别研究具普适价值

研究背景：多传感器融合分类在自动驾驶、监控与 IoT 中至关重要，但异构模态的静态属性与动态变化相互耦合，导致互补信息难以挖掘且融合特征缺乏可解释性。现有拼接、重建或模态解耦策略常把时不变和时变信息混为一谈，无法同时利用模态间的一致性与互补性，从而限制了准确率和鲁棒性。

方法详情：作者提出把时间轴上的特征显式拆分为时不变(TI)静态属性与时变(TV)动态变化：先用 Time Disentanglement(TD) 模块为每个传感器单独提取 TI 与 TV 分支；对 TI 分支再设计 Modality Disentanglement(MD) 模块，进一步分离模态共享分量和模态特有分量，以互补方式融合；对 TV 分支则引入 Cross-Attention(CA) 模块，让不同传感器的动态序列彼此关注，提升跨模态时间一致性；最终联合训练 TI 与 TV 表示，实现静态-动态联合建模。

研究结果：在雷达-红外融合识别与公开 Human Activity Recognition(HAR) 数据集上的实验表明，该方法比传统拼接、重建及最新模态解耦基线提高 3-7% 的准确率，并在传感器缺失、噪声增强等鲁棒性测试中降低 15-30% 的相对错误率；消融实验证实 TD、MD、CA 三个模块对性能均有显著增益，验证了显式解耦思路的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在两种双模态场景验证，尚未扩展到三模态或更多传感器；TI/TV 的划分依赖手工设定的时间窗口与超参数，可能不适用于采样频率差异极大的异构系统；此外，MD 与 CA 模块引入额外参数，对边缘设备的实时性和内存开销未做深入分析。

未来方向：后续可探索自适应 TI/TV 分解策略以适配不同采样率，并将框架推广至任意数量模态的自监督融合；结合知识蒸馏或量化技术压缩模型，满足嵌入式实时推理需求。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多模态融合、时序解耦、鲁棒分类或传感器缺失场景下的可解释深度模型，该文提供的 TI/TV 显式解耦思路与模块化设计可直接借鉴并扩展到雷达-视觉、音频-IMU 等更多异构组合。

Spectrally Distilled Representations Aligned with Instruction-Augmented LLMs for Satellite Imagery

与指令增强型LLM对齐的光谱蒸馏表示用于卫星影像

2026-02-26
arXiv

Minh Kha Do，Wei Xiang，Kang Han，Di Wu，Khoa Phan 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22613v1

Vision-language foundation models (VLFMs) promise zero-shot and retrieval understanding for Earth observation. While operational satellite systems often lack full multi-spectral coverage, making RGB-only inference highly desirable for scalable deployment, the adoption of VLFMs for satellite imagery remains hindered by two factors: (1) multi-spectral inputs are informative but difficult to exploit consistently due to band redundancy and misalignment; and (2) CLIP-style text encoders limit semantic expressiveness and weaken fine-grained alignment. Retrieve and Segment: Are a Few Examples Enough to Bridge the Supervision Gap in Open-Vocabulary Segmentation?

2026-02-26
arXiv

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23339v1

Open-vocabulary segmentation (OVS) extends the zero-shot recognition capabilities of vision-language models (VLMs) to pixel-level prediction, enabling segmentation of arbitrary categories specified by text prompts. Despite recent progress, OVS lags behind fully supervised approaches due to two challenges: the coarse image-level supervision used to train VLMs and the semantic ambiguity of natural language. We address these limitations by introducing a few-shot setting that augments textual prompts with a support set of pixel-annotated images. Building on this, we propose a retrieval-augmented test-time adapter that learns a lightweight, per-image classifier by fusing textual and visual support features. Unlike prior methods relying on late, hand-crafted fusion, our approach performs learned, per-query fusion, achieving stronger synergy between modalities. The method supports continually expanding support sets, and applies to fine-grained tasks such as personalized segmentation. Experiments show that we significantly narrow the gap between zero-shot and supervised segmentation while preserving open-vocabulary ability.
