WS-R-IR Adapter: A Multimodal RGB–Infrared Remote Sensing Framework for Water Surface Object Detection

WS-R-IR Adapter：面向水面目标检测的多模态RGB–红外遥感框架

2026-04-17
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Bin Xue，Qiang Yu，Kun Ding，Mengxin Jiang，Ying Wang 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18081220

Water surface object detection in shipborne remote sensing is challenged by unstable wave-induced backgrounds, illumination variations, extreme scale changes with tiny objects, and limited annotations. Multimodal RGB–infrared (RGB–IR) sensing leverages complementary visible and infrared cues to enhance robustness. However, most existing RGB–IR methods rely on backbones pretrained on limited-scale data, which constrain their performance for complex water surface scenes. In this work, we propose the WS-R-IR Adapter, a parameter-efficient vision foundation model (VFM)-based framework for shipborne RGB–IR object detection. Instead of full fine-tuning, it adapts frozen VFM representations via lightweight task-specific designs. the WS-R-IR Adapter includes (1) a water scene domain-aware modal adapter that progressively guides frozen backbone features with evolving semantic cues, (2) a parallel multi-scale structural perception module for fine-grained, scale-sensitive modeling, (3) an adaptive RGB–IR feature modulation fusion strategy, and (4) a resolution-aligned context semantic and structural detail fusion module. Moreover, we introduce an object-guided global-to-local registration framework to address dynamic cross-modal misalignment, and construct modality-aligned PoLaRIS-DET and ASV-RI-DET datasets that cover diverse water surface scenes. On the two datasets, the proposed method achieves mAP@0.5:0.95 scores of 74.2% and 50.2%, respectively, significantly outperforming existing methods with only 11.9M additional parameters. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of parameter-efficient VFM adaptation for multimodal water surface remote sensing.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决船载RGB-IR遥感水面目标检测中的波浪背景、光照变化、极小尺度与标注稀缺难题。

研究方法：提出WS-R-IR Adapter，冻结VFM主干，以轻量级模态适配、多尺度感知、自适应融合及跨模态配准实现高效微调。

主要发现：在PoLaRIS-DET与ASV-RI-DET上mAP@0.5:0.95分别达74.2%与50.2%，仅用11.9M附加参数显著超越现有方法。

创新点：首提参数高效VFM适配框架，集成水场景模态适配器、跨模态动态配准及对齐数据集，突破复杂水面多模态检测瓶颈。

相关性：为遥感与海事智能提供轻量级多模态解决方案，示范如何低成本迁移大模型至真实水面环境，推动后续研究。

研究背景：船载遥感水面目标检测受波浪扰动、光照剧变、极小人形尺度与稀缺标注困扰，现有RGB-IR方法多依赖小规模预训练骨干，难以泛化复杂水面场景。作者提出以参数高效方式调用冻结视觉基础模型(VFM)，兼顾性能与计算成本。

方法详情：WS-R-IR Adapter保持VFM冻结，仅引入11.9M可训练参数：(1)水域场景模态适配器逐层注入演化语义先验，(2)并行多尺度结构感知模块捕获细粒度尺度敏感特征，(3)自适应RGB-IR特征调制融合策略动态加权互补信息，(4)分辨率对齐的上下文-结构融合模块整合语义与细节。此外，提出目标引导的全局-局部配准框架实时纠正跨模态动态错位，并构建对齐的PoLaRIS-DET与ASV-RI-DET数据集覆盖多样水面环境。

研究结果：在PoLaRIS-DET与ASV-RI-DET上，方法分别取得74.2%与50.2% mAP@0.5:0.95，显著优于现有RGB-IR检测器，同时仅增加11.9M参数，验证参数高效VFM适配在水面多模态遥感中的可行性与优越性。

局限性：方法依赖预训练VFM的表征质量，若基础模型对水域场景先验不足则提升受限；配准框架假设目标可检测以提供配准监督，极端遮挡或极小人形下可能失效；额外11.9M参数对极低功耗船载嵌入式设备仍可能构成负担。

未来方向：后续可探索无监督跨模态配准以减轻对标注的依赖，并将适配器压缩至1-3M参数以满足边缘部署；同时引入时序信息利用视频连续性进一步提升小目标检测鲁棒性。

研究相关性：该文为RGB-IR多模态检测提供参数高效的VFM微调范式，并发布对齐数据集，可为研究小目标、动态背景及跨模态配准的学者提供基准与方法参考。

MDM: Modality Decoupling for Visible and Infrared Mamba-based Object Detection

MDM：面向可见光与红外Mamba目标检测的模态解耦方法

2026-04-17
Neural Networks (IF: 6.3)

Yucheng Zhang，Lin Chai

DOI: 10.1016/j.neunet.2026.108988

Object detection methods that fuse visible and infrared modalities significantly enhance detection accuracy and robustness by leveraging complementary information from both modalities. However, existing methods suffer from the following shortcomings: 1) They are limited to specific scenarios (e.g., conventional or remote sensing small targets), making it difficult to meet the detection needs of multi-scale targets; 2) CNN-based fusion methods, constrained by static convolutional kernels, struggle to handle fusion tasks involving significant modality differences; 3) Transformer-based fusion methods, limited by quadratic computational complexity, hindering practical application. To enhance the model's adaptability to both conventional and remote sensing scenarios, we design the Global and Local Mamba modules, which extract global contextual information from a global perspective and construct local receptive fields from a local perspective, respectively. Secondly, to reduce the computational overhead of long-sequence modeling across modalities during modality fusion, we introduce the Modality Decouple module to decouple features into modality-agnostic and modality-specific features. Based on the degree of modality difference among the decoupled features, differentiated fusion strategies are implemented, effectively reducing the computational resource consumption associated with redundant modality-agnostic features during modality fusion. For modality-agnostic features with small modality differences, we employ a lightweight Spatial Attention module for simple modeling fusion. For modality-specific feature with significant modality differences, we utilize efficient the Spatial Mamba and Channel Mamba modules to perform complex modeling fusion from spatial and channel dimensions, respectively. Simultaneously, to address the potential memory challenges of Mamba in cross modal long sequence modeling, we propose a cross modal interaction paradigm for Mamba within the Spatial and Channel Mamba modules. This paradigm achieves efficient cross modal fusion by interleaving cross modal features at corresponding positions. Extensive experiments on three conventional scene object datasets (FLIR, LLVIP, M3FD) and two remote sensing small object datasets (VEDAI, DroneVehicle) demonstrate that our MDM not only achieves SOTA performance efficiently but also addresses the challenges of small object detection, exhibiting strong robustness. To our knowledge, this study explores the interaction paradigm of Mamba for cross modal fusion. Compared to the simple interaction schemes in existing work, we further unlock the potential of Mamba in cross modal fusion tasks. The code is available at: https://github.com/SEUZYC/MDM .

AI 总结

研究问题：解决可见光-红外跨模态目标检测在多尺度场景下精度与效率不足的问题。

研究方法：提出MDM框架，用Mamba模块分治全局-局部特征，并以模态解耦+差异化融合降低计算量。

主要发现：在五个常规/遥感数据集上达到SOTA，小目标检测与鲁棒性显著提升且计算高效。

创新点：首次设计Mamba跨模态交互范式，并引入模态解耦与差异化融合策略。

相关性：为可见光-红外检测提供高效统一的Mamba方案，推动多模态模型在真实场景落地。

研究背景：可见光-红外双模态目标检测可互补两种成像优势，但在常规场景与遥感小目标等多尺度任务上，现有 CNN 因静态卷积难以弥合模态差异，Transformer 又因二次复杂度难落地。

方法详情：作者提出 MDM 框架，先用 Global Mamba 与 Local Mamba 分别捕获长程上下文与局部感受野；随后设计 Modality Decouple 模块将双模特征解耦为模态无关和模态特定两部分，对前者用轻量空间注意力快速融合，对后者并行部署 Spatial Mamba 与 Channel Mamba，并在序列内部以“对应位置交错”的跨模交互范式实现线性复杂度的高效融合；整体网络在检测头前保持多尺度融合特征金字塔，兼顾常规目标与小目标检测。

研究结果：在 FLIR、LLVIP、M3FD 三个常规数据集与 VEDAI、DroneVehicle 两个遥感小目标数据集上，MDM 均取得 SOTA 精度，同时参数量与计算量显著低于现有 Transformer 融合方法，对小目标和跨模态差异大的场景表现出更强鲁棒性。

局限性：论文未在更大规模 RGB-T 数据集或视频序列上验证，模态解耦阈值需手动设定，且 Mamba 的硬件加速工具链尚不成熟，实际部署速度仍受限制。

未来方向：后续可引入自适应解耦阈值与动态网络剪枝，并探索 MDM 在视频 RGB-T 检测、分割及夜间自动驾驶中的实时迁移。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态融合、线性复杂度长序列建模或小目标检测，本文提供的 Mamba 交互范式与模态解耦策略可直接借鉴并扩展至其他双模或多模视觉任务。

Decoding the Delta: Unifying Remote Sensing Change Detection and Understanding with Multimodal Large Language Models

2026-04-15
arXiv

Xiaohe Li，Jiahao Li，Kaixin Zhang，Yuqiang Fang，Leilei Lin 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14044v1

While Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) excel in general vision-language tasks, their application to remote sensing change understanding is hindered by a fundamental "temporal blindness". Existing architectures lack intrinsic mechanisms for multi-temporal contrastive reasoning and struggle with precise spatial grounding. To address this, we first introduce Delta-QA, a comprehensive benchmark comprising 180k visual question-answering samples. Delta-QA unifies pixel-level segmentation and visual question answering across bi- and tri-temporal scenarios, structuring change interpretation into four progressive cognitive dimensions. Methodologically, we propose Delta-LLaVA, a novel MLLM framework explicitly tailored for multi-temporal remote sensing interpretation. It overcomes the limitations of naive feature concatenation through three core innovations: a Change-Enhanced Attention module that systematically isolates and amplifies visual differences, a Change-SEG module utilizing Change Prior Embedding to extract differentiable difference features as input for the LLM, and Local Causal Attention to prevent cross-temporal contextual leakage. Extensive experiments demonstrate that Delta-LLaVA decisively outperforms leading generalist MLLMs and specialized segmentation models in complex change deduction and high-precision boundary localization, establishing a unified framework for earth observation intelligence.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何克服多模态大模型在遥感变化理解中的“时间盲”与空间定位难题。

研究方法：提出Delta-LLaVA，集成变化增强注意、变化先验嵌入和局部因果注意，并构建18万样本的Delta-QA基准。

主要发现：Delta-LLaVA在复杂变化推理与高精度边界定位上显著优于通用MLLM和专业分割模型。

创新点：首次将多模态大模型扩展至多时间遥感解释，显式建模跨时差异并防止时序信息泄露。

相关性：为地球观测智能提供统一框架，推动遥感变化检测从像素分割迈向语言化精细理解。

研究背景：多模态大语言模型(MLLM)在通用视觉-语言任务中表现突出，但在遥感变化理解上存在“时序盲”——缺乏内生的多时相推理机制，难以精准空间定位。作者希望把像素级分割与高层问答统一到一个框架，实现从检测变化到解释变化的跨越。

方法详情：论文提出Delta-LLaVA，用三处关键设计替代简单拼接：① Change-Enhanced Attention在视觉编码阶段显式分离并放大差异；② Change-SEG将差异先验嵌入可微特征，直接喂给LLM供其推理；③ Local Causal Attention屏蔽跨时序上下文泄露，保证因果顺序。整套流程在自建的180k样本Delta-QA上端到端训练，覆盖双时相、三时相及四个递进认知维度。

Object detection methods that fuse visible and infrared modalities significantly enhance detection accuracy and robustness by leveraging complementary information from both modalities. However, existing methods suffer from the following shortcomings: 1) They are limited to specific scenarios (e.g., conventional or remote sensing small targets), making it difficult to meet the detection needs of multi-scale targets; 2) CNN-based fusion methods, constrained by static convolutional kernels, struggle to handle fusion tasks involving significant modality differences; 3) Transformer-based fusion methods, limited by quadratic computational complexity, hindering practical application. To enhance the model's adaptability to both conventional and remote sensing scenarios, we design the Global and Local Mamba modules, which extract global contextual information from a global perspective and construct local receptive fields from a local perspective, respectively. Secondly, to reduce the computational overhead of long-sequence modeling across modalities during modality fusion, we introduce the Modality Decouple module to decouple features into modality-agnostic and modality-specific features. Based on the degree of modality difference among the decoupled features, differentiated fusion strategies are implemented, effectively reducing the computational resource consumption associated with redundant modality-agnostic features during modality fusion. For modality-agnostic features with small modality differences, we employ a lightweight Spatial Attention module for simple modeling fusion. For modality-specific feature with significant modality differences, we utilize efficient the Spatial Mamba and Channel Mamba modules to perform complex modeling fusion from spatial and channel dimensions, respectively. Simultaneously, to address the potential memory challenges of Mamba in cross modal long sequence modeling, we propose a cross modal interaction paradigm for Mamba within the Spatial and Channel Mamba modules. This paradigm achieves efficient cross modal fusion by interleaving cross modal features at corresponding positions. Extensive experiments on three conventional scene object datasets (FLIR, LLVIP, M3FD) and two remote sensing small object datasets (VEDAI, DroneVehicle) demonstrate that our MDM not only achieves SOTA performance efficiently but also addresses the challenges of small object detection, exhibiting strong robustness. To our knowledge, this study explores the interaction paradigm of Mamba for cross modal fusion.
