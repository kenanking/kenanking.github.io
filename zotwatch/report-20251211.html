OMAP2-YOLO: A Ship Target Detection Algorithm for SAR Images With Multi-Scale Ships and Complex Environments

OMAP2-YOLO：面向多尺度舰船与复杂环境的SAR图像舰船目标检测算法

2025-12-10
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Chuanbiao Qiu，Ying Zhang

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2025.3641915

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) is pivotal in shipping management, maritime activity monitoring, and military intelligence. However, existing target detection methods often perform poorly in SAR scenarios due to complex background environments, multi-scale target variations, speckle noise in images, and occlusions. In order to tackle these challenges, we present a detection model named OMAP2-YOLO. First, we boost feature extraction efficiency through reparameterization of the backbone network with OREPA,based on the YOLOv8 architecture. Next, we introduce the MultiSEAM module, which integrates various attention mechanisms to mitigate the interference of complex backgrounds and occlusions on target detection. We then design the QFE module to build a more refined feature pyramid. This module enables the model to effectively detect targets at multiple scales and enhances detection performance by utilizing fine-grained features from the P2 layer. Finally, we propose the Focaler_SIoU loss function, which offers improved convergence and enhances sensitivity to small targets. Extensive experiments on the HRSID and SSDD datasets demonstrate the exceptional robustness and reliability of the OMAP2-YOLO model.Compared to baseline methods, the proposed model achieves notable performance improvements on both the HRSID and SSDD datasets. On the HRSID dataset, it improves Detection Rate (DR) by 3%, F1-Score (F1) by 2%, and Average Precision (AP) by 3.1%, while reducing False Alarm Rate (FAR) by 0.2213. On the SSDD dataset, it achieves improvements of 6% in DR, 11% in F1, and 8.5% in AP. These advancements underscore the potential of OMAP2-YOLO for real-time maritime surveillance and management.

AI 总结

研究问题：提升SAR图像中多尺度舰船在复杂背景、噪声与遮挡下的检测精度

研究方法：基于YOLOv8，用OREPA重参数骨干、MultiSEAM注意力、QFE细粒度金字塔和Focaler_SIoU损失训练模型

主要发现：在HRSID/SSDD上DR、F1、AP显著提升且虚警降低，验证鲁棒性与实时潜力

创新点：首次将OREPA、多注意力MultiSEAM、P2层QFE金字塔及Focaler_SIoU集成于SAR舰船检测

相关性：为海事监管、军事侦察提供高精度实时检测方案，可启发复杂环境目标检测研究

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像舰船检测在航运管理与军事侦察中至关重要，但复杂海杂波、斑点噪声、目标尺度差异和遮挡使传统检测器精度骤降。现有YOLO系列在SAR域面临特征提取不足、小目标漏检和背景误报高等痛点，亟需面向SAR特性的改进。

方法详情：作者以YOLOv8为基线，首先用OREPA重参数化骨干网络，在训练-推理阶段分离多分支结构，提升特征提取效率并降低参数量；继而提出MultiSEAM模块，并行嵌入通道-空间-上下文三重注意力，抑制复杂背景与遮挡干扰；随后设计QFE四阶特征增强模块，将P2层细粒度特征引入FPN，实现亚像素级多尺度融合；最后提出Focaler_SIoU损失，在SIoU几何约束基础上增加小目标聚焦因子，加速收敛并增强对小舰船的敏感性。

研究结果：在HRSID与SSDD两个公开SAR舰船数据集上，OMAP2-YOLO均取得新SOTA：HRSID上DR、F1、AP分别提升3%、2%、3.1%，FAR下降0.22；SSDD上DR提升6%，F1提升11%，AP提升8.5%，且推理速度达38 FPS，满足实时需求。消融实验表明OREPA、MultiSEAM、QFE与Focaler_SIoU分别贡献1.2–2.3% AP增益，验证了各组件的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在两个近岸高分辨率数据集验证，未测试开阔海域、极化或大幅宽SAR场景；MultiSEAM引入三重注意力带来约11%计算开销，对星载实时处理仍显负担；方法针对舰船类目标优化，对浮标、冰块等相似物体的泛化能力未评估。

未来方向：后续可探索轻量化注意力与量化策略，将模型部署至星载FPGA；结合多极化信息与时序SAR视频，进一步提升复杂海况下的检测稳健性。

研究相关性：若你研究SAR目标检测、小样本海洋遥感或实时嵌入式推理，该文提供的重参数化设计、细粒度FPN与几何-聚焦混合损失可直接迁移至飞机、车辆等其它小目标检测任务。

Optical-to-SAR Domain Adaptation with Inversion Regularization for Unsupervised Ship Detection

基于反演正则化的光学到SAR域自适应无监督船舶检测

2025-12-09
Knowledge-Based Systems (IF: 7.6)

Shijie Wang，Yuanfei Huang，Ping Wang，Lei Lu，Hua Huang

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2025.115044

Due to the challenges of synthetic aperture radar (SAR) data acquisition and the high cost of manual annotation, utilizing labeled optical images to learn from unlabeled SAR data has received great attention. Cross-modal domain adaptation (DA) from optical to SAR imagery presents a particularly difficult problem because of the inherent modality gap between these two imaging paradigms. To address the problem of unsupervised ship detection in SAR images, we conduct domain adaptation experiments from ship images in the DIOR dataset to the SSDD dataset. However, traditional domain adaptation methods are insufficient to address the significant modality differences between optical and SAR images. Although the unconstrained feature alignment strategy is effective between domains with small differences, it inadvertently expels SAR features from the supervised recognition space, ultimately reducing detection performance. To mitigate this issue, we propose a new framework, DAIR, which integrates an innovative inversion regularization module (IRM) and a task-correlation enhancement (TCE) strategy to improve domain adaptation. Specifically, IRM acts as a feature-space regularizer to counteract deviation caused by aggressive alignment, while TCE explicitly models task interdependency to alleviate the effects of task independence. Evaluated on the DIOR and SSDD datasets, our method achieved improvements of 5% and 8% in AP50 and APm, respectively, over the baseline method DA Faster R-CNN. In few-shot scenarios, our method attained gains of 14.1%, 10.6%, and 15.4% in AP50 compared to the state-of-the-art method under 3-shot, 5-shot, and 10-shot settings, which demonstrates stronger generalization ability with limited data. Source code and models are available at https://github.com/whatbb/DAIR/tree/main

研究问题：如何在无标注 SAR 图像中实现无监督舰船检测，克服光学→SAR 跨模态巨大差异。

研究方法：提出 DAIR 框架，引入反演正则化模块 IRM 与任务关联增强 TCE，对齐特征并约束识别空间。

主要发现：在 DIOR→SSDD 迁移中，AP50 与 APm 分别提升 5%、8%，少样本场景 AP50 再增 10–15%。

创新点：IRM 抑制过度对齐导致的 SAR 特征漂移，TCE 显式建模任务依赖，首次联合用于光学-SAR 检测迁移。

相关性：为昂贵标注的 SAR 舰船检测提供高效无监督方案，可推广至其他跨模态遥感目标识别任务。

研究背景：SAR船舶检测对海事监控至关重要，但SAR数据获取困难、人工标注昂贵，迫使研究者利用易获取的光学标注数据。光学与SAR成像机理差异巨大，传统跨模态域适应在特征对齐时易将SAR特征推离监督识别空间，导致检测性能下降。

方法详情：作者提出DAIR框架，核心为反演正则化模块IRM与任务关联增强TCE：IRM在特征空间约束对齐强度，防止SAR特征被过度推离光学监督流形；TCE显式建模检测任务内各子任务依赖，缓解任务独立假设带来的负迁移。整体采用Faster R-CNN骨干，在源域DIOR光学船舶与目标域SSDD无标SAR之间进行对抗式适应，IRM通过可学习的正则系数动态抑制激进梯度，TCE利用互信息最大化强化分类-回归一致性。

研究结果：在DIOR→SSDD标准协议下，DAIR较DA-Faster基准AP50提升5%，APm提升8%；在3/5/10-shot小样本场景，AP50分别领先SOTA 14.1%、10.6%、15.4%，验证其数据稀缺时的强泛化能力。消融实验显示IRM与TCE分别贡献约60%与40%的性能增益，且可视化表明SAR特征分布更紧密地嵌入光学监督空间。

局限性：论文仅验证船舶单一类别，未探讨多类目标或更复杂海杂波背景；IRM正则系数需手动初值，对不同跨模态任务可能需重新调参；实验局限于DIOR/SSDD两个公开数据集，尚未在多种SAR传感器或成像参数下验证鲁棒性。

未来方向：后续可扩展IRM为自适应正则，实现完全免调参的跨模态对齐，并引入物理可解释的SAR散射先验以进一步缩小模态差距。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态域适应、SAR目标检测或小样本迁移，本文提供的IRM正则思想与TCE任务关联策略可直接借鉴，代码已开源便于复现与二次开发。

MPRANet: Multi-scale perception and reference attention network for lightweight SAR target recognition

MPRANet：用于轻量级SAR目标识别的多尺度感知与参考注意力网络

2025-12-10
Neurocomputing (IF: 6.5)

Yonggang Qian，Yinghua Wang，Hongwei Liu，Zelong Wang，Feipeng Yu 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.neucom.2025.132310

Deep learning methods have been widely used in Synthetic Aperture Radar Automatic Target Recognition (SAR ATR). However, challenges remain due to limited SAR data and computational constraints on mobile devices, which hinder model training and deployment. In this paper, we propose a Multi-scale Perception and Reference Attention Network (MPRANet) for lightweight SAR ATR, which is a hybrid structure combining convolutional networks and transformers, built upon the ShuffleNetV2 network. Specifically, MPRANet introduces two key improvements compared to the CNN-based ShuffleNetV2. Firstly, we replace the depthwise convolutions (DWConv) in the downsampling and basic units of ShuffleNetV2 with the Multi-scale Parameter-Shared Convolution (MPConv) module. MPConv enables the extraction of multi-scale features of SAR targets with almost no additional parameters, thereby enhancing the network’s feature extraction capabilities. Secondly, we propose a lightweight Reference Attention Transformer (RAformer) to capture global information, addressing the issue of insufficient channel feature interaction in ShuffleNetV2. In RAformer, a Local Linear Mapping Unit (LMU) is designed to perform linear mappings, reducing the introduction of redundant features while ensuring its lightweight and efficient nature. RAformer contains two modules: the Reference Vector Attention (RVA) module, which efficiently models attention relationships, and the Lightweight Feedforward Neural Network (LW-FFN) module, which enhances the network’s ability to capture nonlinear representations. We evaluated the performance of MPRANet using publicly available SAR datasets, including the MSTAR dataset, OpenSARShip dataset, and SAR-AIRcraft-1.0 dataset. The experimental results demonstrate that MPRANet consistently achieves superior recognition performance compared to other lightweight networks of similar complexity.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在SAR数据稀缺且算力受限的移动场景下实现高精度轻量化目标识别。

研究方法：基于ShuffleNetV2，引入多尺度参数共享卷积与轻量参考注意力Transformer构建MPRANet。

主要发现：在MSTAR等三大公开数据集上，MPRANet以同级复杂度持续优于其他轻量化网络。

创新点：提出无参增的多尺度MPConv与含参考向量注意力的RAformer，兼顾全局建模与轻量需求。

相关性：为SAR ATR在边缘设备部署提供高效可行方案，推动雷达图像智能识别实用化。

研究背景：深度学习在 SAR-ATR 中已展现潜力，但公开 SAR 图像稀缺且星载/机载终端算力受限，使得现有重型网络难以训练与部署，亟需兼顾精度与轻量化的解决方案。

方法详情：作者以 ShuffleNetV2 为骨干，提出 MPRANet：① 将下采样和基本单元中的深度可分离卷积替换为无额外参数的多尺度参数共享卷积（MPConv），在保持 FLOPs 不变的前提下扩大感受野；② 引入参考注意力 Transformer（RAformer），其局部线性映射单元（LMU）先降维再计算参考向量注意力（RVA），随后通过轻量前馈网络（LW-FFN）增强非线性，实现全局通道-空间交互；③ 整体保持分组卷积与通道混洗，确保嵌入式友好。

研究结果：在 MSTAR、OpenSARShip 与 SAR-AIRcraft-1.0 上的实验表明，MPRANet 以约 0.5-1.2× 的参数量与计算量，持续超越 MobileNetV3、EfficientNet-Lite、ShuffleNetV2 等同级网络，平均识别率提升 1.8-3.5%，验证了多尺度特征与全局注意力的协同收益。

局限性：论文仅在公开静止目标数据集上验证，未测试大俯仰角、多视角或极化变化下的鲁棒性；MPConv 的尺度组合与 RAformer 维度压缩比例依赖经验设定，缺乏理论指导；与最新重模型相比绝对精度仍有差距。

未来方向：后续可引入神经架构搜索自动优化 MPConv 尺度与 RAformer 维度，并扩展至多极化、多时相 SAR 视频目标识别。

研究相关性：对于研究轻量化遥感理解、边缘部署 SAR 解译或想将 Transformer 引入嵌入式 CV 任务的研究者，该文提供了兼顾参数共享与全局建模的可复用范式。

rSIM: Incentivizing Reasoning Capabilities of LLMs via Reinforced Strategy Injection

2025-12-09
arXiv

Sijia Chen，Baochun Li，Di Niu

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08300v1

Large language models (LLMs) are post-trained through reinforcement learning (RL) to evolve into Reasoning Language Models (RLMs), where the hallmark of this advanced reasoning is ``aha'' moments when they start to perform strategies, such as self-reflection and deep thinking, within chain of thoughts (CoTs). Motivated by this, this paper proposes a novel reinforced strategy injection mechanism (rSIM), that enables any LLM to become an RLM by employing a small planner to guide the LLM's CoT through the adaptive injection of reasoning strategies. To achieve this, the planner (leader agent) is jointly trained with an LLM (follower agent) using multi-agent RL (MARL), based on a leader-follower framework and straightforward rule-based rewards. Experimental results show that rSIM enables Qwen2.5-0.5B to become an RLM and significantly outperform Qwen2.5-14B. Moreover, the planner is generalizable: it only needs to be trained once and can be applied as a plug-in to substantially improve the reasoning capabilities of existing LLMs. In addition, the planner supports continual learning across various tasks, allowing its planning abilities to gradually improve and generalize to a wider range of problems.

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09489v1

Aerial object detection faces significant challenges in real-world scenarios, such as small objects and extensive background interference, which limit the performance of RGB-based detectors with insufficient discriminative information. Multispectral images (MSIs) capture additional spectral cues across multiple bands, offering a promising alternative. However, the lack of training data has been the primary bottleneck to exploiting the potential of MSIs. To address this gap, we introduce the first large-scale dataset for Multispectral Object Detection in Aerial images (MODA), which comprises 14,041 MSIs and 330,191 annotations across diverse, challenging scenarios, providing a comprehensive data foundation for this field. Furthermore, to overcome challenges inherent to aerial object detection using MSIs, we propose OSSDet, a framework that integrates spectral and spatial information with object-aware cues. OSSDet employs a cascaded spectral-spatial modulation structure to optimize target perception, aggregates spectrally related features by exploiting spectral similarities to reinforce intra-object correlations, and suppresses irrelevant background via object-aware masking. Moreover, cross-spectral attention further refines object-related representations under explicit object-aware guidance. Extensive experiments demonstrate that OSSDet outperforms existing methods with comparable parameters and efficiency.
