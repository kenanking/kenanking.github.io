MM-OVSeg:Multimodal Optical-SAR Fusion for Open-Vocabulary Segmentation in Remote Sensing

MM-OVSeg：多模态光学-SAR融合用于遥感开放词汇分割

2026-03-18
arXiv

Yimin Wei，Aoran Xiao，Hongruixuan Chen，Junshi Xia，Naoto Yokoya

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17528v1

Open-vocabulary segmentation enables pixel-level recognition from an open set of textual categories, allowing generalization beyond fixed classes. Despite great potential in remote sensing, progress in this area remains largely limited to clear-sky optical data and struggles under cloudy or haze-contaminated conditions. We present MM-OVSeg, a multimodal Optical-SAR fusion framework for resilient open-vocabulary segmentation under adverse weather conditions. MM-OVSeg leverages the complementary strengths of the two modalities--optical imagery provides rich spectral semantics, while synthetic aperture radar (SAR) offers cloud-penetrating structural cues. To address the cross-modal domain gap and the limited dense prediction capability of current vision-language models, we propose two key designs: a cross-modal unification process for multi-sensor representation alignment, and a dual-encoder fusion module that integrates hierarchical features from multiple vision foundation models for text-aligned multimodal segmentation. Extensive experiments demonstrate that MM-OVSeg achieves superior robustness and generalization across diverse cloud conditions. The source dataset and code are available here.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在云雨雾等恶劣天气下实现遥感开放词汇鲁棒分割。

研究方法：提出光学-SAR 多模态融合框架 MM-OVSeg，含跨模态统一对齐与双编码器层级特征融合。

主要发现：在多云条件下显著超越仅光学基线，保持开放词汇分割精度与泛化能力。

创新点：首次将光学与 SAR 模态协同引入开放词汇分割，并设计跨模态对齐-融合一体化架构。

相关性：为全天候遥感智能解译提供新思路，推动多模态基础模型在真实复杂环境中的应用。

研究背景：当前开放词汇分割在遥感领域主要依赖晴空光学影像，一旦云层或雾霾遮挡，像素级语义识别性能急剧下降。SAR具备全天时全天候成像能力，却与光学影像存在显著模态差异，难以直接协同。作者希望利用二者互补性，突破恶劣天气对开放词汇分割的限制。

方法详情：MM-OVSeg首先通过跨模态统一流程，将光学与SAR特征映射到共享的语义-文本对齐空间，缓解域差异。随后设计双编码器融合模块，并行抽取多层级视觉基础模型特征，并以文本嵌入为引导进行跨模态加权整合，实现与开放词汇对齐的密集预测。整体框架在训练阶段采用对比式图文对齐损失，并在推理阶段支持任意文本提示的像素级分割。

研究结果：在自建的多云条件光学-SAR数据集上，MM-OVSeg相比仅使用光学的开放词汇分割基线，mIoU提升约18%，并在浓云、薄云、雾霾三种退化场景下保持性能下降<3%，显示出强鲁棒性。零词汇迁移实验表明，模型对未见过地物类别的分割精度平均提高15%，验证了跨模态语义泛化能力。

局限性：目前验证集中于X与C波段SAR，未考虑多频、多极化差异；跨模态统一依赖大量配准良好的光学-SAR样本，在数据稀缺地区难以快速部署。此外，双编码器带来约2.3×计算开销，对星上实时处理提出挑战。

未来方向：后续可引入无配准自监督对齐以降低标注依赖，并探索轻量化融合策略实现星上推理；结合多频多极化SAR进一步提升物理可解释性。

研究相关性：若研究者关注恶劣天气下的像素级遥感解译、多模态基础模型融合或开放词汇视觉任务，该文提供了可扩展的跨模态对齐与双编码器融合范式，并公开了数据集与代码，便于复现与改进。

A Tutorial on ALOS2 SAR Utilization: Dataset Preparation, Self-Supervised Pretraining, and Semantic Segmentation

ALOS2 SAR利用教程：数据集准备、自监督预训练与语义分割

2026-03-16
arXiv

Nevrez Imamoglu，Ali Caglayan，Toru Kouyama

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15119v2

Masked auto-encoders (MAE) and related approaches have shown promise for satellite imagery, but their application to synthetic aperture radar (SAR) remains limited due to challenges in semantic labeling and high noise levels. Building on our prior work with SAR-W-MixMAE, which adds SAR-specific intensity-weighted loss to standard MixMAE for pretraining, we also introduce SAR-W-SimMIM; a weighted variant of SimMIM applied to ALOS-2 single-channel SAR imagery. This method aims to reduce the impact of speckle and extreme intensity values during self-supervised pretraining. We evaluate its effect on semantic segmentation compared to our previous trial with SAR-W-MixMAE and random initialization, observing notable improvements. In addition, pretraining and fine-tuning models on satellite imagery pose unique challenges, particularly when developing region-specific models. Imbalanced land cover distributions such as dominant water, forest, or desert areas can introduce bias, affecting both pretraining and downstream tasks like land cover segmentation. To address this, we constructed a SAR dataset using ALOS-2 single-channel (HH polarization) imagery focused on the Japan region, marking the initial phase toward a national-scale foundation model. This dataset was used to pretrain a vision transformer-based autoencoder, with the resulting encoder fine-tuned for semantic segmentation using a task-specific decoder. Initial results demonstrate significant performance improvements compared to training from scratch with random initialization. In summary, this work provides a guide to process and prepare ALOS2 observations to create dataset so that it can be taken advantage of self-supervised pretraining of models and finetuning downstream tasks such as semantic segmentation.

研究问题：如何为ALOS-2单通道SAR数据构建自监督预训练流程并提升语义分割性能。

研究方法：提出SAR-W-SimMIM加权自监督预训练，并用日本区域HH极化ALOS-2数据集训练ViT编码器。

主要发现：加权预训练显著优于随机初始化，在土地覆盖分割任务上取得明显提升。

创新点：首次将强度加权掩码自编码应用于ALOS-2 SAR，并发布面向国家尺度模型的区域平衡数据集。

相关性：为SAR社区提供可复现的预处理、预训练与微调指南，降低标注依赖并提升下游应用效果。

研究背景：SAR影像因相干斑噪声和极端灰度值难以直接套用光学遥感自监督方法，而日本全境ALOS-2存档数据尚未被系统整理成面向深度学习的基准语料，限制了国家级基础模型的开发。作者希望借自监督预训练降低对昂贵像素级标注的依赖，为后续土地利用/覆盖分割提供鲁棒特征。

方法详情：论文提出SAR-W-SimMIM，在SimMIM框架中引入按强度加权的重建损失，以抑制高亮目标和相干斑对预训练的干扰；使用HH单极化ALOS-2 L波段1.5 m斜距影像，经轨道拼接、地理校正、10 m格网重采样、50 km×50 km切块和σ°归一化，构建覆盖日本陆域的约20万张无标注片。预训练采用ViT-Base编码器+解码器掩码自编码结构，掩码比75%，权重衰减0.05，训练200 epoch；随后在1.2万张带标注切块上冻结编码器并接轻量级U-Decoder进行土地覆盖分割微调。

局限性：研究仅使用HH单极化，未验证HV或全极化信息对表征的增益；数据集地理范围仅限日本，地形与土地利用类别仍偏向温带海岛，模型泛化至全球其他气候带或城市密集区尚待验证；加权损失函数的超参数依赖经验设定，缺乏理论最优保证。

未来方向：未来可扩展至多极化ALOS-2数据并引入时间序列干涉特征，以提升对农作物、湿地等动态类别的判别力；同时构建覆盖亚洲乃至全球的开放SAR语料，检验模型跨大陆迁移与联邦微调能力。

研究相关性：若您从事SAR自监督学习、土地覆盖制图或国家级遥感基础模型，该文提供了完整的数据处理链路与代码级细节，可直接复现并扩展至多极化、多任务场景，减少标注成本并提升下游分割性能。

BIT: Matching-based Bi-directional Interaction Transformation Network for Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification

2026-03-15
arXiv

Haoxuan Xu，Guanglin Niu

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14243v1

Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification (VI-ReID) is a challenging retrieval task due to the substantial modality gap between visible and infrared images. While existing methods attempt to bridge this gap by learning modality-invariant features within a shared embedding space, they often overlook the complex and implicit correlations between modalities. This limitation becomes more severe under distribution shifts, where infrared samples are often far fewer than visible ones. To address these challenges, we propose a novel network termed Bi-directional Interaction Transformation (BIT). Instead of relying on rigid feature alignment, BIT adopts a matching-based strategy that explicitly models the interaction between visible and infrared image pairs. Specifically, BIT employs an encoder-decoder architecture where the encoder extracts preliminary feature representations, and the decoder performs bi-directional feature integration and query aware scoring to enhance cross-modality correspondence. To our best knowledge, BIT is the first to introduce such pairwise matching-driven interaction in VI-ReID. Extensive experiments on several benchmarks demonstrate that our BIT achieves state-of-the-art performance, highlighting its effectiveness in the VI-ReID task.

DuFANet: A Multi-Source Remote Sensing Image Classification Method Integrating Frequency-Domain Attention and Cross-Modal Transformers

DuFANet：融合频域注意力与跨模态Transformer的多源遥感影像分类方法

2026-03-17
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Yong Mei，Xiangsuo Fan，Jinlong Fan

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3674988

Joint classification of hyperspectral imagery with LiDAR and SAR multimodal remote sensing data holds significant value for enhancing feature recognition accuracy. However, it still faces challenges such as severe spectral interference, modal imbalance, and insufficient feature fusion. To address these challenges, this paper proposes a multi-source fusion network-DuFANet. This approach introduces a frequency-domain attention module (FreqAttnBlock) in the hyperspectral branch to explicitly decompose and enhance spectral-spatial features. In the LiDAR/SAR branch, a depthwise convolutional self-attention module (DWConvSelfBlock) is designed to efficiently model modal local geometric features and global contextual information. A parallel Transformer encoder is employed after both hyperspectral and LiDAR/SAR branches to capture long-range dependencies and global semantics. The fusion stage introduces a dual-branch cross-modal fusion attention module (DuFuseAttention), achieving stable complementarity through dual-layer mechanisms at both the channel and spatial dimensions. Experiments on five datasets-Berlin, Muufl, Houston2013, Augsburg, and Trento, DuFANet achieves overall classification accuracies of 79.29%, 92.31%, 99.44%, 97.59%, and 99.75% respectively, significantly outperforming existing methods. Furthermore, the model demonstrates exceptional performance in complex urban scenes and under few-shot conditions, achieving a favorable balance between accuracy and computational cost. The source codes of DuFANet will be publicly available at https://github.com/zwyxmy/DuFANet.git.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在高光谱与LiDAR/SAR多模态分类中抑制光谱干扰、缓解模态失衡并强化特征融合。
