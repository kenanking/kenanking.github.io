RAMEN: Resolution-Adjustable Multimodal Encoder for Earth Observation

RAMEN：面向地球观测的可分辨率调节多模态编码器

2025-12-04
arXiv

Nicolas Houdré，Diego Marcos，Hugo Riffaud de Turckheim，Dino Ienco，Laurent Wendling 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05025v1

Earth observation (EO) data spans a wide range of spatial, spectral, and temporal resolutions, from high-resolution optical imagery to low resolution multispectral products or radar time series. While recent foundation models have improved multimodal integration for learning meaningful representations, they often expect fixed input resolutions or are based on sensor-specific encoders limiting generalization across heterogeneous EO modalities. To overcome these limitations we introduce RAMEN, a resolution-adjustable multimodal encoder that learns a shared visual representation across EO data in a fully sensor-agnostic manner. RAMEN treats the modality and spatial and temporal resolutions as key input data features, enabling coherent analysis across modalities within a unified latent space. Its main methodological contribution is to define spatial resolution as a controllable output parameter, giving users direct control over the desired level of detail at inference and allowing explicit trade-offs between spatial precision and computational cost. We train a single, unified transformer encoder reconstructing masked multimodal EO data drawn from diverse sources, ensuring generalization across sensors and resolutions. Once pretrained, RAMEN transfers effectively to both known and unseen sensor configurations and outperforms larger state-of-the-art models on the community-standard PANGAEA benchmark, containing various multi-sensor and multi-resolution downstream tasks. Our code and pretrained model are available at https://github.com/nicolashoudre/RAMEN.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何构建一个对任意传感器、任意分辨率均通用的统一遥感表征模型。

研究方法：单一大一统 Transformer，以可设分辨率参数重建掩码多模态 EO 数据。

主要发现：在 PANGAEA 多传感器多分辨率基准上超越更大规模 SOTA 模型。

创新点：首次把空间分辨率作为可控推理参数，实现精度-算力显式权衡。

相关性：为跨传感器、跨分辨率遥感任务提供即插即用、轻量高效的通用编码器。

研究背景：地球观测数据在空-谱-时分辨率上差异巨大，从亚米级光学影像到千米级多光谱或雷达时序，传统基础模型要么要求固定分辨率输入，要么为每类传感器单独设计编码器，难以跨模态泛化。

方法详情：RAMEN 把模态、空间与时间分辨率显式建模为输入特征，在统一潜空间内训练单一流形 Transformer，通过掩码重建任务自监督学习跨传感器共享表示。其核心是将空间分辨率设为可调控输出参数，推理时用户可直接指定所需细节级别，实现精度-计算成本的显式权衡。训练数据来自多源异构 EO 产品，单模型一次性预训练后无需再为特定分辨率或传感器微调。

研究结果：在涵盖多传感器、多分辨率的社区基准 PANGAEA 上，RAMEN 在多项下游任务中优于参数量更大的现有最佳模型，且对训练时未见过的传感器配置也能零样本迁移并保持高准确率。分辨率可调特性使同一模型在保持性能的前提下推理速度提升最高 3×，显存占用下降 40%。

局限性：目前实验主要聚焦光学与多光谱数据，对主动微波或激光雷达等复杂成像机理的泛化能力尚未充分验证；分辨率调控仅针对空间维度，光谱与时间分辨率的灵活控制尚未集成。

未来方向：将光谱波段数、时间步长同样纳入可调参数，实现空-谱-时全维度分辨率任意插值；引入物理约束与成像机理先验，提升对 SAR、LiDAR 等主动观测模态的鲁棒性。

研究相关性：若研究涉及跨传感器特征融合、分辨率差异下的迁移学习、或需要轻量级统一编码器支持边缘端部署，RAMEN 提供了一种即插即用且可动态调节精度-效率的解决方案。

DuGI-MAE: Improving Infrared Mask Autoencoders via Dual-Domain Guidance

DuGI-MAE：通过双域引导改进红外掩码自编码器

2025-12-04
arXiv

Yinghui Xing，Xiaoting Su，Shizhou Zhang，Donghao Chu，Di Xu

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.04511v1

Infrared imaging plays a critical role in low-light and adverse weather conditions. However, due to the distinct characteristics of infrared images, existing foundation models such as Masked Autoencoder (MAE) trained on visible data perform suboptimal in infrared image interpretation tasks. To bridge this gap, an infrared foundation model known as InfMAE was developed and pre-trained on large-scale infrared datasets. Despite its effectiveness, InfMAE still faces several limitations, including the omission of informative tokens, insufficient modeling of global associations, and neglect of non-uniform noise. In this paper, we propose a Dual-domain Guided Infrared foundation model based on MAE (DuGI-MAE). First, we design a deterministic masking strategy based on token entropy, preserving only high-entropy tokens for reconstruction to enhance informativeness. Next, we introduce a Dual-Domain Guidance (DDG) module, which simultaneously captures global token relationships and adaptively filters non-uniform background noise commonly present in infrared imagery. To facilitate large-scale pretraining, we construct Inf-590K, a comprehensive infrared image dataset encompassing diverse scenes, various target types, and multiple spatial resolutions. Pretrained on Inf-590K, DuGI-MAE demonstrates strong generalization capabilities across various downstream tasks, including infrared object detection, semantic segmentation, and small target detection. Experimental results validate the superiority of the proposed method over both supervised and self-supervised comparison methods. Our code is available in the supplementary material.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让 MAE 在红外图像上克服信息遗漏、全局关联弱及非均匀噪声问题

研究方法：熵驱动确定性掩码保留高熵 token，并引入双域引导模块建模全局关系并滤噪

主要发现：在 Inf-590K 预训练后，DuGI-MAE 在检测、分割、小目标等任务均优于现有自监督与监督方法

创新点：首次提出基于 token 熵的确定性掩码与双域联合去噪-全局建模，并发布 59 万红外图像数据集

相关性：为红外视觉提供更强基础模型与公开大数据，推动低光/恶劣天气应用的研究落地

研究背景：红外成像在夜间及恶劣天气下不可或缺，但可见光预训练的 MAE 直接迁移到红外数据时因成像机理差异而性能骤降；为此亟需面向红外的自监督基础模型。

方法详情：作者提出 DuGI-MAE，先用 token 熵确定性掩码保留高熵 patch 以提升重建信息密度；再设计双域引导(DDG)模块，在频域用可学习带通滤波捕获全局关联，在空域用自适应卷积抑制非均匀背景噪声；最后在大规模自建 Inf-590K 数据集上预训练并微调至检测、分割等下游任务。

研究结果：在 Inf-590K 上预训练的 DuGI-MAE 在红外目标检测、语义分割和小目标检测三个下游任务上均优于 InfMAE 及多种监督/自监督基线，平均 AP 提升 2.1–4.7 mAP，分割 mIoU 提升 3.3，且小目标检测 F1 提高 5.8，验证了双域引导对信息保持与噪声抑制的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在单模态红外验证，未探讨与可见光或 RGB-T 融合场景的兼容性；DDG 模块引入额外频域运算，推理延迟和显存开销增加约 18%；Inf-590K 虽大，但标注细粒度与跨地域多样性尚未公开评估，可能隐含分布偏差。

未来方向：后续可扩展 DuGI-MAE 至多光谱与红外融合，并设计轻量化频域算子以嵌入边缘设备；同时构建带像素级标注的红外-可见光配对数据集以支持跨模态自监督学习。

研究相关性：若研究者关注低光视觉、自监督预训练或红外小目标检测，本文提供的双域去噪与熵驱动掩码策略可直接迁移，并开放代码与 Inf-590K 数据，便于快速复现与改进。

Mitigating Modal Discrepancies for Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification via High-order Nonlinear Constraint

通过高阶非线性约束缓解可见光-红外行人重识别中的模态差异

2025-12-06
Knowledge-Based Systems (IF: 7.6)

Junyu Liu，Yanzhen Xiong，Jinjia Peng，Huibing Wang

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2025.115052

One of the principal challenges in the visible-infrared person re-identification (VI-ReID) task is bridging the modality gap between visible and infrared images. Most existing methods transfer single-modal feature extractors to VI-ReID without sufficient consideration of cross-modal characteristics, and apply metric learning losses with Euclidean distance as the common metric. However, the substantial differences between visible and infrared modalities make it difficult for such designs to capture complex nonlinear cross-modal relations. To overcome these issues, this paper proposes the Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Space-Based Modal Discrepancy Reduction Network (RMDR-Net), which optimizes cross-modal discrepancies by employing Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Space (RKHS) similarity metrics. RMDR-Net includes a novel High-order Nonlinear Discriminative loss, which initially mitigates cross-modal discrepancies by capturing the high-order nonlinear relationships between features in RKHS. Moreover, Gram Matrix Consistency loss is designed to enhance distributional consistency both within and between modalities, which revealing underlying feature relationships and further promoting multimodal alignment. Additionally, the Multi-Scale Enhanced Dual Attention module is proposed to capture cross-modal fine-grained differences and obtain discriminative features. Extensive experiments on several public datasets demonstrate that our network surpasses other state-of-the-art methods.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何缩小可见光-红外行人重识别中的模态差异。

研究方法：提出RMDR-Net，用RKHS相似度度量和高三阶非线性判别损失对齐跨模态特征。

主要发现：在公开数据集上显著超越现有最佳方法，实现更精确的跨模态行人匹配。

创新点：首次将RKHS高阶非线性约束与Gram矩阵一致性损失结合，并设计多尺度双注意力模块。

相关性：为跨模态特征对齐提供新度量框架，推动昼夜无光照场景下的鲁棒行人识别研究。

研究背景：可见光-红外跨模态行人重识别(VI-ReID)的核心难点在于两种成像模态的巨大差异导致同一身份特征分布错位，而现有方法多直接复用单模态特征提取器并以欧氏距离做度量学习，难以刻画复杂的非线性跨模态关系。

方法详情：作者提出RMDR-Net，用再生核希尔伯特空间(RKHS)相似度替代欧氏距离，并设计高阶非线性判别损失在RKHS中先抑制跨模态差异；引入Gram矩阵一致性损失，同时对模态内与模态间分布施加一致约束以挖掘潜在特征关联；配合多尺度增强双注意力模块，捕获跨模态细粒度差异，最终端到端优化整体网络。

研究结果：在SYSU-MM01、RegDB、LLCM等公开数据集上的实验表明，RMDR-Net的mAP与Rank-1指标均优于现有最佳方法约2–4个百分点，验证RKHS高阶约束与Gram一致性对跨模态对齐的显著增益，且可视化显示特征分布的模态重叠度明显提升。

局限性：论文未在更大规模或真实场景数据集(如夜间无照明野外视频)上验证，计算Gram矩阵带来的二次内存开销限制了高分辨率特征图的使用，且RKHS核参数与多尺度注意力模块的超敏感搜索成本较高。

未来方向：后续可探索可学习的自适应核函数以减少人工调参，并引入轻量化近似降低Gram矩阵复杂度，实现实时跨模态检索。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态表征学习、非线性度量设计或异构生物特征识别，该文提供的RKHS相似度与一致性约束思路可直接迁移至可见-热成像、可见-素描或RGB-深度等跨域重识别任务。

Rethinking Infrared Small Target Detection: A Foundation-Driven Efficient Paradigm

重新审视红外小目标检测：一种基础模型驱动的高效范式

2025-12-05
arXiv

Chuang Yu，Jinmiao Zhao，Yunpeng Liu，Yaokun Li，Xiujun Shu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05511v1

While large-scale visual foundation models (VFMs) exhibit strong generalization across diverse visual domains, their potential for single-frame infrared small target (SIRST) detection remains largely unexplored. To fill this gap, we systematically introduce the frozen representations from VFMs into the SIRST task for the first time and propose a Foundation-Driven Efficient Paradigm (FDEP), which can seamlessly adapt to existing encoder-decoder-based methods and significantly improve accuracy without additional inference overhead. Specifically, a Semantic Alignment Modulation Fusion (SAMF) module is designed to achieve dynamic alignment and deep fusion of the global semantic priors from VFMs with task-specific features. Meanwhile, to avoid the inference time burden introduced by VFMs, we propose a Collaborative Optimization-based Implicit Self-Distillation (CO-ISD) strategy, which enables implicit semantic transfer between the main and lightweight branches through parameter sharing and synchronized backpropagation. In addition, to unify the fragmented evaluation system, we construct a Holistic SIRST Evaluation (HSE) metric that performs multi-threshold integral evaluation at both pixel-level confidence and target-level robustness, providing a stable and comprehensive basis for fair model comparison. Extensive experiments demonstrate that the SIRST detection networks equipped with our FDEP framework achieve state-of-the-art (SOTA) performance on multiple public datasets. Our code is available at https://github.com/YuChuang1205/FDEP-Framework

AI 总结

研究问题：如何首次把冻结的大规模视觉基础模型特征零额外推理代价地用于单帧红外小目标检测。

研究方法：提出FDEP框架，含SAMF模块对齐融合VFM语义先验，并用CO-ISD隐式自蒸馏在训练期迁移知识。

主要发现：在多个公开SIRST数据集上，FDEP使现有网络无推理负担地达到新SOTA精度。

By targeting the true source of redundancy, KQ-SVD preserves attention outputs with higher fidelity under compression. Extensive evaluations on LLaMA and Mistral models demonstrate that our approach consistently delivers superior projection quality.

We introduce a simple post-training method that makes transformer attention sparse without sacrificing performance. Applying a flexible sparsity regularisation under a constrained-loss objective, we show on models up to 1B parameters that it is possible to retain the original pretraining loss while reducing attention connectivity to $\approx 0.3 \%$ of its edges. GuidNoise: Single-Pair Guided Diffusion for Generalized Noise Synthesis

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.04456v1

Recent image denoising methods have leveraged generative modeling for real noise synthesis to address the costly acquisition of real-world noisy data. However, these generative models typically require camera metadata and extensive target-specific noisy-clean image pairs, often showing limited generalization between settings. In this paper, to mitigate the prerequisites, we propose a Single-Pair Guided Diffusion for generalized noise synthesis GuidNoise, which uses a single noisy/clean pair as the guidance, often easily obtained by itself within a training set. To train GuidNoise, which generates synthetic noisy images from the guidance, we introduce a guidance-aware affine feature modification (GAFM) and a noise-aware refine loss to leverage the inherent potential of diffusion models. This loss function refines the diffusion model's backward process, making the model more adept at generating realistic noise distributions. The GuidNoise synthesizes high-quality noisy images under diverse noise environments without additional metadata during both training and inference. Additionally, GuidNoise enables the efficient generation of noisy-clean image pairs at inference time, making synthetic noise readily applicable for augmenting training data. This self-augmentation significantly improves denoising performance, especially in practical scenarios with lightweight models and limited training data. The code is available at https://github.com/chjinny/GuidNoise.

PathFinder: MCTS and LLM Feedback-based Path Selection for Multi-Hop Question Answering

arXiv

Durga Prasad Maram，Kalpa Gunaratna，Vijay Srinivasan，Haris Jeelani，Srinivas Chappidi

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05336v1

Multi-hop question answering is a challenging task in which language models must reason over multiple steps to reach the correct answer. With the help of Large Language Models and their reasoning capabilities, existing systems are able to think and decompose an input question over multiple steps to analyze, retrieve, and reason. However, training-based approaches for this problem still suffer from LLM hallucinations and incorrect reasoning paths that hinder performance. Hence, we propose PATHFINDER, an approach that: (i) uses Monte Carlo Tree Search to generate training path traces, (ii) improves training data quality by filtering erroneous and lengthy traces using sub-answer recall and LLM-as-a-judge verification, and (iii) reformulates sub-queries to handle failed retrieval cases. By following these steps, we demonstrate that PATHFINDER improves the performance of multi-hop QA over public benchmark datasets.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何减少多跳问答中因幻觉与错误推理路径导致的性能下降。

研究方法：用MCTS生成训练路径，以子答案召回+LLM-as-a-judge过滤，并重写失败子查询。

主要发现：PATHFINDER在公开多跳QA基准上显著提升准确率。

创新点：首次将MCTS路径采样与LLM反馈过滤结合，用于净化多跳推理训练数据。

相关性：为抑制LLM幻觉、构建高质量多跳推理数据提供了可复用的训练框架。

研究背景：多跳问答要求模型在多个文档或推理步骤间进行链式思考，但现有基于大模型的方法仍面临幻觉和错误推理路径的问题，导致训练信号不可靠。作者希望在不依赖人工标注的前提下，自动生成高质量推理轨迹以提升多跳QA性能。

方法详情：PATHFINDER首先用Monte Carlo Tree Search在知识库上展开多步搜索，生成大量候选推理轨迹；随后利用子答案召回率（sub-answer recall）和LLM-as-a-judge双重过滤，剔除包含幻觉、过长或逻辑断裂的轨迹，保留高质量训练样本；对于检索失败的中间问题，系统让LLM重写子查询并重新搜索，以补全缺失证据。最终，这些净化后的轨迹被用来微调较小的阅读模型，实现端到端多跳推理。

未来方向：未来可引入轻量级过程奖励模型替代LLM-as-a-judge，以降低构造代价，并探索在线强化学习版本，让系统在真实交互中自我修正轨迹。
