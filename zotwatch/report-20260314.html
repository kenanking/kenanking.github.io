PolSAR-Tokenized Foundation Model for Robust Target Recognition

面向稳健目标识别的PolSAR标记化基础模型

2026-03-12
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Fan Gao，Qingjun Zhang，Dawei Ren，Xunhao Lin，Junjun Yin 等

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3673107

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) Automatic Target Recognition (ATR) is critical for all-weather monitoring and has been widely used. Polarimetric information offers rich electromagnetic scattering signatures that are vital for characterizing complex target structures. However, current methods predominantly rely on single-polarization data due to the scarcity of paired polarimetric datasets for ATR. Although the recently released ATRNet-STAR fills this gap and existing studies have achieved promising results, we identify a critical data leakage risk in the benchmark and establish a stricter evaluation protocol. To capitalize on large-scale single-polarization datasets while exploiting the polarimetric richness, we pre-train a DINOv2 backbone on SARDet-186K and adapt this foundation model on ATRNet-STAR into a ChannelViT architecture, which processes polarimetric channels as distinct token sequences. Furthermore, we introduce a sensor-mode-aware masking strategy, yielding a unified model adaptable to single, dual, and full-polarization inputs. Experiments demonstrate that our method achieves state-of- the-art accuracy while maintaining robustness across varying sensor modes.

研究问题：如何在极化SAR稀缺且存在数据泄露的情况下，实现鲁棒的全天候目标识别。

相关性：为PolSAR-ATR提供可扩展基础模型与严格评测基准，推动全天候监测应用。

方法详情：作者先在单极化检测数据集SARDet-186K上用DINOv2自监督预训练ViT骨干，再把该基础模型迁移到ATRNet-STAR；提出ChannelViT，将HH、HV、VH、VV四个极化通道分别编码为独立token序列并行输入Transformer；引入传感器模式感知遮蔽策略，在训练时随机丢弃通道以统一支持单、双、全极化输入；整体框架无需额外极化标注即可利用大规模单极化知识。

未来方向：未来可在更多极化SAR数据集上验证并引入跨域评估，同时探索通道token融合策略以降低计算量并提升效率。

Glance-Focus-Gaze: A novel Eagle-Eye Vision-Inspired Panorama-Population-Individual progressive screening paradigm to capture ships in SAR images

Glance-Focus-Gaze：一种受鹰眼视觉启发的全景-群体-个体渐进式筛选新范式，用于SAR图像中的船舶捕获

2026-03-12
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Tianwen Zhang，Gui Gao，Xiaoling Zhang

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.03.001

It is challenging to directly capture ships in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) remote sensing images, which often leads to inaccurate positioning and missed detections of low-scattering targets. To address this, inspired by eagle-eye vision (EEV), we propose a panorama-population-individual (PPI) progressive screening paradigm, termed glance-focus-gaze (GFG), for promising SAR ship detection. The ship capture task is decomposed into three phases, i.e., glance panorama, focus population, and gaze individual. Glance panorama refers to promptly previewing the entire panoramic image to establish spatial relationship dependencies, yielding preliminary coarse-grained integrated global perception. Focus population refers to narrowing the attention from the panorama to the population subregion where ships are gathered. Based on the recommendation strength derived from the previously perceived panoramic view, it explores the spatial correlation patterns among ships. Gaze individual refers to attentively and exquisitely catching each ship individual within its corresponding population. With attention shifting from the preceding population to the individual, it accomplishes precise capture of each ship. An upstream GFG branch is designed to implement the above phases. Additionally, we also propose another one downstream single outlier-specific shot (SOSS) branch for individual direct detection. It is instructed by a dynamic population-awareness orchestration (DPAO) from the upstream GFG branch to capture ships that are not in any population. Finally, GFG and SOSS are integrated successfully to form complementarity, allowing for a dual pathway synergistic transcendence (DPST). Results on the SSDD and HRSID datasets show the effectiveness of the proposed solution. On SSDD, DPST achieves an 78.0% AP, which is 1.8% higher than the second-best M 2 S-DETR. On HRSID, it achieves an 76.3% AP, which is 3.5% higher than the second-best EDHC. The SSDD dataset can be available at https://github.com/TianwenZhang0825/Official-SSDD, and the HRSID dataset can be available at https://github.com/chaozhong2010/HRSID. The code can be available at https://github.com/TianwenZhang0825/GFG.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决SAR图像中低散射舰船漏检与定位不准问题

研究方法：仿鹰眼“扫视-聚焦-凝视”三阶段渐进筛选，结合单异常分支与动态群体感知协同检测

主要发现：SSDD/HRSID上AP达78.0%/76.3%，分别领先第二名1.8%与3.5%

创新点：提出全景-群体-个体PPI范式与双路径协同超越DPST架构

相关性：为复杂SAR舰船检测提供高精度、低漏检新思路，可推广至小目标遥感任务

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)船舶检测常因低散射目标与复杂海杂波并存，导致定位不准与漏检。传统端到端检测器一次性回归框，难以兼顾全局上下文与局部细节。作者受鹰眼扫视-聚焦-凝视行为启发，提出渐进式筛选思路，将大场景船舶捕获任务分解为全景-群体-个体三级感知。

方法详情：论文设计Glance-Focus-Gaze(GFG)上游支路：Glance阶段用轻量主干快速生成全图粗粒度特征并构建空间关系依赖；Focus阶段依据全景推荐强度，将注意力收缩至船舶聚集的群体子区域，学习舰间空间关联；Gaze阶段在群体特征上精细捕捉每艘船，实现个体级精确定位。并行引入Single Outlier-Specific Shot(SOSS)下游支路，对未被群体推荐的孤立目标做一步检测，其搜索范围由动态群体感知调度(DPAO)从GFG分支在线生成掩码引导。GFG与SOSS通过双路径协同超越(DPST)框架互补融合，输出最终检测结果。

研究结果：在SSDD与HRSID两大公开SAR船舶数据集上，DPST分别取得78.0%与76.3%的AP，较第二名方法提升1.8和3.5个百分点，验证渐进式范式对低散射与小目标的有效性。消融实验显示，移除Gaze或SOSS任一模块均导致AP下降3–5%，证明双路径互补必要。可视化表明，GFG能显著抑制虚警并召回密集停泊舰只，SOSS则补全离岸孤立小船。

局限性：方法采用三阶段级联推理，整体参数量与计算开销高于单阶段检测器，实时性受限。DPAO掩码生成依赖群体推荐阈值，极端稀疏场景下可能失效并引入漏检。论文未在更大尺寸影像(如万×万像素)与多极化、多波段SAR数据上验证，泛化能力待考。

未来方向：后续可引入轻量化共享主干与知识蒸馏，将三阶段压缩为端到端可训练网络，提升实时性；同时探索可学习阈值与自监督预训练，使DPAO自适应不同散射强度与成像参数。

研究相关性：对于研究SAR目标检测、小目标识别、生物视觉启发网络或渐进式检测范式的学者，本文提供了可复现的代码与完整实验设置，可直接作为基准或扩展至多类遥感目标提取任务。

Mask-Supervised Dual-Branch Detection Network for Oriented SAR Ship Detection

面向有向SAR舰船检测的掩码监督双分支检测网络

2026-03-12
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Zhengheng Liu，Yong Wang，Mengyu Li，Yiqun Zou

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3673209

Oriented ship detection in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images is widely used for maritime surveillance. However, existing methods suffer from two issues: 1) the feature enhancement process lacks explicit supervision, making it difficult to reliably enhance object features; and 2) only a prediction is generated for each proposal, which may cause missed detections. To address these two issues, we propose a mask-supervised dual-branch detection network (MSDD-Net), which integrates a mask-supervised feature enhancement (MSFE) module and a dual-branch detection head. In the MSFE module, multi-level features are first fused to generate a saliency map, and then this saliency map is explicitly guided to focus on the object regions by joint supervision of an object-aware mask and a center decay mask, with the aim of obtaining enhanced object features (i.e., addressing the first issue). Afterward, the enhanced object features are processed by a region proposal network to output multiple proposals. In addition, the dual-branch detection head generates two predictions for each proposal and matches each proposal with two ground-truth (GT) bounding boxes. Finally, a triplet loss is designed to improve the consistency between two predictions and their corresponding GT bounding boxes and the discrimination between two predictions, with the aim of reducing missed detections (i.e., addressing the second issue). Extensive experiments demonstrate the superiority of MSDD-Net over state-of-the-art methods.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决SAR图像有向舰船检测中特征增强无监督与单预测易漏检的问题。

研究方法：提出MSDD-Net，含掩膜监督特征增强模块和双分支检测头，并设计三元组损失。

主要发现：在公开SAR舰船数据集上取得优于现有方法的检测精度与鲁棒性。

创新点：首次引入对象-中心双掩膜显式监督特征增强，并为每候选框并行双预测配GT加三元组约束。

相关性：为海事监视提供高可靠SAR舰船检测新框架，可推广至其他小目标旋转检测任务。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像中的旋转舰船检测是海上监视的核心任务，但现有深度检测器在特征增强阶段缺乏显式监督，难以可靠突出目标；同时单分支检测头对每个候选框仅给出一组预测，易因匹配失败造成漏检。
