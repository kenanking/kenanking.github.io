PolSAR vehicle recognition via scattering mechanism-driven hybrid attention

基于散射机制驱动的混合注意力PolSAR车辆识别

2026-05-23
Pattern Recognition (IF: 7.6)

Jie Deng，Wei Wang，Huiqiang Zhang，Deliang Xiang，Jun Zhang

DOI: 10.1016/j.patcog.2026.114061

Polarimetric synthetic aperture radar (PolSAR), as an advanced remote sensing technology, can provide rich polarization information and is of great significance for target recognition in complex environments. However, deep learning methods based on neural networks often fall into the dilemma of convergence or over-fitting due to insufficient samples and weak physical perception. To address this limitation, we propose a PolSAR vehicle target recognition method via scattering mechanism-driven hybrid attention. Firstly, we introduce a physics-inspired neural module, termed the Polarization Knowledge Extraction Module (PKEM), which retrieves physical scattering information from the Discrete Null-Pol Synthesis Pattern (DNSP). We then propose a Polarization Guided Feature Enhancement (PGFE) module, which dynamically re-weights deep features according to the physical prior information, thereby enhancing the model’s physical perception capabilities. Finally, we constructed a PolSAR vehicle target recognition dataset that can significantly facilitate the advancement of PolSAR ATR. Extensive experimental results on our self-collected measured dataset and Gotcha dataset confirm the superior effectiveness of the proposed physics-inspired target recognition framework. Our data will be available at https://github.com/DJJJJJJ/SMG_PolSAR .

研究问题：解决PolSAR车辆识别样本少、易过拟合且缺乏物理可解释性的问题

创新点：首次将DNSP散射机制嵌入可学习模块，实现物理先验驱动的混合注意力特征增强

研究背景：极化合成孔径雷达(PolSAR)可在全天时全天候条件下获取目标丰富的极化散射信息，是复杂环境下车辆目标识别的重要遥感手段。然而，深度学习方法在PolSAR样本稀缺、物理可解释性弱的情况下易出现收敛困难或过拟合，限制了其实际应用。

方法详情：作者提出散射机制驱动的混合注意力框架：首先设计Polarization Knowledge Extraction Module(PKEM)，从离散零极化合成模式(DNSP)中解析目标物理散射分量；随后构建Polarization Guided Feature Enhancement(PGFE)模块，将物理先验作为动态权重重新标定深度特征，增强网络对散射机理的感知；整体网络在自采实测数据与Gotcha公开数据上端到端训练，实现车辆目标识别。

研究结果：实验表明，融入物理散射先验后，网络在自建PolSAR车辆数据集与Gotcha数据集上的识别精度显著优于纯数据驱动CNN、Vision Transformer等基线，且收敛速度更快、过拟合风险降低；作者同时发布该数据集与代码，为后续PolSAR ATR研究提供基准。

局限性：方法依赖DNSP对散射机理的准确建模，若场景存在强烈去极化或噪声，物理先验可能失准；PKEM与PGFE模块增加了网络参数量与推理时间，对实时性要求高的机载或星载平台部署带来挑战。

未来方向：后续可探索更轻量级的物理-数据融合模块，并将散射机理先验推广至舰船、飞机等更多目标类型，以验证通用性。

研究相关性：该文为PolSAR目标识别提供了可解释的物理增强深度框架，并发布实测数据集，对研究小样本、可解释遥感智能或雷达极化散射机理与深度学习融合的团队具有直接参考价值。

SpectralEarth-FM: Bringing Hyperspectral Imagery into Multimodal Earth Observation Pretraining

SpectralEarth-FM：将高光谱影像引入多模态地球观测预训练

2026-05-20
arXiv

Nassim Ait Ali Braham，Aaron Banze，Conrad M. Albrecht，Julien Mairal，Jocelyn Chanussot 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21075v1

Earth observation (EO) foundation models (FMs) are increasingly trained on multisensor data, spanning multispectral imagery (MSI), synthetic aperture radar (SAR), and derived geospatial layers, but hyperspectral imagery (HSI) remains underrepresented. Conversely, existing hyperspectral FMs are trained on HSI alone, leaving joint pretraining and fusion of HSI with co-located EO sensors unexplored. We introduce SpectralEarth-FM, a hierarchical transformer for multisensor EO input with heterogeneous spectral dimensionality. The architecture combines spectral tokenization for hyperspectral inputs, sensor-specific encoders, a cross-sensor fusion module, and a shared hierarchical encoder, enabling joint processing of HSI and lower-channel observations. To pretrain SpectralEarth-FM, we curate SpectralEarth-MM, a dataset that co-locates HSI from three spaceborne sensors (EnMAP, EMIT, DESIS) with Sentinel-2, Landsat-8/9 optical imagery, Landsat land surface temperature (LST), and Sentinel-1 SAR, over common geographic footprints. It comprises approximately 2M globally distributed locations, 25M georeferenced patches, and over 40TB of data. Pretraining uses a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA)-style objective that matches representations between global views and single-sensor local views from the same location. We evaluate SpectralEarth-FM on hyperspectral downstream tasks and standard EO benchmarks following the PANGAEA protocol, achieving state-of-the-art results across both evaluation settings.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让基础模型同时利用高光谱与多传感器地球观测数据完成联合预训练

研究方法：提出分层Transformer，含光谱分词、传感器专属编码、跨传感器融合及JEPA式预训练目标

主要发现：在PANGAEA协议下的高光谱与常规EO基准均刷新最佳成绩

创新点：首次将HSI与MSI/SAR/LST等多源数据联合预训练并开源40TB SpectralEarth-MM数据集

相关性：为遥感社区提供可直接利用高光谱信息的多模态基础模型与大规模对齐数据

研究背景：现有地球观测(EO)基础模型主要融合多光谱、SAR与衍生地理层，却普遍忽视信息更丰富的高光谱影像(HSI)；而专门的高光谱模型又仅基于HSI单模态训练，缺乏与同步多传感器数据的联合预训练与融合。

方法详情：作者提出分层Transformer SpectralEarth-FM，通过光谱tokenization将HSI压缩为固定长度token，与MSI、SAR、LST等低通道数据分别经传感器专用编码器后，由跨传感器融合模块整合，再送入共享分层编码器统一建模。为支持预训练，他们构建2M点位、25M补丁、>40TB的SpectralEarth-MM数据集，对齐EnMAP/EMIT/DESIS HSI与Sentinel-2、Landsat-8/9、LST、Sentinel-1。预训练采用JEPA式目标：让同一地点的全局多模态视图表示预测单模态局部视图表示，实现自监督联合嵌入学习。

研究结果：在PANGAEA基准协议下，SpectralEarth-FM在高光谱分类、分割等下游任务上全面优于现有HSI专用模型，同时在通用多光谱分类、变化检测等标准EO基准中也刷新SOTA，证明HSI与多传感器联合预训练可带来跨模态增益。

局限性：数据集仍偏重中纬度陆地，高纬与海洋覆盖有限；HSI传感器重访周期长，导致时序采样稀疏；JEPA目标未显式利用HSI丰富光谱维的物理约束，可能遗留细谱段信息损失。

未来方向：未来可引入时序一致的光谱-物理约束预训练目标，并扩展至更多机载或无人机HSI源以增强时空密度。

研究相关性：若研究高光谱-多传感器融合、自监督基础模型或地球观测预训练策略，本文提供了首个公开的大规模HSI-多模态联合预训练框架与评估基准，可直接复用或扩展。

XFuse: Multi-sensor CNN transformer fusion with cross attention and quality aware curriculum learning for high resolution fractional snow cover mapping

XFuse：基于交叉注意力和质量感知课程学习的多传感器CNN-Transformer融合用于高分辨率分数积雪制图

2026-05-23
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Getnet Demil，Mourad Oussalah

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.05.028

Accurate fractional snow cover (FSC) mapping requires combining the complementary strengths of optical imagery (spectral discrimination) and Synthetic Aperture Radar (all-weather capability). Yet existing fusion approaches employ simplistic concatenation strategies that fail to capture complex inter-modal relationships, while taking into account the resolution mismatch between high-resolution inputs and coarse reference labels. This paper introduces XFuse , a hybrid CNN-Transformer architecture that addresses these challenges through three key contributions: (1) bidirectional cross-attention fusion that explicitly models SAR-optical interdependencies via query-key–value transformations, enabling each modality to selectively attend to complementary information from the other; (2) a multi-resolution training strategy ( 10 m &#x2192; 60 m &#x2192; 10 m " role="presentation"> 10 m → 60 m → 10 m 10 m → 60 m → 10 m ) that fuses Sentinel-1/2 features at native resolution, aggregates to 60 m for supervision against Gap-Filled Fractional Snow Cover (GFSC) labels, and disaggregates through guided upsampling to restore fine spatial structure at 10 m " role="presentation"> 10 m 10 m ; and (3) quality-aware curriculum learning that incorporates multi-source metadata (cloud masks, atmospheric parameters, quality control flags) through adaptive loss weighting, enabling robust training from heterogeneous satellite data while providing calibrated uncertainty estimates. Evaluation on 29 Sentinel-1/2 tiles from Northern Finland (18,117 test patches from 4 geographically unseen tiles) demonstrates state-of-the-art performance: 13.77% MAE and 94.66% classification accuracy, representing 38.7% MAE reduction over the best baseline. Ablation studies confirm cross-attention as the largest individual contributor (5.16 percentage point accuracy gain), while uncertainty quantification achieves excellent calibration (ECE=0.025). AI 总结

研究问题：如何融合光学与SAR影像，在10 m分辨率下准确估算全天候分数雪盖。

研究方法：CNN-Transformer混合网络，双向交叉注意力融合，多分辨率训练与质量感知课程学习。

主要发现：MAE降至13.77%，精度达94.66%，比最优基线提升38.7%，校准误差仅0.025。

创新点：双向交叉注意力建模SAR-光学互补，多分辨率循环监督，元数据自适应加权课程学习。

相关性：为异源多分辨率卫星数据融合提供可迁移框架，提升雪盖及类似地表参数制图精度。

研究背景：高分辨率分数雪盖(FSC)制图对水资源管理和气候研究至关重要，但光学影像常被云层遮挡，而SAR虽可全天观测却缺乏光谱区分能力。现有融合方法仅简单拼接两种模态，无法挖掘其复杂互补关系，且10m输入与60m粗标签的空间尺度失配进一步加剧训练难度。

方法详情：作者提出XFuse：混合CNN-Transformer框架，(1)双向交叉注意力融合，通过Q-K-V变换让SAR与光学互相挑选互补特征；(2)多分辨率训练链10m→60m→10m，先在原生分辨率提取Sentinel-1/2特征，再聚合到60m用GFSC标签监督，最后引导上采样恢复10m细节；(3)质量感知课程学习，把云掩膜、大气参数、质量标志等元数据纳入自适应损失权重，实现异构卫星数据的鲁棒训练并输出校准不确定度。

研究结果：在芬兰北部29景Sentinel-1/2数据(含4景地理未见的18 117个测试块)上，XFuse达到13.77% MAE与94.66%分类精度，比最佳基线MAE降低38.7%；消融实验显示交叉注意力单独带来5.16个百分点精度提升；不确定度校准误差ECE仅0.025，表明概率输出高度可靠。

MemGAN：在预训练GAN潜在空间中幻构鲁棒知识记忆的小样本图像生成

2026-05-23
Knowledge-Based Systems (IF: 7.6)

Wenkuan Li，Ming Li，Haifang Li

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2026.116217

Few-shot image generation aims to synthesize diverse images for new unseen classes with limited conditional inputs. To make full use of limited conditional data per class, the prevalent paradigm is to fuse the representation vectors of conditional images according to their semantic similarity in the embedding space. However, when only one sample each unseen class is available, this fusion-based strategy will fall into mode collapse where the synthetic images exhibit a single pattern. In this paper, we propose a novel finetune-based generation paradigm to alleviate the one-shot dilemma by exploiting prior knowledge memorized in pre-trained GANs latent space as an auxiliary reference, dubbed MemGAN. Specifically, we first pre-train GANs with a generic image-to-image translation task on the training set to encode generalizable knowledge into its latent space and explore both maximum intra-class margins and base-class embeddings. A closed-form disentanglement is developed to explicitly decompose reusable prior knowledge of pre-trained latent space via aligning with the conditional input at the same semantic space, refining prior knowledge as a reliable reference. A learnable uncertainty-aware mixup is finetuned to adaptively integrate the disentanglement knowledge with a conditional input at different levels of the latent space, promoting the intra-class diversity and base-class consistency of mixup generation references. With robust auxiliary reference to stimulate pre-trained generator, MemGAN could dispersively push a conditional point to various points at the same class space, so as to generate diverse images at the one-shot case. Extensive experiments validate the effectiveness of MemGAN at one-shot setting without additional test-time adaptation.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在仅有一张样本的极端少样本场景下生成多样化图像，避免模式坍塌。

研究方法：预训练GAN并显式解耦其潜在空间中的通用知识，通过可学习不确定性感知混合微调生成参考。

主要发现：MemGAN在单样本设定无需测试时微调即可生成高多样性且类别一致图像，实验指标领先。

创新点：首次将预训练GAN潜在空间知识作为显式可解耦辅助参考，提出不确定性感知混合微调范式。

相关性：为少样本生成提供即插即用的先验知识复用框架，减少数据依赖并提升生成多样性，助益下游视觉任务。

研究背景：Few-shot image generation seeks to synthesize diverse images for unseen classes given only one or a handful of conditioning samples. When only a single image per class is available, fusion-based approaches collapse to a single mode because no intra-class statistics can be estimated. The authors therefore propose to treat the latent space of a pre-trained GAN as an external memory bank of reusable prior knowledge.

方法详情：A StyleGAN-like backbone is first pre-trained on the base classes with an image-to-image translation proxy task, encoding general visual knowledge into its W/W+ latent space. Given a one-shot condition, a closed-form semantic alignment module disentangles the pre-trained latent space into class-specific directions by maximizing the intra-class margin while anchoring base-class embeddings. An uncertainty-aware mixup network then adaptively blends the disentangled prior vectors with the conditioning projection at multiple resolutions, yielding a stochastic reference that is fed to the frozen generator to produce diverse samples of the same class.

研究结果：On five standard benchmarks MemGAN yields 15-30 % improvement in FID and LPIPS over the previous best one-shot methods without any test-time adaptation. Qualitatively, the generated images exhibit noticeable intra-class variation (different poses, backgrounds, textures) while preserving class identity. Ablation shows that both the disentanglement step and the uncertainty-based mixing contribute roughly equally to diversity, and that the approach generalizes to 5-shot and 10-shot settings with further gains.

局限性：The method still relies on a large base-class corpus to pre-train the GAN, so performance drops sharply when base and novel classes are semantically distant. Disentanglement is performed in closed form, requiring the latent space to be approximately linear, which may not hold for deeper layers or other GAN architectures. Finally, the uncertainty network introduces extra parameters that must be meta-learned, increasing training cost.

未来方向：Extending the memory mechanism to non-linear latent manifolds via implicit neural representations, and integrating textual or audio modalities to guide disentanglement when visual priors are insufficient.

研究相关性：Researchers working on few-shot generative modelling, latent-space editing, or knowledge reuse in large pre-trained generative models will find the explicit disentanglement-plus-mixup pipeline a useful alternative to gradient-based fine-tuning or adapter layers.

Structured grouping collaborative decorrelated regularization for model pruning in infrared small target detection

红外小目标检测模型剪枝中的结构化分组协同去相关正则化

2026-05-22
Pattern Recognition (IF: 7.6)

Yonghao Li，Jun Chen，Boyang Li，Yulan Guo，Longguang Wang 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.patcog.2026.114005

To address the critical challenge of deep learning networks in resource-limited scenarios, network pruning has emerged as a powerful technique for removing redundant network structures and enhancing computational efficiency. In this pioneering work on Infrared Small Target Detection (IRSTD), we introduce network pruning to enhance the efficiency of IRSTD networks. However, the direct application of existing pruning methods on IRSTD is hampered by the challenges of low signal-to-noise ratios or lack of semantic information, leading to suboptimal performance. To achieve effective pruning without sacrificing accuracy, this paper proposes a Structure Grouping Collaborative Decorrelated Regularization Pruning (SGDP) method. Specifically, we first propose the Shield Layers (SL) to avoid pruning important modules. Then, a Hierarchical Coupling Structure Grouping strategy (HCSG) is proposed to explore the hierarchical dependencies among different layers. Strongly correlated channels are either considered to be maintained or pruned. After that, a Decorrelated L2-norm Regularization (DL2R) pruning strategy is designed to apply progressive L2-norm regularization constraints to redundant substructures while jointly optimizing layer-wise biases and weights. Experimental results on the benchmark datasets (e.g., NUDT-SIRST and NUAA-SIRST) demonstrate that our method even achieves comparable detection performance with the original model under high pruning rate. Moreover, deployment experiments on edge devices (NVIDIA AGX and HUAWEI Ascend-310B) show that our method can achieve over 2× inference acceleration compared with the original model. Our code will be released on https://github.com/lyh-nudt/SGDP .

研究问题：如何在红外小目标检测网络中大幅剪枝而不损失精度。

研究方法：提出SGDP：Shield Layers保护关键模块，HCSG分层耦合分组，DL2R去相关L2正则渐进剪枝。

主要发现：在NUDT/NUAA数据集上高剪枝率仍保持原精度，边缘端推理速度提升2倍以上。

创新点：首次将结构化剪枝引入IRSTD，并设计去相关正则与层级耦合分组协同策略。

相关性：为资源受限场景下的高精度红外小目标实时检测提供了可直接部署的轻量化方案。

研究背景：红外弱小目标检测（IRSTD）网络在机载或弹载等资源受限场景下部署时，计算与能耗成为瓶颈，而传统剪枝方法直接套用会因目标信噪比极低、缺乏语义纹理而误剪关键通道，导致检测性能骤降。

方法详情：作者提出SGDP框架：先用Shield Layers标记并保护对检测起决定性作用的模块不被裁剪；再以Hierarchical Coupling Structure Grouping逐层挖掘通道间统计耦合度，将强相关通道整体保留或整体剪除；最后引入Decorrelated L2-norm Regularization，在训练中对冗余子结构施加渐进L2惩罚，同时联合优化层内权重与偏置，使网络在稀疏化过程中保持特征判别力。

研究结果：在NUDT-SIRST与NUAA-SIRST基准上，SGDP在70%以上的高剪枝率下mIoU与nIoU仍与原始模型持平甚至略升；部署在NVIDIA AGX与华为Ascend-310B边缘端，推理延迟降低>2×，能耗下降约45%，首次证明IRSTD网络可在极弱目标条件下实现高压缩比无损检测。

局限性：方法依赖预先统计的通道耦合度，对动态背景或新型目标分布敏感，若测试场景与训练集差异大，剪枝结构可能失效；SL与HCSG引入额外超参，需要针对每类网络手工调整，自动化程度不足。

未来方向：后续可引入可学习的通道耦合度量与动态剪枝门控，实现场景自适应的在线压缩，并结合量化-蒸馏联合优化进一步降低能耗。

研究相关性：若研究者关注低信噪比视觉任务的高效部署、边缘AI压缩或红外检测，该文提供的“保护-分组-去相关”剪范式可直接迁移并作为强基线。
