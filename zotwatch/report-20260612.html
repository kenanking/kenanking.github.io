Earth-OneVision: Extending Remote Sensing Multimodal Large Language Models to More Sensor Modalities and Tasks

Earth-OneVision：将遥感多模态大型语言模型扩展到更多传感器模态和任务

2026-06-09
arXiv

Miaoxin Cai，Guanqun Wang，Wei Zhang，Guangyao Zhou，Yin Zhuang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10819v1

RS-MLLMs enable natural-language understanding and spatial reasoning over earth observation imagery. However, existing models support only a narrow range of sensor types and tasks, yielding a fragmented view of the earth and leaving cross-modal geoscientific knowledge largely unexploited. This work presents Earth-OneVision, a 2B RS-MLLM that unifies six sensor modalities (i.e., optical, SAR, infrared, multispectral, temporal, and video) and cross-sensor fusion across 9 task categories within a single autoregressive framework. Three dedicated mechanisms address three bottlenecks. Full-Granularity Vision-Language Alignment (FGVLA) aligns multi-level visual features with the multi-dimensional language space. Spatial-Linguistic Isomorphic Serialization (SLIS) unifies heterogeneous spatial outputs as autoregressive tokens. Progressive Cross-Modality Adaptation (PCMA) decomposes the compound domain gap into sequential stages, tackling the viewpoint and imaging physics gaps in turn. To support joint training, MMRS-OneVision is constructed with ~34M QA pairs spanning all six sensor modalities and cross-sensor fusion across 9 task categories, substantially exceeding existing RS multimodal instruction datasets. With only 2B parameters, Earth-OneVision achieves competitive or state-of-the-art results across extensive benchmarks, consistently matching or outperforming 4B-72B RS-MLLMs. It achieves 87.52% P@0.5 on the OPT-RSVG testset for optical visual grounding and 80.68% on the SAR VQA benchmark SARLANG-Bench, exceeding 7B models by over 7%. It further achieves 75.74% recall on the BigEarthNet-MS testset for multispectral classification, and 81.94% MCQ accuracy on EarthMind-Bench for cross-modality reasoning.

研究问题：解决现有遥感多模态大语言模型支持的传感器类型和任务范围狭窄、跨模态知识未被充分利用的问题。

研究方法：提出Earth-OneVision，采用全粒度视觉语言对齐、空间语言同构序列化和渐进式跨模态适应三个机制。

主要发现：仅2B参数模型在光学视觉定位、SAR问答等多基准上达到或超越4B-72B模型，取得SOTA结果。

创新点：首次在单一自回归框架中统一六种传感器模态和九类任务，并设计专用机制解决领域差距瓶颈。

相关性：为遥感多模态大语言模型向更多传感器和任务扩展提供了系统性的方法与数据集支持。

研究背景：现有遥感多模态大语言模型（RS-MLLMs）仅支持有限的传感器类型和任务，导致地球观测视角碎片化，未能充分利用跨模态地理科学知识。本研究旨在通过统一六种传感器模态（光学、SAR、红外、多光谱、时序和视频）及九类任务，解决这一局限性。

方法详情：提出Earth-OneVision，一个2B参数的RS-MLLM，基于自回归框架集成六种传感器模态和跨传感器融合。设计了三个关键机制：全粒度视觉语言对齐（FGVLA）对齐多级视觉特征与多维语言空间；空间语言同构序列化（SLIS）将异构空间输出统一为自回归令牌；渐进跨模态适应（PCMA）通过分阶段处理视角和成像物理差异来分解复合域差距。同时构建了包含约3400万QA对的MMRS-OneVision数据集，覆盖所有模态和九类任务。

研究结果：Earth-OneVision在多个基准上取得竞争力或最先进结果：光学视觉定位任务OPT-RSVG上达到87.52% P@0.5，SAR视觉问答SARLANG-Bench上80.68%准确率，超过7B模型7%以上；多光谱分类BigEarthNet-MS上75.74%召回率，跨模态推理EarthMind-Bench上81.94%多选题准确率。这些结果证明2B参数模型能够匹配甚至超越4B-72B规模的RS-MLLMs。

局限性：模型参数规模（2B）可能限制复杂跨模态推理能力，尤其在需要深度几何或物理理解的场景中。数据集尽管庞大，但不同传感器模态和任务的样本分布可能存在不均，影响泛化；此外，未评估对实时或高噪声数据时的鲁棒性。

未来方向：未来可扩展至更多传感器类型（如激光雷达、高光谱）和下游任务（如灾害监测、气候变化分析），并探索更高效的微调策略或多语言支持以增强地理通用性。

研究相关性：该论文提出统一多传感器、多任务的框架，对研究遥感多模态大模型、跨模态视觉-语言理解、空间推理及指令调优的研究者具有重要参考价值，其数据集和方法可复用于相关方向。

VI-MSFFN: A Visible-Infrared Multi-Scale Feature Fusion Network for Cross-Modal Detection in Remote Sensing

VI-MSFFN：面向遥感跨模态检测的可见光-红外多尺度特征融合网络

2026-06-11
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Yurong Yue，Weiwei Qin，Hao Chi，Baiwei An，Dingyi Wu 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18121938

To address the issues of insufficient single-modality robustness and limited multi-scale object detection accuracy in remote sensing image detection (RSID) in complex environments, this paper proposes a multimodal RSID network named VI-MSFFN. The model adopts a symmetric parallel dual-branch architecture to achieve independent extraction and collaborative modeling of visible and infrared modal features. A cross-modal multi-scale sparse cross-attention fusion module is proposed and applied to the P4 and P5 feature layers, and a high-low-level feature collaborative cross-modal fusion strategy was constructed to achieve efficient and robust cross-modal feature fusion while enhancing multi-scale object modeling capability and suppressing feature redundancy and noise. Additionally, a progressive feature interaction and fusion architecture was designed to combine spatial and frequency domain information to strengthen deep object representation. The experimental results on the VEDAI and Drone Vehicle datasets demonstrate that VI-MSFFN achieves state-of-the-art (SOTA) performance in detection accuracy, robustness, and generalization ability. The proposed method effectively solves the detection challenges of RSID and has significant application value in the field of multi-modal RSID.

方法详情：该模型采用对称并行双分支架构，分别独立提取可见光和红外特征，并提出跨模态多尺度稀疏交叉注意力融合模块应用于P4和P5特征层，同时构建高低级特征协同跨模态融合策略，结合空间与频域信息设计渐进式特征交互与融合架构，以增强多尺度目标建模能力并抑制特征冗余与噪声。

ZODS-RS -- Zero-training Oriented Detection & Segmentation for Remote Sensing

ZODS-RS——面向遥感任务的零训练检测与分割

2026-06-09
arXiv

Zuan Gu，Tianhan Gao，Langxu Zhao

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10769v1

Remote-sensing and UAV applications need models that generalize across platforms and viewpoints without task-specific training. Yet training-free pipelines often falter on oriented geometry, scale/rotation variation, and crowded ports or airfields, and rarely unify detection and segmentation. We introduce ZODS-RS, a training-free, closed-form pipeline that outputs horizontal boxes (HBB) and instance masks. Built on DINOv3 dense features and SAM-style proposals, ZODS-RS chains: PP (prototype purification via Tyler covariance), R-SEM (rotation-scale equivariant matching with separable kernels and global Hungarian assignment), and UAM (uncertainty-aware pixelwise merging with adaptive priors and optional negative prototypes). A lightweight CWLA fuses multiple DINOv3 layers. On FAIR1M (HBB) we obtain $\mathrm{mAP}_{0.50:0.95}=\mathbf{13.06}$ and $\mathrm{AP}_S=\mathbf{2.93}$ \emph{(class-averaged over ship/airplane)}; on xView (HBB) we report $\mathrm{mAP}=\mathbf{16.69}$. On our UAV dataset, ZODS-RS achieves mask $\mathrm{mIoU}=\mathbf{31.10}$ and improves small-object AP by $\mathbf{+30.70}$ over Grounded-SAM on a single 5090. This work offers a unified, \emph{no-training} solution for horizontal-box detection plus instance segmentation in aerial imagery; provides explicit closed-form formulations for PP/R-SEM/UAM tightly coupled with DINOv3; and demonstrates \emph{consistent} gains on small and crowded targets and under cross-domain shifts while keeping deployment simple.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决遥感图像中无需训练的通用检测与分割问题，应对方向、尺度变化和拥挤场景。

研究方法：基于DINOv3和SAM，采用原型纯化、旋转尺度匹配和不确定感知像素合并等闭式流程。

主要发现：在FAIR1M和xView上取得mAP 13.06和16.69，小目标AP较Grounded-SAM提升+30.70。

创新点：首次提出无训练的闭式统一水平框检测与实例分割流程，紧密耦合DINOv3特征。

相关性：为遥感研究提供零训练、易部署的通用解决方案，显著提升小目标和跨域场景性能。

研究背景：遥感与无人机应用需要模型能在不同平台和视角下泛化而无需特定任务训练，但现有无训练方法在处理旋转、尺度变化、拥挤场景（如港口、机场）时表现不佳，且很少统一检测与分割。本文提出ZODS-RS，一个无需训练的统一管道，同时输出水平边界框和实例掩码。

方法详情：ZODS-RS基于DINOv3密集特征和SAM风格候选区域，包含四个核心模块：原型净化（PP）使用Tyler协方差去除噪声；旋转-尺度等变匹配（R-SEM）通过可分离核和全局匈牙利分配实现鲁棒匹配；不确定性感知像素合并（UAM）结合自适应先验和可选负原型进行精细分割；以及轻量级层融合（CWLA）融合多个DINOv3特征层。所有模块均具有封闭形式，无需训练。

研究结果：在FAIR1M数据集上，ZODS-RS在水平框检测中达到mAP_{0.50:0.95}=13.06，小物体AP_S=2.93（船/飞机类别平均）；在xView上mAP=16.69。在自建无人机数据集上，掩码mIoU=31.10，小物体AP较Grounded-SAM提升+30.70。结果表明该方法在拥挤场景、小目标及跨域迁移中表现一致且稳定。

局限性：当前方法在极端遮挡或严重类间相似场景下可能仍存在误检；依赖于DINOv3和SAM的预训练特征，当基础模型特征质量下降时性能可能受限；封闭形式公式虽简洁但可能难以处理更复杂的上下文关系。

未来方向：未来工作可探索将ZODS-RS扩展到旋转边界框检测，或结合多模态数据（如红外、雷达）进一步提升鲁棒性；另外可研究自适应特征层选择策略以减少计算开销。

研究相关性：该论文为遥感图像的无训练检测与分割提供了统一解决方案，对于关注域迁移、小目标检测及训练效率的研究者具有直接参考价值，其封闭形式设计也为理论分析提供了便利。

IB-HFN: Information Bottleneck-Driven SAR-Optical Fusion Network for High-Fidelity Cloud Removal

IB-HFN：信息瓶颈驱动的SAR-光学融合网络用于高保真云去除

2026-06-08
arXiv

Haojun Guo，Fan Feng，Ziquan Wang，Yongsheng Zhang，Ying Yu

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09347v2

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR)-assisted optical cloud removal aims to recover surface information obscured by clouds in optical remote sensing images by exploiting complementary SAR observations. Existing multimodal fusion methods typically rely on direct spatial concatenation and pixel-wise supervision, which can propagate SAR speckle noise into optical reconstruction and lead to over-smoothed results. 3D human pose estimation (HPE) from monocular 2D videos faces a critical trade-off between joint position accuracy and structural plausibility. Existing methods relying on joint coordinates suffer from implausible bone lengths, while bone vector-based approaches incur error accumulation, degrading position accuracy. To address this challenge, we propose a Decouple-then-Collaborate framework that exploits the complementary strengths of joint coordinates and bone vectors. Our approach decouples the learning of the two representations—joint coordinates for positional accuracy and bone vectors for structural consistency—and then aligns their feature spaces to fuse these advantages. Specifically, we first train two separate branches, each equipped with a motion enhancement module to learn motion-aware features. A feature transfer network then maps high-precision joint features into the structurally stable bone feature space, allowing both representations to be obtained with a single backbone forward pass during inference. Finally, a geometric calibration module integrates both predictions under explicit bone-length constraints. Extensive experiments on Human3.6M and MPI-INF-3DHP demonstrate that our method not only achieves state-of-the-art accuracy but also significantly improves structural stability and robustness to input noise, providing a practical solution for accurate and stable 3D pose estimation in video.
