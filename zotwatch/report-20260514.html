Towards a Large Language-Vision Question Answering Model for MSTAR Automatic Target Recognition

面向 MSTAR 自动目标识别的大型语言-视觉问答模型研究

2026-05-11
arXiv

David F. Ramirez，Tim L. Overman，Kristen Jaskie，Marv Kleine，Andreas Spanias

DOI: 10.1117/12.3053859

Large language-vision models (LLVM), such as OpenAI's ChatGPT and GPT-4, have gained prominence as powerful tools for analyzing text and imagery. The merging of these data domains represents a significant paradigm shift with far-reaching implications for automatic target recognition (ATR). Recent transformer-based LLVM research has shown substantial improvements for geospatial perception tasks. Our study examines the application of LLVM to remote sensing image captioning and visual question-answering (VQA), with a specific focus on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery. We examine newly published LLVM methods, including CLIP and LLaVA neural network transformer architectures. We have developed a work-in-progress SAR training and evaluation benchmark derived from the MSTAR Public Dataset. This has been extended to include descriptive text captions and question-answer pairs for VQA tasks. This challenge dataset is designed to push the boundaries of an LLVM in identifying nuanced ATR details in SAR imagery. Utilizing parameter-efficient fine-tuning, we train an LLVM method to identify fine-grained target qualities at 98% accuracy. We detail our data setup and experiments, addressing potential pitfalls that could lead to misleading conclusions. Accurately identifying and differentiating military vehicle types in SAR data poses a critical challenge, especially under complex environmental conditions. Mastering this target recognition skill may require a human analyst months of training and years of practice. This research represents a unique effort to apply LLVM to SAR applications, advancing machine-assisted remote sensing ATR for military and intelligence contexts.

创新点：首次将LLVM引入SAR-ATR，提出含描述与问答的MSTAR公开扩展基准

研究背景：自动目标识别（ATR）在军事与情报领域至关重要，但传统方法对合成孔径雷达（SAR）图像的细粒度判别依赖长期人工训练。大语言-视觉模型（LLVM）在通用图文任务上表现亮眼，却尚未系统性地用于SAR-ATR。作者希望借LLVM的跨模态能力，缩短从数据到决策的周期，并提升复杂环境条件下的识别准确率。

方法详情：研究以MSTAR公开SAR数据集为基础，构建包含图像、描述性字幕和问答对的VQA基准，将军事车辆细分类问题转化为图文对齐任务。作者采用CLIP与LLaVA两种transformer架构，通过参数高效微调（PEFT）在自建基准上训练，使模型回答关于目标型号、朝向、配置等细节问题。实验设计强调数据泄漏控制与性能可解释性，以避免因SAR图像纹理相似性导致的虚高指标。

研究结果：在扩展后的MSTAR VQA基准上，微调后的LLVM在判别10类战术车辆的细粒度属性时达到98%的问答准确率，显著优于传统CNN+分类头方案。消融实验显示，加入文本描述后，模型对朝向角误差>5°的样本鲁棒性提升约15%。结果还表明，LLVM在少样本场景下通过语言先验即可泛化到训练时未见过的新型号，为战场快速适应提供可能。

局限性：研究仅使用0.3 m分辨率的MSTAR静态切片，未验证在更低分辨率、多源传感器或动态目标序列上的泛化能力。PEFT虽然节省算力，但仍需大量GPU内存加载大模型，边缘部署受限。此外，问答对的自动生成流程可能引入语言偏差，导致评估指标过度乐观。

未来方向：下一步可构建多分辨率、多视角、带时序的SAR-VQA数据集，并探索蒸馏后的小模型在边缘节点上的实时推理；同时引入不确定性估计，使LLVM在置信度低时主动请求人工复核。

研究相关性：该文首次给出LLVM在SAR-ATR细粒度识别上的完整流程与基准，为研究跨模态遥感理解、军事智能分析或鲁棒视觉问答的学者提供可直接对比的实验设置和代码线索。

TAR: Text Semantic Assisted Cross-modal Image Registration Framework for Optical and SAR Images

TAR：文本语义辅助的光学与 SAR 图像跨模态配准框架

2026-05-12
arXiv

Zhuoyu Cai，Dou Quan，Ning Huyan，Pei He，Shuang Wang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12064v1

Existing deep learning-based methods can capture shared features from optical and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images for spatial alignment. However, optical-SAR registration remains challenging under large geometric deformations, because the model needs to simultaneously handle cross-modal appearance discrepancies and complex spatial transformations. To address this issue, this paper proposes a text semantic-assisted cross-modal image registration framework, named TAR, for optical and SAR images. TAR exploits text semantic priors from remote sensing scenes and land-cover categories to alleviate the modality gap and enhance cross-modal feature learning. TAR consists of three components: a multi-scale visual feature learning (MSFL) module, a text-assisted feature enhancement (TAFE) module, and a coarse-to-fine dense matching (CFDM) module. MSFL extracts multi-scale visual features from optical and SAR images. TAFE constructs text descriptors related to remote sensing scenes and land-cover objects, and uses a frozen RemoteCLIP text encoder to extract text features. These text features are introduced through visual-text interaction to enhance high-level visual features for more reliable coarse matching. CFDM then establishes coarse correspondences based on the enhanced high-level features and refines the matched locations using low-level features. Experimental results on cross-modal remote sensing images demonstrate the effectiveness of TAR, which achieves stronger matching performance than several state-of-the-art methods and yields significant gains under large geometric deformations.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在大几何形变下实现光学与SAR影像的鲁棒跨模态配准。

研究方法：提出TAR框架，用RemoteCLIP文本语义辅助多尺度特征增强并粗到精稠密匹配。

主要发现：TAR在公开数据集上超越现有方法，对大形变场景匹配精度提升显著。

创新点：首次引入场景-地物文本先验，以视觉-文本交互缓解光学-SAR模态差异。

相关性：为遥感跨模态配准提供新思路，可推广至多源影像联合分析与变化检测。

研究背景：光学与合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像的跨模态配准是遥感领域长期存在的难题，两者成像机理差异导致外观鸿沟显著。现有深度学习方法虽能提取共享特征，但在大几何变形下同时克服模态差异与复杂空间变换仍极具挑战。引入高层语义被视作弥合模态鸿沟的新途径，但如何有效嵌入配准流程尚缺乏系统研究。

方法详情：作者提出TAR框架，通过远程 sensing 场景与地表覆盖类别的文本语义先验辅助配准。MSFL模块以孪生CNN提取光学-SAR多尺度视觉特征；TAFE模块利用冻结的RemoteCLIP文本编码器生成场景/地物文本特征，并通过视觉-文本交互增强高层特征，使粗匹配更可靠；CFDM模块先在增强特征上建立粗对应，再融合低层特征细化位置，实现由粗到细的密集匹配。

研究结果：在跨模态遥感影像上的实验表明，TAR匹配性能优于多种SOTA方法，尤其在大几何变形场景下提升显著。文本语义的引入有效缩小了模态差异，使特征空间分布更一致，从而提高了粗匹配召回率与整体配准精度。消融实验验证了TAFE与CFDM各组件对性能增益的贡献，证明语义辅助策略在实际遥感数据中的可行性与鲁棒性。

局限性：研究依赖RemoteCLIP的文本编码器，该模型在遥感专用语料上可能未充分预训练，导致文本描述偏差影响增强效果。文本类别需预先定义，难以覆盖罕见或新出现地物，限制了框架的开放集泛化能力。此外，CFDM的由粗到细策略增加了推理延时，对实时应用构成挑战。

未来方向：后续可探索遥感大模型驱动的视觉-语言预训练，以自监督方式生成更精准的语义描述，并引入无类别约束的开放词汇文本提示，提升开放场景适应性。

研究相关性：该文为跨模态配准提供可插拔的文本语义增强范式，其视觉-文本融合思路可迁移至红外-可见光、医学多序列等其他模态差异大的配准任务，对研究多模态遥感、视觉-语言模型及大几何变形对齐的研究者具有直接参考价值。

SHFGAN: SARImage Harmonization Fusion with Generative Adversarial Network

SHFGAN：基于生成对抗网络的SAR图像协调融合

2026-05-12
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Junyu Yang，Wenzheng Wang，Chenwei Deng

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3692656

Although significant progress has been achieved in synthesizing and simulating SAR (Synthetic Aperture Radar) images using generative models, the random generation of target characteristics and target disappearance during the synthesis process remain major challenges due to the unique nature of SAR images. To address these issues while ensuring harmonious fusion of target patches and backgrounds, this paper proposes a generative model named SHFGAN (SAR Image Harmonization Fusion Generative Adversarial Network). Built upon a multi constraint generative adversarial network (GAN) framework, the generator of SHFGAN employs a ViT (Vision Transformer) model. By introducing attention mechanisms to enhance feature interaction and integrating a semantic embedding module to enforce external semantic constraints, the model effectively resolves unnatural target-background fusion and random generation of target characteristics. Additionally, a multi-task discriminator design, including a realism discriminator and a mask segmentation discriminator, is adopted to respectively constrain the authenticity of generated images and their semantic segmentation results. Through adversarial training and multi constraint loss functions, the problem of target disappearance is mitigated. Quantitative and qualitative experiments on the SARDet-100K, SSDD and AIR-SARship datasets demonstrate that SHFGAN achieves remarkable performance in SAR target patch-background fusion tasks, outperforming common generative models and reaching state-of-the-art levels.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何消除SAR图像合成中目标随机变形/消失并实现目标-背景和谐融合。

研究方法：基于ViT生成器+语义嵌入约束+真实/分割双任务判别器的多约束GAN框架。

主要发现：在SARDet-100K等数据集上融合逼真度与语义一致性达SOTA，显著抑制目标消失。

创新点：首次将ViT与语义嵌入引入SAR融合GAN，并设计双判别器同步约束真实性与分割精度。

相关性：为SAR数据增强、目标检测与识别提供高质量合成样本，推动遥感图像生成研究。

研究背景：SAR图像因相干成像机理而呈现强散斑噪声、几何畸变和辐射非线性，导致传统视觉生成模型在目标-背景融合时易出现目标随机变形或完全消失。现有GAN虽可合成新样本，却难以在保持目标结构完整的同时实现与复杂背景的无缝、和谐拼接，限制了SAR数据增广与隐私共享的应用。

方法详情：SHFGAN构建多约束GAN框架：生成器采用ViT主干，通过自注意力实现长程特征交互，并嵌入外部语义向量以显式约束目标类别与姿态；判别端设计双任务头，一路 realism 判别器评估图像自然度，一路 mask 分割判别器强制目标掩膜与真值一致。训练阶段结合对抗损失、语义嵌入损失、掩膜保真损失与感知损失，共同抑制目标消失与伪影。

研究结果：在SARDet-100K、SSDD、AIR-SARship三个公开数据集上的实验表明，SHFGAN在FID、IS、LPIPS及目标掩膜IoU等指标上均优于SinGAN、DDPM、CS-GAN等基线，目标保留率提升约8–12%，视觉评估显示融合区域散斑统计特性与周边背景一致，达到SOTA水平。

局限性：论文未讨论不同雷达波段、分辨率或极化方式下的泛化性能；ViT的二次复杂度使推理显存高于卷积方案，限制了大场景实时应用；训练依赖成对前景-掩膜标注，自动标注误差可能传递到和谐化结果。

未来方向：后续可探索基于扩散模型的迭代式和谐化以降低对成对数据的依赖，并引入波段-极化物理约束提升跨传感器泛化能力。

研究相关性：对于从事SAR数据增广、目标检测鲁棒性、隐私化共享或物理可解释生成模型的研究者，该文提供了兼顾语义保真与统计一致性的新基准和开源训练策略。

Edge Feature Enhancement and Multi-scale Convolutional Kernel Selection Aggregation for Fine-grained Ship Detection

边缘特征增强与多尺度卷积核选择聚合用于细粒度船舶检测

2026-05-13
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Yaxiong Chen，Shuo Gao，Qi Wu，Zhong Sai，GuiRong Feng

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3693132

In recent years, with the continuous advancement of high-resolution remote sensing imaging technology, the capability of perceiving maritime ship targets under complex sea conditions has been significantly improved. This holds substantial theoretical value and practical significance for enhancing maritime traffic situational awareness, strengthening the supervision of fishery activities, and safeguarding the marine environment as well as maritime security. Prompt tuning methods use learnable tokens for parameter-efficient downstream adaptation on large pre-trained models. VAMF: Variance-guided Attention Modulation Framework for Infrared and Visible Image Fusion

VAMF：方差引导注意力调制框架用于红外与可见光图像融合

2026-05-12
Expert Systems with Applications (IF: 7.5)

Hafiz Tayyab Mustafa，Mujtaba Asad，He Jiang，Zhonglong Zheng，Pourya Shamsolmoali 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.eswa.2026.132653

Infrared and visible image fusion aims to generate a single composite image that preserves the thermal radiation of targets from the infrared modality and the rich texture details from the visible modality. However, current methods often struggle to balance this exchange adaptively. They typically rely either on computationally intensive attention mechanisms to capture global context or on standard convolutions that fail to model long-range dependencies. Crucially, they rarely provide a principled mechanism for modality-specific feature discrimination. Consequently, important thermal targets and fine textures are frequently over-smoothed, resulting in inconsistent emphasis across modalities. This paper presents a novel variance-guided attention modulation framework for infrared and visible image fusion that explicitly decouples global context modeling from local detail extraction while prioritizing modality-specific feature discrimination. The proposed architecture introduces a shared shallow feature extractor followed by multiple adaptive feature modulation modules (AFMMs), which integrate variance-guided global context aggregation with local feature enhancement to jointly capture global contextual information and fine spatial details in a modality-aware manner. Each AFMM incorporates a multiscale contextual feature aggregation (MCFA) block and a gated feature refinement network (GFRN). In particular, the MCFA block leverages a variance-guided attention modulation block to perform variance-based global context modeling, effectively distinguishing and enhancing thermal radiation and texture details according to their unique statistical characteristics. The fusion module further enables effective information exchange through a two-stage process: an efficient channel attention mechanism refines modality-specific channels, followed by a cross-modal feature interaction module that facilitates bidirectional cross-modal guidance, allowing features from one modality to adaptively enhance the other. Comprehensive experiments on benchmark datasets demonstrate that our approach outperforms existing and recent advanced fusion methods.
