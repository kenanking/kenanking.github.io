Joint exploitation of ISAR autofocus and wake patterns for ship kinematic estimation in VHR spaceborne SAR images

联合利用ISAR自聚焦与尾迹模式进行甚高分辨率星载SAR图像中的船舶运动学估计

2026-07-13
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Ilaria Nasso，Fabrizio Santi，Lorenzo Piazzo，Debora Pastina

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3712499

This letter addresses ship position and full velocity vector estimation in very high-resolution (VHR) spaceborne synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imaging by jointly exploiting inverse SAR (ISAR) processing and wake patterns analysis. A dual-path architecture is proposed. The first path performs ISAR autofocus to put ship images into focus also providing azimuthal velocity, while vessel heading is obtained by fusing independent ship and wake orientation measurements according to a maximum likelihood criterion, eventually providing radial velocity and ship position. Alternatively, the radial velocity is retrieved from the azimuthal displacement between the ship and its wake, enabling azimuthal velocity to be recovered through ML-based heading estimate. Such twofold strategy allows not only for verifying the coherence of the estimates but also ensures kinematic retrieval when ISAR focusing is ill-posed. Validation using VHR SAR data demonstrates the framework’s effectiveness and underscores sub-meter resolution as a critical prerequisite for joint ISAR and wake-based motion analysis.

研究问题：如何在超高分辨率星载SAR图像中联合利用ISAR自聚焦和尾迹模式估计船舶位置和速度矢量？

研究方法：提出双路径架构：ISAR自聚焦获方位速度，融合船与尾迹朝向的ML估计获得航向和径向速度。

主要发现：框架有效验证了估计一致性，并在ISAR不适定条件下仍能恢复运动参数，亚米级分辨率是关键前提。

创新点：首次联合ISAR自聚焦与尾迹模式分析，实现双路径交叉验证及运动参数鲁棒估计。

相关性：为星载SAR高分辨率船舶运动估计提供新范式，对海上监视和雷达遥感有重要应用价值。

研究背景：本研究的背景是VHR空间SAR图像中船舶运动参数（位置和全速度矢量）估计的挑战，由于船舶在SAR图像中可能因运动而模糊，传统方法难以同时获取精确的径向和方位速度。动机是通过联合利用ISAR自聚焦技术（聚焦船舶并提取方位速度）和船舶尾流模式分析（提取航向和径向速度），克服单一方法的局限性，实现高精度运动估计。

方法详情：提出了一种双路径架构：第一路径执行ISAR自聚焦，使船舶图像聚焦的同时提供方位向速度估计；第二路径通过最大似然准则融合独立的船舶和尾流方向测量，获得船舶航向，进而结合尾流信息推导径向速度和船舶位置。另外，也可通过船舶与尾流之间的方位向位移直接获取径向速度，再结合ML航向恢复方位速度，形成互验机制，尤其在ISAR聚焦不适定情况下仍能保证运动参数估计。

研究结果：使用VHR SAR数据验证了该框架的有效性，证明了亚米级分辨率是实现联合ISAR与尾流运动分析的关键先决条件。该方法能够同时提供船舶位置和完整速度矢量，且通过双重策略确保了估计的相干性和鲁棒性。主要意义在于为高分辨率SAR海洋监视中的船舶动态监测提供了新的技术途径。

局限性：该方法可能依赖于清晰的船舶尾流模式，在尾流被海浪或噪声淹没时估计性能可能下降；此外，ISAR自聚焦需要船舶具有足够的姿态变化，对于静止或极低速目标可能不适用。联合估计的一致性依赖ML准则的假设，实际数据中航向测量误差模型可能影响最优性。

未来方向：后续研究可扩展到多船舶场景下的交互尾流分析，或结合深度学习提升尾流检测鲁棒性；亦可探索将运动估计与船舶分类识别结合，用于更复杂的海事监控任务。

研究相关性：该论文对从事空间SAR海洋遥感、船舶监视与运动分析、ISAR成像以及尾流探测的研究者高度相关，提供了联合利用多源信息提升运动参数估计精度的新思路，尤其在VHR数据集中具有直接应用价值。

FGFMamba: A Fuzzy-Enhanced Gated Fusion Mamba for HSI and SAR Image Classification

FGFMamba：一种用于高光谱图像和SAR图像分类的模糊增强门控融合Mamba

2026-07-13
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Menghui Su，Jindong Xu，Zongqin Yue，Zhiyuan Yang，Yu Sun

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3711977

Multimodal remote sensing classification capitalizes on the synergistic advantages of diverse data sources (e.g., HSI, LiDAR, and SAR) to facilitate robust land-cover characterization. However, contemporary methods often struggle with intrinsic spectral uncertainty and cross-modal heterogeneity, failing to adequately address adaptive feature fusion. To overcome these limitations, we propose a novel Fuzzy-Enhanced Gated Fusion Mamba (FGFMamba) framework for HSI–SAR classification. Specifically, we design an HSI-specific Fuzzy Block that leverages Gaussian-modulated kernels to formulate a probabilistic uncertainty descriptor. This module maps raw spectral intensities into a high-dimensional fuzzy feature space, thereby capturing the intrinsic stochasticity of HSI data. Furthermore, a Gated Cross-Modal Fusion Block (GCFB) is introduced to extend selective state space modeling (SSM) to a dual-branch interaction paradigm, dynamically weighting modalities to reconcile heterogeneity and enhance cross-modal synergy. Extensive experiments on the Berlin and Augsburg benchmark datasets demonstrate that FGFMamba achieves state-of-the-art performance with linear computational complexity. This makes it a highly efficient and robust solution for large-scale geospatial intelligent recognition.

研究问题：解决多模态遥感分类中光谱不确定性和跨模态异质性导致的自适应特征融合不足问题。

研究方法：提出FGFMamba框架，含HSI模糊块和门控交叉模态融合块，结合高斯模糊与状态空间模型。

方法详情：方法包括两个核心模块：一是针对HSI的模糊块（Fuzzy Block），使用高斯调制核构建概率不确定性描述子，将原始光谱强度映射到高维模糊特征空间以捕获内在随机性；二是门控跨模态融合块（GCFB），将选择性状态空间建模（SSM）扩展为双分支交互范式，动态加权模态以缓解异质性并增强协同。整体框架在保持线性计算复杂度的同时实现高效融合。

局限性：方法依赖高斯调制核的假设，可能不适用于所有HSI数据的不确定性分布；此外，双分支交互范式对SAR模态的建模相对简单，未充分挖掘SAR的纹理或极化信息。框架在极端复杂场景下的泛化能力有待验证。

Scalable Visual Pretraining for Language Intelligence

面向语言智能的可扩展视觉预训练

2026-07-10
arXiv

Yiming Zhang，Zhonghan Zhao，Wenwei Zhang，Haiteng Zhao，Tianyang Lin 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09657v1

The rapid progress of large foundation models has been driven predominantly by pretraining on large-scale text corpora. However, many forms of knowledge are conveyed through visual representations, where figures, typeset equations, and page layouts carry rich information that cannot be faithfully or completely captured by text alone. Yet current pretraining approaches discard these visual cues by converting visually rich sources, such as documents and web pages, into plain text for learning language intelligence. This paper challenges the default assumption that language models must be trained on text-only representations and shows that Visual Pretraining is a scalable learner for foundation model intelligence. To this end, we conduct a systematic study of unsupervised visual pretraining paradigms that directly leverage visual documents without text extraction. Across multiple backbones and benchmarks, visual pretraining on the same underlying corpora consistently outperforms text-only pretraining, offering an efficient pathway to scalable language intelligence.

Robustifying Vision-Language Models via Test-Time Prompt Adaptation

通过测试时提示自适应增强视觉-语言模型的鲁棒性

2026-07-10
arXiv

Xingyu Zhu，Huanshen Wu，Shuo Wang，Beier Zhu，Jiannan Ge 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09450v1

Pre-trained Vision-Language Models (VLMs) such as CLIP achieve strong zero-shot generalization, but their performance degrades sharply under adversarial perturbations. Existing test-time adaptation methods typically rely on sample-level confidence heuristics, overlooking the intrinsic distributional structure of the data. This sample-centric approach limits robustness, as it fails to distinguish confident adversarial mispredictions from true semantic consistency. REBASE: Reference-Background Subspace Elimination for Training-Free In-Context Segmentation

REBASE：用于无需训练的情境分割的参考-背景子空间消除

2026-07-10
arXiv

Mantha Sai Gopal，Jaison Saji Chacko，Harsh Nandwana，Sandesh Hegde，Debarshi Banerjee 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09082v1

Training-free in-context segmentation enables new object categories to be introduced at inference time from a single annotated reference image, eliminating the retraining and memory overhead of class-incremental learning. Recent approaches achieve this by combining vision foundation models for semantic correspondence with promptable segmentation networks like SAM. However, their performance is fundamentally limited by the quality of the cross-image similarity map; shared contextual backgrounds between the reference and query systematically elevate similarity in non-target regions, degrading prompt localization. We present REBASE, a training-free framework that explicitly suppresses these spurious contextual correspondences. Our method identifies the low-rank background feature subspace from the reference image and project the reference and query features onto its orthogonal complement in closed form, yielding cleaner semantic matching. We then generate positive point prompts using similarity-weighted farthest-point sampling, paired with a refined dense similarity prior. Without any training or parameter updates, our approach establishes a new state of the art among training-free methods on PACO-Part, FSS-1000, and cross-domain datasets such as ISIC2018, demonstrating that explicit background subspace removal is a highly effective principle for one-shot localization.
