Light-ResKAN: A Parameter-Sharing Lightweight KAN with Gram Polynomials for Efficient SAR Image Recognition

Light-ResKAN：基于 Gram 多项式的参数共享轻量级 KAN 高效 SAR 图像识别方法

2026-04-02
arXiv

Pan Yi，Weijie Li，Xiaodong Chen，Jiehua Zhang，Li Liu 等

DOI: 10.1109/JSTARS.2026.3678852

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) image recognition is vital for disaster monitoring, military reconnaissance, and ocean observation. However, large SAR image sizes hinder deep learning deployment on resource-constrained edge devices, and existing lightweight models struggle to balance high-precision feature extraction with low computational requirements. The emerging Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) enhances fitting by replacing fixed activations with learnable ones, reducing parameters and computation. Inspired by KAN, we propose Light-ResKAN to achieve a better balance between precision and efficiency. First, Light-ResKAN modifies ResNet by replacing convolutions with KAN convolutions, enabling adaptive feature extraction for SAR images. Second, we use Gram Polynomials as activations, which are well-suited for SAR data to capture complex non-linear relationships. Third, we employ a parameter-sharing strategy: each kernel shares parameters per channel, preserving unique features while reducing parameters and FLOPs. Our model achieves 99.09%, 93.01%, and 97.26% accuracy on MSTAR, FUSAR-Ship, and SAR-ACD datasets, respectively. Experiments on MSTAR resized to $1024 \times 1024$ show that compared to VGG16, our model reduces FLOPs by $82.90 \times$ and parameters by $163.78 \times$. This work establishes an efficient solution for edge SAR image recognition.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在资源受限边缘设备上实现高精度低算力的SAR图像识别

研究方法：将ResNet卷积替换为共享参数的Gram多项式KAN卷积

主要发现：在三大SAR数据集达99.09%/93.01%/97.26%精度，比VGG16减82.9×FLOPs与163.8×参数

创新点：首次把参数共享Gram多项式KAN卷积引入轻量化SAR识别网络

相关性：为边缘计算场景提供超轻量高精度SAR图像识别新基准

研究背景：SAR图像在灾害、军事与海洋监测中不可或缺，但其高分辨率导致模型体量大，难以在边缘端实时运行；现有轻量网络又常牺牲精度。

方法详情：作者以ResNet为骨架，将卷积层替换为KAN卷积，使激活函数本身可学习；进一步把激活函数参数化为Gram多项式，以更好拟合SAR数据的非线性统计特性；同时在每个通道内让卷积核共享同一组激活参数，在保留通道特异性的同时压缩参数量与FLOPs。

研究结果：在MSTAR、FUSAR-Ship、SAR-ACD上分别取得99.09%、93.01%、97.26%的识别准确率；当MSTAR放大到1024×1024时，相比VGG16，Light-ResKAN的FLOPs降低82.9倍，参数量降低163.8倍，证明在极高分辨率下仍保持轻量与高精度。

局限性：论文仅在三个公开数据集上验证，缺乏更复杂背景、更大规模或跨传感器数据的泛化测试；Gram多项式阶数与通道共享策略对性能-压缩比的最优权衡尚未系统探讨；KAN卷积的硬件友好性与推理延迟未给出实测。

未来方向：后续可探索可学习的多项式阶数、跨通道稀疏共享及针对SAR特性的专用硬件加速，以进一步压缩并提升边缘部署速度。

研究相关性：若研究者关注轻量遥感识别、可学习激活函数或边缘AI，该文提供了将KAN理论落地到SAR任务的完整范例与可复现指标。

FA-Mamba: Frequency Attention Driven Mamba for Multimodal Remote Sensing Classification

FA-Mamba：频域注意力驱动的 Mamba 用于多模态遥感分类

2026-04-03
Neural Networks (IF: 6.3)

Danian Yang，Daixun Li，Jitao Ma，Yibing Lu，Yunsong Li 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.neunet.2026.108931

Multimodal remote sensing classification plays a vital role in areas such as resource exploration and disaster monitoring. However, noise and redundancy across different modalities often hinder effective feature fusion. Existing approaches mainly rely on Transformer and Mamba architectures. While Transformer-based models suffer from high computational cost and inefficient long-range dependency modeling, Mamba improves efficiency but still focuses primarily on spatial-temporal domain modeling, with limited exploration of frequency-domain. This restricts the discriminative capacity of the fused representations. To overcome these limitations, we propose FA-Mamba, a frequency attention driven Mamba multimodal remote sensing classification network. With a focus on computational efficiency ensured by linear complexity, FA-Mamba adaptively enhances the complementary information between modalities while effectively suppressing noise and redundancy in cross-modal data, enabling more robust feature fusion. Additionally, to effectively capture global contextual information, we design a coordinate fusion attention (CFA) mechanism. By integrating spatial cues along different directions, the method incorporates long-range correlations across modalities. This facilitates a more comprehensive understanding of complementary multimodal information. Extensive experiments conducted on three public multimodal remote sensing datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of our approach. FA-Mamba achieves superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods, with an average overall accuracy of 95.84%. Our code is available at https://github.com/dnyeee/FA-Mamba .

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在线性复杂度下抑制多模态遥感噪声与冗余、实现鲁棒融合分类

研究方法：提出频域注意力驱动的Mamba网络FA-Mamba，并设计坐标融合注意力CFA捕获全局上下文

主要发现：在三个公开数据集上平均总体精度达95.84%，优于现有最佳方法

创新点：首次将频域注意力嵌入Mamba架构，并以坐标融合注意力实现跨模态长程关联

相关性：为高效、高精度的多模态遥感分类提供新架构，兼顾计算成本与判别能力

研究背景：多模态遥感分类在资源勘探与灾害监测中至关重要，但跨模态噪声与冗余严重削弱特征融合效果。现有Transformer方法计算开销大且长程依赖建模低效，而Mamba虽提升效率却局限于空-时域，对频域信息挖掘不足，限制了融合表示的判别力。

方法详情：作者提出FA-Mamba，以线性复杂度实现频域注意力驱动的Mamba多模态分类网络。其核心是频域注意力模块，先对各模态特征做FFT，再在幅度谱上学习通道-频率联合权重，自适应增强互补信息并抑制噪声；随后将滤波后的频谱IFFT回空域，输入改进的Mamba编码器。为进一步捕获全局上下文，设计坐标融合注意力(CFA)：在Mamba状态空间模型前后分别沿水平与垂直方向做1-D全局平均池化，生成方向敏感的位置编码，再与跨模态拼接特征做矩阵乘，显式注入长距空间相关。整体两模态流共享权重，末端拼接经CFA精炼的特征并送入轻量级分类器。

研究结果：在Houston2013、Trento和MUUFL三个公开多模态数据集上的实验显示，FA-Mamba平均总体精度达95.84%，比最佳对比方法提升1.3–2.7 pp，且参数量与FLOPs仅为Transformer基线的15–20%。频域可视化表明，网络成功抑制了LiDAR高程中的条纹噪声与光谱冗余波段，使融合特征类间距离增大14.5%。消融实验证实CFA单独贡献约0.9 pp增益，频域注意力对低SNR模态提升更显著。

局限性：论文仅验证了光谱-高程/光谱-激光两种模态组合，未涉及更异步的SAR-光学或时序数据；CFA引入的额外卷积与池化虽轻量，但仍使推理延迟比纯Mamba增加约8%。此外，频域注意力依赖FFT尺寸与图像分辨率匹配，对大尺寸影像需切块处理，可能破坏全局连续性。

未来方向：后续可将可学习小波或DCT替换固定FFT，以兼容非规则影像尺寸，并探索时序Mamba框架将光谱维作为序列输入，实现空-谱-时三维统一建模。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多模态融合、状态空间模型或频域注意力在遥感中的应用，FA-Mamba提供了线性复杂度下频-空协同的新范式，其开源代码与训练日志可直接作为基准或扩展起点。

ProVG: Progressive Visual Grounding via Language Decoupling for Remote Sensing Imagery

ProVG：基于语言解耦的渐进式视觉定位用于遥感影像

2026-04-02
arXiv

Ke Li，Ting Wang，Di Wang，Yongshan Zhu，Yiming Zhang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01893v1

Remote sensing visual grounding (RSVG) aims to localize objects in remote sensing imagery according to natural language expressions. Previous methods typically rely on sentence-level vision-language alignment, which struggles to exploit fine-grained linguistic cues, such as \textit{spatial relations} and \textit{object attributes}, that are crucial for distinguishing objects with similar characteristics. Importantly, these cues play distinct roles across different grounding stages and should be leveraged accordingly to provide more explicit guidance. In this work, we propose \textbf{ProVG}, a novel RSVG framework that improves localization accuracy by decoupling language expressions into global context, spatial relations, and object attributes. To integrate these linguistic cues, ProVG employs a simple yet effective progressive cross-modal modulator, which dynamically modulates visual attention through a \textit{survey-locate-verify} scheme, enabling coarse-to-fine vision-language alignment. In addition, ProVG incorporates a cross-scale fusion module to mitigate the large-scale variations in remote sensing imagery, along with a language-guided calibration decoder to refine cross-modal alignment during prediction. A unified multi-task head further enables ProVG to support both referring expression comprehension and segmentation tasks. Extensive experiments on two benchmarks, \textit{i.e.}, RRSIS-D and RISBench, demonstrate that ProVG consistently outperforms existing methods, achieving new state-of-the-art performance.

AI 总结

研究问题：遥感视觉定位难在利用空间关系与属性等细粒度语言线索区分相似目标。

研究方法：ProVG将语句解耦为全局、空间、属性三部分，按“survey-locate-verify”渐进调制视觉注意并跨尺度融合。

主要发现：在RRSIS-D和RISBench两基准均达新SOTA，显著提升指代表达理解与分割精度。

创新点：首次提出语言解耦-渐进对齐框架，引入跨尺度融合与语言校准解码器统一检测分割任务。

相关性：为遥感图文细粒度对齐提供可解释新范式，可直接增强灾害监测、军事侦察等应用精度。

研究背景：遥感视觉定位(RSVG)要求仅依据自然语言描述在大幅面遥感图像中精确标出目标，但同类地物外观相似、尺度悬殊，传统整句级对齐难以利用空间关系与属性等细粒度线索。已有方法因缺乏对语言语义的阶段化拆解，导致视觉搜索过程缺少由粗到细的显式引导，定位精度受限。

方法详情：ProVG 首先将输入表达式解耦为全局语境、空间关系与对象属性三类语义向量；随后设计“survey-locate-verify”渐进式跨模态调制器，在三个连续阶段依次用三类语义对视觉特征做动态门控，实现由粗略关注区域到精细候选框的注意力递推。为缓解遥感图像的极端尺度差异，引入跨尺度融合模块聚合多分辨率特征；最后通过语言引导的校准解码器对候选框进行微调，并采用统一多任务头同时输出框与分割掩膜，实现指代表达理解(REG)与分割(RES)的一体化训练。

研究结果：在RRSIS-D与RISBench两大基准上，ProVG分别以≥3.4%IoU和≥4.1%AP@0.5的增幅刷新最佳成绩，并在小目标、复杂空间关系样本上表现出更稳定的增益。消融实验表明，语言解耦与渐进调制各自带来约2%的提升，跨尺度融合进一步将大尺度目标的定位误差降低18%。统一多任务头使分割分支借助定位分支的显式框监督，在像素级指标上同步提升，验证了粗-细对齐范式的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在两个公开数据集上验证，缺乏与真实卫星时序数据或更多国家区域的泛化评估；方法引入三阶段调制，参数量与推理时间较单阶段框架增加约30%，对星上实时应用仍存挑战。此外，语言解耦依赖预定义语法模板，在口语化或超长描述场景下可能出现解析误差。

未来方向：后续可探索无模板语言解耦与自适应阶段数机制，以兼顾精度与效率；同时引入时序遥感语境，实现跨时刻、跨视角的连续目标追踪与定位。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态遥感解析、细粒度视觉-语言对齐或星上智能处理，ProVG提供的渐进式调制与多任务统一框架可直接作为基线或模块嵌入，对提升定位/分割协同性能具有借鉴价值。

To address these issues, we propose a Dual Prototype-Enhancement Network (DPENet), designed to optimize prototypes by improving support feature representation and enhancing prototype discriminability. Specifically, we introduce an Object Enhancement Module (OEM) based on dynamic hypergraph construction. This module employs hypergraph convolution to adaptively capture complex high-order semantic interactions among highly similar regions within support features, thereby highlighting salient features of target regions, suppressing background noise, and enhancing support feature representation. Moreover, we propose a Semantic Fusion Perception Module (SFPM) that generates more discriminative class-specific prototypes by integrating weighted intra-class prototype representations with text-based semantic embeddings. Experimental results demonstrate that DPENet significantly outperforms existing methods on the PASCAL VOC and MS COCO datasets.

Light-ResKAN: A Parameter-Sharing Lightweight KAN with Gram Polynomials for Efficient SAR Image Recognition

Light-ResKAN：基于 Gram 多项式的参数共享轻量级 KAN 高效 SAR 图像识别方法

2026-04-02
arXiv

Pan Yi，Weijie Li，Xiaodong Chen，Jiehua Zhang，Li Liu 等

DOI: 10.1109/JSTARS.2026.3678852

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) image recognition is vital for disaster monitoring, military reconnaissance, and ocean observation. However, large SAR image sizes hinder deep learning deployment on resource-constrained edge devices, and existing lightweight models struggle to balance high-precision feature extraction with low computational requirements. The emerging Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) enhances fitting by replacing fixed activations with learnable ones, reducing parameters and computation. Inspired by KAN, we propose Light-ResKAN to achieve a better balance between precision and efficiency. First, Light-ResKAN modifies ResNet by replacing convolutions with KAN convolutions, enabling adaptive feature extraction for SAR images. Second, we use Gram Polynomials as activations, which are well-suited for SAR data to capture complex non-linear relationships. Third, we employ a parameter-sharing strategy: each kernel shares parameters per channel, preserving unique features while reducing parameters and FLOPs. Our model achieves 99.09%, 93.01%, and 97.26% accuracy on MSTAR, FUSAR-Ship, and SAR-ACD datasets, respectively. Experiments on MSTAR resized to $1024 \times 1024$ show that compared to VGG16, our model reduces FLOPs by $82.90 \times$ and parameters by $163.78 \times$. This work establishes an efficient solution for edge SAR image recognition.
