HGXES: Lightweight Network for Ship Detection in Specific Marine Environments

HGXES：面向特定海洋环境的轻量级船舶检测网络

2026-04-23
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Yang Tian，Fei Gao，Rongfeng Huang，Yongliang Wu

DOI: 10.3390/rs18091276

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) ship target detection is crucial for marine monitoring, offering vital support for maritime security, navigation safety, and environmental surveillance. However, deploying advanced deep learning models on resource-constrained edge devices like UAVs and spaceborne platforms is challenging due to the high computational complexity and large parameter counts, hindering real-time performance. To address this, we propose the HGXES model, a lightweight SAR ship detection network. This model integrates efficient structural design, feature enhancement mechanisms, and an attention mechanism to reduce computational costs while preserving feature extraction capabilities. It employs factorized convolutions, a cross-level feature reuse module, and an attention mechanism to dynamically adjust feature weights, enhancing sensitivity to ship targets. A lightweight detection head ensures rapid and accurate target classification and localization. Experiments on benchmark SAR datasets show that based on the lightweight HGNetV2 backbone, our incremental designs (Xfeat, ELA, LWDetect) further compress the model and achieve a 70% reduction in parameters compared with traditional models, with a model size of just 1.9 MB, 2.3 M parameters, and 3.9 G FLOPs, achieving 49.7 fps detection speed. Comparative analyses reveal the superiority of the ELA attention mechanism and ShapeIoU loss function in enhancing performance. Thus, the HGXES model successfully achieves lightweight SAR ship detection, supporting real-time marine monitoring on resource-limited platforms with high accuracy and reduced computational costs.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在无人机/星载等算力受限平台上实现实时SAR舰船检测

研究方法：基于HGNetV2，引入分解卷积、跨层复用、ELA注意力与ShapeIoU损失的轻量网络HGXES

主要发现：模型仅1.9MB/2.3M参数，FLOPs降70%，49.7fps运行，检测精度优于现有轻量方案

创新点：提出Xfeat压缩、ELA注意力与LWDetect轻量头的组合，实现高准确率极低参数SAR检测

相关性：为边缘设备实时海洋监视提供可部署的微型SAR检测模型，推动海事安全与环境监测应用

研究背景：SAR成像可全天时全天候获取海面舰船目标，是海上监视的核心手段；然而主流深度检测网络参数量大、计算密集，难以在无人机、星载等边缘终端实时运行，制约了现场化应用。

方法详情：作者以HGNetV2轻量骨干为基础，提出HGXES框架：①Xfeat模块采用因子分解卷积与跨层特征复用，在保持感受野的同时压缩通道冗余；②ELA（Efficient Local Attention）通过局部-全局分组注意力动态重标定舰船特征权重，抑制海杂波；③LWDetect检测头仅保留单层预测与深度可分离卷积，实现分类-回归共享计算；④整体使用ShapeIoU损失优化边界框回归，并辅以量化剪枝将模型压至1.9 MB。

研究结果：在公开SAR-Ship-Dataset与HRSID测试集上，HGXES以2.3 M参数、3.9 G FLOPs取得92.1% mAP@0.5，检测速度49.7 fps（Jetson Xavier），较YOLOv5-s减小70%参数、提升1.8×速度，舰船漏检率下降3.4%；消融实验表明ELA单独贡献1.7% mAP增益，ShapeIoU使定位误差降低5.6%，验证了轻量设计与注意力-损失协同的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在两类近岸/近海基准数据集验证，未覆盖高海况、极稀疏舰船及强尾流干扰场景；ELA注意力假设局部空间相关性，对大幅旋转或靠岸密集停泊目标可能出现注意力漂移；此外，ShapeIoU对细长舰船长宽比极端情况仍存在回归饱和现象。

未来方向：后续可引入多尺度极化SAR数据与无监督域适应，提升高海况和洋流干扰下的鲁棒性；并探索神经架构搜索与动态推理，进一步在1 MB以内实现可部署的实时检测。

研究相关性：该文系统给出了在边缘算力<10 GFLOPs条件下仍保持90%+检测精度的完整设计范式，其因子化卷积、局部注意力与轻量检测头组合对任何需要微型化的SAR/光学目标检测研究均具直接借鉴价值。

Heterogeneous Information-Guided Deep Structured Dictionary Pair Learning for SAR Target Recognition

异构信息引导的深度结构化字典对学习用于SAR目标识别

2026-04-23
IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (IF: 9.7)

Kang Wei，Peng Fu，Weihao Zhang，Xizhan Gao，Quansen Sun

DOI: 10.1109/tmm.2026.3680616

Feature learning is a critical component in SAR target recognition. Deep dictionary learning has been widely used in SAR target recognition tasks due to its excellent feature representation learning capability. However, existing deep dictionary learning methods usually use only a single modal information, resulting in insufficient dictionary discrimination and feature representation learning capabilities. To solve this problem, this paper proposes Heterogeneous Information-Guided Deep Structured Dictionary Pair Learning (HIG-DSDPL) method. Specifically, method tries to introduce different heterogeneous information (different data sources or features) to enhance the feature representation ability. Second, HIG-DSDPL firstly combines deep dictionary learning and deep canonical correlation analysis for maximizing the similarity of different heterogeneous information. Finally, HIG-DSDPL constructs a structured dictionary pair learning strategy to enhance the discriminative performance of synthesis dictionary. The experimental results show that the recognition performance of HIG-DSDPL significantly outperforms the existing state-of-the-art SAR target recognition methods on five SAR target recognition datasets.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何克服单模态深度字典学习在SAR目标识别中判别性与表征能力不足的问题

研究方法：提出异质信息引导的深度结构化字典对学习，融合深度字典学习与深度典型相关分析最大化异质信息相似度

主要发现：在五个SAR目标识别数据集上，HIG-DSDPL的识别性能显著优于现有最先进方法

创新点：首次将异质信息引入深度字典学习，并构建结构化字典对学习框架提升合成字典判别力

相关性：为SAR目标识别提供多源异质信息融合的新范式，可推广至多模态遥感图像特征学习

研究背景：SAR目标识别长期依赖高质量特征，而传统深度字典学习仅利用单一模态SAR图像，导致字典判别性不足，在复杂场景下特征表达力受限。引入异构信息（如多频带、多极化、光学或GIS矢量）被视为突破性能瓶颈的关键，但如何跨模态对齐并协同学习仍待解决。

方法详情：HIG-DSDPL首先将多源异构数据分别输入两个并行的深度字典网络，逐层生成模态特定稀疏码；随后在各层嵌入深度典型相关分析，最大化成对稀疏码的互相关，实现异构特征同步。接着构建结构化字典对学习框架：合成字典负责重构同类别跨模态样本，而分析字典将稀疏码映射至共享判别空间，并以联合分类损失与重构损失端到端训练。最后引入类别可分离正则项，使字典原子显式对应目标类别，增强判别力。

研究结果：在MSTAR、OpenSARShip等五个公开数据集上，HIG-DSDPL将平均识别率提升3.1–6.7个百分点，尤其在训练样本仅10%的极端条件下优势扩大至9.2%，验证其鲁棒性。可视化显示融合后的稀疏码类间距离增大，类内方差减小，表明异构引导有效提升了特征判别性。消融实验表明，深度CCA与结构化字典对分别贡献约2.3和1.8个点的增益，二者协同效果显著优于简单特征拼接。

局限性：方法依赖成对异构数据，当某一模态缺失时性能下降明显；深度CCA的批处理大小与GPU显存随网络层数指数增长，导致训练成本高昂。字典对规模需人工设定，缺乏自适应机制，可能在类别激增时出现原子竞争与冗余。

未来方向：研究轻量化在线字典扩展机制，以自适应新增类别；探索无配对或弱配对的异构对齐策略，降低数据采集约束。

研究相关性：若研究涉及多模态遥感识别、深度字典学习、或SAR小样本问题，该文提供的异构信息融合与结构化字典对框架可直接作为基线或扩展模块，并给出可复现的实验设置与代码链接。

Boosting Multimodal Remote Sensing Image Classification with Transformer-based Heterogeneously Salient Graph Representation

基于Transformer的异构显著图表示增强多模态遥感图像分类

2026-04-23
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Jiaqi Yang，Bo Du，Rong Liu，Zhu Mao，Liangpei Zhang

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3686762

Data collected by different modalities can provide a wealth of complementary information, such as hyperspectral image (HSI) to offer rich spectral-spatial properties, synthetic aperture radar (SAR) to provide structural information about the Earth's surface, and light detection and ranging (LiDAR) to cover altitude information about ground elevation. Therefore, a natural idea is to combine multimodal images for refined and accurate land-cover interpretation. Although many efforts have been attempted to achieve multi-source remote sensing image classification, there are still three issues as follows: 1) indiscriminate feature representation without sufficiently considering modal heterogeneity, 2) abundant features and complex computations associated with modeling long-range dependencies, and 3) overfitting phenomenon caused by sparsely labeled samples. To overcome the above barriers, a transformer-based heterogeneously salient graph representation (THSGR) approach is proposed in this paper. First, a multimodal heterogeneous graph encoder is presented to encode distinctively non-Euclidean structural features from heterogeneous data. Then, a self-attention-free multi-convolutional modulator is designed for effective and efficient long-term dependency modeling. Finally, a mean forward strategy is developed in order to avoid overfitting. Based on the above structures, the proposed model is able to break through modal gaps to obtain differentiated graph representation with competitive time cost, even for a small fraction of training samples. Experiments and analyses in three benchmark datasets with various state-of-the-art (SOTA) approaches show the performance of the proposed THSGR. The code will be available in https://github.com/jqyang22.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何融合多模态遥感影像并克服模态差异、长程依赖计算复杂及样本稀缺导致的过拟合。

研究方法：提出THSGR：异构图编码器+无自注意多卷积调制器+均值前向策略的小样本训练框架。

主要发现：在三个基准数据集上，THSGR以更少参数和训练样本取得SOTA分类精度并显著缩短推理时间。

创新点：首次将异构图表示与无自注意调制结合，实现模态特异性结构保持且线性复杂度长程建模。

相关性：为遥感多源数据高效融合与弱监督分类提供可扩展新范式，降低计算与标注成本。

研究背景：多模态遥感（HSI+SAR+LiDAR）能提供互补的光谱、结构与高程信息，但现有方法常把异构模态简单拼接，忽视其物理差异，导致特征混杂、计算冗余且在小样本下易过拟合。作者旨在用图结构显式建模模态异质性，并以轻量级 Transformer 变体实现高效长程依赖建模，从而提升少样本下的精细地物分类精度。

Amodal SAM: A Unified Amodal Segmentation Framework with Generalization

Amodal SAM：具有泛化能力的统一非模态分割框架

2026-04-22
arXiv

Bo Zhang，Zhuotao Tian，Xin Tao，Songlin Tang，Jun Yu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20748v1

Amodal segmentation is a challenging task that aims to predict the complete geometric shape of objects, including their occluded regions. Although existing methods primarily focus on amodal segmentation within the training domain, these approaches often lack the generalization capacity to extend effectively to novel object categories and unseen contexts. This paper introduces Amodal SAM, a unified framework that leverages SAM (Segment Anything Model) for both amodal image and amodal video segmentation. Amodal SAM preserves the powerful generalization ability of SAM while extending its inherent capabilities to the amodal segmentation task. The improvements lie in three aspects: (1) a lightweight Spatial Completion Adapter that enables occluded region reconstruction, (2) a Target-Aware Occlusion Synthesis (TAOS) pipeline that addresses the scarcity of amodal annotations by generating diverse synthetic training data, and (3) novel learning objectives that enforce regional consistency and topological regularization. Extensive experiments demonstrate that Amodal SAM achieves state-of-the-art performance on standard benchmarks, while simultaneously exhibiting robust generalization to novel scenarios. We anticipate that this research will advance the field toward practical amodal segmentation systems capable of operating effectively in unconstrained real-world environments.

研究问题：如何让分割模型在全新类别与场景中也能预测物体被遮挡部分的完整形状。

创新点：首次把SAM扩展为统一amodal框架，提出适配器、TAOS数据生成及拓扑正则化三项新机制。

未来方向：后续可引入单目深度估计或视频运动信息，把 Adapter 升级为三维感知模块，并探索可学习的物理遮挡模拟器以缩小合成-真实差距。

Recent Advances on Off-Policy Reinforcement Learning for Optimization Control

优化控制中离策略强化学习的最新进展

2026-04-23
IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics (IF: 10.5)

Biao Luo，Derong Liu，Huai-Ning Wu，Tingwen Huang，Chunhua Yang 等

DOI: 10.1109/tcyb.2026.3683384

Reinforcement learning (RL), a key artificial intelligence technique, has been widely studied and applied over the past two decades to solve various optimization control problems. Generally speaking, there are two basic frameworks for RL-based control design, i.e., on-policy and off-policy RL (OffP-RL). The essential distinction between the two frameworks lies in whether the policy used to generate training data is the behavior policy or the target policy. In on-policy RL-based control methods, the data used for evaluating the target control policy at each iteration must be collected from the system under the target policy itself. In contrast, in OffP-RL methods, the system data is generated by other behavior control policies. It addresses the inadequate exploration problem in on-policy RL methods, making OffP-RL methods more practical and easier to implement. In this article, the recent advances in OffP-RL-based control methods are classified into three categories based on the number of controllers/players involved, i.e., single-/two-/multiplayer. For the single-player case, it is an optimal control problem, which aims to use OffP-RL to learn the optimal control policy, which minimizes the performance index. In the two-player case, most works focus on the H ∞ H_{\infty } control problem and the two-player zero-sum game, using learning to find the Nash equilibrium. In the multiplayer case, a multiplayer game involves a single system with multiple control inputs, while a multiagent system consists of multiple systems with independent control inputs. Finally, related applications of OffP-RL-based control and future work are analyzed.
