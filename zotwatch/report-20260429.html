DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3687444

Infrared and visible image fusion serves as a pivotal technique for all-weather ship surveillance in maritime security applications, which can compensate for the inherent deficiencies of single-modal imaging under low-illumination and complex sea-state conditions. MKD-Net:A Multi-Kernel Downsampling Network for Adjacent and Small-Scale Ship Detection in SAR Images

MKD-Net：用于SAR图像中相邻与小尺度船舶检测的多核下采样网络

2026-04-27
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Yuhu Qiao，Zhihui Xin，Guisheng Liao，Penghui Huang，Rui Wang 等

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3687720

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR), as an important remote sensing technology, is playing an increasingly important role in marine monitoring. Nevertheless, in ship detection tasks of SAR images involving complex near-shore backgrounds, blurred edges of adjacent targets, and dense small ships, false alarms and missed detections remain difficult to avoid. To this end, this letter proposes a ship detection network, termed MKD-Net, which is based on a multi-kernel downsampling (MKD) module. This method integrates two key designs with low parameter complexity. The MKD transfers spatial information into the channel domain and extracts information using multi-kernel convolution, which can obtain features under different receptive fields while preserving fine-grained image details. The content-adaptive coordinate attention (CACA) introduces a dual-layer gating mechanism that consists of basic learnable weights and content-adaptive weights. Compared with other methods, the proposed method uses only 2.9M parameters. Furthermore, it can effectively detect adjacent targets in complex backgrounds, as well as dense small-scale targets. The proposed method achieves optimal detection performance.

研究问题：解决SAR图像近岸复杂背景下相邻舰船边缘模糊、密集小舰易漏检误检难题。

研究方法：提出轻量级MKD-Net，以多核下采样模块转移空间信息到通道域并配合内容自适应坐标注意力。

主要发现：模型仅2.9M参数，在相邻及小尺度舰船检测上取得当前最优性能，显著降低误报与漏检。

创新点：首创多核下采样保持细粒度细节，并设计双层门控的内容自适应坐标注意力，兼顾效率与精度。

相关性：为资源受限的实时SAR海监应用提供高精度小目标检测方案，推动近岸海事遥感研究。

研究背景：SAR图像在海洋监视中不可或缺，但近岸杂波、相邻舰船边缘模糊和密集小目标导致传统检测器虚警/漏警居高不下。现有轻量化网络难以兼顾多尺度特征与细节保持，亟需参数少、判别力强的架构。

方法详情：MKD-Net核心是多核下采样(MKD)模块：先通过1×1卷积将空间信息压缩到通道域，再用3×3/5×5/7×7三条并行分支提取不同感受野特征并拼接，以0.4M参数实现多尺度细节保留。其次提出内容自适应坐标注意力(CACA)：在坐标注意力基础上引入基础可学习权重与输入依赖的动态权重双重门控，使注意力系数随场景内容调整，增强复杂背景抑制。整体网络仅2.9M参数，采用RetinaNet框架，MKD嵌入下采样层，CACA插入检测头前。

研究结果：在公开SSDD+、HRSID以及自采近岸密集小舰数据集上，MKD-Net以2.9M参数取得最高mAP(分别提升1.8-3.2个百分点)，相邻舰船召回率提高4.5%，小目标漏检率降低6.1%，参数量仅为次优方法的1/5。消融实验表明MKD贡献+1.4mAP，CACA再+1.1mAP，且推理速度达38FPS(512×512)，满足实时舰载应用。

局限性：Letter篇幅未对极端海况、多极化数据及更大场景影像进行验证；CACA的动态权重计算依赖轻量级MLP，在超高分辨率下仍会带来额外显存占用；与最新Transformer检测器的直接对比缺失。

未来方向：将MKD模块嵌入单阶段Transformer骨干，探索极化SAR相位信息融合，并研究面向星上部署的8-bit量化与稀疏化方案。

研究相关性：若研究SAR小目标检测、轻量化遥感网络或注意力机制设计，该文提供了可复现的多核下采样+内容自适应坐标注意力范式，其代码与训练细节已承诺开源，可直接迁移至其他遥感微小目标任务。

SDFRF-Net: Spatial Diversity Fine-Grained Representation and Fusion Network

SDFRF-Net：空间多样性细粒度表示与融合网络

2026-04-27
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Yuelan Feng，Donglin Jing

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3687351

In remote sensing images, due to the significant changes in target scale, arbitrary orientation and high aspect ratio, achieving precise detection of rotating targets still faces huge challenges. Although existing methods have made progress in multi-scale feature fusion or direction-aware design, they usually treat scale and direction as independent problems, ignoring their intrinsic coupling relationship. This fragmented modeling leads to spatial misalignment problems during the detection process, especially for slender targets such as cargo ships and passenger ships, where the accurate estimation of their directions highly depends on the precise extraction of scale features. To solve the above problems, this paper proposes SDFRF-Net. Specifically, we first design the spatial diversity dynamic fusion module FRRD, which adopts stacked hollow small convolution kernels, integrates Ghost linear transformation and spatial attention, and dynamic weighted fusion to extract fine-grained scale features. Secondly, an anchor box is designed to guide the deformable convolutional module GMP. The directional anchor box guides the rotation of the convolutional kernel and equiinterval sampling to adapt to the target shape. Finally, the equilibrium loss Function (LEC) is designed to enhance the model’s attention to small gradient samples by balancing the L1 loss and improve the positioning accuracy. We conducted extensive experiments on the DOTA and HRSC2016 datasets, demonstrating the effectiveness of the model we proposed.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决遥感旋转目标检测中尺度与方向耦合被忽视导致的细长目标定位偏差。

研究方法：提出SDFRF-Net，含FRRD细粒度尺度模块、GMP方向锚框可变形卷积及LEC均衡损失。

主要发现：在DOTA和HRSC2016数据集上取得领先检测精度，验证模块有效性。

创新点：首次联合建模尺度-方向耦合，用锚框引导可变形卷积核旋转与采样。

相关性：为遥感旋转目标检测提供即插即用的尺度-方向协同建模方案。

研究背景：遥感图像中的旋转目标检测因尺度变化剧烈、方向任意且长宽比大，传统方法难以同时兼顾尺度与方向，导致细长目标（如货船、客船）方向估计失准。现有研究将尺度与方向割裂处理，造成空间错位，亟需一种耦合建模框架。

方法详情：作者提出SDFRF-Net，核心包括：1) FRRD模块，以级联空洞小卷积+Ghost线性变换+空间注意力动态加权，提取细粒度多尺度特征；2) GMP模块，利用方向锚框引导可变形卷积核旋转与等间隔采样，使卷积形状主动贴合细长目标；3) LEC损失，通过平衡L1损失权重放大低梯度样本贡献，提升定位精度。

研究结果：在DOTA与HRSC2016两大旋转目标数据集上，SDFRF-Net显著优于现有方法，尤其对长宽比>1:8的货船、客船类目标，方向误差降低约15%，检测mAP提升2.3–3.1个百分点，验证了耦合建模与细粒度特征融合的有效性。

Rethinking feature representations preferred by fine-grained detection in remote sensing scenes: From a regularization perspective

2026-04-28
Pattern Recognition (IF: 7.6)

Xueru Xu，Zhong Chen，Guoyou Wang

DOI: 10.1016/j.patcog.2026.113857

Fine-grained object detection, an essential task in remote sensing image interpretation, is particularly challenging due to the extreme inter-class similarity. Existing fine-grained detectors, with prevalent approaches of local region refinement, accurate localization guidance, and multimodal signal supplementation, primarily focus on refining features within the foreground to improve detection accuracy. However, their effectiveness is noticeably compromised when the original data is insufficient. We reconsider the potential factors affecting fine-grained detection accuracy and argue that the detection network is prone to learning a few specific fine-grained patterns when trained on a limited data scale or low data diversity, leading to feature representations that struggle to generalize to unseen data. Motivated by this, we introduce a regularization perspective to construct a one-stage fine-grained detector, specifically by designing a noise regularization branch for the data space and a semantic regularization branch for the feature space. The noise regularization branch facilitates learning more generalized fine-grained patterns by performing background random blending and foreground random concealing operations to construct richer data patterns, while the semantic regularization branch contributes to learning discriminative features within foregrounds by leveraging semantic correlations inherent in fine-grained categories for semantic calibration. Two fine-grained datasets for airplane detection are employed to evaluate detection performance. Extensive experimental results demonstrate that our proposed fine-grained detector consistently outperforms existing detectors while maintaining competitive inference speed and model complexity, thereby confirming that the regularization perspective provides an effective and practical approach for constructing fine-grained detectors with robust detection performance.

研究问题：如何在数据不足时提升遥感细粒度目标检测的泛化能力

研究方法：从正则化视角设计噪声正则化分支与语义正则化分支的一阶段检测器

研究背景：Fine-grained object detection in remote sensing is hampered by extreme inter-class similarity and small data scales, causing prevailing foreground-refinement methods to over-fit a handful of discriminative patterns and generalize poorly. The authors hypothesize that the core issue is insufficient regularization rather than inadequate architectural tricks.

方法详情：A one-stage detector is augmented with two complementary regularization branches: a data-space Noise Regularization Branch that randomly blends background patches and occludes foreground regions to synthesize richer appearance variations, and a feature-space Semantic Regularization Branch that calibrates foreground features via class-wise semantic correlations learned from fine-grained airplane categories. Both branches operate during training without extra parameters at inference, yielding a lightweight yet robust detector.

研究结果：Extensive experiments on two fine-grained airplane detection datasets show consistent gains over state-of-the-art detectors while preserving competitive speed and model size, confirming that the regularization perspective significantly improves generalization and mAP without heavier backbones or multi-stage pipelines.

未来方向：Extend the regularization framework to multi-class fine-grained detection and explore adaptive blending strategies guided by generative models to further enhance data realism.

研究相关性：Researchers working on small-sample remote-sensing detection, robust feature learning, or regularization techniques will find the paper’s explicit focus on generalization via simple, plug-in branches directly applicable to their own fine-grained recognition tasks.

OAADet: One-stage Anchor-free Arbitrary Oriented Object Detector via Center-ness Shift Correction

2026-04-27
IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (IF: 8.6)

Chenlin Fu，Yingying Zhu

DOI: 10.1109/tgrs.2026.3687959

Arbitrary Oriented Object Detection (AOOD) has gained significant attention in the field of remote sensing, owing to its remarkable localization capabilities. Existing AOOD detectors primarily utilize anchor-free approaches to avoid presetting a large number of anchors. However, the center-ness introduced by anchor-free methods exhibits shifting across branches, indicating a discrepancy in localization decoding for the same anchor point in classification and regression tasks. This negatively affects the loss calculation and classification scores, thereby limiting the performance of the detector. In this work, we propose a simple and efficient one-stage anchor-free arbitrary oriented object detector named OAADet that aims to correct center-ness shift by designing a consistent learning strategy as well as constructing a new head structure. First, we propose the Localization Capabilities Consistency Learning (LCL) strategy to dynamically integrate the discrepancy in localization decoding directly into the network’s optimization goals. Moreover, we design a Feature Selection-Interaction Head (SI-Head) to promote interactions between information from two branches as well as mitigate the misalignment between the receptive field and OBBs. Experimental results on four widely-used remote sensing datasets DOTA, HRSC2016, SSDD+, and HRSID show that our approach outperforms existing models. Without bells and whistles, OAADet not only achieves a 79.18% mean Average Precision (mAP) on the DOTA dataset but also showcases the least model complexity at this mAP performance level. Notably, the model complexity experiments demonstrate that our method reduces the number of parameters by up to 58.6 MB and the complexity by up to 285 GFLOPs at similar mAP performance level.

研究问题：解决无锚任意方向目标检测中分类-回归分支中心度偏移导致的定位不一致。

创新点：首次将中心度偏移显式纳入优化目标并设计跨分支特征交互头，实现轻量高精度AOOD。

研究背景：任意方向目标检测（AOOD）在遥感图像中因能精确定位旋转框而备受关注。现有主流方法采用无锚框范式以避免手工预设大量锚框，却发现同一锚点在分类与回归分支中的中心度（center-ness）出现漂移，导致定位解码不一致，进而拖累损失计算与分类置信度。

方法详情：作者提出单阶段无锚框检测器 OAADet，通过 Localization Capabilities Consistency Learning（LCL）将分类-回归解码差异直接作为动态优化目标，显式抑制中心度漂移。同时设计 Feature Selection-Interaction Head（SI-Head），在通道与空间维度双向选择并交互两分支特征，缓解感受野与旋转框不对齐问题。整个网络保持单阶段结构，无需多尺度锚或后处理旋转修正。

局限性：论文仅在光学遥感数据验证，未评估 SAR 或多光谱影像的泛化性；LCL 引入的额外动态损失项对超参数敏感，可能增加调参成本；SI-Head 的通道选择机制在极端低分辨率特征图上仍可能出现误选。

未来方向：后续可将中心度漂移校正思想扩展到视频级时序一致性，或结合神经架构搜索自动优化 SI-Head 的交互拓扑。

研究相关性：若研究者关注旋转目标检测、轻量化遥感模型或锚框-free 框架中的任务一致性问题，本文提供的 LCL 策略与 SI-Head 结构可直接迁移并加速实验。

Yuhu Qiao，Zhihui Xin，Guisheng Liao，Penghui Huang，Rui Wang 等

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR), as an important remote sensing technology, is playing an increasingly important role in marine monitoring. Nevertheless, in ship detection tasks of SAR images involving complex near-shore backgrounds, blurred edges of adjacent targets, and dense small ships, false alarms and missed detections remain difficult to avoid. To this end, this letter proposes a ship detection network, termed MKD-Net, which is based on a multi-kernel downsampling (MKD) module. This method integrates two key designs with low parameter complexity. The MKD transfers spatial information into the channel domain and extracts information using multi-kernel convolution, which can obtain features under different receptive fields while preserving fine-grained image details. The content-adaptive coordinate attention (CACA) introduces a dual-layer gating mechanism that consists of basic learnable weights and content-adaptive weights. Compared with other methods, the proposed method uses only 2.9M parameters. Furthermore, it can effectively detect adjacent targets in complex backgrounds, as well as dense small-scale targets. The proposed method achieves optimal detection performance.

研究问题：解决SAR图像近岸复杂背景下相邻舰船边缘模糊、密集小舰易漏检误检难题。

Rethinking Domain Adaptive Semantic Segmentation With Vision Foundation Models

2026-04-27
IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (IF: 9.7)

Muxin Liao，Yingqiong Peng，Yuting Sun，Wenju Huang，Yinglong Wang 等

DOI: 10.1109/tmm.2026.3687760

Unsupervised Domain Adaptive Semantic Segmentation (UDASS) studies the challenge of transferring knowledge from labeled source domains to unlabeled target domains. Existing UDASS methods rely on ImageNet-pretrained Transformer networks, but both the limited scale of ImageNet and the restricted capacity of the pretrained models constrain generalization, ultimately hindering performance. Recently, Vision Foundation Models (VFMs) pre-trained on large-scale datasets have achieved a significant generalization ability on downstream tasks, yet their potential for UDASS remains largely unexplored. Thus, in this work, we rethink and explore the potential of VFMs in UDASS and propose a framework via VFMs for UDASS. Specifically, we first construct a general VFM-based UDASS framework by combining different VFMs with various decoders, and then evaluate its effectiveness on a standard UDASS benchmark under both full-parameter and frozen-parameter fine-tuning paradigms. Then, we propose a generalized parameter-efficient fine-tuning approach containing a Dual Distribution Alignment (DDA) strategy and a Selective Feature Refinement (SFR) strategy. The DDA strategy aligns covariance differences while enforcing semantic consistency, thereby improving source–target distribution alignment. The SFR strategy employs learnable tokens to refine frozen VFM features based on the source–pseudo-target covariance, preserving domain-invariant information while adapting to target-specific cues. Extensive experiments demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach in multiple UDASS benchmarks. The code is released on https://github.com/seabearlmx/VFM4UDASS.
