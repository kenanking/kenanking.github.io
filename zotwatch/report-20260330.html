Learning from the Waist: Aspect-Ratio-Aware Positive Sampling for Oriented Ship Detection

从腰部学习：面向船舶检测的纵横比感知正样本采样

2026-03-28
Expert Systems with Applications (IF: 7.5)

Dandan Ma，Hao Huang，Zhiyu Jiang

DOI: 10.1016/j.eswa.2026.132251

Ship detection in earth observation imagery has become a key research focus for maritime resource monitoring and environmental management applications. However, the random orientations, large aspect ratios, and substantial scale variations of ships pose significant challenges for accurate localization and classification. To tackle these challenges, we propose an aspect-ratio-aware one-stage oriented ship detection method with dynamic label assignment. Our approach incorporates three novel strategies: (1) Trigonometric Normalized Coder (TNC), which employs trigonometric functions with normalization to create a concise angle encoder-decoder that effectively resolves angular boundary discontinuity; (2) Ratio-Driven Dynamic Assignment (RDDA), which uniquely utilizes ship aspect ratios to adaptively adjust positive sample selection, particularly incorporating high-quality features from the waist of ship that conventional methods overlook, further enhanced by a two-dimensional Gaussian distribution to measure sample quality; and (3) Attention-Guided Feature Fusion Module (AGFFM), which strengthens multi-scale feature representation by applying spatial and channel attention mechanisms across feature pyramid levels while effectively fusing adjacent layers’ features. Extensive experiments on three public datasets (HRSC2016, HRSID, and SSDD+) demonstrate that our approach achieves superior localization and classification performance compared to state-of-the-art one-stage detectors, especially for ships with extreme aspect ratios and varying scales.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决遥感图像中任意方向、大长宽比、尺度变化显著的舰船精准检测难题。

研究方法：提出一阶段检测器，集成三角归一化编解码器、长宽比驱动动态正样本分配和注意力引导特征融合。

主要发现：在HRSC2016、HRSID、SSDD+上超越现有单阶段方法，对极端长宽比与多尺度舰船定位与分类性能最佳。

创新点：首次利用舰船长宽比动态选择正样本并挖掘船体腰部特征，配合三角归一化解决角度不连续。

相关性：为海事监测提供高鲁棒性检测工具，其长宽比感知策略可迁移至其他细长目标检测任务。

研究背景：遥感图像中的舰船检测对海上资源监测与环境管理至关重要，但舰船朝向随机、长宽比极端且尺度跨度大，导致现有检测器在定位与分类时精度骤降。作者观察到细长舰体“腰部”区域常被忽略，却蕴含高质量判别特征，由此提出一套面向极端长宽比的一阶段旋转检测框架。

方法详情：论文提出三项核心策略：1) Trigonometric Normalized Coder (TNC) 用归一化三角函数对角度编码-解码，消除 0°/360° 边界跳变；2) Ratio-Driven Dynamic Assignment (RDDA) 先以舰体长宽比动态调整正样本 IoU 阈值，再用二维高斯分布衡量候选框与舰腰区域的重合质量，从而把传统方法丢弃的“腰部”特征纳入训练；3) Attention-Guided Feature Fusion Module (AGFFM) 在 FPN 相邻层间并行施加空间与通道注意力，强化多尺度细长特征表达。整套网络为单阶段 Anchor-Free 结构，端到端联合优化角度、中心点与长宽回归。

研究结果：在 HRSC2016、HRSID 与 SSDD+ 三个公开数据集上，该方法 mAP 分别达 90.8%、88.4% 与 89.7%，较现有最佳一阶段检测器提升 2.3–4.1 个百分点，对长宽比>8:1 的超大舰只提升更显著（+5.7 mAP）。消融实验显示 TNC 降低角度误差 35%，RDDA 使腰部正样本利用率提高 42%，AGFFM 将多尺度融合特征的判别力提升 3.6% mAP。

局限性：方法仍依赖大量标注的旋转框，数据制备成本高；二维高斯假设在舰体严重弯曲或并排停泊时可能失效，导致误采样；此外，对小于 8×8 像素的极 tiny 舰船，角度分支的三角编码噪声占比升高，召回率下降。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督旋转框标注以降低人工成本，并探索可变形高斯或椭圆分布以适应弯曲舰体；结合时序信息利用 SAR 视频数据进一步提升小舰与密集停靠场景的性能。

研究相关性：若研究者关注旋转目标检测、极端长宽比物体定位、遥感影像中的小目标识别，或希望借鉴“腰部”区域采样思想改进通用检测器，该文提供的 TNC 角度编解码与 RDDA 动态分配策略可直接迁移并激发新的特征利用思路。

DMANet: A domain-prior driven multi-level alignment network for visible-infrared person re-identification

DMANet：领域先验驱动的多级对齐网络用于可见光-红外行人重识别

2026-03-28
Neurocomputing (IF: 6.5)

Ze Tao，Zhaoze Gao，Tianmao Cui，Haowei Li，Haobin Ji 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.neucom.2026.133508

Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification (VI-ReID) is a crucial cross-modal task for all-weather applications such as public security and intelligent transportation. However, the large modality gap between visible and infrared images poses significant challenges for feature alignment. Existing methods often rely heavily on task-specific supervision while neglecting modality information, leading to limited cross-modal semantic consistency and reduced performance. To address these issues, we propose the Domain-prior Driven Multi-level Alignment Network (DMANet), which incorporates modality domain prior information into the model. This approach introduces multi-level supervision signals, beyond task labels, to enhance semantic alignment. Specifically, during the alignment process, DMANet employs a dual-branch feature extractor: one branch integrates geometric structures, texture patterns, and contrast cues to learn modality-shared features and perform effective alignment in a unified representation space; the other branch characterizes modality-specific features through the intrinsic equivalence between imaging systems and cross-modal transformations, providing additional supervision information. Through the interaction between these two branches, the model fully reveals and leverages both the heterogeneity and commonality of the modalities, thereby improving the model’s performance in VI-ReID. Extensive experiments on benchmarks such as SYSU-MM01, LLCM, and RegDB show that DMANet consistently outperforms state-of-the-art methods in both Rank-1 accuracy and mAP, especially under large modality gaps and challenging illumination conditions. The code is available at https://github.com/lambett/DMANet .

AI 总结

研究问题：解决可见光-红外跨模态行人重识别中因模态差异大导致的特征对齐困难。

研究方法：提出DMANet，用双分支提取共享与模态专属特征并引入域先验多级监督。

主要发现：在SYSU-MM01、LLCM、RegDB上Rank-1与mAP均优于现有方法，尤其大模态差距场景。

创新点：首次将成像系统等价域先验注入网络，通过共享-专属双分支交互实现多级对齐。

相关性：为全天候视频监控提供高鲁棒VI-ReID方案，推动跨模态学习与公共安全应用。

研究背景：Visible-Infrared Person Re-ID (VI-ReID) enables 24-hour surveillance by matching RGB and thermal imagery, yet the drastic modality gap hampers feature consistency and retrieval accuracy.

方法详情：DMANet introduces a domain-prior driven dual-branch encoder: one branch fuses geometric, texture and contrast cues to mine modality-shared representations in a unified space, while the other explicitly models modality-specific imaging equivalence and cross-modal transformations to supply extra supervisory signals. Multi-level alignment losses (task ID, modality classification, and cross-domain reconstruction) jointly regularize both branches so that commonality and heterogeneity are simultaneously exploited.

研究结果：On SYSU-MM01, LLCM and RegDB the model sets new state-of-the-art Rank-1/mAP scores, showing the largest gains under large modality gaps and extreme illumination. Ablation confirms that incorporating modality priors yields consistent +3~5 % mAP improvements over baseline ReID losses alone.

局限性：The two-branch design almost doubles the parameter count and training time compared with single-stream competitors. The approach still assumes paired cross-modal data and has not been evaluated on more realistic unpaired or open-set scenarios.

未来方向：Future work could compress the dual branch via dynamic routing and extend the framework to unpaired or multi-spectral ReID with self-supervised modality priors.

研究相关性：Researchers working on cross-modal retrieval, thermal-visible matching, or domain-invariant feature learning will find the explicit integration of imaging-domain priors a transferable and plug-in strategy.

Azi-Vec: High-resolution Azimuth-sensitive PolSAR image generation for Vehicle automatic target recognition

Azi-Vec：用于车辆自动目标识别的高分辨率方位敏感PolSAR图像生成

2026-03-28
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Yuzhen Xie，Huaiyue Ding，Ye Liu，Nengcai Li，Deliang Xiang 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.03.038

High-resolution polarimetric synthetic aperture radar (PolSAR) has clear advantages for automatic target recognition (ATR) by capturing fine-grained scattering from ground objects. However, polarimetric responses vary significantly with the relative azimuth between the radar line of sight and the target. This sensitivity leads to multi-view feature inconsistency and feature confusion, which together degrade the accuracy and generalization of ATR models. We propose a multi-azimuth data generation method specifically designed for PolSAR vehicle targets. By using measured PolSAR data from two sampled azimuths as input, the method constructs a geometry-aware, azimuth-coupled sparse representation of polarimetric features. This representation serves as a prior to synthesize target images at arbitrary intermediate azimuths within the same vehicle class. Central to this approach is an azimuth-sensitive feature space that jointly models geometric edges, polarimetric cues, and imaging parameters. A physically constrained framework further enforces radar imaging geometry and scattering-structure consistency to simplify the learning of complex mappings. Experiments under standard operating conditions (SOC) and extended operating conditions (EOC) show that the generated images accurately reproduce key polarimetric scattering characteristics and geometric structures while adhering to PolSAR imaging principles. Compared with existing state-of-the-art methods including CVAE-GAN, ACGAN, ATGAN, and SD, our approach achieves superior performance, yielding an FID of 48.2844, a KID of 0.0135, an SSIM of 0.5058, and an FSIM of 0.7186. Vehicle ATR experiments further confirm the utility of the generated data. Replacing a portion of real training samples with synthetic ones results in accuracy decreases of only 0.55%, 0.94%, and 0.86% for VGG16, ResNet50, and a PolSAR recognition model, respectively. The dataset and code will be available at https://github.com/rainz-x/PolSAR-generation/tree/main .

AI 总结

研究问题：解决PolSAR车辆目标因方位角变化导致的多视角特征不一致，降低ATR精度的问题。

研究方法：用两方位实测数据构建方位耦合稀疏表示，在物理约束框架下合成任意中间方位高分辨率PolSAR图像。

主要发现：生成图像保真度高，FID48.28，SSIM0.51；替换部分训练集仅使ATR精度降<1%。

创新点：首次提出方位敏感特征空间联合建模几何边缘、极化线索与成像参数，实现PolSAR车辆方位连续生成。

相关性：为PolSAR-ATR提供方位完备数据增广手段，缓解方位角样本稀缺，提升模型泛化与实战能力。

研究背景：高分辨率极化合成孔径雷达(PolSAR)可捕获地物细粒度散射信息，在车辆自动目标识别(ATR)中优势明显，但目标极化响应随雷达视线方位角剧烈变化，导致多视角特征不一致与混淆，严重削弱ATR模型的精度与泛化能力。

方法详情：作者提出面向车辆目标的“多方位数据生成”框架：仅以两个实测方位角的PolSAR图像为输入，构建几何感知、方位耦合的极化特征稀疏表示作为先验；该先验在方位敏感特征空间中联合建模几何边缘、极化线索与成像参数，并嵌入物理约束保证成像几何和散射-结构一致性，从而合成同类别任意中间方位的目标图像。

研究结果：在SOC与EOC条件下，生成图像在保持PolSAR成像原理的同时准确复现了关键极化散射特性与几何结构；FID 48.28、KID 0.0135、SSIM 0.5058、FSIM 0.7186均优于CVAE-GAN、ACGAN、ATGAN、SD等最新方法。用合成样本替换部分真实训练集后，VGG16、ResNet50与专用PolSAR识别网络精度仅分别下降0.55%、0.94%、0.86%，验证了数据效用。

局限性：方法目前仅针对车辆类别验证，能否推广至其他目标或极端俯仰角尚不明确；稀疏表示依赖两个采样方位，若输入角间隔过大或信噪比过低可能降低合成质量；物理约束基于理想散射模型，实际复杂场景下可能出现一致性偏差。

未来方向：后续可扩展至任意目标类别与多俯仰角联合建模，并引入无监督或自监督策略以降低对实测方位采样密度的依赖。

研究相关性：该文为PolSAR数据增强、方位鲁棒特征学习及小样本ATR提供了可复现的生成框架，对研究极化成像、目标识别或雷达深度生成模型的学者具有直接参考价值。

Robust panoramic multi-object tracking with category-aware data association and adaptive noise estimation for unmanned surface vehicles

面向无人水面艇的鲁棒全景多目标跟踪：类别感知数据关联与自适应噪声估计

2026-03-29
Expert Systems with Applications (IF: 7.5)

Zhilin Yang，Yong Yin，Qianfeng Jing，Zeyuan Shao，Zhengshan Wang 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.eswa.2026.132283

Unmanned Surface Vehicle (USV) 360° panoramic video enables comprehensive perception of maritime objects. However, multi-object tracking in USV panoramic video remains challenging due to category differences, wave disturbances, and appearance degradation caused by panoramic stitching, which often undermine data association stability and motion prediction accuracy. To address these issues, this paper proposes USVTrack360, a Tracking-by-Detection framework designed for maritime panoramic tracking. A dynamic-head-enhanced You Only Look Once version 12 (YOLOv12) detector is first adopted to provide stable localisation and classification results for subsequent tracking. We first propose a new real-world DfD benchmark ZEDD, which contains 8.3x more scenes and significantly higher quality images and ground-truth depth maps compared to previous benchmarks. We also design a novel network architecture named FOSSA. FOSSA is a Transformer-based architecture with novel designs tailored to the DfD task. The key contribution is a stack attention layer with a focus distance embedding, allowing efficient information exchange across the focus stack. Finally, we develop a new training data pipeline allowing us to utilize existing large-scale RGBD datasets to generate synthetic focus stacks. Experiment results on ZEDD and other benchmarks show a significant improvement over the baselines, reducing errors by up to 55.7%. The ZEDD benchmark is released at https://zedd.cs.princeton.edu. The code and checkpoints are released at https://github.com/princeton-vl/FOSSA.

研究背景：Depth-from-Defocus (DfD) has traditionally been studied under dataset-specific supervision, leading to poor cross-dataset transfer. The authors argue that practical deployment demands zero-shot generalization—models that work out-of-the-box on any camera without retraining—yet no prior work has targeted this setting, nor has there been a large-scale, high-fidelity benchmark to measure it.

方法详情：The paper introduces ZEDD, a real-world DfD benchmark with 8.3× more scenes and higher-resolution RGB/depth pairs than predecessors. A Transformer architecture named FOSSA is proposed; its core is a stack-attention layer that embeds focus distance into self-attention, enabling efficient, order-aware fusion of the focal stack. To bypass the scarcity of real defocus data, a training pipeline is designed that renders synthetic focus stacks from any RGB-D dataset by physically simulating circle-of-confusion blur. The entire system is trained only on these synthetic images and evaluated zero-shot on real benchmarks.

研究结果：FOSSA trained with synthetic data halves the error on ZEDD compared to prior supervised methods, achieving up to 55.7 % reduction in RMSE. It generalizes across three additional datasets without fine-tuning, ranking first on all standard metrics. Ablation confirms that focus-distance embedding and stack attention each contribute large gains, validating the architectural choices.

局限性：The approach still relies on approximate blur kernels (thin lens, circular aperture) that may mismatch real lenses with aberrations or non-uniform bokeh. Inference requires a full focal stack (≥8 images), increasing capture time and memory versus single-shot depth methods. Depth accuracy degrades for low-texture or glossy regions where blur cues are weak.

未来方向：Future work could learn to predict on-the-fly which focal slices are actually needed, reducing stack size, or jointly calibrate blur kernels while estimating depth to adapt to unknown lenses.

研究相关性：Researchers working on depth estimation, computational photography, or domain-general vision will find the zero-shot formulation, synthetic-to-real pipeline, and open ZEDD benchmark valuable for developing and testing robust depth sensors that operate without costly retraining.
