SARLO-80: Worldwide Slant SAR Language Optic Dataset 80cm

SARLO-80：全球斜视SAR语言光学数据集（80厘米分辨率）

2026-06-18
arXiv

Solène Debuysère，Nicolas Trouvé，Nathan Letheule，Elise Colin，Georgia Channing

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20523v1

Multimodal foundation models have advanced rapidly thanks to large optical benchmarks, but comparable resources for synthetic aperture radar (SAR) remain limited. Existing SAR--optical datasets largely rely on low-resolution, intensity-only Ground Range Detected~(GRD) products and do not preserve complex-valued SAR measurements or native acquisition geometry, which restricts physically grounded multimodal learning. In particular, large-scale public datasets combining very-high-resolution (VHR) SAR SLC, aligned optical imagery, and natural-language descriptions are still lacking. We present a VHR SAR--optical--text dataset built from open-access Umbra spotlight acquisitions distributed as Sensor Independent Complex Data (SICD). From around 2,500 worldwide scenes (VV/HH, 20cm--2m native resolution), we standardize all SAR data to an 80cm slant-range grid via band-limited FFT resampling and tile the imagery into 1024 by 1024 patches. For each SAR patch, we retrieve a high-resolution optical tile and warp it into the SAR grid using local coordinate correspondences for local pixel-level alignment. We further generate three caption variants (SHORT/MID/LONG) per sample to support vision--language training and evaluation. Our dataset contains 119,566 triplets (complex and amplitude slant-range SAR patch, aligned optical patch, natural-language description) covering 257 locations across 72 countries and a broad range of land types and infrastructures. We release fixed train/validation/test splits and the full preprocessing and baseline code to enable reproducible benchmarks for multimodal alignment on cross-modal retrieval and conditional generation in native SAR geometry. The dataset is publicly available on the Hugging Face Hub at https://huggingface.co/datasets/ONERA/SARLO-80.

研究问题：解决高分辨率SAR SLC与光学图像及文本描述对齐的大规模公开数据集缺失问题。

研究方法：从Umbra SICD数据构建，进行80cm斜距重采样、坐标对应像素对齐并生成三种字幕变体。

主要发现：创建了119,566个三元组，覆盖72国257个地点，含复杂幅度SAR、光学图像与自然语言描述。

创新点：首个结合VHR SAR SLC、对齐光学图像和自然语言描述的大规模公开数据集，保留原生SAR几何。

相关性：为多模态对齐、跨模态检索和条件生成提供可复现基准，推动SAR与视觉语言融合研究。

研究背景：大规模光学基准推动了多模态基础模型的快速发展，但合成孔径雷达（SAR）领域缺乏类似资源。现有SAR-光学数据集多基于低分辨率、仅强度的地面距离检测产品，未保留复数SAR测量或原生获取几何，限制了物理基础的多模态学习。高分辨率SAR SLC与对齐光学图像及自然语言描述的大规模公共数据集仍属空白。

方法详情：基于开放获取的Umbra聚束模式SICD数据（VV/HH极化，原生分辨率20cm-2m），通过带限FFT重采样将所有SAR数据标准化到80cm斜距网格，并切分为1024×1024图块。每个SAR图块通过本地坐标对应获取高分辨率光学图块并变形到SAR网格，实现像素级对齐。为每个样本生成长/中/短三种字幕变体，最终形成119,566个三元组（复数及幅度斜距SAR图块、对齐光学图块、自然语言描述），覆盖72个国家257个地点及多种土地类型与基础设施。

研究结果：构建了首个大规模公开的VHR SAR-光学-文本数据集（SARLO-80），包含119,566个样本，支持跨模态检索与条件生成任务。提供了固定训练/验证/测试划分及完整预处理与基线代码，可实现可重复的多模态对齐基准实验。该数据集保留了原生SAR几何与复数信息，为物理基础的SAR-光学多模态学习提供了关键资源。

局限性：数据来源仅限Umbra卫星聚束模式，可能缺乏其他传感器（如Sentinel-1）的多样性与全局覆盖。80cm斜距网格虽标准化，但可能与某些应用需求（如极地或城市精细分析）的更高分辨率不匹配。字幕自动生成可能存在语义噪声，且未人工验证所有描述的质量。

未来方向：未来可扩展至更多SAR传感器（如TerraSAR-X、COSMO-SkyMed）及不同入射角与极化模式，并探索基于该数据集的跨模态预训练模型（如对比学习或生成式架构），进一步推动SAR图像理解与自然语言接口的融合。

研究相关性：该论文为从事遥感多模态学习、SAR图像处理、视觉-语言联合建模的研究者提供了标准化的基准数据集与代码，尤其适合关注物理对齐、跨模态检索及低资源SAR-光学融合的学者。

FUSE: Frequency-domain Unification and Spectral Energy Alignment for Multi-modal Object Re-Identification

FUSE: 频域统一与频谱能量对齐用于多模态目标重识别

2026-06-18
arXiv

Xuanhao Qi，Tom H. Luan，Yukang Zhang，Jinkai Zheng，Zhou Su 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20044v1

Despite significant progress in multi-modal Re-Identification (ReID), existing methods tend to emphasize low-frequency cues. Consequently, they focus on attributes such as color, illumination, and coarse appearance, while overlooking mid and high-frequency structures that encode geometric, textural, and identity-discriminative details. This imbalance leads to incomplete spectral representations and unstable cross-modal alignment. To overcome these limitations, we introduce FUSE, a frequency-domain framework that reformulates multi-modal ReID as a two-stage process of spectral disentanglement and energy alignment. The proposed Spectral Decomposition Module (SDM) adaptively partitions features into low, mid, and high-frequency subspaces, enabling hierarchical spectral modeling. The Cross-Modal Alignment Module (CAM) further enforces energy alignment and subspace complementarity across modalities via frequency-consistency regularization. In addition, FUSE incorporates learnable frequency modulation to enhance robustness under varying illumination and heterogeneous sensor conditions. Extensive experiments on RGBNT201, RGBNT100, and MSVR310 show that FUSE achieves 9.1\% mAP and 9.5\% Rank-1 improvements, establishing an interpretable frequency-domain paradigm for multi-modal representation learning.

AI 总结

研究问题：多模态行人重识别中忽视中高频结构信息导致表征不完备和跨模态对齐不稳定。

研究方法：提出频域框架FUSE，包含频谱解耦模块和跨模态对齐模块，实现分层频谱建模与能量对齐。

主要发现：FUSE在三个数据集上mAP提升9.1%，Rank-1提升9.5%，优于现有方法。

创新点：首次将多模态ReID建模为频谱解耦与能量对齐的两阶段频域过程。

相关性：为多模态表示学习提供可解释的频域范式，提升复杂光照和传感器条件下的鲁棒性。

研究背景：现有的多模态ReID方法主要关注低频线索（如颜色、光照和粗略外观），忽略了编码几何、纹理和身份判别细节的中高频结构，导致频谱表示不完整和跨模态对齐不稳定。

方法详情：FUSE提出了一种频域框架，将多模态ReID重构为频谱解缠和能量对齐的两阶段过程。具体地，频谱分解模块（SDM）自适应地将特征划分为低、中、高频子空间，实现分层频谱建模；跨模态对齐模块（CAM）通过频率一致性正则化强制能量对齐和子空间互补性，同时引入可学习频率调制以增强在不同光照和异质传感器条件下的鲁棒性。

研究结果：在RGBNT201、RGBNT100和MSVR310三个数据集上，FUSE在mAP和Rank-1指标上分别提升了9.1%和9.5%，建立了可解释的频域范式用于多模态表示学习。

局限性：该方法可能依赖于特定的频带划分策略，对不同模态的噪声敏感性未充分讨论；此外，可学习频率调制增加了模型复杂度，可能在实际部署中带来计算开销。

未来方向：未来可探索更灵活的频带自适应划分机制，以及将频域分析与时序或空间信息结合以扩展至视频或点云多模态ReID场景。

研究相关性：该论文为多模态ReID提供了全新的频域视角，其频谱解缠和能量对齐思想可推广至其他跨模态任务（如图文检索、红外-可见光匹配），对研究频域表示学习和模态对齐的研究者具有重要参考价值。

Multimodal Concept Bottleneck Models

多模态概念瓶颈模型

2026-06-18
arXiv

Tongqing Shi，Ge Yan，Tuomas Oikarinen，Tsui-Wei Weng

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19882v1

Concept Bottleneck Models (CBMs) enhance the interpretability of deep learning networks by aligning the features extracted from images with natural concepts. However, existing CBMs are constrained in their ability to generalize beyond a fixed set of predefined classes and the risk of non-concept information leakage, where predictive signals outside the intended concepts are inadvertently exploited. In this paper, we propose Multimodal Concept Bottleneck Model (MM-CBM) to address these issues and extend CBMs into CLIP. MM-CBM utilizes dual Concept Bottleneck Layers (CBLs) to align both the image and text embeddings into interpretable features. This allows us to perform new vision tasks like zero-shot classification or image retrieval in an interpretable way. Compared to existing methods, MM-CBM achieves up to 51.26% accuracy improvement on average across four standard benchmarks. Our method maintains high accuracy, staying within ~5% of black-box performance while offering greater interpretability.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决现有概念瓶颈模型泛化能力有限及非概念信息泄露的问题。

研究方法：提出MM-CBM，利用双概念瓶颈层对齐图像和文本嵌入到可解释特征。

主要发现：在四个标准基准上平均准确率提升高达51.26%，精度接近黑盒模型（~5%内）。

创新点：将概念瓶颈模型扩展到CLIP，支持零样本分类和图像检索等新任务。

相关性：提升深度学习模型可解释性与泛化，对可解释AI和视觉语言模型研究有价值。

研究背景：Concept Bottleneck Models (CBMs) enhance interpretability by aligning image features with natural concepts, but they are limited to predefined classes and suffer from non-concept information leakage. This paper introduces Multimodal Concept Bottleneck Model (MM-CBM) to extend CBMs into CLIP, enabling interpretable zero-shot classification and image retrieval.

方法详情：MM-CBM employs dual Concept Bottleneck Layers (CBLs) to align both image and text embeddings into interpretable features, leveraging CLIP's multimodal capabilities. It performs new vision tasks like zero-shot classification or image retrieval in an interpretable way, while using a training process that reduces information leakage and improves generalizability.

研究结果：MM-CBM achieves up to 51.26% accuracy improvement on average across four standard benchmarks compared to existing methods. It maintains high accuracy within ~5% of black-box performance while offering greater interpretability, demonstrating a strong trade-off between performance and transparency.

局限性：The method still relies on predefined concept sets for bottleneck layers, which may limit flexibility in capturing novel concepts. Additionally, the dual CBL architecture could introduce extra computational overhead, and the interpretability benefits may diminish for highly abstract tasks.

未来方向：Future work could explore dynamic concept selection or hierarchical concept structures to adapt to diverse tasks. Extending MM-CBM to other modalities (e.g., audio or video) and investigating its robustness against adversarial attacks are also promising directions.

研究相关性：This paper is highly relevant for researchers working on interpretable AI, multimodal learning, and concept bottleneck models, as it provides a novel framework that combines CLIP's generalization with interpretable bottlenecks, enabling new applications in zero-shot and retrieval tasks.

Multi-Modal Contrastive Learning for Implicit Earth Embeddings via Location Tying

通过位置绑定的隐式地球嵌入的多模态对比学习

2026-06-18
arXiv

Jonathan Hecht，Lukas Arzoumanidis，Ziyue Li，Youness Dehbi

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20167v1

Spatial prediction tasks are often limited by a lack of high-quality labelled ground-truth observations. To overcome this challenge, self-supervised pre-training is a possible solution, with contrastive learning dominant for location encoders. Those approaches usually align geographic coordinates with just one additional modality. We propose two multimodal contrastive learning architectures: Multimodal Embedding via Location Tying (MELT) and Sequential Alternating Location Training (SALT). These architectures expand this framework beyond two modalities by utilising unpaired geospatial data. Both methods are technically viable and match the performance of the strongest two-modality baseline (SATCLIP) across four downstream tasks. However, increasing the number of modalities does not consistently improve performance, suggesting that the chosen location encoder is the main limitation - the contrastive objective reaches its peak early, regardless of modality diversity or pre-training volume. MELT provides more stable training than SALT and presents a stronger foundation for future scaling.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何利用多模态未配对地理空间数据改进自监督位置编码预训练？

研究方法：提出MELT和SALT两种多模态对比学习架构，通过位置绑定对齐多种模态。

主要发现：增加模态数不持续提升性能，位置编码器容量是主要瓶颈，MELT训练更稳定。

创新点：将对比学习从双模态扩展到多模态，利用未配对数据实现位置嵌入学习。

相关性：为缺乏标签的地理空间预测任务提供可扩展的自监督预训练新范式。

Using a synthetic generator-separator pipeline, we define ground-truth latent features, build tasks with tunable sparsity and overlap, and introduce measurable quantities for representation strength and superposition (directional overlap among features).
