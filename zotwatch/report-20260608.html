T-SAR-JEPA: Self-Supervised Temporal Anomaly Detection in SAR Amplitude Stacks via Latent Prediction

T-SAR-JEPA: 基于潜在预测的SAR幅度堆栈自监督时间异常检测

2026-06-04
arXiv

Kerod Woldesenbet，Abem Woldesenbet

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05700v1

We present T-SAR-JEPA, a self-supervised framework for temporal anomaly detection in SAR amplitude stacks via latent prediction. A ViT-Base/16 encoder from SAR-JEPA is domain-adapted on 39,300 Capella patches using local masked reconstruction with gradient feature prediction. A temporal transformer with sinusoidal time encoding forecasts future latent states from K=7 acquisitions, with progressive unfreezing substantially reducing validation loss. The model operates on amplitude alone; InSAR coherence serves exclusively as independent pseudo-ground-truth. On the DFC 2026 dataset (300 time-series, three AOIs), T-SAR-JEPA achieves ROC-AUC of 77.0% on the Hawaii eruption window, outperforming RX, PaDiM, Linear AR, and LSTM baselines (~50%). Spatial coherence of 99.9% (p < 0.001, permutation test) confirms structured detections. Code: https://github.com/TerraLatent/t-sar-jepa

研究背景：合成孔径雷达（SAR）时序异常检测在灾害监测等领域具有重要意义，但传统监督方法依赖大量标注数据，且SAR幅度数据本身缺乏明确的真值标签。本研究旨在探索自监督学习方法，通过域适应和时序预测来实现无需标签的异常检测，克服数据标注瓶颈。

方法详情：提出T-SAR-JEPA框架，首先使用SAR-JEPA的ViT-Base/16编码器，在39,300个Capella影像块上进行域适应，采用局部掩码重建和梯度特征预测。然后构建时序Transformer，结合正弦时间编码，从K=7次历史采集中预测未来潜在状态，并通过渐进解冻策略显著降低验证损失。模型仅使用幅度数据，以独立生成的InSAR相干性作为伪真实标签进行验证。

研究结果：在DFC 2026数据集的三个感兴趣区域共300个时间序列上，T-SAR-JEPA在夏威夷火山喷发窗口取得77.0%的ROC-AUC，显著优于RX、PaDiM、线性AR和LSTM等基线方法（约50%）。空间一致性检验显示99.9%的检测具有空间结构（p<0.001，置换检验），表明异常并非随机噪声。

局限性：该研究仅使用Capella卫星的幅度数据，且伪真值依赖InSAR相干性的独立性假设可能引入偏差。实验仅在一个数据集上进行，泛化性尚未验证。此外，模型需要连续时间序列输入，对缺失或不规则采样数据的鲁棒性未知。

未来方向：未来可探索将模型扩展到其他SAR传感器（如Sentinel-1）和多模态数据融合，同时研究如何在更少的时间步长下仍保持检测性能，并引入不确定性估计来增强异常判据的可解释性。

Towards SAR-to-optical image translation via perception correlative learning and global-local feature collaboration

基于感知相关学习与全局-局部特征协作的SAR到光学图像翻译

2026-06-06
Pattern Recognition (IF: 7.6)

Yu Chen，Weida Zhan，Yichun Jiang，Depeng Zhu，Xiaoyu Xu 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.patcog.2026.114186

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) enables all-weather, day-and-night Earth observation, but it suffers from speckle noise and geometric distortions that hinder human visual interpretation. SAR-to-optical (S2O) image translation can enhance the interpretability of SAR images. However, existing methods often neglect scene complexity, leading to color distortion and detail loss, which degrade both visual quality and semantic consistency. To address these issues, this paper proposes the S2O image translation method based on perception correlative learning and global-local feature collaboration (PCGL-GAN). Specifically, the perception correlative learning is introduced to model perceptual similarity in the feature space, thereby improving visual consistency. The global-local cascade extractor is further designed to enable collaboration learning of global structures and fine-grained textures, enhancing the model’s ability to restore structure and preserve details in high-density scenes. In addition, the cross-scale collaboration compensation is proposed, which jointly exploits frequency-domain features and spatial attention to achieve deep multi-scale fusion and semantic refinement. Extensive experimental results demonstrate that the proposed PCGL-GAN achieves competitive overall performance on the SEN1-2, QXS-SAROPT, and SAR2Opt datasets. It consistently improves multiple evaluation metrics, while a certain degree of trade-off remains among different metrics, reflecting its balanced performance under a multi-objective optimization setting. The code and models are available on GitHub .

研究问题：解决SAR到光学图像翻译中的颜色失真和细节丢失问题。

研究方法：提出PCGL-GAN，采用感知关联学习、全局局部特征协作及跨尺度补偿。

主要发现：在多个数据集上提升多项指标，实现视觉与语义一致性的平衡。

创新点：感知关联学习建模特征相似性，全局局部级联提取器与跨尺度协作补偿。

相关性：增强SAR图像可解释性，对遥感视觉理解研究具有重要价值。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达（SAR）能够实现全天候、昼夜地球观测，但受斑点噪声和几何畸变影响，人类视觉解译困难。SAR到光学（S2O）图像转换可增强SAR图像的可解释性，但现有方法常忽略场景复杂性，导致颜色失真和细节丢失，降低视觉质量和语义一致性。因此，本文提出基于感知相关学习和全局-局部特征协作的S2O图像转换方法（PCGL-GAN）来解决这些问题。

方法详情：本文提出PCGL-GAN方法，包含三个核心模块：首先，引入感知相关学习（perception correlative learning）在特征空间中建模感知相似性，提升视觉一致性；其次，设计全局-局部级联提取器（global-local cascade extractor），实现全局结构与细粒度纹理的协作学习，增强模型在高密度场景中恢复结构和保留细节的能力；最后，提出跨尺度协作补偿（cross-scale collaboration compensation），联合利用频域特征和空间注意力实现深度多尺度融合与语义精炼。

研究结果：在SEN1-2、QXS-SAROPT和SAR2Opt数据集上的大量实验表明，PCGL-GAN在多个评估指标上取得了具有竞争力的整体性能，并在多目标优化下实现了平衡表现，但不同指标间仍存在一定程度的权衡。该方法在视觉质量和语义一致性上优于现有方法，有效缓解了颜色失真和细节丢失问题。

局限性：尽管PCGL-GAN在多个指标上表现优异，但不同指标间存在权衡，可能难以在所有场景下同时达到最优。此外，方法对高密度复杂场景的细节恢复能力仍有提升空间，且未深入讨论模型在不同SAR数据源（如不同分辨率或极化模式）上的泛化性能。

未来方向：未来可探索更高效的感知相关性建模方式，并引入自监督学习或弱监督方法以减少对配对数据的依赖；同时可研究自适应跨模态特征融合策略以进一步提升在极端地物场景（如城市密集区）下的翻译质量。

研究相关性：对于从事遥感图像处理、跨模态翻译、生成对抗网络（GAN）应用的学者，本文提出了一种结合感知相似性与全局-局部特征协作的新框架，在SAR-光学图像转换领域提供了有价值的技术方案，其多尺度融合和注意力机制的设计思路也可推广至其他图像翻译任务。

Dr.MEA: Leveraging large language model to enhance multi-modal entity alignment

Dr.MEA：利用大型语言模型增强多模态实体对齐

2026-06-06
Knowledge-Based Systems (IF: 7.6)

Jizhao Zhu，Quanxin Jin，Xiang Li，Xinlong Pan，Lei Lin

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2026.116264

Multi-modal entity alignment (MMEA) aims to identify entities representing the identical real-world object across two multi-modal knowledge graphs (MMKGs), serving as a crucial and challenging task in MMKG fusion. Prior methods rely on the integration of various uni-modal representations of entities within MMKGs, and employ similarity-based measures (e.g., Euclidean distance or cosine) to determine whether entity pairs from different MMKGs refer to the same real-world object. However, these methods suffer from two key limitations: i ) If there is a lack of semantic similarity between the uni-modal features of two entities, the fused cross-modal representations struggle to achieve high semantic matching; ii ) Using a simple similarity-based calculation for entity alignment seriously lacks deep reasoning. To address these issues, we propose Dr.MEA, a novel M MEA method enhanced by large language model (LLM) , leveraging the powerful generation and d eep r easoning capabilities of LLM. First, by taking advantage of the structural, relational, attribute, and image information of entities within the MMKG, we further employ LLM to generate holistic and coherent textual descriptions of entities from a global perspective, compensating for the limitations and fragmentation of uni-modal information. Second, we design a multi-turn conversational deep reasoning mechanism based on LLM to enhance the reliability of alignment results. Extensive experiments on public benchmark datasets demonstrate that Dr.MEA significantly outperforms existing works, outperforming state-of-the-art performance in MMEA, with an average improvement of 13.03% on the Hits@1 metric. In addition, it has significant advantages especially in few-shot scenarios. Our code is available at https://github.com/quanquan01/Dr.MEA .

研究问题：解决多模态实体对齐中语义相似性不足和缺乏深度推理的问题。

研究方法：利用大语言模型生成全局文本描述，并设计多轮对话深度推理机制。

主要发现：在Hits@1上平均提升13.03%，尤其在小样本场景表现优异。

创新点：首次将LLM的生成与深度推理能力用于增强多模态实体对齐。

相关性：为知识图谱融合和多模态对齐提供新范式，提升准确性和鲁棒性。

研究背景：多模态实体对齐（MMEA）旨在跨两个多模态知识图谱中识别指向同一真实世界对象的实体，是多模态知识图谱融合的关键任务。现有方法依赖单模态特征融合和相似度计算，但在语义不相似或缺乏深度推理时性能受限。

方法详情：Dr.MEA利用大语言模型（LLM）的生成与深度推理能力：首先，整合实体的结构、关系、属性和图像信息，由LLM生成全局一致的文本描述以弥补单模态信息的碎片化；其次，设计基于多轮对话的深度推理机制，通过逐步推理增强对齐结果的可靠性。

研究结果：在公开基准数据集上，Dr.MEA显著优于现有方法，在Hits@1指标上平均提升13.03%，达到最先进水平。尤其在少样本场景下优势突出，验证了LLM用于多模态实体对齐的有效性。

局限性：LLM的生成和推理过程可能引入幻觉或偏见，影响描述准确性；依赖大量计算资源，对实时或大规模应用存在效率瓶颈。此外，多轮对话推理的鲁棒性尚未充分验证。

未来方向：未来可探索更高效的LLM微调策略以减少推理开销，并引入跨模态对齐的显式约束以缓解幻觉问题。

研究相关性：该研究为多模态知识图谱融合提供了LLM驱动的创新范式，对从事实体对齐、多模态学习及知识图谱推理的研究者具有重要参考价值。

TEVI: Text-Conditioned Editing of Visual Representations via Sparse Autoencoders for Improved Vision-Language Alignment

TEVI：通过稀疏自编码器实现文本条件视觉表示编辑以改进视觉-语言对齐

2026-06-05
arXiv

Sweta Mahajan，Sukrut Rao，Jiahao Xie，Alexander Koller，Bernt Schiele

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07451v1

Vision-language models such as CLIP are highly useful for diverse tasks due to their shared image-text embedding space. Despite this, the image and text embeddings are often poorly aligned, affecting downstream performance. Recent work has shown that this can be attributed to an information imbalance: images contain more information than their captions describe. In this work, we propose TEVI, a framework that uses captions as a signal for what to retain from image embeddings. Specifically, we use sparse autoencoders to disentangle image embeddings and train a masking module to selectively reconstruct the embedding based on a given caption. In a controlled setup with synthetic captions, we show that TEVI is effective at preserving caption-described attributes while discarding others. By applying TEVI to CLIP models trained on natural images, we further achieve improved retrieval performance across coarse-grained short-caption (MS COCO, Flickr) and fine-grained long-caption (IIW, DOCCI) benchmarks, with stronger gains on richer captions, and improved robustness on the RoCOCO benchmark.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何改善视觉-语言模型中图像与文本嵌入的对齐问题？

研究方法：使用稀疏自编码器解耦图像嵌入，训练遮蔽模块根据文本描述选择性重建。

主要发现：TEVI有效保留文本描述属性，提升检索性能，尤其对丰富描述效果更佳。
