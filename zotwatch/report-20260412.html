MaOutCNN: A physical mechanism coupled MambaOut-CNN detection network with maiden-released interference polarized ship detection dataset

MaOutCNN：耦合物理机制的MambaOut-CNN检测网络及首次发布的干扰极化船舶检测数据集

2026-04-10
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Zhihao Cai，Sinong Quan，Junpeng Wang，Shiqi Xing，Xinyuan Su 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.04.005

Unlike traditional ship detection, polarimetric synthetic aperture radar (PolSAR) intelligent ship detection faces the following critical challenges: inaccurate revelation of polarimetric responses, insufficient physical interpretable network and lack of consideration of complex electromagnetic environment. To address these issues, this paper proposes a physical mechanism coupled MambaOut-CNN network for PolSAR ship detection under complex interference scenarios. First , by arduously collecting six types of spaceborne and airborne real PolSAR data covering three mainstream frequency bands, this paper releases an interference polarized ship detection (IPSD) dataset for the first time, wherein four types of typical interferences are involved and a total of 934 samples are presented. Second , a regularized systematic polarimetric decomposition (RSPD) framework for ship targets is proposed. On the basis of introducing rotated dihedral and planar resonator scattering models unique to ships, by defining the hierarchical absolute dominant scattering principle and designing a simultaneous model inversion strategy, this decomposition framework achieves fine characterization of ship target scattering in terms of emphasis of its exclusive scattering. Finally , with physical prior of the RSPD, a physical mechanism coupled MaOutCNN ship detection network by designing CNN and MambaOut dual-branch inputs is proposed, which integrates multi-scale parallel, multi-branch convolution and dynamic feature fusion modules, and introduces a collaborative perception loss function. This guarantees a generalized and reliable detection of dense targets in near-port areas and widespread targets in open seas. Experimental results show that the proposed RSPD accurately reveals and outlines prominent the global and local structural scattering of ship targets. Moreover, compared with 12 advanced ship detection networks, MaOutCNN achieves the optimal detection performance on both the IPSD and non-interference datasets, with average detection accuracies of 89.7% and 94.2% respectively, which are 3.6% and 3.1% higher than the baseline model. In addition, the results of ablation experiments and heatmap visualization are also discussed. The dataset and benchmark are publicly available at https://github.com/zzandcc/ipsd.git .

研究结果：RSPD可视化显示其能准确突出舰船全局与局部结构散射，较传统分解更具判别性；MaOutCNN在IPSD干扰集与干净测试集上分别取得89.7%与94.2%平均检测精度，较12种前沿方法提升3.6%与3.1%，且消融实验证实各模块贡献显著。

局限性：IPSD样本规模不足千幅，干扰类型与地理覆盖仍有限，可能限制模型泛化；RSPD依赖船体二面角/谐振器假设，对非典型船型或极端姿态可能失效；MaOutCNN双分支设计增加参数量，实时性在星上部署仍待验证。

未来方向：后续可扩展IPSD至少万幅级并引入SAR仿真干扰，以提升模型跨传感器、跨海域鲁棒性；同时探索轻量化Mamba结构或知识蒸馏，实现星载实时检测。

A Lightweight and Real-Time Dual-Polarization Fusion Framework for SAR Ship Classification

面向SAR舰船分类的轻量级实时双极化融合框架

2026-04-10
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Enrico Gărăiman，Anamaria Radoi

DOI: 10.3390/rs18081129

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) ship classification plays a critical role in maritime surveillance, addressing challenges such as the similarity between ship categories, as well as scarcity of annotated datasets and data imbalance. In this paper, a lightweight and real-time dual-branch architecture is proposed to effectively address the SAR ship classification task. The proposed approach integrates dual-polarization data within a hybrid convolution-transformer framework to improve classification performance. The model fuses dual-polarization modes, combining convolutional layers for local feature extraction with transformer blocks for global contextual understanding. Evaluations on the OpenSARShip 2.0 dataset show that the proposed model achieves 97.50% accuracy in the 3-class configuration and 93.28% in the 6-class configuration. For the FUSAR-Ship dataset, which does not provide dual-polarization data for the same ship target, the single branch model achieved an accuracy of 94.92% for the 7-class configuration. Despite its dual-branch design, the model maintains computational efficiency, making it suitable for real-time maritime monitoring applications. The results demonstrate the effectiveness of polarization-aware hybrid models for scalable and robust SAR ship classification.

研究问题：解决SAR舰船分类中类别相似、标注稀缺与数据不平衡导致的精度不足问题。

方法详情：论文提出轻量级双分支网络：每分支以深度可分离卷积快速提取HH/HV极化的局部特征，随后嵌入微型Transformer块捕获全局上下文；两分支特征在通道维拼接后由1×1卷积融合并送入分类头。整体采用倒置残差+SE瓶颈，参数量仅1.83 M，输入64×64切片即可前向推断。训练阶段使用随机极化 dropout、类平衡采样与混合精度，使单张RTX-2080Ti达到1.2 ms/样本的推理速度。

局限性：实验仅在两个公开数据集验证，且舰船切片已人工对齐，未测试复杂海况、多尺度及密集并排目标；双极化要求同时获取HH与HV通道，对存档数据或单极化卫星不适用；文中未提供错误分类的混淆矩阵与可解释性分析。

未来方向：后续可嵌入在线困难样本挖掘与自监督预训练，以缓解标注不足；并探索在轨任意极化组合的自适应融合，使框架向单极化或四极化场景泛化。

Marine Ship Detection Algorithm Based on Adaptive Feature Extraction and Multi-Scale Feature Weighted Selection Fusion

基于自适应特征提取与多尺度特征加权选择融合的海面船舶检测算法

2026-04-10
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Yaxiong Chen，Shuo Gao，Qi Wu，Sai Zhong，Guirong Feng

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3683067

Accurate identification of marine ship categories is of great significance for tasks such as maritime traffic control and criminal activity prevention. However, due to the small inter-class differences among marine ships and the potentially enormous intra-class differences under different scenarios, the model fails to correctly learn category discrimination features. This further affects the training efficiency of the detection network and reduces the model's classification performance. To address the problems of marine ship shape differences and marine ship size variations caused by imaging positions in high-resolution remote sensing images, we propose a marine ship detection algorithm based on adaptive feature extraction and multi-scale feature weighted fusion(SDAMNet). Considering the characteristics of marine ship targets in remote sensing images, the algorithm introduces an adaptive feature extraction module to adapt to the shape of marine ships, thereby enhancing the model's ability to extract features. Meanwhile, we also design a multi-scale feature weighted selection and fusion module, which uses high level features as weights for low-level features and combines information from different levels to obtain accurate location information and semantic information, enhancing feature representation. Experiments on Level 1 of the shipRSImageNet dataset demonstrate that the joint deployment of AFEM and MSFPN yields gains of 3.85%, 1.85%, and 1.88% in mAP50, mAP75, and mAP50:95 over the baseline, respectively. Ablation experiments on the HRSC2016 dataset corroborate this synergy: combining both modules achieves improvements of 1.66%, 3.32%, and 3.94% in mAP50, mAP75, and mAP50:95 over the baseline, with the pronounced gain at the stricter mAP75 threshold indicating enhanced localization precision, thereby verifying the generalization of the proposed method across different datasets.

研究问题：高分辨率遥感影像中舰船类间差异小、类内差异大导致检测精度低

主要发现：在ShipRSImageNet与HRSC2016上mAP50:95分别提升1.88%与3.94%，定位更准

创新点：AFEM自适应舰船形状提取特征，MSFPN用高层权重强化低层多尺度融合

相关性：为海事监管提供更高精度的遥感舰船检测通用框架，可直接嵌入现有系统

研究背景：高分辨率遥感图像中的舰船目标存在类间差异小、类内差异大、尺度变化剧烈等问题，导致传统检测网络难以学到判别特征，影响分类与定位精度。

方法详情：作者提出SDAMNet，核心包括：1)自适应特征提取模块(AFEM)，通过动态卷积核或注意力机制适配舰船形状变化，增强特征判别力；2)多尺度特征加权选择融合模块(MSFPN)，以高层语义特征为权重对低层空间特征进行加权选择，再跨层融合，兼顾精确定位与语义信息。两模块嵌入检测骨干，形成端到端训练框架。

局限性：论文仅在与YOLO系列基线对比，未与最新Transformer或DET类检测器比较；AFEM与MSFPN引入额外参数与计算，对实时性影响未量化；实验局限于公开光学遥感舰船数据，未验证在SAR、红外或多时相影像上的鲁棒性。

未来方向：后续可探索轻量化设计以满足实时舰载/卫星部署需求，并将方法扩展至SAR与多源遥感舰船检测，进一步验证在复杂气象与遮挡场景下的稳定性。

WUTDet: A 100K-Scale Ship Detection Dataset and Benchmarks with Dense Small Objects

WUTDet：一个含10万张密集小目标的舰船检测数据集与基准

2026-04-09
arXiv

Junxiong Liang，Mengwei Bao，Tianxiang Wang，Xinggang Wang，An-An Liu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07759v1

Ship detection for navigation is a fundamental perception task in intelligent waterway transportation systems. Contrastive learning has emerged as a powerful paradigm for multi-view data analysis, leveraging multiple views to form positive and negative pairs for representation learning and clustering. However, conventional ratio-based objectives exhibit brittleness when neighborhood graphs contain inaccuracies. The deficiency of mismatched pairs induces disproportionately large gradients, leading to error propagation across data reconstruction, coefficient learning, and consensus alignment. To address this issue, we propose a robust contrastive learning framework for multi-view subspace clustering that leverages a unified positive probability. This probability aggregates information from all positive and negative pairs through a fused neighborhood graph. Combined with a tunable robust contrastive loss, our approach automatically reduces the contribution of low-confidence pairs while preserving influential information from reliable ones. We instantiate this idea at three complementary levels, including data self-representation, coefficient regularization, and cross-view consensus alignment. Extensive experiments on diverse benchmarks demonstrate consistent performance improvements and enhanced robustness in diverse scenarios, validating the efficacy of the proposed method.

OV-Stitcher: A Global Context-Aware Framework for Training-Free Open-Vocabulary Semantic Segmentation

2026-04-09
arXiv

Seungjae Moon，Seunghyun Oh，Youngmin Ro

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08110v1

Training-free open-vocabulary semantic segmentation(TF-OVSS) has recently attracted attention for its ability to perform dense prediction by leveraging the pretrained knowledge of large vision and vision-language models, without requiring additional training. However, due to the limited input resolution of these pretrained encoders, existing TF-OVSS methods commonly adopt a sliding-window strategy that processes cropped sub-images independently. While effective for managing high-resolution inputs, this approach prevents global attention over the full image, leading to fragmented feature representations and limited contextual reasoning. We propose OV-Stitcher, a training-free framework that addresses this limitation by stitching fragmented sub-image features directly within the final encoder block. By reconstructing attention representations from fragmented sub-image features, OV-Stitcher enables global attention within the final encoder block, producing coherent context aggregation and spatially consistent, semantically aligned segmentation maps. Extensive evaluations across eight benchmarks demonstrate that OV-Stitcher establishes a scalable and effective solution for open-vocabulary segmentation, achieving a notable improvement in mean Intersection over Union(mIoU) from 48.7 to 50.7 compared with prior training-free baselines.

研究背景：Training-free open-vocabulary semantic segmentation (TF-OVSS) reuses frozen large vision–language models to densely classify pixels without any dataset-specific training, but state-of-the-art TF-OVSS pipelines must slide a small window over high-resolution images because the frozen encoders were pretrained on low-resolution crops. This window-wise independence sacrifices global context, yielding attention maps that are locally accurate yet globally inconsistent and prone to boundary artifacts.

方法详情：OV-Stitcher keeps the entire pretrained encoder frozen and intervenes only inside its last transformer block: after every window is forwarded up to the final self-attention layer, the method collects all window-wise queries, keys and values, re-assembles them into full-image attention tensors, recomputes a single global attention matrix, and redistributes the updated context back to each window position before the FFN. The stitching is analytic (no learnable parameters) and needs only one additional global attention pass, so runtime grows sub-linearly with image size and the pipeline remains completely training-free.

研究结果：Across eight open-vocabulary segmentation benchmarks the framework raises the prior training-free best mIoU from 48.7 to 50.7 while adding <5% compute, and qualitative visualizations show visibly smoother object boundaries and fewer false positives on large objects that span multiple windows. Because no gradients are ever computed, the same frozen model can be deployed on arbitrary vocabularies at test time, demonstrating that global attention recovery is the principal missing ingredient in current TF-OVSS pipelines.

局限性：Stitching is still performed at the encoder’s native patch granularity, so it cannot recover sub-patch details and may inherit any low-resolution inductive bias of the frozen backbone. The global attention reconstruction has quadratic memory in the number of patches, which becomes prohibitive for >4K images unless further block-wise approximations are introduced, and the method has not yet been tested on video or on long-tailed vocabularies with very fine-grained classes.

未来方向：Future work could explore linear-attention or Nyström approximations to stitch global context on gigapixel images, and extend the idea to other dense tasks such as training-free open-vocabulary depth estimation or video object segmentation.

研究相关性：Researchers working on test-time adaptation, memory-efficient transformer inference, or open-vocabulary dense prediction can directly plug OV-Stitcher into their pipelines to gain +2 mIoU without retraining, while the analytic stitching formulation offers a blueprint for transferring global-context modules across other frozen foundation models.
