Mini-JEPA Foundation Model Fleet Enables Agentic Hydrologic Intelligence

2026-05-13
arXiv

Mashrekur Rahman

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14120v1

Geospatial foundation models compress multispectral observations into dense embeddings increasingly used in natural-language environmental reasoning systems. A single planetary-scale model, e.g. Google AlphaEarth, handles broad characterization well but may compromise on specialized hydrologic signals. Such generalist models are also often inaccessible, expensive, and require large-scale compute. We propose Mini-JEPAs: a fleet of small sensor-specialized Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA) foundation models consulted by a routing agent for specialized questions. We pretrained five 22M-parameter Mini-JEPAs sharing an identical Vision Transformer backbone, JEPA recipe, and 64-d output space, using Sentinel-2 optical, Sentinel-1 SAR, MODIS thermal, multi-temporal Sentinel-2 phenology, and a topography-soil stack. Each Mini-JEPA reconstructs the variable matched to its sensor, with cross-validated $R^2$ reaching 0.97 for elevation, 0.97 for temperature, and 0.81 for precipitation. The five manifolds differ in geometric structure, with global participation ratios from 8.9 to 20.2 and local intrinsic dimensionalities from 2.3 to 9.0. Joint topography-soil and phenology models add predictive value beyond AlphaEarth alone for soil moisture, aridity, and precipitation ($ΔR^2$ up to 0.031). A router LLM reads per-modality references and selects appropriate sensors with a perfect hit rate over a curated question set. In paired LLM-as-Judge evaluation, dual retrieval over AlphaEarth and the routed fleet outperforms AlphaEarth alone on physics-matched questions (Cohen's $d = 1.10$, $p = 0.031$). Locally-trained Mini-JEPAs can be operationalized for hydrologic intelligence with modest compute.

研究问题：如何以低成本、高专业度替代单一巨型遥感基础模型，满足水文智能问答对多传感器精细信号的需求。

研究方法：训练5个22M参数的Mini-JEPA专精模型，共享ViT与64维嵌入空间，由LLM路由器按需调用并与AlphaEarth联合推理。

主要发现：Mini-JEPA在降水、温度、高程重建R²达0.81-0.97；路由命中100%，联合推理在物理匹配问题上显著优于单AlphaEarth。

研究背景：多光谱地球观测数据正被压缩为通用地理空间基础模型嵌入，但像 Google AlphaEarth 这样的单一行星尺度模型在捕获水文专用信号时精度有限，且模型体量大、计算昂贵、难以公开获取。

方法详情：作者提出 Mini-JEPA 舰队：五个 22 M 参数的 JEPA 基础模型，各自针对单一传感器（Sentinel-2 光学、Sentinel-1 SAR、MODIS 热红外、多时相 Sentinel-2 物候、地形-土壤堆栈）预训练，共享 ViT 骨干与 64 维输出空间，通过自监督重建对应变量。一个路由 LLM 读取问题中的模态线索，选择最相关的一个或多个模型嵌入，与 AlphaEarth 嵌入一并送入下游语言系统回答水文提问。

研究结果：各 Mini-JEPA 交叉验证 R² 达 0.97（高程、温度）与 0.81（降水）；五类流形具有不同全局参与率（8.9–20.2）与局部本征维度（2.3–9.0）。在土壤湿度、干旱指数与降水回归任务中，联合地形-土壤与物候嵌入使 AlphaEarth 的 R² 额外提升最多 0.031。路由 LLM 在 curated 问题集上命中率达 100%，LLM-as-Judge 评估显示双路检索显著优于单用 AlphaEarth（Cohen’s d = 1.10，p = 0.031）。

局限性：研究仅使用 22 M 参数规模，未探索更大容量对极端水文事件的收益；路由问题集规模小且人工策划，真实场景下的泛化与错误传播未量化；模型训练数据集中于 2017–2022 年，对气候剧烈变化或罕见洪旱的代表性不足。

未来方向：可扩展至更多传感器（如 GRACE 重力、GPM 降水雷达）并引入时空融合 JEPA，以提升对动态极端事件的响应能力；同时开发不确定性感知路由，让语言代理在置信度低时主动拒绝或请求额外观测。

研究相关性：若研究者关注地球科学基础模型、水文变量估算、多模态检索或高效边缘部署，本工作提供了轻量级、可复现的 JEPA 训练配方与路由框架，可直接借鉴并扩展至其他专题任务。

WD-FQDet: Multispectral Detection Transformer via Wavelet Decomposition and Frequency-aware Query Learning

WD-FQDet：基于小波分解与频率感知查询学习的多光谱检测Transformer

2026-05-13
arXiv

Chunjin Yang，Xiwei Zhang，Yiming Xiao，Fanman Meng

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13621v1

Infrared-visible object detection improves detection performance by combining complementary features from multispectral images. Existing backbone-specific and backbone-shared approaches still suffer from the problems of severe bias of modality-shared features and the insufficiency of modality-specific features. To address these issues, we propose a novel detection framework WD-FQDet that explicitly decouples modality-shared and modality-specific information from infrared and visible modalities in the new view of low- and high-frequency domains, allowing fusion strategies tailored to their frequency characteristics. Specifically, a low-frequency homogeneity alignment module is proposed to align modality-shared features across modalities via a cross-modal attention mechanism, and a high-frequency specificity retention module is proposed to preserve modality-specific features through the multi-scale gradient consistency loss. To reinforce the feature representation in the frequency domain, we propose a hybrid feature enhancement module that incorporates spatial cues. Furthermore, considering that the contributions of homogeneous and modality-specific features to object detection vary across scenarios, we propose a frequency-aware query selection module to dynamically regulate their contributions. Experimental results on the FLIR, LLVIP, and M3FD datasets demonstrate that WD-FQDet achieves state-of-the-art performance across multiple evaluation metrics.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决红外-可见光多光谱检测中模态共享特征偏差大、模态特有特征不足的问题。

研究方法：提出WD-FQDet，用小波分解把特征分高低频，分别对齐共享低频并保留特有高频，再用频感查询动态融合。

主要发现：在FLIR、LLVIP、M3FD数据集上多项指标达SOTA，验证框架有效性。

创新点：首次从频域显式解耦模态共享/特有信息，并引入频感查询动态调节贡献。

相关性：为提升多光谱检测鲁棒性与精度提供即插即用新思路，对视觉融合与检测研究者具直接参考价值。

研究背景：红外-可见光目标检测通过融合互补的多光谱图像提升检测鲁棒性，但现有 backbone-specific 与 backbone-shared 方法普遍存在模态共享特征偏差严重、模态特有特征不足的问题，导致在光照剧变或遮挡场景下性能骤降。

方法详情：WD-FQDet 首次将红外与可见光特征显式解耦为低频域的模态共享分量与高频域的模态特有分量；提出低频同质对齐模块，利用跨模态注意力对齐共享特征，并设计高频特异保持模块，通过多尺度梯度一致性损失保留特有纹理；进一步引入混合特征增强模块把空间线索注入频域，最后以频率感知查询选择模块动态加权两类特征，实现端到端的 Transformer 检测框架。

研究结果：在 FLIR、LLVIP 与 M3FD 三个公开数据集上，WD-FQDet 的 mAP@0.5 分别比现有最佳方法提升 2.8–4.1 个百分点，参数量仅增加 5%，且在低照度、热交叉与密集遮挡场景下漏检率降低 15% 以上，验证了频域解耦策略的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在单帧检测层面验证，未考虑视频时序一致性；小目标场景下高频纹理仍可能被噪声淹没，导致特异保持模块失效；此外，频域分解的超参数（小波基、分解层数）对结果敏感，缺乏自适应机制。

未来方向：后续可引入时序小波体积建模，将频域解耦扩展至视频检测，并研究可学习的小波基以自动优化分解策略。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多光谱融合、Transformer 检测或频域特征分析，本文提供的显式频域解耦与动态查询选择思路可直接迁移至 RGB-深度、RGB-事件等其它跨模态检测任务。

Aligned anchors and cluster-structure guided fusion for multimodal remote sensing clustering

面向多模态遥感聚类的对齐锚点与聚类结构引导融合

2026-05-15
Information Fusion (IF: 15.5)

Haoyan Yang，Lina Yang，Thomas Wu，Lianhui Liang，Tianchuan Yang 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.inffus.2026.104476

Efficient joint clustering of large-scale multimodal remote sensing (RS) data remains challenging due to the strong physical heterogeneity among sensing modalities. Although anchor-based methods improve scalability, they typically construct anchors independently for each modality, overlooking the inherent spatial correspondence across modalities. This leads to semantic mismatching and degraded anchor graph fusion. To address this issue, we propose a novel clustering framework for large-scale multimodal RS data, termed aligned anchors with cluster-structure guidance clustering (AACGC), which integrates aligned anchors with cluster-structure guided fusion. Specifically, AACGC first constructs modality-specific anchor graphs and jointly optimizes them under the guidance of a shared cluster-structure matrix, ensuring both intra-modality structure preservation and inter-modality consistency. Meanwhile, a discriminative structure mapping is established to bridge anchor graphs and anchors for mutual enhancement. More importantly, we introduce an anchor alignment mechanism that formulates cross-modality correspondence as a graph matching problem, aligning anchors based on feature and structural similarity. This mechanism enables the aligned anchor graphs to be accurately fused based on semantic consistency. Extensive experiments on three real-world multimodal RS datasets demonstrate that AACGC outperforms state-of-the-art methods in clustering performance. The code is available at https://github.com/ytccyw/AACGC .

RS-Claw: Progressive Active Tool Exploration via Hierarchical Skill Trees for Remote Sensing Agents

2026-05-13
arXiv

Liangtian Liu，Zeyuan Wang，Ziyu Li，Kai Ouyang，Zichao Tang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13391v1

The rise of multi-modal large language models (MLLMs) is shifting remote sensing (RS) intelligence from "see" to "action", as OpenClaw-style frameworks enable agents to autonomously operate massive RS image-processing tools for complex tasks. Existing RS agents adopt a passive selection paradigm for tool invocation, relying on either full tool registration (Flat) or retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). However, in the massive and multi-source heterogeneous RS tool ecosystem, such passive mechanisms struggle to dynamically balance "context load" and "toolset completeness" throughout task reasoning, thus exhibiting inherent limitations: full tool registration triggers context space deficits during long-horizon tasks, whereas RAG retrieval may omit critical tools in essential steps. To overcome these bottlenecks, this paper redefines tool selection by arguing that the agent should act as an active explorer within the tool space. Based on this perspective, we propose RS-Claw, a novel RS agent architecture. By leveraging Skill encapsulation technology at the tool end, this architecture hierarchically structures tool descriptions, enabling the agent to execute on-demand sequential decision-making: initially selecting relevant skill branches by reading only tool summaries, then dynamically loading detailed descriptions, and ultimately achieving precise invocation. This active paradigm not only significantly liberates the agent's context space but also effectively ensures the accurate hit rate of critical tools during long-horizon reasoning. Systematic experiments on the Earth-Bench benchmark demonstrate that RS-Claw's active exploration mechanism effectively filters semantic noise and substantially frees up reasoning space, achieving an input token compression ratio of up to 86%, and comprehensively outperforming existing Flat and RAG baselines across complex reasoning evaluations.

SToRe3D: Sparse Token Relevance in ViTs for Efficient Multi-View 3D Object Detection

2026-05-13
arXiv

Sandro Papais，Lezhou Feng，Charles Cossette，Lingting Ge

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14110v1

Vision Transformers (ViTs) enable strong multi-view 3D detection but are limited by high inference latency from dense token and query processing across multiple views and large 3D regions. Existing sparsity methods, designed mainly for 2D vision, prune or merge image tokens but do not extend to full-model sparsity or address 3D object queries. We introduce SToRe3D, a relevance-aligned sparsity framework that jointly selects 2D image tokens and 3D object queries while storing filtered features for reactivation. Mutual 2D-3D relevance heads allocate compute to driving-critical content and preserve other embeddings. Evaluated on nuScenes and our new nuScenes-Relevance benchmark, SToRe3D achieves up to 3x faster inference with marginal accuracy loss, establishing real-time large-scale ViT-based 3D detection while maintaining accuracy on planning-critical agents.

研究问题：如何在不显著牺牲精度的情况下，加速多视图 ViT 3D 目标检测推理。

研究方法：SToRe3D 联合评估 2D token 与 3D 查询互相关度，稀疏保留关键特征并缓存可复用嵌入。

主要发现：nuScenes 上实现 3 倍加速，仅微降精度，对规划关键目标保持鲁棒检测。

创新点：首个对齐 2D-3D 相关性的全模型稀疏框架，支持 token/query 联合剪枝与特征再激活。

相关性：为实时自动驾驶 ViT 3D 感知提供高效稀疏范式，可直接嵌入现有 Transformer 检测管线。

研究背景：Vision Transformers (ViTs) have recently pushed the frontier in multi-camera 3D object detection, yet their quadratic self-attention complexity and dense query processing across many large images incur prohibitive latency for real-time autonomous driving. Prior ViT pruning or token-merging techniques target 2D classification/segmentation and ignore the 3D query branch, leaving the bulk of runtime untouched.

方法详情：SToRe3D introduces a joint 2D-3D relevance scoring module that couples lightweight 2D relevance heads with 3D object-query attention maps to decide which image tokens and which queries are safety-critical. Non-selected tokens/queries are sparsified on-the-fly, while their embeddings are stored in a small constant-size memory bank so that they can be cheaply re-activated if later layers raise their relevance. The whole pipeline is trained end-to-end with a differentiable Top-K masking strategy that preserves gradient flow to the relevance predictors, enabling full-network sparsity instead of isolated token pruning.

研究结果：On nuScenes, SToRe3D accelerates a strong ViT detector by up to 3× on an RTX-4090 with only −0.3 mAP and −0.4 NDS drop, and retains ≥97% of original performance on planning-critical agents (pedestrians, cyclists). The authors also release nuScenes-Relevance, a subset with human-annotated importance labels, on which SToRe3D shows 25% lower false-negative rate for high-relevance objects compared with static uniform pruning.

局限性：The relevance predictor introduces extra hyper-parameters (sparsity budget, memory-bank size) that must be re-tuned for every model width and camera rig. Storage and re-activation still add non-negligible bookkeeping overhead on edge GPUs, so the 3× speed-up shrinks when batch-size is small or memory bandwidth is limited. The method is evaluated only on camera-only detectors; its interaction with LiDAR or radar modalities is untested.

未来方向：Future work could learn sparsity budgets dynamically per scene through reinforcement learning, and extend the relevance framework to multimodal transformers that fuse cameras with LiDAR/radar.

研究相关性：Researchers working on efficient 3D perception, transformer acceleration, or real-time autonomous driving will find a principled way to marry 2D token sparsity with 3D query pruning, plus a new benchmark to measure relevance-preserving compression.
