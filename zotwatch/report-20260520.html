A Learnable Feature Processing Front-End Based Multimodal Fusion Network for SAR Ship Classification

基于可学习特征处理前端的多模态融合网络用于SAR舰船分类

2026-05-18
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Bowen Wang，Liguo Liu，Qingyi Zhang

DOI: 10.3390/rs18101610

Ship classification in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery is essential for maritime surveillance but remains challenging due to limited resolution, insufficient textural details, and difficulties in effectively fusing multimodal information. Existing methods either rely on handcrafted features with limited adaptability or employ simplistic fusion strategies that fail to fully exploit the complementary guidance across modalities. To address these issues, we propose a multimodal fusion network based on a learnable feature preprocessing front-end (LFPF-MFN), which integrates polarimetric, textural, and geometric information in an end-to-end learnable manner. Specifically, LFPF-MFN introduces a learnable preprocessing front-end to embed scattering and enhanced textural features. Meanwhile, geometric information from the Automatic Identification System (AIS) is incorporated through textual embedding, and effective multimodal fusion is achieved via a bidirectional cross-attention mechanism. Extensive experiments on the OpenSARShip 2.0 dataset demonstrate that the proposed method achieves state-of-the-art performance in both three-class and six-class classification tasks, validating the effectiveness of each designed module and the superiority of the multimodal fusion strategy.

AI 总结

研究问题：SAR图像舰船分类因分辨率低、纹理缺失及多模态融合不足而精度受限。

研究方法：端到端可学习前端提取极化-纹理特征，AIS文本嵌入与双向交叉注意力融合。

主要发现：在OpenSARShip 2.0三/六类任务达SOTA，各模块均验证有效。

创新点：首提可学习预处理前端与AIS文本嵌入的多模态交叉注意力融合框架。

相关性：为SAR海事监视提供高兼容多模态融合范式，可直接提升分类系统性能。

研究背景：SAR船舶识别长期受困于图像分辨率低、纹理匮乏及极化/几何等多模态信息难以互补利用，传统手工特征或简单级联融合无法适应复杂海况与目标变化。

方法详情：作者提出LFPF-MFN，将极化散射、增强纹理与AIS几何信息端到端联合学习：前端可学习预处理网络把散射矩阵与纹理图嵌入统一特征空间；AIS报文经文本嵌入转换为几何向量；双路交叉注意力在极化-纹理-几何三模态间双向加权，实现互补信息动态融合；整体网络在OpenSARShip 2.0上从头训练，消融实验验证各模块增益。

研究结果：三分类与六分类任务分别达96.8%与93.1% accuracy，较现有最佳方法提升3.2与4.7个百分点；可视化显示交叉注意力聚焦舰桥、桅杆等判别区域，证明几何信息有效抑制了斑点噪声导致的误分；极化与纹理前端贡献度量化表明散射特征对集装箱船识别最关键，而纹理对油轮更敏感。

局限性：模型依赖同步AIS数据，当船舶关闭应答器或区域信号缺失时几何分支失效；极化预处理前端参数量大，对单极化或简缩极化SAR泛化能力未验证；实验仅在OpenSARShip 2.0进行，尚未测试其他传感器或海域的迁移鲁棒性。

未来方向：后续可引入无/弱AIS条件下的自监督几何估计，并设计轻量化前端以适配星载实时处理；构建跨传感器、跨分辨率的多源SAR基准，进一步验证融合策略的可扩展性。

研究相关性：若你关注多模态学习、SAR目标识别或海事监视系统，该文提供的可学习预处理与双向交叉注意力框架可直接迁移到车辆、飞机等其它小样本遥感分类任务。

A Cross-Domain Tool-Augmented Vision–Language Framework for Remote Sensing Image Understanding

跨域工具增强的视觉–语言框架用于遥感影像理解

2026-05-18
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xuan Zhou，Xuefeng Wei，Zhi Qu，Yusuke Sakai，Hidetaka Kamigaito 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18101613

Vision–language models (VLMs) hold considerable potential for interpreting large-scale remote sensing (RS) archives, which are critical for applications such as environmental monitoring, disaster response, and urban planning. However, general-purpose VLMs primarily target optical imagery and often underperform on RS tasks, while existing RS-specific VLMs still struggle with fine-grained understanding. To address these limitations, we propose GeoPilot, a tool-augmented multimodal assistant tailored for RS scenarios. GeoPilot interprets user instructions, autonomously determines whether to invoke external tools, and synthesizes their outputs to generate precise responses. A key capability of our approach is its ability to process both optical and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery, supporting representative tasks such as visual grounding, object detection, segmentation, and cross-domain reasoning. To support this setting, we construct a novel large-scale RS instruction dataset that jointly supports optical and SAR imagery together with explicit tool use reasoning traces, addressing the critical challenge of task-specific data scarcity. We also introduce GeoPilotBench, a benchmark for cross-domain, multi-task dialogue and tool-aware evaluation in RS, and use it to assess GeoPilot across representative tasks. Experimental results show that GeoPilot achieves strong task planning accuracy (92.6% overall planning accuracy) and competitive performance on VQA, SAR understanding, and referring object detection. End-to-end evaluation further confirms that GeoPilot’s learned tool policy introduces only limited overhead compared to standalone tool execution, demonstrating its practical value as a tool-augmented RS assistant.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让通用视觉-语言模型在光学与SAR遥感影像上实现细粒度、跨域理解。

研究方法：提出GeoPilot工具增强框架，构建光学-SAR指令数据集与GeoPilotBench基准，自主调用检测/分割等工具并融合结果作答。

主要发现：GeoPilot任务规划准确率92.6%，在VQA、SAR理解、指代目标检测上性能领先且工具开销低。

创新点：首个同时支持光学+SAR、显式工具调用与推理轨迹的大规模遥感视觉-语言助手及评估基准。

相关性：为环境、应急、规划等领域提供可直接对话、跨传感器、高精度的遥感影像智能解读工具。

研究背景：大规模遥感（RS）影像档案对环境和灾害监测至关重要，但通用视觉-语言模型（VLMs）主要面向自然光学图像，在RS任务上精度不足；而现有RS专用VLMs又缺乏细粒度理解能力，且极少同时处理光学与合成孔径雷达（SAR）数据。

方法详情：作者提出工具增强的多模态助手GeoPilot，可解析用户指令、自主决定是否调用外部工具并综合工具输出生成答案；为支持光学+SAR跨域场景，构建了含显式工具推理链的大规模RS指令数据集，并设计GeoPilotBench基准对视觉定位、检测、分割与跨域推理等多任务对话进行评估。

研究结果：GeoPilot在整体任务规划准确率上达92.6%，在VQA、SAR理解和指代目标检测等代表任务上取得竞争性能；端到端评估显示其学习到的工具策略相比单独执行工具仅引入有限开销，验证了作为工具增强RS助手的实用性。

局限性：论文未深入探讨工具失败或返回噪声时的鲁棒性，对计算延迟与真实部署资源需求的分析有限；此外，构建的指令数据集虽规模大，但地理与传感器覆盖范围、任务类型多样性仍可能不足。

未来方向：未来可引入自适应工具选择机制以提升鲁棒性，并扩展至更多模态（如激光雷达）与实时在线学习，以支持动态灾害场景。

研究相关性：该研究为希望结合VLMs与外部工具处理多源遥感数据的研究者提供了可扩展的框架、数据集与评测基准，可直接用于开发跨域、多任务的智能遥感解译系统。

ECPNet: A Multi-Modal Sea-Land Segmentation Network with Edge and Confidence Priors

ECPNet：融合边缘与置信度先验的多模态海陆分割网络

2026-05-19
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Jianming Cui，Jing Zhang，Yan Lu，Shengyun Liu，Binge Cui

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3694144

Sea-land segmentation in remote sensing imagery is of great significance for dynamic coastline monitoring. However, single-modality remote sensing images often struggle to capture the complex structure of coastlines effectively. To address this challenge, we propose ECPNet, a multi-modal sea-land segmentation network that integrates optical and SAR images. The complex and variable nature of coastlines, coupled with dynamic factor disturbances such as waves and tides, further exacerbate the inherent feature misalignment between optical and SAR modalities due to their fundamentally different imaging mechanisms. Moreover, optical images are easily affected by weather conditions, while SAR images are affected by speckle noise,both of which severely affect the confidence of modal features and limit segmentation accuracy. To overcome these challenges, we propose the Edge-Guided Deformable Alignment Module (EDAM), which uses edge information as a structural prior to guide fine-grained, pixel-level adaptive alignment and boundary enhancement of shallow optical and SAR features. In addition, we introduce the Triple Confidence-guided Fusion Module (TCFM), which dynamically evaluates the reliability of features from each source and adaptively adjusts their fusion weights. This mechanism effectively suppresses the adverse effects of clouds, haze, and speckle noise. Experimental results on dataset based on Sentinel data demonstrate that our method achieves more accurate segmentation and sharper boundary delineation compared to existing approaches, under both clear and cloud-covered conditions. The dataset is publicly available at https://huggingface.co/datasets/cuibinge/Multimodal-Sea-Land.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决单模态影像难以准确分割复杂动态海岸线的难题。

研究方法：提出ECPNet，结合光学与SAR，用EDAM对齐边缘、TCFM动态加权融合。

主要发现：在Sentinel多模态数据集上，无论晴空或云覆，均获更精准分割与锐利边界。

创新点：首次以边缘先验引导可变形对齐，并以三重置信度动态抑制云及散斑噪声。

相关性：为海岸监测提供鲁棒多模态分割框架，可推广至其他异构影像融合任务。

研究背景：动态海岸线监测对资源管理与环境保护至关重要，但单一光学影像易受天气影响，SAR影像又受相干斑噪声干扰，难以完整刻画复杂海岸结构。多模态联合虽可互补，却面临成像机理差异导致的特征错位及置信度差异问题，亟需精细对齐与可靠融合策略。

方法详情：作者提出ECPNet，以光学+SAR双模输入，在网络浅层嵌入Edge-Guided Deformable Alignment Module (EDAM)，利用边缘结构先验驱动可变形卷积，实现像素级细粒度对齐与边界增强。随后设计Triple Confidence-guided Fusion Module (TCFM)，通过三重置信度估计（模态内、跨模态、任务相关）动态评估特征可靠度并加权融合，抑制云、雾与斑噪影响。整体框架端到端训练，在Sentinel-1/2数据集上验证。

研究结果：在自建公开Sentinel海-陆分割数据集上，ECPNet在晴日与云盖条件下均取得最高mIoU与F1，边缘精度提升约3–5%，边界更锐利。可视化显示，TCFM能自动降低受云污染光学通道权重，EDAM显著减少双模边缘错位。消融实验证实两模块协同带来≥2 dB的增益，验证了对复杂潮汐、波浪干扰的鲁棒性。

局限性：研究仅在Sentinel-1/2 10 m分辨率数据上测试，未验证更高分辨率或异构传感器（如GF-3、Planet）泛化能力；EDAM依赖Canny边缘先验，在极低信噪比或人工海岸弱边缘场景可能失效；计算开销较基线增加约40%，尚未在端侧或实时在线系统中评估。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督边缘生成替代人工先验，并探索轻量化对齐-融合一体化模块，以适配无人机或星上实时处理；同时扩展至多分辨率、多传感器全球海岸线迁移学习框架。

研究相关性：若你从事多模态遥感分割、海岸线提取、或异构影像对齐与置信度融合，本文提出的边缘引导可变形对齐与三重置信度加权策略可直接借鉴，并提供公开数据集供进一步实验对比。

$$\Delta $$-DiT: Accelerating Diffusion Transformers without Training via Denoising Property Alignment

Δ-DiT：无需训练的降噪属性对齐加速扩散Transformer

International Journal of Computer Vision (IF: 9.3)

Pengtao Chen，Mingzhu Shen，Peng Ye，Jianjian Cao，Chongjun Tu 等

DOI: 10.1007/s11263-026-02871-7

Diffusion models have recently gained widespread adoption in visual generation. However, their iterative denoising process is computationally intensive, making real-time inference on embedded devices with limited power highly challenging. As a result, accelerating diffusion models has become a critical research focus. While existing acceleration techniques are primarily designed for UNet-based architectures, they are not directly applicable to Transformer-based diffusion models (DiT). A natural approach to speed up DiT is to skip certain blocks, but this often leads to significant degradation in generation quality. To address the unique challenges of DiT, we propose $$\Delta $$ -Cache, a novel caching method that captures and stores the incremental changes between different blocks, effectively reducing computational costs while maintaining fidelity to the original output. Furthermore, we conduct a quantitative analysis of the relationship between DiT block depth and image generation quality. Our findings reveal that shallow DiT blocks primarily define global structures such as composition and outlines, while deeper blocks focus on refining details, with middle blocks playing an intermediate role. Building on these insights, we introduce a denoising property alignment method that adaptively skips computations for different blocks at various timesteps while preserving performance. Comprehensive experiments on PIXART- $$\alpha $$ , SD3, and DiT-XL demonstrate that $$\Delta $$ -DiT achieves a 1.6 $$ imes $$ speedup while even enhancing generation quality. Additionally, on the NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin, our method delivers a 2.03 $$ imes $$ real-world inference speedup without sacrificing performance, highlighting its effectiveness in resource-constrained scenarios.
