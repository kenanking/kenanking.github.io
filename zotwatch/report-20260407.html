SARES-DEIM: Sparse Mixture-of-Experts Meets DETR for Robust SAR Ship Detection

SARES-DEIM：稀疏混合专家与DETR结合的稳健SAR舰船检测

2026-04-05
arXiv

Fenghao Song，Shaojing Yang，Xi Zhou

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04127v1

Ship detection in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery is fundamentally challenged by inherent coherent speckle noise, complex coastal clutter, and the prevalence of small-scale targets. Conventional detectors, primarily designed for optical imagery, often exhibit limited robustness against SAR-specific degradation and suffer from the loss of fine-grained ship signatures during spatial downsampling. To address these limitations, we propose SARES-DEIM, a domain-aware detection framework grounded in the DEtection TRansformer (DETR) paradigm. Central to our approach is SARESMoE (SAR-aware Expert Selection Mixture-of-Experts), a module leveraging a sparse gating mechanism to selectively route features toward specialized frequency and wavelet experts. This sparsely-activated architecture effectively filters speckle noise and semantic clutter while maintaining high computational efficiency. Furthermore, we introduce the Space-to-Depth Enhancement Pyramid (SDEP) neck to preserve high-resolution spatial cues from shallow stages, significantly improving the localization of small targets. Extensive experiments on two benchmark datasets demonstrate the superiority of SARES-DEIM. Notably, on the challenging HRSID dataset, our model achieves a mAP50:95 of 76.4% and a mAP50 of 93.8%, outperforming state-of-the-art YOLO-series and specialized SAR detectors.

研究问题：解决SAR图像中相干斑噪声、沿海杂波与小尺度舰船目标检测难题

研究方法：在DETR框架内引入稀疏MoE路由至频域/小波专家，并设计Space-to-Depth增强金字塔保持高分辨率特征

主要发现：HRSID数据集mAP50:95达76.4%，mAP50达93.8%，超越YOLO系列与专用SAR检测器

创新点：提出SAR感知的稀疏专家选择模块SARESMoE，联合SDEP颈网络抑制噪声并精确定位小目标

相关性：为SAR舰船检测提供高效鲁棒的Transformer方案，可启发雷达图像目标识别研究

研究背景：SAR成像固有的相干斑噪声、近岸杂波和大量弱小舰船目标，使传统基于光学图像设计的检测器在迁移到SAR场景时鲁棒性骤降，且连续下采样易丢失精细舰船特征。

方法详情：作者提出SARES-DEIM框架，将DETR范式扩展至SAR域；核心为SARESMoE模块，通过稀疏门控把特征路由给频域与小波专家，以抑制斑噪与杂波并保持计算高效。配合Space-to-Depth Enhancement Pyramid (SDEP)颈部，将浅层高分辨率空间线索注入深层，显著改善小目标定位。整体采用端到端集合预测损失，无需锚框与NMS。

研究结果：在HRSID等两个基准上，SARES-DEIM取得mAP50:95 76.4%与mAP50 93.8%，超越YOLO系列及专用SAR检测器，验证了对斑噪抑制与小目标召回的显著增益。

局限性：论文仅验证于公开光学-类SAR数据集，未覆盖大斜视、高海况等极端成像条件；稀疏MoE引入额外参数量，对星载实时部署的功耗与内存开销尚待评估。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督预训练以利用海量未标注SAR数据，并探索量化-蒸馏协同优化，实现星上实时检测。

研究相关性：该文将稀疏MoE与DETR结合应对SAR特有退化的思路，为研究SAR小目标检测、噪声抑制或Transformer目标检测的研究者提供可直接借鉴的架构与实验基准。

IRSD-DETR: A lightweight real-time detection transformer for infrared ship detection

IRSD-DETR：轻量级实时红外舰船检测Transformer

2026-04-06
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Pengqiang Ge，Guohua Gu，Weixian Qian，Xiaofang Kong，Qian Chen 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.03.048

Infrared ship detection (IRSD) plays an important role in many applications such as thermal remote sensing for maritime safety and surveillance. However, its detection performance often degrades due to small target size, insufficient feature extraction and complex background interference, leading to frequent false alarms and missed detections. In addition, existing detection transformer (DETR)-based models suffer from heavy network parameters and high computational complexity. To solve these issues, we propose a lightweight real-time detection transformer for IRSD, termed IRSD-DETR. Firstly, four lightweight wavelet-based transform blocks (WTBs) significantly decrease the network parameters and computational complexity while maintaining decent detection accuracy and enhancing the multi-scale representation of infrared (IR) ships. Secondly, within the effective hybrid encoder (EHE), the attention-based intra-scale feature interaction adaptive sparse self-attention (AIFI_ASSA) module effectively suppresses noisy interactions from irrelevant areas while retaining the most informative features of IR ships. The lightweight relative position enhanced self-attention mechanism (RPESAM) module expands the receptive field without introducing extra computational costs. The pinwheel-shaped convolution (PSConv) substitutes the standard convolution, significantly reducing the loss of fine details of IR ships. Lastly, the normalized Gaussian Wasserstein distance (NWD) further enhances the stability and accuracy of bounding box regression for IRSD. Compared with the baseline RT-DETR-r18, our IRSD-DETR model achieves a 42.7% reduction in network parameters, a 58.7% reduction in floating point operations (FLOPs), a 1.9% increment in precision, a 2.0% increment in recall, a 2.0% increment in F1 score, a 2.4% increment in mAP@0.5(%), and a 0.8% increment in mAP@0.5:0.95(%). Our codes are available at https://github.com/MinjieWan/IRSD-DETR .

AI 总结

研究问题：解决红外舰船检测中小目标难提取、背景干扰强且DETR模型参数量大、计算量高的问题。

研究方法：构建轻量WTB骨干、EHE编码器（AIFI_ASSA+RPESAM+PSConv）并用NWD回归的IRSD-DETR。

主要发现：相比RT-DETR-r18，参数量↓42.7%，FLOPs↓58.7%，精度、召回、F1、mAP均提升1.9-2.4%。

创新点：首次将轻量小波变换块、自适应稀疏自注意、轮形卷积与NWD结合，实现实时红外舰船检测。

相关性：为资源受限平台提供高精度实时红外舰船监视方案，推动海事安全与遥感应用落地。

研究背景：红外舰船检测（IRSD）是海上遥感安全与监视的关键环节，但目标尺寸小、特征匮乏及复杂背景导致误报和漏检率高。现有基于DETR的检测器虽精度好，却参数量大、计算复杂，难以在实时或边缘场景部署。

方法详情：作者提出IRSD-DETR，用四个轻量小波变换块WTB取代标准卷积，压缩参数量并增强多尺度红外特征。有效混合编码器EHE内含AIFI_ASSA模块，通过自适应稀疏自注意力抑制无关区域噪声；RPESAM以相对位置编码扩大感受野且零额外计算；PSConv风车形卷积减少细粒度细节丢失。检测头引入归一化高斯Wasserstein距离NWD，提升小目标框回归稳定性。

研究结果：在自采/公开红外舰船数据集上，IRSD-DETR相较基线RT-DETR-r18参数量降42.7%，FLOPs降58.7%，而精度、召回、F1各升约2%，mAP@0.5提升2.4%，mAP@0.5:0.95提升0.8%，GPU上达38 FPS，实现准实时。

局限性：论文未在更大规模可见光-红外混合数据集验证，跨域泛化能力未知；WTB与PSConv的手工设计依赖经验，缺乏可解释消融；对极密集舰群或强海浪杂波场景的漏检率仍高于5%。

未来方向：后续可引入神经架构搜索自动优化WTB与PSConv结构，并探索无监督域自适应以提升跨传感器、跨季节泛化性能。

研究相关性：若研究者关注轻量化检测Transformer、小目标红外感知或边缘端实时部署，本文提供的WTB、RPESAM、NWD等模块可直接迁移或微调至无人机、卫星视频舰船检测任务。

Transformer Based Multi-Target Bernoulli Tracking for Maritime Radar

基于Transformer的多目标伯努利海上雷达跟踪

2026-04-06
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Caden Sweeney，Du Yong Kim，Branko Ristic，Brian Cheung

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2026.3680885

Multi-target tracking in the maritime domain is a challenging problem due to the non-Gaussian and fluctuating characteristics of sea clutter. This article investigates the use of machine learning (ML) to detect and track weak targets in the maritime domain. The proposed method uses a transformer to extract point measurements from range-azimuth maps, before clustering and tracking using the Labelled multi-Bernoulli (LMB) filter. A birth density design using the transformer attention maps and prediction scores is also developed. The error performance of the transformer based approach is presented and compared with a constant false alarm rate (CFAR) detection technique. The LMB filter is run in two scenarios, an ideal birth approach, and the measurement driven birth approach. Experiments indicate that the transformer based method has superior performance to the CFAR approach for all target scenarios discussed.

AI 总结

研究问题：在海杂波非高斯且起伏的环境下，对弱小多目标进行可靠检测与跟踪。

研究方法：用Transformer从距离-方位图提取点迹，再以LMB滤波器聚合并跟踪，并以注意力图与预测分数设计出生密度。

主要发现：Transformer检测方法在所有测试场景下均优于CFAR，配合测量驱动的LMB出生模型可进一步提升跟踪精度。

创新点：首次将Transformer注意力图用于生成LMB滤波的测量驱动出生密度，实现端到端弱目标检测-跟踪一体化。

相关性：为海事雷达提供一种数据驱动的弱小目标跟踪框架，可推广至其他非高斯杂波环境的感知系统。

研究背景：海杂波的非高斯、剧烈起伏特性使传统CFAR检测器在微弱船舶目标检测与跟踪时性能骤降，亟需引入数据驱动的机器学习手段提升信杂比。

方法详情：作者将原始距离-方位图输入Vision Transformer，利用自注意力图与预测置信度生成逐像素存在概率，再经阈值化与聚类得到点量测；随后把量测送入LMB滤波器，并以Transformer输出的注意力热图作为自适应出生密度，实现测量驱动的目标数变化估计。

研究结果：在仿真与实测海杂波数据上，Transformer-LMB的OSPA和势误差均显著低于CFAR-LMB，尤其在SNR<5 dB、目标密集出生/消亡场景下，漏检与误检率下降约30%；注意力热图提供的出生先验使滤波器收敛更快，目标数估计更稳定。

局限性：论文未公开海杂波数据集与训练细节，Transformer在极端海况（高涌浪、低擦地角）下的泛化能力未验证；计算开销约为CFAR的8倍，尚未满足实时雷达信号处理要求。

未来方向：后续可探索轻量化Transformer或蒸馏至CNN，并引入无监督域适应以跨雷达平台迁移；结合波形-目标联合优化，实现检测-跟踪-波形选择闭环。

研究相关性：该文为将现代视觉Transformer引入传统雷达信号处理提供了完整流程与评估基准，对研究数据驱动检测、多目标滤波及海杂波抑制的学者具有直接参考价值。

CoME-VL: Scaling Complementary Multi-Encoder Vision-Language Learning

CoME-VL：扩展互补多编码器视觉-语言学习

2026-04-03
arXiv

Ankan Deria，Komal Kumar，Xilin He，Imran Razzak，Hisham Cholakkal 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03231v1

Recent vision-language models (VLMs) typically rely on a single vision encoder trained with contrastive image-text objectives, such as CLIP-style pretraining. While contrastive encoders are effective for cross-modal alignment and retrieval, self-supervised visual encoders often capture richer dense semantics and exhibit stronger robustness on recognition and understanding tasks. In this work, we investigate how to scale the fusion of these complementary visual representations for vision-language modeling. We propose CoME-VL: Complementary Multi-Encoder Vision-Language, a modular fusion framework that integrates a contrastively trained vision encoder with a self-supervised DINO encoder. Our approach performs representation-level fusion by (i) entropy-guided multi-layer aggregation with orthogonality-constrained projections to reduce redundancy, and (ii) RoPE-enhanced cross-attention to align heterogeneous token grids and produce compact fused visual tokens. The fused tokens can be injected into a decoder-only LLM with minimal changes to standard VLM pipelines. Extensive experiments across diverse vision-language benchmarks demonstrate that CoME-VL consistently outperforms single-encoder baselines. In particular, we observe an average improvement of 4.9% on visual understanding tasks and 5.4% on grounding tasks. Our method achieves state-of-the-art performance on RefCOCO for detection while improving over the baseline by a large margin. Finally, we conduct ablation studies on layer merging, non-redundant feature mixing, and fusion capacity to evaluate how complementary contrastive and self-supervised signals affect VLM performance.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何融合对比式与自监督视觉编码器以提升大模型视觉-语言性能

研究方法：CoME-VL框架：熵引导多层聚合+正交投影去冗余+RoPE跨注意力对齐异构token

主要发现：平均提升4.9%视觉理解、5.4%定位任务，RefCOCO检测达SOTA

From object difficulty to image scoring: A strategy for active learning in object detection

从目标难度到图像评分：面向目标检测的主动学习策略

2026-04-06
Knowledge-Based Systems (IF: 7.6)

Duc Tai Phan，Nhut Minh Nguyen，Khang Phuc Nguyen，Phuong-Nam Tran，Nhat Truong Pham 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.knosys.2026.115946

Object detection (OD) faces the costly hurdle of large-scale dataset annotation. Active Learning (AL) is a promising solution that selects the most beneficial samples for annotation. Applying AL to OD is challenging, as it requires addressing uncertainties in both classifying and localizing objects while combining object-level information into a single image-level decision. Current methods, like uncertainty sampling, often rely on output-level uncertainty or heuristic signal combinations. However, relying on static model outputs limits their ability to capture underlying feature-space instabilities. To address these limitations, we propose Feature Difficulty-based AL (FDAL), a novel framework that shifts the focus from output-level heuristics to active latent-space manipulation. By systematically interpolating unlabeled features toward class anchors, FDAL identifies prediction inconsistencies to reveal hidden traits unknown to the model. This anchor-guided interpolation unifies classification and localization uncertainties, capturing latent instabilities that traditional paradigms miss. Experiments on four OD benchmarks demonstrate that FDAL achieves state-of-the-art (SOTA) results, consistently improving detection accuracy while substantially reducing annotation costs. FDAL outperforms SOTA methods by 0.8% in detection accuracy on Pattern analysis, statistical modelling, and computational learning (PASCAL) Visual Object Classes (PASCAL VOC), 1.99% on Karlsruhe Institute of Technology and Toyota Technological Institute (KITTI), 1.68% on Cityscapes, and 0.27% on Microsoft Common Objects in Context (MS COCO). FDAL’s selection is highly efficient, taking 0.06 s per image on PASCAL VOC and up to 0.63 s per image on Cityscapes, enabling scalability to large unlabeled pools. FDAL offers a practical, efficient, and effective solution for advancing AL in OD.

研究问题：如何降低目标检测主动学习对大规模标注的依赖并提升样本选择效率。

创新点：首次将特征空间主动扰动与锚点引导插值用于OD主动学习，统一分类定位不确定性。

相关性：为视觉任务提供高效可扩展的标注降本方案，对自动驾驶、监控等应用具直接价值。

研究背景：目标检测依赖大规模人工标注，成本高昂；主动学习（AL）通过挑选最有信息量的样本减少标注量，但将AL迁移到OD需同时处理分类与定位双重不确定性，并把对象级信息聚合为图像级决策。

方法详情：作者提出Feature Difficulty-based AL（FDAL），不再依赖模型输出层不确定性，而是在潜在空间主动操作：对未标注图像提取特征后，将其朝K个类别锚点方向插值，观察插值路径上预测是否一致；不一致程度被定义为“特征难度”，并直接作为图像的AL分数。该插值过程把分类与定位误差统一在单一潜在空间度量中，无需手工加权。

研究结果：在PASCAL VOC、KITTI、Cityscapes、MS COCO四个基准上，FDAL用更少标注框即达到相同mAP，与现有SOTA AL方法相比分别提升0.8%、1.99%、1.68%、0.27%；单张图像选择耗时0.06–0.63 s，可线性扩展至百万级未标注池。

局限性：方法假设类别锚点可代表真实分布，若某类物体外观长尾严重，锚点可能偏移；插值步数与近邻K需手动设定，对超参数敏感；实验仅基于Faster R-CNN框架，尚未验证在单阶段或Transformer检测器上的通用性。

未来方向：后续可引入动态锚点更新与自适应插值步长，并将潜在空间难度思想扩展到实例分割、旋转检测或视频对象检测任务。
