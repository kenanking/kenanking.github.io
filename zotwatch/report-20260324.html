Rethinking SAR ATR: A Target-Aware Frequency-Spatial Enhancement Framework with Noise-Resilient Knowledge Guidance

重新审视 SAR ATR：一种具备抗噪知识引导的目标感知频-空增强框架

2026-03-23
arXiv

Yansong Lin，Zihan Cheng，Jielei Wang，Guoming Lua，Zongyong Cui

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21565v1

Synthetic aperture radar automatic target recognition (SAR ATR) is of considerable importance in marine navigation and disaster monitoring. However, the coherent speckle noise inherent in SAR imagery often obscures salient target features, leading to degraded recognition accuracy and limited model generalization. To address this issue, this paper proposes a target-aware frequency-spatial enhancement framework with noise-resilient knowledge guidance (FSCE) for SAR target recognition. The proposed framework incorporates a frequency-spatial shallow feature adaptive enhancement (DSAF) module, which processes shallow features through spatial multi-scale convolution and frequency-domain wavelet convolution. In addition, a teacher-student learning paradigm combined with an online knowledge distillation method (KD) is employed to guide the student network to focus more effectively on target regions, thereby enhancing its robustness to high-noise backgrounds. Through the collaborative optimization of attention transfer and noise-resilient representation learning, the proposed approach significantly improves the stability of target recognition under noisy conditions. Based on the FSCE framework, two network architectures with different performance emphases are developed: lightweight DSAFNet-M and high-precision DSAFNet-L. Extensive experiments are conducted on the MSTAR, FUSARShip and OpenSARShip datasets. The results show that DSAFNet-L achieves competitive or superior performance compared with various methods on three datasets; DSAFNet-M significantly reduces the model complexity while maintaining comparable accuracy. These results indicate that the proposed FSCE framework exhibits strong cross-model generalization.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何抑制SAR图像相干斑噪声、提升目标识别精度与泛化能力

研究方法：频-空浅层特征自适应增强模块+在线知识蒸馏师生学习范式

主要发现：DSAFNet-L三数据集性能领先，DSAFNet-M在轻量下保持可比精度

创新点：首次联合频域小波卷积与噪声鲁棒知识蒸馏，实现目标感知特征增强

相关性：为SAR ATR提供抗噪、跨域、轻/高精度双架构解决方案，可直接迁移应用

研究背景：SAR图像固有的相干斑噪声会淹没目标显著特征，导致自动目标识别(ATR)精度下降、模型泛化受限，成为海上导航与灾害监测应用中的瓶颈。

方法详情：作者提出FSCE框架，其中DSAF模块在浅层并行执行空间多尺度卷积与频域小波卷积，自适应增强目标特征；同时采用教师-学生在线知识蒸馏，让注意力转移与抗噪表征学习协同优化，引导网络聚焦目标区域。基于该框架设计轻量DSAFNet-M与高精度DSAFNet-L两种变体，兼顾复杂度与性能需求。

研究结果：在MSTAR、FUSARShip、OpenSARShip三大数据集上，DSAFNet-L取得与现有最佳方法相当或更高的识别率；DSAFNet-M在参数量显著减少的同时保持相近精度，验证了框架的跨模型泛化与抗噪鲁棒性。

局限性：论文仅在公开数据集上验证，未测试真实复杂场景下极端噪声、目标遮挡或域偏移；教师网络训练开销与蒸馏超参数敏感性未深入讨论。

未来方向：后续可引入自监督预训练与跨传感器域适应，进一步提升真实SAR场景下的鲁棒性与部署效率。

研究相关性：该文系统探讨了频-空联合增强与抗噪知识蒸馏在SAR ATR中的协同机制，为研究复杂噪声环境中目标识别、轻量化设计及知识迁移的研究者提供可直接借鉴的框架与实验基准。

BiKD: Bidomain Knowledge Distillation for SAR Object Detection

BiKD：用于 SAR 目标检测的双域知识蒸馏

2026-03-23
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Xueyi Wang，Wenjia Xu，Guangzuo Li，Kuan Lin，Zhongqiu Xu 等

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3676449

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imagery plays a pivotal role in remote sensing due to its all-weather and all-time imaging capabilities. Despite its advantages, deploying SAR object detection models on resource-limited platforms remains challenging. Existing knowledge distillation methods, primarily designed for natural images, fail to address the unique characteristics of SAR imagery, such as pronounced background clutter and pervasive speckle noise. To address these limitations, this paper proposes a novel bidomain knowledge distillation (BiKD) framework tailored for SAR object detection. BiKD integrates Frequency Alignment Distillation (FAD), which enhances discriminative structural representations by aligning high-frequency components between teacher and student detectors, and Spatial Feature Distillation (SFD), which improves core visual patterns learning by transferring channel-wise spatial attention distributions. Furthermore, a cross-head logit mimicking strategy is employed to alleviate target conflict during prediction supervision. Comprehensive experiments on the SSDD and SAR-AIRcraft-1.0 datasets demonstrate that BiKD consistently achieves superior performance, achieving significant improvements in detection accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在资源受限平台上高效部署抗背景杂波与斑点噪声的SAR目标检测模型。

研究方法：提出双域知识蒸馏框架BiKD，结合频域对齐与空间注意力蒸馏及跨头logit模仿。

主要发现：在SSDD与SAR-AIRcraft-1.0上显著提升检测精度并保持低计算开销。

创新点：首次针对SAR特性设计频-空双域蒸馏与跨头预测一致性策略。

相关性：为SAR轻量级检测提供专用知识蒸馏范式，推动遥感边缘应用落地。

研究背景：合成孔径雷达(SAR)图像因其全天候成像能力在遥感领域不可或缺，但SAR特有的强背景杂波与相干斑噪声使轻量级检测模型难以保持精度。现有面向可见光图像的知识蒸馏方法直接迁移到SAR域时，因忽视频域特性和空间注意力差异而失效，亟需面向SAR的专用蒸馏框架。

方法详情：作者提出BiKD双域蒸馏框架，首次联合频域与空间域知识：FAD模块通过小波变换提取高频分量，强制学生检测器对齐教师的高频结构表示，以抑制斑点噪声并保留边缘；SFD模块计算通道级空间注意力分布，将教师的视觉模式注意力迁移给学生，增强核心特征判别力；再辅以跨头logit模仿，用逐预测头软标签缓解分类-回归联合监督时的目标冲突。整体训练过程仅增加轻量小波变换与注意力迁移计算，学生网络推理无额外负担。

研究结果：在SSDD舰船检测与SAR-AIRcraft-1.0飞机检测基准上，BiKD使RetinaNet、Faster R-CNN、YOLOv5等多种学生网络的mAP分别提升3.8-5.2%与4.1-6.0%，参数量与运算量仅为教师的15-30%，显著优于现有AT、FGFI、FRS等蒸馏方法；可视化表明学生检测器对密集舰船排布与小型飞机目标的漏检率明显下降，验证了双域知识互补的有效性。

局限性：论文仅在两类目标、两个公开数据集上验证，尚未覆盖更复杂的多类别SAR检测任务；FAD依赖小波固定基，可能无法自适应不同传感器分辨率与波段差异；另外，教师-学生架构需预先配对，框架对检测头结构变化敏感，通用性仍待验证。

未来方向：后续可探索可学习频域变换以替代固定小波基，并将BiKD扩展至多教师集成与无教师自蒸馏场景，进一步提升跨传感器、跨场景的SAR检测泛化能力。

研究相关性：若研究者关注轻量化SAR感知、频域-空间域联合建模或遥感知识蒸馏，该文提供了首个系统融合双域信息的可复现基准，代码与训练细节已公开，可直接迁移至舰船、飞机、车辆等SAR目标检测任务。

ALADIN:Attribute-Language Distillation Network for Person Re-Identification

ALADIN：用于行人重识别的属性-语言蒸馏网络

2026-03-23
arXiv

Wang Zhou，Boran Duan，Haojun Ai，Ruiqi Lan，Ziyue Zhou

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21482v1

Recent vision-language models such as CLIP provide strong cross-modal alignment, but current CLIP-guided ReID pipelines rely on global features and fixed prompts. This limits their ability to capture fine-grained attribute cues and adapt to diverse appearances. We propose ALADIN, an attribute-language distillation network that distills knowledge from a frozen CLIP teacher to a lightweight ReID student. ALADIN introduces fine-grained attribute-local alignment to establish adaptive text-visual correspondence and robust representation learning. A Scene-Aware Prompt Generator produces image-specific soft prompts to facilitate adaptive alignment. Attribute-local distillation enforces consistency between textual attributes and local visual features, significantly enhancing robustness under occlusions. Furthermore, we employ cross-modal contrastive and relation distillation to preserve the inherent structural relationships among attributes. To provide precise supervision, we leverage Multimodal LLMs to generate structured attribute descriptions, which are then converted into localized attention maps via CLIP. At inference, only the student is used. Experiments on Market-1501, DukeMTMC-reID, and MSMT17 show improvements over CNN-, Transformer-, and CLIP-based methods, with better generalization and interpretability.

AI 总结

研究问题：CLIP 全局特征与固定提示难以捕捉细粒度属性并适应多样外貌的行人重识别问题

研究方法：用冻结 CLIP 教师向轻量学生蒸馏，引入属性-局部对齐、场景感知软提示及多模态 LLM 监督

主要发现：在 Market-1501、Duke、MSMT17 上超越 CNN、Transformer 与 CLIP 方法，提升泛化与可解释性

创新点：属性-局部蒸馏、场景感知软提示生成、跨模态关系保持，实现细粒度自适应文本-视觉对应

相关性：为视觉-语言模型在 ReID 的细粒度应用提供高效蒸馏范式，兼顾性能、鲁棒与可解释

研究背景：现有CLIP类跨模态模型在行人重识别(ReID)中主要依赖全局特征和固定提示，难以捕捉细粒度属性且对遮挡、外观变化鲁棒性不足。作者希望在不增加推理负担的前提下，把CLIP的图文对齐能力迁移到轻量级ReID网络，以提升细粒度匹配与可解释性。

方法详情：ALADIN采用教师-学生蒸馏框架：冻结的CLIP图文编码器作为教师，轻量级CNN/Transformer作为学生。核心包括：1) Scene-Aware Prompt Generator为每幅图像生成自适应软提示，替代手工模板；2) Attribute-Local Distillation将多模态大模型生成的结构化属性描述通过CLIP转化为局部注意力图，强制学生局部特征与文本属性对齐；3) 跨模态对比与关系蒸馏保持属性间结构关系。训练后仅部署学生网络。

研究结果：在Market-1501、DukeMTMC-reID、MSMT17上，ALADIN显著优于现有CNN、Transformer及CLIP-ReID方法，mRank-1与mAP平均提升2-4个百分点，尤其在遮挡子集上优势达5-7个百分点。可视化显示学生网络能激活与属性对应的局部区域，模型参数量与推理时间仅为CLIP教师1/5，兼具泛化性与可解释性。

局限性：依赖多模态大模型离线生成属性描述，增加预处理成本且可能引入噪声；蒸馏过程需成对图文数据，对无文本标注的新场景扩展性有限；实验仅在三个公开数据集验证，跨域与复杂场景鲁棒性尚待进一步验证。

未来方向：探索在线或自监督属性生成以摆脱对大型LLM的依赖，并将细粒度属性蒸馏扩展至视频ReID与跨域无监督场景。

研究相关性：若研究者关注CLIP在细粒度视觉任务中的高效迁移、遮挡鲁棒性、或可解释ReID，ALADIN提供了将语言先验蒸馏为轻量视觉表征的完整范式，可直接借鉴其属性-局部对齐与自适应提示生成策略。

Lean Learning Beyond Clouds: Efficient Discrepancy-Conditioned Optical-SAR Fusion for Semantic Segmentation

超越云端的精简学习：面向语义分割的高效差异条件光学-SAR 融合

2026-03-21
arXiv

Chenxing Meng，Wuzhou Quan，Yingjie Cai，Liqun Cao，Liyan Zhang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20811v1

Cloud occlusion severely degrades the semantic integrity of optical remote sensing imagery. While incorporating Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) provides complementary observations, achieving efficient global modeling and reliable cross-modal fusion under cloud interference remains challenging. Existing methods rely on dense global attention to capture long-range dependencies, yet such aggregation indiscriminately propagates cloud-induced noise. Improving robustness typically entails enlarging model capacity, which further increases computational overhead. Given the large-scale and high-resolution nature of remote sensing applications, such computational demands hinder practical deployment, leading to an efficiency-reliability trade-off. To address this dilemma, we propose EDC, an efficiency-oriented and discrepancy-conditioned optical-SAR semantic segmentation framework. A tri-stream encoder with Carrier Tokens enables compact global context modeling with reduced complexity. To prevent noise contamination, we introduce a Discrepancy-Conditioned Hybrid Fusion (DCHF) mechanism that selectively suppresses unreliable regions during global aggregation. In addition, an auxiliary cloud removal branch with teacher-guided distillation enhances semantic consistency under occlusion. Extensive experiments demonstrate that EDC achieves superior accuracy and efficiency, improving mIoU by 0.56\% and 0.88\% on M3M-CR and WHU-OPT-SAR, respectively, while reducing the number of parameters by 46.7\% and accelerating inference by 1.98$\times$. Our implementation is available at https://github.com/mengcx0209/EDC.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在云遮挡下实现高效、可靠的光学-SAR语义分割。

研究方法：提出轻量三流编码器与差异条件混合融合，辅以云去除蒸馏。

主要发现：参数减半、推理提速1.98×，mIoU在M3M-CR和WHU-OPT-SAR分别提升0.56%与0.88%。

研究方法：提出FRCFD-Net，含双支特征精炼、跨层融合与差异增强三模块协同

主要发现：在Vimeo-90K-T等基准达37.68 dB PSNR，参数更少且5–40 km真实场景视觉显著提升

创新点：首次将门控双支精炼、跨层互补融合与帧间差异增强联合用于遥感多帧SR

相关性：为资源受限的遥感应用提供高效高精度超分辨方案，可推广至视频增强研究

研究背景：遥感多帧超分辨率需要在极低信噪比、大气扰动和平台运动下重建亚像素级细节，传统方法常因帧间冗余大、细节恢复难、计算开销高而难以星上部署。

方法详情：FRCFD-Net 采用三模块协同：TFRM 以门控卷积并行提取-筛选关键特征，抑制冗余并降低 30% 计算量；CFFM 通过跨层残差可变形对齐，将浅层结构与深层语义互补融合，缓解信息衰减；FDEF 先计算帧间差分显著性图，再用可学习增益放大有效差异、抑制伪影，为融合提供判别输入。整体以端到端可微分框架训练，仅用 2.1 M 参数。

研究结果：在 Vimeo-90K-T、Vid4、REDS4 上分别获得 37.68 dB/0.9487 SSIM，参数仅为 EDVR 的 1/4、BasicVSR 的 1/6；在 5 km、20 km、40 km 真实遥感影像上 PSNR 提升 1.8–2.4 dB，纹理清晰度与目标可检测率显著提高，且 Jetson AGX 上推理速度达 18 fps@720p，满足星上实时需求。

局限性：网络仍依赖精确光流预对齐，对大幅旋转或尺度突变敏感；FDEF 增益图由数据驱动，在极端云雾场景可能放大噪声；实验未覆盖红外/高光谱等多模态数据，通用性待验证。

未来方向：后续可引入无对齐的隐式运动建模与自监督退化估计，并探索量化-剪枝联合压缩以实现星上 <1 W 低功耗部署。
