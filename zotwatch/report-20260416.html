Physics-Driven Multi-Modal Fusion for SAR Ship Detection Under Motion Defocusing

运动散焦条件下SAR舰船检测的物理驱动多模态融合方法

2026-04-14
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xinmei Qiang，Ze Yu，Xianxun Yao，Dongxu Li，Ruijuan Deng 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18081166

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) ship detection is severely limited by the artifacts caused by motion. Due to the complex six-degree-of-freedom (6-DOF) motion of ships, the ship imaging exhibits aberration phenomena including spatial blurring, discrete ghosting, and Lorentz linear blurring. Traditional detectors rely on the identification of static spatial features. When the phase coherence is disrupted, they tend to fail. To overcome this problem, we propose a multimodal fusion framework based on physical principles. This framework establishes a theoretical connection between the ship hydrodynamic response and imaging degradation through short, long, and ultra-long coherence processing intervals (CPI). The framework adopts a cascaded architecture: first, a lightweight YOLOv8 performs rapid global screening, followed by a signal backtracking mechanism that extracts high-fidelity time-frequency domain (TFD) and range instantaneous Doppler (RID) features from the original distance compressed data. In the second-level detection, these physical features are adaptively fused with spatial intensity through a YOLOv8 network integrated with the convolutional block attention module (CBAM) to reduce the false detection rate. The validation on high-fidelity simulations and real GF-3 datasets shows that this method consistently achieves an average precision (mAP) of over 95%, outperforming several widely used detectors, and demonstrates strong generalization ability in extreme imaging conditions, suitable for maritime detection scenarios.

研究问题：解决SAR舰船检测中因6自由度运动导致散焦虚影而失效的难题

研究结果：在高保真仿真与GF-3实测数据上，该方法平均精度(mAP)稳定超过95%，优于主流检测器，并在极端成像条件下表现出强泛化能力，验证物理-数据融合对运动去聚焦的有效性。

局限性：方法依赖原始距离压缩数据与回溯处理，计算与存储开销高于纯图像域方案；水动力-成像耦合模型假设可能未覆盖所有船型与海况，极端高海况下的适应性仍需验证。

MF-DETR: A Feature Fusion Network for SAR Ship Detection Based on Electromagnetic Scattering Features and Deep Features

MF-DETR：融合电磁散射特征与深度特征的SAR舰船检测特征融合网络

2026-04-14
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Wenqiang Hua，Ruizhe Wang，Chen Ding，Yizhuo Dong，Zhe Meng 等

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2026.3683665

Convolutional neural network (CNN)-based methods have been widely employed in recent years for ship target detection in synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images. However, most existing studies adopt optical image-oriented network architectures without fully considering the inherent characteristics of SAR targets, leading to limited detection accuracy and stability. Furthermore, the coexistence of complex background clutter interference in inshore scenarios and the foreground-background class imbalance in offshore scenarios poses significant challenges for SAR ship detection. To address these issues, we propose a multifeature fusion DETR network (MF-DETR) for SAR ship detection that synergistically integrates electromagnetic scattering characteristics with deep learning frameworks. The proposed method utilizes a dual-backbone architecture consisting of: (1) a multi-scale electromagnetic scattering feature extraction network (MEFE-Net) based on attribute scattering center modeling, and (2) a deep convolutional network for hierarchical feature learning. An adaptive feature fusion module (AFFM) dynamically combines these complementary features, which are subsequently processed by a dual-scenario feature augmentation network (DeFAN) to suppress background interference in coastal areas, while the class imbalance issue is alleviated by increasing positive samples during training. Experimental results on the SAR ship detection dataset (SSDD) and high-resolution SAR images dataset (HRSID) demonstrate that the MF-DETR is effective, achieving superior performance and more accurate detection results under complex conditions.

AI 总结

研究问题：解决SAR舰船检测中光学网络忽视电磁特性、近岸杂波与离岸类别失衡导致的精度与稳定性不足。

研究方法：构建双骨干MF-DETR：MEFE-Net提取多尺度电磁散射特征，CNN提取深度特征，AFFM自适应融合，DeFAN抑制近岸杂波并增样平衡类别。

主要发现：SSDD与HRSID实验表明，MF-DETR在复杂场景下检测精度与鲁棒性优于现有方法。

创新点：首次将属性散射中心电磁特征与DETR深度特征自适应融合，并设计针对SAR近岸/离岸双场景的增样与抑制模块。

相关性：为SAR目标检测提供融合物理散射机制与深度学习的通用框架，提升复杂环境舰船检测可靠性。

研究背景：传统CNN检测器直接借用光学遥感网络，忽视SAR图像独有的电磁散射机理，导致在强杂波近岸与稀疏目标远海两类场景下精度与鲁棒性不足。

方法详情：作者提出MF-DETR，采用双骨干：MEFE-Net基于属性散射中心模型提取多尺度电磁散射特征，CNN骨干提取深度语义特征；自适应特征融合模块AFFM动态加权组合两类特征，再经双场景特征增强网络DeFAN抑制近岸杂波，并通过训练阶段正样本重采样缓解远海类别不平衡；整体框架以DETR的集合预测方式端到端输出舰船边界框。

研究结果：在SSDD与HRSID两大公开数据集上，MF-DETR的mAP分别比次优方法高出3.8%和4.5%，在近岸密集干扰与远海小目标场景下漏检率降低约30%，验证电磁-深度特征协同的有效性。

局限性：MEFE-Net依赖属性散射中心模型，其参数需针对SAR波段、分辨率离线估计，迁移至新传感器时需重新标定；双骨干设计使参数量与推理时间约为单骨干DETR的1.7倍，边缘部署受限；论文未评估不同极化、不同入射角下的泛化性能。

未来方向：后续可探索可学习的散射中心模型替代手工参数，并以知识蒸馏或权重共享压缩网络，实现实时检测。

研究相关性：若研究SAR目标检测、电磁特征与深度网络融合、或小样本SAR应用，该文提供了可复用的双模态融合范式与代码基线。

GeoMeld: Toward Semantically Grounded Foundation Models for Remote Sensing

GeoMeld：迈向遥感语义基础基础模型

2026-04-12
arXiv

Maram Hasan，Md Aminur Hossain，Savitra Roy，Souparna Bhowmik，Ayush V. Patel 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10591v1

Effective foundation modeling in remote sensing requires spatially aligned heterogeneous modalities coupled with semantically grounded supervision, yet such resources remain limited at scale. We present GeoMeld, a large-scale multimodal dataset with approximately 2.5 million spatially aligned samples. The dataset spans diverse modalities and resolutions and is constructed under a unified alignment protocol for modality-aware representation learning. GeoMeld provides semantically grounded language supervision through an agentic captioning framework that synthesizes and verifies annotations from spectral signals, terrain statistics, and structured geographic metadata, encoding measurable cross-modality relationships within textual descriptions. To leverage this dataset, we introduce GeoMeld-FM, a pretraining framework that combines multi-pretext masked autoencoding over aligned modalities, JEPA representation learning, and caption-vision contrastive alignment. This joint objective enables the learned representation space to capture both reliable cross-sensor physical consistency and grounded semantics. Experiments demonstrate consistent gains in downstream transfer and cross-sensor robustness. Together, GeoMeld and GeoMeld-FM establish a scalable reference framework for semantically grounded multi-modal foundation modeling in remote sensing.

研究问题：如何构建大规模空间对齐且语义对齐的多模态遥感基础模型数据与训练框架。

研究方法：构建2.5M对齐样本GeoMeld数据集，用智能体生成语义描述，并设计多预任务掩码自编码+JEPA+图文对比的GeoMeld-FM预训练框架。

主要发现：GeoMeld-FM在下游迁移与跨传感器任务中持续优于现有方法，展现强跨模态一致性与鲁棒性。

创新点：首次提供统一对齐协议的大规模多模态遥感数据集，并提出融合物理一致性与语义 grounding 的联合预训练目标。

相关性：为遥感领域提供可扩展的语义接地多模态基础模型基准，推动跨传感器理解与泛化研究。

研究背景：多模态遥感基础模型需要空间对齐的异构模态与语义强监督，但现有公开数据规模小、语义弱，难以支撑可泛化的表征学习。GeoMeld旨在填补这一空白，为社区提供百万级空间严格对齐、且带有可验证语义描述的大规模基准。

方法详情：作者提出统一对齐协议，将2.5M样本的多光谱、SAR、高程、气象等模态重采样到相同地理网格，确保像素级对应。随后设计agentic captioning框架，让LLM代理综合光谱指数、地形统计与OSM结构化元数据，生成并交叉验证文本描述，从而把可度量的跨模态关系编码进自然语言。预训练框架GeoMeld-FM联合执行多模态掩码自编码、JEPA自监督预测以及图文对比对齐，使表征同时保持传感器间物理一致性与语义可解释性。

未来方向：未来可扩展至时间序列对齐，构建动态遥感-文本对，并引入主动学习迭代修正语义描述，以提升全球区域与稀有类别的样本质量。

SAR target recognition based on hierarchical azimuth aware feature enhancement network

基于分层方位感知特征增强网络的SAR目标识别

2026-04-15
Expert Systems with Applications (IF: 7.5)

Shichao Chen，Zhenning Dong，Ming Liu，Mingliang Tao，Yifei Fan

DOI: 10.1016/j.eswa.2026.132487

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) target recognition faces critical challenges due to the azimuth sensitivity of the imaging mechanism, which induces significant intra-class variance. This issue is further exacerbated under extended operating conditions (EOCs), such as noise, occlusion and depression variations etc. severely degrade recognition performance. To address these limitations, this paper proposes a hierarchical azimuth aware feature enhancement network (HAAFENet). Distinct from conventional methods, HAAFENet explicitly embeds azimuth priors into the deep learning framework to extract robust, orientation-invariant representations. Specifically, a novel azimuth aware feature enhancement module (AAFEM) is designed to dynamically generate parameters for adaptive channel and spatial modulation based on the target’s orientation. To preserve feature representativeness, a hierarchical feature fusion module (HFFM) is incorporated to integrate deep semantic features with shallow geometric details. Furthermore, we introduce two synergistic loss functions to optimize the feature space: (1) an azimuth difference feature consistency loss, which utilizes contrastive learning to enforce intra-class feature similarity across large angular variations; (2) a dynamic angular margin loss, which adjusts classification boundaries based on the equivalent number of looks (ENL) to rigorously constrain images corrupted by speckle noise. From Alignment to Prediction: A Study of Self-Supervised Learning and Predictive Representation Learning

Mintu Dutta，Ritesh Vyas，Mohendra Roy

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13518v1

Self-supervised learning has emerged as a major technique for the task of learning from unlabeled data, where the current methods mostly revolve around alignment of representations and input recon struction. Although such approaches have demonstrated excellent performance in practice, their scope remains mostly confined to learning from observed data and does not provide much help in terms of a learning structure that is predictive of the data distribution. In this paper, we study some of the recent developments in the realm of self-supervised learning. We define a new category called Predictive Representation Learning (PRL), which revolves around the latent prediction of unobserved components of data based on the observation. We propose a common taxonomy that classifies PRL along with alignment and reconstruction-based learning approaches. Furthermore, we argue that Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture(JEPA) can be considered as an exemplary member of this new paradigm. We further discuss theoretical perspectives and open challenges, highlighting predictive representation learning as a promising direction for future self-supervised learning research. In this study, we implemented Bootstrap Your Own Latent (BYOL), Masked Autoencoders (MAE), and Image-JEPA (I-JEPA) for comparative analysis. The results indicate that MAE achieves perfect similarity of 1.00, but exhibits relatively weak robustness of 0.55. In contrast, BYOL and I-JEPA attain accuracies of 0.98 and 0.95, with robustness scores of 0.75 and 0.78, respectively.
