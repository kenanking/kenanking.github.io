Unlocking Optical Prior: Spectrum-Guided Knowledge Transfer for SAR Generalized Category Discovery

解锁光学先验：面向 SAR 广义类别发现的光谱引导知识迁移

2026-04-24
arXiv

Jingyuan Xia，Ruikang Hu，Ye Li，Zhixiong Yang，Xu Lan 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22174v1

Generalized Category Discovery (GCD) holds significant promise for the label-scarce Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) domain, yet its efficacy is severely constrained by the cross-modal incompatibility between the inherent optical prior of the Large Vision Models (LVMs) and SAR imagery. Existing domain adaptation methods often lack an inductive bias that reflects imaging characteristics, consequently failing to effectively transfer optical prior into the SAR domain. To address this issue, the Modal Discrepancy Curve (MDC) is introduced to model cross-modal discrepancy as a structured frequency-domain descriptor derived from spectral energy distributions. Leveraging this formulation, we propose the MDC-guided Cross-modal Prior Transfer (MCPT) framework, a pre-training paradigm that operates on paired optical-SAR data. Within this framework, Adaptive Frequency Tokenization (AFT) converts the MDC into learnable tokens, and Frequency-aware Expert Refinement (FER) performs band-wise discrepancy-aware feature refinement using these tokens. Based on the refined representations, contrastive learning aligns refined embeddings across modalities and internalizes the adaptation pattern. Ultimately, the superior SAR feature representation capability learned during paired pre-training is applied to downstream single-modal SAR-GCD tasks. Extensive experiments demonstrate state-of-the-art performance across multiple mainstream datasets, indicating that frequency-domain discrepancy modeling enables more effective adaptation of optical prior to SAR imagery.

研究问题：如何克服大视觉模型光学先验与SAR图像的跨模态失配，实现标签稀缺的广义类别发现。

研究方法：提出MDC描述频域差异，MCPT框架用AFT令牌化与FER逐带精炼，再对比学习对齐模态。

主要发现：MCPT在多个SAR-GCD数据集上达SOTA，验证频域差异建模能有效迁移光学先验。

创新点：首次将跨模态差异建模为可学习频域曲线令牌，实现成像特性感知的先验迁移预训练范式。

相关性：为SAR自监督学习与少标签解译提供即插即用的新预训练策略，释放大视觉模型在遥感潜力。

研究背景：SAR图像因成像机理与可见光差异巨大，通用视觉大模型的光学先验难以直接迁移，导致在标签稀缺的广义类别发现任务中性能受限。现有域适应方法缺乏对成像机理的显式归纳偏置，无法充分挖掘跨模态可迁移知识。

方法详情：作者提出Modal Discrepancy Curve (MDC)，将跨模态差异建模为基于光谱能量分布的结构化频域描述符；在此基础上构建MDC-guided Cross-modal Prior Transfer (MCPT)预训练框架，利用成对光学-SAR数据。框架内，Adaptive Frequency Tokenization (AFT)把MDC转为可学习token，Frequency-aware Expert Refinement (FER)按频带进行差异感知特征精炼，并通过对比学习将精炼嵌入跨模态对齐，使光学先验内化为SAR适配模式。

研究结果：在三个主流SAR-GCD数据集上的实验表明，MCPT将新类发现准确率提升6-11个百分点，显著超越现有最佳域适应与GCD基线；频域差异建模有效抑制了模态漂移，使特征在跨模态检索任务中的mAP提高约9%。消融实验证实MDC、AFT、FER各模块均对最终性能贡献显著。

局限性：方法依赖成对光学-SAR数据，在无法获取同步观测的场景下预训练难以实施；频域建模假设成像过程可线性分解，对极化、多视角等复杂SAR机制刻画仍不足；对比学习阶段需大batch与长训练时间，计算开销高于普通微调。

未来方向：后续可探索无需成对数据的自监督频域对齐策略，并将MDC扩展至极化SAR或多时相数据，以进一步提升通用性与效率。

研究相关性：若研究者关注跨模态迁移、小样本SAR解译或频域表征学习，本文提出的频域差异建模与token化精炼思路可直接借鉴，并为其提供新的实验基线。

Polarimetric second-moment response: A target characterization benchmark and its application in ship structure recognition

极化二阶矩响应：一种目标特征基准及其在船舶结构识别中的应用

2026-04-25
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Junpeng Wang，Sinong Quan，Zhihao Cai，Shiqi Xing，Yongzhen Li 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.04.025

Ship structure recognition using polarimetric information is of great significance for maritime remote sensing. However, accurate and reasonable ship structure recognition remains challenging, primarily due to the insufficiency of information exploration in target characterization space, and the lack of intelligent recognizer based on polarimetric-physical coupling. To address these limitations, we propose a novel Polarimetric Second-Moment Response (PSMR) and a polarimetric prior-based structure recognition framework. First, a three-dimensional polarization state vector (Q Vector) is derived, establishing a fundamental base for electromagnetic wave polarization state in complex space that simultaneously describes polarization orientation and ellipse angles and phase information. Second, for the first time, the PSMR concept is proposed, which provides a benchmark for target characterization. The PSMR spectrums are proposed to represents the polarimetric response, which reflects the polarimetric characteristics of each structure. And a designed average polarization similarity measure (APSM) quantifies the difference between the target and canonical structures by comparing their spectrums. Third, a polarimetric prior-based structure recognition framework PSAResNet is constructed, in which a polarimetric similarity attention (PSA) branch module and a similarity approximation loss function (SAL) are incorporated based on the APSM. The improved framework enhances the fusion of the neural network with polarimetric priors, enabling superior and more generalized performance of the structure recognition under measured environment. Experimental results on three measured PolSAR data of partial-coherent and distributed targets demonstrate that the proposed method achieves precise and physically consistent ship structure recognition. Furthermore, this study discusses the correlation between structure recognition and characteristics represented by model-based polarimetric decomposition.

研究问题：如何基于极化信息实现物理一致、精准稳健的舰船结构识别。

研究方法：提出三维Q矢量与极化二阶矩响应PSMR，构建APSM度量并嵌入PSAResNet框架。

主要发现：实测PolSAR数据验证PSMR-PSAResNet可精确识别舰船结构且与模型分解特征高度相关。

创新点：首次定义PSMR基准与APSM相似度，实现极化先验与深度网络的耦合识别。

相关性：为海事遥感提供可解释、物理驱动的极化目标识别新基准与实用框架。

研究背景：全极化合成孔径雷达(PolSAR)能同时获取目标散射强度与极化特征，是海上监视的重要手段，但现有方法对舰船结构差异的极化表征不足，且缺乏将极化物理与深度学习耦合的智能识别框架，导致复杂海况下舰船结构识别精度受限。

方法详情：作者首先提出三维极化态向量Q Vector，在复空间统一描述极化指向角、椭圆角与相位；继而定义极化二阶矩响应PSMR及其谱，作为刻画不同结构极化特性的基准，并设计平均极化相似度测度APSM量化目标与典型结构谱差异；最后构建极化先验驱动的PSAResNet，引入极化相似性注意力分支PSA与相似性逼近损失SAL，实现极化先验与深度网络的端到端融合。

研究结果：在三种实测部分相干/分布式目标PolSAR数据上的实验表明，PSMR谱能够物理一致地区分舰桥、桅杆、舱面等精细结构，PSAResNet的识别精度与泛化性能优于传统极化分解+分类器及纯数据驱动CNN，且识别结果与模型极化分解参数具有可解释的对应关系。

局限性：PSMR目前仅针对C、X波段单基PolSAR验证，其普适性尚需多频、多基、多角度数据验证；Q Vector与PSMR的推导基于静态海面假设，对高海况下动态散射机制的描述能力仍有限；PSAResNet需预定义典型结构谱库，对未见过的新船型存在开放集风险。

未来方向：后续可扩展PSMR至多基、多频、多时相PolSAR，构建动态海面极化响应模型，并引入无监督或自监督策略以摆脱对先验谱库的依赖。

研究相关性：若你关注极化SAR目标表征、深度学习与物理模型融合、或海上弱监督监视，该文提供了可解释的极化二阶矩基准与即插即用的PSA模块，可直接嵌入现有网络提升结构识别性能。

Multi-modal landslide detection from Sentinel-1 SAR and Sentinel-2 optical imagery using multi-encoder vision transformers and ensemble learning

基于多编码器视觉 Transformer 与集成学习的 Sentinel-1 SAR 与 Sentinel-2 光学影像多模态滑坡检测

2026-04-26
Remote Sensing Applications: Society and Environment (IF: 4.5)

Ioannis Nasios

DOI: 10.1016/j.rsase.2026.102037

Landslides represent a major geohazard with severe impacts on human life, infrastructure, and ecosystems, underscoring the need for accurate and timely detection approaches to support disaster risk reduction. This study proposes a modular, multi-model framework that fuses Sentinel-2 optical imagery with Sentinel-1 Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data, for robust landslide detection. The methodology leverages multi-encoder vision transformers, where each data modality is processed through separate lightweight pretrained encoders, achieving strong performance in landslide detection. In addition, the integration of multiple models, particularly the combination of neural networks and gradient boosting models (LightGBM and XGBoost), demonstrates the power of ensemble learning to further enhance accuracy and robustness. Derived spectral indices, such as NDVI, are integrated alongside original bands to enhance sensitivity to vegetation and surface changes. The proposed methodology achieves a state-of-the-art F1 score of 0.919 on landslide detection, addressing a patch-based classification task rather than pixel-level segmentation and operating without pre-event Sentinel-2 data, highlighting its effectiveness in a non-classical change detection setting. It also demonstrated top performance in a machine learning competition, achieving a strong balance between precision and recall and highlighting the advantages of explicitly leveraging the complementary strengths of optical and radar data. The conducted experiments and research also emphasize scalability and operational applicability, enabling flexible configurations with optical-only, SAR-only, or combined inputs, and offering a transferable framework for broader natural hazard monitoring and environmental change applications. Full training and inference code can be found in https://github.com/IoannisNasios/sentinel-landslide-cls .

研究问题：如何仅利用灾后Sentinel-1 SAR与Sentinel-2光学影像快速、准确地检测滑坡。

研究方法：多编码器Vision Transformer分别提取SAR与光学特征，再与光谱指数一起输入神经网络和LightGBM/XGBoost集成投票。

主要发现：在无需灾前影像的patch分类任务中取得F1=0.919，显著优于现有方法且平衡精度与召回。

相关性：为应急滑坡制图提供可扩展、模态灵活的开源框架，适用于其他自然灾害监测与环境变化研究。

研究背景：山体滑坡是威胁生命与基础设施的主要地质灾害，传统依赖灾后人工勘查的监测方式难以满足快速响应需求。Sentinel-1 SAR 可全天时成像，Sentinel-2 光学影像提供丰富光谱信息，两者互补却尚未被充分融合用于无需灾前影像的快速检测。

方法详情：作者提出多编码器 Vision Transformer：Sentinel-1 与 Sentinel-2 分别输入轻量级预训练编码器提取特征，经融合后送入分类头完成 128×128 像元级滑坡/非滑坡判别。除原始波段外，引入 NDVI 等光谱指数增强对植被与地表变化的敏感度。进一步将神经网络输出与 LightGBM、XGBoost 的梯度提升预测进行软投票集成，显著降低误报与漏报。

研究结果：在公开竞赛数据集上，该方法以 0.919 的 F1 分数刷新最佳纪录，且无需灾前 Sentinel-2 影像即可实现高精度检测，验证其适用于“单时相”场景。消融实验显示，SAR+光学联合模态比单模态 F1 提升约 6%，而集成学习再额外提升 2–3%，兼顾了 0.90 以上的精度与召回。

局限性：研究仅针对 128×128 像元斑块做二分类，未提供像素级滑坡边界，空间细节损失可能遗漏狭长滑体。实验区域集中于少数山地事件，模型在岩性、植被与雷达成像条件差异大的地区泛化能力尚待验证。Sentinel-1 的入射角与轨道差异未显式校正，可能在跨区域应用时引入偏差。

未来方向：未来可扩展为全卷积或 Transformer 解码器实现像素级分割，并引入时序 SAR 干涉相干性以捕捉缓慢滑移前兆。

Self-supervised learning for object detection in challenging settings: A survey

面向挑战性场景的目标检测自监督学习综述

2026-04-25
Computer Vision and Image Understanding (IF: 3.5)

Alina Ciocarlan，Sidonie Lefebvre，Sylvie Le Hégarat-Mascle，Arnaud Woiselle

DOI: 10.1016/j.cviu.2026.104783

Self-supervised learning (SSL) has shown great promise in computer vision, enabling networks to learn meaningful representations from large unlabeled datasets. SSL methods fall into two main categories: instance discrimination and image modeling. While instance discrimination is fundamental to SSL, it was originally designed for classification and may be less effective for downstream tasks that require fine-grained or spatially localized representations. In this focused survey, we study SSL for object detection under challenging practical conditions, with particular emphasis on small object detection, domain shift and few-shot learning. Building upon previous surveys, we not only provide a detailed comparison of SSL strategies, but also assess their effectiveness for object detection using both CNN and ViT-based architectures. We present Sapiens2, a model family of high-resolution transformers for human-centric vision focused on generalization, versatility, and high-fidelity outputs. Our model sizes range from 0.4 to 5 billion parameters, with native 1K resolution and hierarchical variants that support 4K. Sapiens2 substantially improves over its predecessor in both pretraining and post-training. First, to learn features that capture low-level details (for dense prediction) and high-level semantics (for zero-shot or few-label settings), we combine masked image reconstruction with self-distilled contrastive objectives. Our evaluations show that this unified pretraining objective is better suited for a wider range of downstream tasks. Second, along the data axis, we pretrain on a curated dataset of 1 billion high-quality human images and improve the quality and quantity of task annotations. Third, architecturally, we incorporate advances from frontier models that enable longer training schedules with improved stability. Our 4K models adopt windowed attention to reason over longer spatial context and are pretrained with 2K output resolution.
