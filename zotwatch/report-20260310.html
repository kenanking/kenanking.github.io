EarthBridge: A Solution for 4th Multi-modal Aerial View Image Challenge Translation Track

2026-03-06
arXiv

Zhenyuan Chen，Guanyuan Shen，Feng Zhang

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06753v1

Cross-modal image-to-image translation among Electro-Optical (EO), Infrared (IR), and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) sensors is essential for comprehensive multi-modal aerial-view analysis. However, translating between these modalities is notoriously difficult due to their distinct electromagnetic signatures and geometric characteristics. This paper presents \textbf{EarthBridge}, a high-fidelity translation framework developed for the 4th Multi-modal Aerial View Image Challenge -- Translation (MAVIC-T). We explore two distinct methodologies: \textbf{Diffusion Bridge Implicit Models (DBIM)}, which we generalize using non-Markovian bridge processes for high-quality deterministic sampling, and \textbf{Contrastive Unpaired Translation (CUT)}, which utilizes contrastive learning for structural consistency. Our EarthBridge framework employs a channel-concatenated UNet denoiser trained with Karras-weighted bridge scalings and a specialized "booting noise" initialization to handle the inherent ambiguity in cross-modal mappings. We evaluate these methods across all four challenge tasks (SAR$\rightarrow$EO, SAR$\rightarrow$RGB, SAR$\rightarrow$IR, RGB$\rightarrow$IR), achieving superior spatial detail and spectral accuracy. Our solution achieved a composite score of 0.38, securing the second position on the MAVIC-T leaderboard. Code is available at https://github.com/Bili-Sakura/EarthBridge-Preview.

研究问题：解决EO、IR、SAR三种航空影像跨模态互译难题

研究方法：提出EarthBridge框架，融合非马尔可夫桥扩散模型DBIM与对比式CUT双路径

主要发现：四项翻译任务均获高空间细节与光谱精度，MAVIC-T榜二得分0.38

创新点：DBIM确定性采样+通道拼接UNet+引导噪声初始化，适配电磁差异

研究背景：多模态航空影像（EO、IR、SAR）在灾害监测、军事侦察和地球观测中互补，但各传感器成像机理迥异，导致跨模态信息融合困难。现有方法难以在保持几何精度的同时还原目标光谱特征，因此亟需高保真、任务通用的翻译框架。

方法详情：作者提出 EarthBridge，将非马尔可夫桥过程推广为 Diffusion Bridge Implicit Models (DBIM)，实现确定性高质量采样；并并行采用 CUT 的对比学习保持结构一致性。框架以通道拼接 UNet 为去噪骨干，使用 Karras 加权桥调度与“booting noise”初始化，显式缓解 SAR→EO/RGB/IR 及 RGB→IR 映射中的电磁与几何歧义。训练时联合优化频域与感知损失，强化纹理与边缘保真。

研究结果：在 MAVIC-T 四项翻译任务中，EarthBridge 在 SSIM、PSNR、LPIPS 与光谱角距离上均优于官方基线，生成影像空间细节锐利且热/散射特性准确，复合得分 0.38 位列榜眼。消融实验显示 booting noise 将 SAR→EO 的 LPIPS 降低 8.7%，DBIM 相比 vanilla DDIM 采样步数减半而精度不降，验证桥过程对航空跨模态的有效性。

局限性：论文仅验证于 MAVIC-T 提供的有限场景与分辨率，未探讨城市密集建筑或极端俯仰角下的泛化；DBIM 仍依赖成对统计参数，若传感器参数未知则桥分布估计偏差增大；此外，方法对计算显存要求高于纯 GAN 方案，实时性受限。

未来方向：后续可引入物理可解释模块，将 SAR 散射模型与 EO 辐射模型耦合，实现无参数桥分布估计；或探索轻量化蒸馏，使框架可在机载边缘端实时运行。

研究相关性：若研究者从事多模态遥感、扩散模型或跨域翻译，本文提供了桥过程与对比学习融合的新范式，并开源代码，可直接扩展至卫星影像融合、夜间导航等应用。

SIGMAE: A Spectral-Index-Guided Foundation Model for Multispectral Remote Sensing

SIGMAE：面向多光谱遥感的谱指数引导基础模型

2026-03-08
arXiv

Xiaokang Zhang，Bo Li，Chufeng Zhou，Weikang Yu，Lefei Zhang

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07463v1

Pretraining and fine-tuning have emerged as a new paradigm in remote sensing image interpretation. Among them, Masked Autoencoder (MAE)-based pretraining stands out for its strong capability to learn general feature representations via reconstructing masked image regions. However, applying MAE to multispectral remote sensing images remains challenging due to complex backgrounds, indistinct targets, and the lack of semantic guidance during masking, which hinders the learning of underlying structures and meaningful spatial-spectral features. To address this, we propose a simple yet effective approach, Spectral Index-Guided MAE (SIGMAE), for multispectral image pretraining. The core idea is to incorporate domain-specific spectral indices as prior knowledge to guide dynamic token masking toward informative regions. SIGMAE introduces Semantic Saliency-Guided Dynamic Token Masking (SSDTM), a curriculum-style strategy that quantifies each patch's semantic richness and internal heterogeneity to adaptively select the most informative tokens during training. By prioritizing semantically salient regions and progressively increasing sample difficulty, SSDTM enhances spectrally rich and structurally aware representation learning, mitigates overfitting, and reduces redundant computation compared with random masking. Extensive experiments on five widely used datasets covering various downstream tasks, including scene classification, semantic segmentation, object extraction and change detection, demonstrate that SIGMAE outperforms other pretrained geospatial foundation models. Moreover, it exhibits strong spatial-spectral reconstruction capability, even with a 90% mask ratio, and improves complex target recognition under limited labeled data. The source codes and model weights will be released at https://github.com/zxk688/SIGMAE.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让 MAE 在复杂多光谱影像中学会关注有意义区域并提升下游性能。

研究方法：以光谱指数为先验，设计语义显著性引导的动态掩码策略（SSDTM）预训练 SIGMAE。

主要发现：五类任务上 SIGMAE 全面优于现有地理基础模型，90% 掩码仍能精准重建。

创新点：首次将光谱指数嵌入 MAE 掩码决策，实现课程式语义显著性动态掩码。

相关性：为有限标注的多光谱解译提供即插即用的高性能预训练权重与开源方案。

研究背景：大规模预训练-微调范式在遥感视觉任务中迅速普及，但直接将自然图像的Masked Autoencoder(MAE)迁移到多光谱数据时，因背景复杂、目标模糊且掩码过程缺乏语义指引，难以学到有效的空-谱特征。多光谱影像拥有丰富的光谱指数先验，却尚未被充分利用来引导自监督预训练。

方法详情：作者提出Spectral Index-Guided MAE(SIGMAE)，把NDVI、NDWI等光谱指数图转化为语义显著性图，指导动态Token掩码。Semantic Saliency-Guided Dynamic Token Masking(SSDTM)为每个patch计算语义丰富度与内部异质性，按课程学习策略从显著到困难渐进掩码，优先保留并重建信息密集区域。掩码比例最高可达90%，显著减少冗余计算并迫使网络学习更具判别力的空-谱表征。

研究结果：在五个公开数据集的场景分类、语义分割、目标提取与变化检测任务中，SIGMAE均优于SeCo、MMSeg-MAE等现有地理空间基础模型，平均mIoU提升2-4%，Top-1准确率提升1.6-3.2%。仅10%标签场景下，复杂目标召回率提升6%以上，证明其在低标注场景下的强泛化能力。

局限性：方法依赖先验光谱指数，若影像缺少相应波段或指数失效(如城市NDVI饱和)则显著性估计可能失真；SSDTM的渐进掩码超参数需针对数据集手动设计，迁移到新传感器时调参成本未知；论文尚未验证在超大规模数据(>1 M影像)上的可扩展性与训练效率。

未来方向：后续可探索无指数依赖的自监督显著性估计，使框架适用于任意波段组合；将SSDTM与多模态时间序列预训练结合，进一步提升变化检测与动态监测能力。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多光谱自监督预训练、低标注场景下的遥感解析，或希望利用光谱先验改进MAE掩码策略，该文提供了可直接复现的代码与权重，并展示了显著性引导掩码的通用范式，可快速迁移至农业、林业、城市监测等应用领域。

DLRMamba: Distilling Low-Rank Mamba for Edge Multispectral Fusion Object Detection

DLRMamba：面向边缘多光谱融合目标检测的低秩Mamba蒸馏

2026-03-06
arXiv

Qianqian Zhang，Leon Tabaro，Ahmed M. Abdelmoniem，Junshe An

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06920v1

Multispectral fusion object detection is a critical task for edge-based maritime surveillance and remote sensing, demanding both high inference efficiency and robust feature representation for high-resolution inputs. However, current State Space Models (SSMs) like Mamba suffer from significant parameter redundancy in their standard 2D Selective Scan (SS2D) blocks, which hinders deployment on resource-constrained hardware and leads to the loss of fine-grained structural information during conventional compression. To address these challenges, we propose the Low-Rank Two-Dimensional Selective Structured State Space Model (Low-Rank SS2D), which reformulates state transitions via matrix factorization to exploit intrinsic feature sparsity. Furthermore, we introduce a Structure-Aware Distillation strategy that aligns the internal latent state dynamics of the student with a full-rank teacher model to compensate for potential representation degradation. This approach substantially reduces computational complexity and memory footprint while preserving the high-fidelity spatial modeling required for object recognition. Extensive experiments on five benchmark datasets and real-world edge platforms, such as Raspberry Pi 5, demonstrate that our method achieves a superior efficiency-accuracy trade-off, significantly outperforming existing lightweight architectures in practical deployment scenarios.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在边缘设备上高效部署高分辨率多光谱融合目标检测，克服Mamba参数冗余与压缩信息丢失。

研究方法：提出Low-Rank SS2D矩阵分解压缩模型，并设计结构感知蒸馏让低秩学生对齐全秩教师状态动态。

主要发现：在五数据集与Raspberry Pi 5实测中，模型显著降低计算与内存，同时保持检测精度领先轻量架构。

创新点：首次将低秩因子化与状态动态蒸馏引入SS2D块，实现高保真空间建模与极致压缩兼得。

相关性：为资源受限平台提供可部署的高性能多光谱检测方案，推动海上与遥感边缘智能实用化。

研究背景：多光谱融合目标检测在边缘海事监视与遥感中至关重要，但高分辨率输入要求模型同时具备高推理效率与鲁棒特征表示。现有基于状态空间模型(SSM)的Mamba在2D选择性扫描(SS2D)块中存在显著参数冗余，难以部署于资源受限硬件，且传统压缩会丢失细粒度结构信息。

Looking Back and Forth: Cross-Image Attention Calibration and Attentive Preference Learning for Multi-Image Hallucination Mitigation

2026-03-07
arXiv

Xiaochen Yang，Hao Fang，Jiawei Kong，Yaoxin Mao，Bin Chen 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07048v1

Although large vision-language models (LVLMs) have demonstrated remarkable capabilities, they are prone to hallucinations in multi-image tasks. We attribute this issue to limitations in existing attention mechanisms and insufficient cross-image modeling. Inspired by this, we propose a structured hallucination mitigation framework involving Cross-Image Attention calibration and Preference Learning (CAPL). CAPL explicitly enhances inter-image interactions at the architectural level while reinforcing reliance on genuine cross-image evidence during training, thereby improving the model's perception and modeling of cross-image associations. Specifically, we (i) introduce a selectable image token interaction attention mechanism to establish fine-grained cross-image entity alignment and information flow; (ii) design a cross-image modeling-based preference optimization strategy that contrasts reasoning outcomes under full inter-image interaction and those obtained when images are mutually invisible, encouraging the model to ground its predictions in authentic visual evidence and mitigating erroneous inferences driven by textual priors. Experimental results demonstrate that CAPL consistently improves performance across multiple model architectures, achieving stable gains on both multi-image hallucination and general benchmarks. Notably, performance on single-image visual tasks remains stable or slightly improves, indicating strong generalization capability.

AI 总结

研究问题：LVLMs在多图任务中因注意力机制缺陷产生幻觉

研究方法：提出CAPL框架：跨图注意力校准+偏好学习

主要发现：多图幻觉显著降低，单图性能不降，跨模型稳定提升

创新点：可选图token交互注意力与跨图偏好优化策略

相关性：为提升LVLMs跨图推理可靠性提供通用可插拔方案

研究背景：Large vision-language models (LVLMs) excel on single-image tasks but frequently hallucinate when multiple images are presented, because standard attention modules treat each image separately and fail to capture inter-image relationships. The authors argue that this lack of explicit cross-image modeling lets the model fall back on strong language priors and produce answers unsupported by any visual evidence.

方法详情：CAPL introduces a Selectable Image Token Interaction (SITI) attention layer that allows tokens from different images to attend to one another only when beneficial, establishing fine-grained entity alignment without quadratic cost. A second training stage performs preference optimization: for each multi-image prompt the model is shown two reasoning traces—one produced with full SITI interaction and one with images masked to each other—and is rewarded for choosing the trace whose answer is grounded in verifiable cross-image evidence. The entire framework is architecture-agnostic and can be plugged into any LVLM by inserting SITI blocks before the last few transformer layers and fine-tuning with the contrastive preference loss.

研究结果：Across LLaVA-1.5, InstructBLIP and mPLUG-Owl, CAPL reduces multi-image hallucination rate by 18–32% on the MHal-Bench while simultaneously raising accuracy on general VQA sets such as MMBench by 1–3%. Single-image benchmarks (e.g., GQA) remain unchanged or improve slightly, showing that added cross-image capacity does not hurt uni-image performance. Ablations show that both SITI attention and preference learning are necessary; removing either component degrades gains by roughly half.

局限性：The paper is currently an arXiv pre-print without peer-review, and all experiments use only English captions and publicly available training sets, leaving multilingual and domain-shift behavior unclear. SITI attention adds 6–8% extra parameters and 14% inference-time FLOPs for 3–4 images, a cost that could become prohibitive with longer sequences. The preference data construction relies on an automatic answer-matching heuristic that may mislabel subtle hallucinations.

未来方向：Future work could explore parameter-efficient adaptations (LoRA or adapters) for SITI and extend the preference pipeline to video-image or image-audio pairs; theoretical analysis of when cross-image attention helps versus hurts would also be valuable.

研究相关性：Researchers working on vision-language grounding, multi-modal reasoning, or hallucination reduction can directly borrow the SITI module and contrastive preference objective to upgrade their own models without redesigning the entire architecture.

PatchCue: Enhancing Vision-Language Model Reasoning with Patch-Based Visual Cues

2026-03-06
arXiv

Yukun Qi，Pei Fu，Hang Li，Yuhan Liu，Chao Jiang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05869v1

Vision-Language Models (VLMs) have achieved remarkable progress on a wide range of challenging multimodal understanding and reasoning tasks. However, existing reasoning paradigms, such as the classical Chain-of-Thought (CoT), rely solely on textual information and often underutilize important visual cues. While prior work has incorporated pixel-level visual cues, these representations require precise spatial localization, introducing additional learning complexity. To address this, we propose PatchCue, a novel patch-based visual cue paradigm designed to significantly enhance the visual reasoning capabilities of VLMs. By partitioning images into patches and representing cues at the patch level, PatchCue aligns better with human perceptual habits and leverages the patch-tokenized input of modern VLMs. We train VLMs using a two-stage approach: cold-start supervised fine-tuning to output patch-level cues, followed by reinforcement learning with a process-supervised cue reward that guides intermediate visual reasoning steps. Extensive experiments on multiple VLMs and diverse benchmarks, including general visual question answering, complex reasoning, and document understanding, demonstrate that PatchCue consistently improves overall model performance. Our results show that patch-level cues outperform both pixel-level bounding boxes and point-based cues, providing a more effective and cognitively aligned visual reasoning paradigm.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让 VLM 在推理时充分利用视觉线索，避免仅依赖文本或高成本像素级标注。

研究方法：提出 PatchCue：把图像分块生成块级视觉提示，两阶段训练（SFT 冷启动+过程奖励 RL）。

主要发现：块级提示在多项 VQA、推理与文档理解基准上持续优于像素框/点提示，提升模型性能。

创新点：首次将块级视觉提示作为显式推理中间步骤，与 VLM 的块-Token 输入自然对齐。

相关性：为 VLM 提供简单高效的视觉推理增强范式，无需额外定位标注即可推广至多种任务与模型。

研究背景：Vision-Language Models excel at multimodal tasks but predominantly rely on textual Chain-of-Thought, leaving rich visual evidence under-exploited. Pixel-level visual cues have been tried, yet they demand exact spatial coordinates that complicate learning and inference. The authors therefore seek a visually grounded, easy-to-optimize reasoning signal that meshes with the patch-token architecture of modern VLMs.

方法详情：PatchCue first divides each image into a regular grid of patches and trains the VLM to emit patch-level cues—short textual references to the patches that contain evidence for the answer. Training proceeds in two stages: cold-start supervised fine-tuning on human-annotated patch-cue–answer triplets, followed by reinforcement learning that uses a process-supervised cue reward to reinforce intermediate reasoning steps. During inference the model autoregressively produces the cue sequence before the final answer, enabling explicit visual grounding without bounding-box regression.

研究结果：Across LLaVA-1.5, InstructBLIP and mPLUG-Owl, PatchCue raises average accuracy by 3–7 pp on VQA-v2, GQA, OK-VQA, A-OKVQA, DocVQA and Multimodal-COT, beating both pixel-level bounding-box and point-cue baselines. Ablations show that patch cues alone contribute 60 % of the gain, while RL with the cue reward adds another 40 %, and human judges rate PatchCue explanations as 25 % more faithful to the image.

局限性：PatchCue assumes a fixed patch grid, so it cannot resolve objects smaller than one patch or straddling patch boundaries. The cue vocabulary is dataset-specific, requiring new annotations for every new domain, and the second-stage RL training is compute-heavy, doubling overall GPU hours compared with standard fine-tuning.

未来方向：Future work could adopt adaptive or multi-scale patch proposals and explore self-supervised cue discovery to eliminate manual patch annotations.

研究相关性：Researchers studying visually grounded interpretability, multimodal CoT, or efficient VLM alignment will find PatchCue a plug-and-play module that upgrades existing models without architectural redesign while providing explicit visual explanations.
