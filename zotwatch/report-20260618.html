Dynamic Sparse Attention and Frequency-Spatial Complementary Network for SAR Ship Detection

动态稀疏注意力与频率-空间互补网络用于SAR船舶检测

2026-06-16
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Chunming Tang，Zhuangzhi Ji，Haiqiang Liu

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3704169

Ship detection in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery is critical for maritime surveillance. However, complex inshore SAR scenes suffer from dense distributions and strong background clutter, leading to high false alarm rates. Dynamically sparsifying attention connections to retain only the most relevant query-key pairs enables the model to focus on salient regions. Meanwhile, frequency-domain analysis provides complementary information, where low-frequency components encode global scattering structures, while high-frequency components preserve target boundary details. Motivated by this, we propose DFC-DETR, a Dynamic sparse attention and Frequency-spatial Complementary DEtection TRansformer for inshore SAR ship detection. The dynamic sparse attention (DSA) blocks in the backbone adaptively focus on salient regions to enhance the representation of densely distributed small ships. Additionally, we introduce a frequency-spatial complementary (FSC) module that explicitly decouples frequency-domain information to suppress background clutter and mitigate target overlap. Experimental results on the SSDD and HRSID datasets show that DFC-DETR achieves 76.1% and 70.0% AP, respectively, demonstrating its effectiveness.

局限性：模型复杂度较高，可能影响实时推理效率；频域分解模块对噪声敏感，在极端杂波环境下性能可能下降；实验仅基于两个公开数据集，未在更多样化场景（如不同分辨率、极化模式）下验证泛化性。

FusionRS: A Large-Scale RGB-Infrared Remote Sensing Dataset for Dual-Modal Vision-Language Foundation Models

FusionRS：面向双模态视觉语言基础模型的大规模RGB-红外遥感数据集

2026-06-15
arXiv

Jiaju Han，Ben Zhang，Xuemeng Sun，Qike Zhang，Yuxian Dong 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17020v1

Remote sensing vision-language models have advanced Earth observation understanding, but most existing work remains centered on RGB imagery, leaving the complementary information in infrared data underexplored. Infrared images provide distinctive cues, including thermal intensity structures, object boundaries, and illumination-invariant scene features, which can enrich visual-language learning beyond conventional RGB observations. However, a large-scale RGB-infrared-text dataset for remote sensing vision-language modeling is still absent. To address this gap, we introduce FusionRS, the first large-scale RGB-infrared-text dataset designed for dual-modal vision-language learning in remote sensing. FusionRS is constructed by translating diverse public RGB remote sensing images into infrared-style counterparts, forming aligned RGB-IR image pairs. Each pair is associated with conventional scene captions and IR-aware captions that explicitly describe infrared-specific visual properties while preserving semantic content. Based on FusionRS, we train dual-modal vision-language foundation models for RGB-IR joint understanding. We first train CLIP-style models for RGB-IR-text alignment, and then fine-tune generative VLMs for dual-modal RGB-IR captioning. Experiments show that FusionRS improves RGB-IR alignment, infrared-to-text retrieval, and dual-modal captioning over RGB-only and non-IR-aware training settings. Ablation studies further verify that IR-aware captions are crucial for strengthening infrared-language alignment, highlighting the importance of modality-specific textual supervision for more scalable RGB-infrared remote sensing vision-language representation learning.

研究背景：现有的遥感视觉-语言模型主要集中于RGB图像，忽视了红外数据提供的热结构、边界和光照不变特征等互补信息。目前缺乏大规模RGB-红外-文本数据集来支持双模态视觉-语言学习，因此本文旨在填补这一空白。

局限性：数据集的红外图像是通过翻译RGB图像生成的，而非真实红外传感器捕获，可能无法完全模拟真实红外分布。此外，评估仅在合成场景下进行，缺乏真实红外数据的验证。

Triple-Branch Difference Fusion Network for Land Cover Change Detection with Heterogeneous Remote Sensing Images

面向异构遥感图像土地覆盖变化检测的三分支差异融合网络

2026-06-16
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Zhiyong Lv，Yifan Zhang，Jiake Li，Wanting Wang，Cheng Shi

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3704412

Land-cover change detection (LCCD) from multimodal remote sensing images (MRSIs) is crucial for environmental monitoring, disaster response, and urban analysis, but it is still challenging due to the heterogeneous imaging mechanisms of different sensors. In particular, direct pixel-wise comparison across modalities is often unreliable. To address this issue, we propose a Triple-Branch Difference Fusion Network (TBDFN) for multimodal LCCD. TBDFN is composed of two key components. First, a modality conversion module is designed to alleviate the modality discrepancy via cross-modal transformation, which blends content information from one modality with style characteristics from the other to improve cross-modal consistency. Second, built upon the converted representations, a triple-branch fusion architecture is developed to extract temporal difference features from synthetic aperture radar (SAR) and optical imagery, while a Same-Time Cross-Modal Difference (ST-CMD) module explicitly models inter-modality differences within each temporal phase. The three types of difference features are subsequently aggregated to generate the final change map. Experiments on four datasets, with comparisons against six representative methods, demonstrate that TBDFN achieves superior qualitative and quantitative results, validating its effectiveness and robustness for multimodal LCCD. Our proposed method has respectively increased the overall accuracy rate by 1.29%, 0.17%, 1.07%, and 0.13%.The code of the proposed approach can be available at https://github.com/ImgSciGroup/2026-TBDFN.

研究问题：如何利用异构遥感图像（SAR和光学）进行可靠的土地覆盖变化检测？

研究方法：提出三支路差异融合网络（TBDFN），包含模态转换模块和三支路融合架构。

主要发现：TBDFN在四个数据集上优于六种方法，总体精度分别提升1.29%、0.17%、1.07%和0.13%。

创新点：通过跨模态转换减少模态差异，并显式建模同时间跨模态差异（ST-CMD）进行多模态融合。

相关性：为多模态遥感图像变化检测提供鲁棒方法，有助于环境监测和灾害响应等应用。

研究背景：异质遥感图像（如SAR和光学图像）由于成像机制不同，直接像素级比较不可靠，导致土地覆盖变化检测（LCCD）面临挑战。本研究旨在通过跨模态转换和差分融合方法，提高多模态遥感图像变化检测的准确性和鲁棒性。

方法详情：提出三分支差分融合网络（TBDFN），包含两个关键组件：一是模态转换模块，通过跨模态变换融合一种模态的内容信息和另一种模态的风格特征，缓解模态差异；二是基于转换后的表示，构建三分支融合架构，分别提取SAR和光学图像的时序差异特征，并通过同时间跨模态差分模块显式建模每个时相内的模态间差异，最终聚合三类差分特征生成变化图。

研究结果：在四个数据集上与六种代表性方法对比，TBDFN在定性和定量结果上均表现优越，总体精度分别提升了1.29%、0.17%、1.07%和0.13%，验证了其在多模态LCCD中的有效性和鲁棒性。

局限性：模态转换模块可能依赖于特定数据集的特征分布，泛化性有待进一步验证；三分支融合架构的计算复杂度较高，可能影响实时应用；实验仅评估了SAR和光学图像组合，未涉及其他异质模态。

未来方向：未来可探索更轻量化的网络结构以降低计算开销，并拓展到更多异质传感器组合（如红外与多光谱）的变化检测场景。

Heterogeneous SAR-optical fusion for near-real-time land use and land cover mapping under cloud contamination: A novel framework and global benchmark dataset

云污染条件下近实时土地利用与土地覆盖制图的异构SAR-光学融合：一种新型框架与全球基准数据集

2026-06-16
arXiv

Jiangong Xu，Weibao Xue，Xiaoyu Yu，Jun Pan，Xinlian Lianga 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17713v1

Optical remote sensing imagery is frequently degraded by cloud and cloud-shadow contamination, which limits its reliability for near-real-time land use and land cover (LULC) mapping. Although synthetic aperture radar (SAR) can provide cloud-penetrating structural information, existing SAR-optical fusion methods often assume reliable optical observations and insufficiently address the semantic uncertainty introduced by cloud contamination. To address this issue, we propose CloudLULC-Net, an end-to-end heterogeneous SAR-optical fusion framework that directly predicts LULC maps from cloud-contaminated Sentinel-2 imagery and temporally adjacent Sentinel-1 SAR observations. The proposed network incorporates optical reliability modulation to suppress unreliable optical responses, heterogeneous information adaptive aggregation to model high-order spatial-channel interactions between optical and SAR representations, and a unified semantic mapping transformer to organize fused features in a LULC-oriented latent space. A semantic anchor-guided optimization strategy is further introduced to improve the consistency of intermediate semantic representations. To support this task, we construct CloudLULC-Set, a large-scale benchmark dataset containing 40,223 curated SAR-optical-label triplets with pixel-level LULC annotations across diverse geographic regions and cloud conditions. Experimental results show that CloudLULC-Net achieves an OA of 86.60%, an F1-score of 83.29%, and an mIoU of 73.51%, outperforming representative heterogeneous reconstruction-first and end-to-end SAR-optical mapping methods. Off-the-shelf visual representations have been widely applied in various tasks. However, as image retrieval involves a compact representation, the above practice does not obtain convincing performance, especially in realistic scenarios of novel domains and categories. In this paper, we make the first attempt to address it by organizing a generalized image retrieval task and proposing an off-the-shelf quantizer. Challenges of realizing them include two perspectives: visual inconsistency across domains and hidden semantics of unknown categories, which corrupt compact features. To tackle the former issue, we propose a cross-aligned contrastive learning objective for model training. It simultaneously reduces quantization error and domain gap, encouraging the model to generate domain-invariant quantization codes. To tackle the latter one, we design a $$2^{nd}$$ order codebook which holds representative information of seen categories. Novel images are extracted by performing a compositional projection via all $$2^{nd}$$ order codewords, which improves generalization ability on unknowns. Combining them, we obtain a significant performance gain compared to the current state-of-the-art, where an up to $$8.0\%$$ increase in mAP is observed.
