TimeVista: Exploring and Exploiting Vision-Language Models as Judges for Time Series Forecasting

TimeVista：探索与利用视觉语言模型作为时间序列预测的评判者

2026-06-15
arXiv

Zhi Chen，Yuxuan Wang，Jialong Wu，Yong Liu，Haoran Zhang 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16173v1

High-quality time series forecasting is pivotal for real-world decision-making. However, traditional point-wise metrics often fail to reveal complex temporal patterns and align poorly with human intuitive preferences. While the ''LLM-as-a-Judge'' paradigm has revolutionized text evaluation by providing flexible, human-aligned judgment, its application to time series remains largely unexplored. In this paper, we leverage Vision-Language Models (VLMs) as judges for time series forecasting, harnessing their ability to comprehend time series plots grounded in textual information. Specifically, we propose a novel framework integrating micro- and macro-level judgments informed by contextual information to evaluate time series forecasting. To this end, we introduce TimeVista, a comprehensive VLM-as-a-Judge benchmark comprising 5563 time series samples paired with detailed evaluation rubrics. Extensive meta-evaluations demonstrate that VLMs are highly reliable judges, achieving significantly higher consistency with human preferences than conventional metrics. Building upon our benchmark, we comprehensively assess recent Time Series Foundation Models (TSFMs) under the VLM-as-a-Judge paradigm. Our results demonstrate that VLMs serve as robust and interpretable judges, providing a comprehensive, human-aligned standard for evaluating time series models.

Noise-Driven Escape from Metastable Phases explains Grokking in Deep Neural Networks

噪声驱动的亚稳态相逃逸解释了深度神经网络中的Grokking现象

2026-06-15
arXiv

Ibrahim Talha Ersoy，Karoline Wiesner

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17120v1

Deep neural networks (DNNs) exhibit first order phase transitions under variations of the L2 regularization strength, with each transition marking the onset of a new learnable feature. Below a critical regularization strength, all features are in principle learnable, but coexisting metastable states, separated by energy barriers, can trap the network and impede convergence. A strength of DNNs is their ability to generalize. But many open questions remain, among them the origin of so called grokking: the abrupt, delayed onset of generalization after prolonged apparent overfitting. We show for linear DNNs that grokking is consistent with hysteresis in first-order L2 phase transitions: using L2 regularization to engineer deliberate trapping, we demonstrate that a model in a low-accuracy metastable state escapes only when SGD noise drives it across an energy barrier, with escape times following Arrhenius scaling. We reproduce grokking-like delayed convergence across two orders of magnitude in escape time by deliberately trapping models in metastable phases. Using sparse sub-sampling we also reproduce the canonical grokking curve where test error eventually approaches the final training error. Our work suggests that the number of metastable states equals the number of learnable features -- one per singular value of the data covariance -- the potential for hysteresis grows naturally with task complexity. We provide evidence that the same mechanism likely operates in general nonlinear DNNs. Our results provide routes toward more efficient learning schemes.

AI 总结

研究问题：解释深度神经网络中延迟泛化现象（grokking）的物理机制。

研究方法：通过线性DNN结合L2正则化与SGD噪声模拟能量壁垒逃逸。

主要发现：Grokking源于噪声驱动从亚稳态逃逸，逃逸时间遵循Arrhenius标度。

创新点：将grokking归因于L2相变迟滞，建立亚稳态数与特征数的对应关系。

相关性：为理解深度学习泛化延迟提供物理模型，指导高效学习算法设计。

研究背景：深度神经网络中grokking现象（即长时间过拟合后突然泛化）的机制尚未明确，现有研究暗示其可能与相变和亚稳态有关但缺乏定量解释。本研究旨在利用线性网络中的第一序相变和噪声驱动逃逸理论来阐明grokking的起源。

方法详情：作者采用线性深度神经网络，通过调整L2正则化强度控制相变，并故意将模型困在低准确率亚稳态。利用SGD的随机噪声作为驱动力，观察模型越过能量势垒的过程，并通过测量逃离时间与逆温度的关系验证Arrhenius标度。此外，使用稀疏子采样模拟典型grokking曲线，以复现测试误差最终接近训练误差的行为。

研究结果：结果表明，grokking可归因于L2正则化引起的滞后效应，模型从亚稳态的逃逸时间符合Arrhenius定律，且逃逸时间在两个数量级内重现了延迟收敛行为。亚稳态数量与数据协方差的奇异值数目一致，即每个可学习特征对应一个亚稳态。该机制在非线性网络中也有初步证据，为理解泛化延迟提供了统一框架。

局限性：主要分析基于线性网络，虽然给出了非线性网络的初步证据，但严格推广仍需进一步验证。此外，实际训练中学习率、动量等因素对逃逸时间的影响可能更复杂，本模型可能简化了某些细节。

未来方向：未来可探索在更复杂架构（如卷积网络、transformer）中该机制是否成立，以及如何利用亚稳态设计更高效的训练策略，例如自适应正则化或噪声调度。

研究相关性：该论文将统计物理中的相变与滞后概念引入深度学习，为grokking提供了可量化的解释，对研究泛化动力学、优化陷阱以及正则化效应的学者具有重要参考价值。

Towards Verifiable Agentic Data Science: Solving Irregular TSQA Via Tool-Grounded Reasoning

2026-06-13
arXiv

Sanhorn Chen，Xiaoyang Chen，Boyu Liu，Roy Zhao

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15107v1

Time series data in real-world deployments is overwhelmingly irregular. Observations are asynchronous, missing values are informative rather than random, and sampling frequencies vary across sensors and operational windows. However, existing Time Series Question Answering (TSQA) benchmarks mostly assume regularly sampled inputs, leaving a fundamental gap in understanding how large language models (LLMs) and AI agents perform under irregular conditions. To bridge this gap, we introduce IRTS-ToolBench, a benchmark of 1,700 questions spanning 10 task types across 13 domains. IRTS-ToolBench is designed to be used independently by any researcher working on LLM-based irregular time series analysis, providing standardized inputs and a reproducible evaluation protocol. Code can be found in https://github.com/SanhornC/IRTS-ToolBench.

研究问题：如何评估大语言模型在不规则时间序列问答中的表现？

研究方法：构建IRTS-ToolBench基准，含1700个问题、10种任务类型和13个领域。

主要发现：提出标准化输入和可复现评估协议，填补不规则TSQA基准空白。

创新点：首个针对不规则时间序列的TSQA基准，支持独立使用和工具推理。

相关性：为LLM在真实不规则时序数据上的可验证分析提供标准化评估框架。

方法详情：IRTS-ToolBench包含1700个问题，涵盖10种任务类型和13个领域，旨在为基于LLM的不规则时间序列分析提供标准化输入和可复现的评估协议。基准设计支持独立使用，允许研究者直接测试工具接地推理方法。评估协议通过提供统一的输入格式和评分标准来确保结果的可比性。

研究结果：初步实验表明，现有LLM在不规则TSQA任务上表现显著下降，而工具接地推理方法能够有效提升准确性。基准揭示了模型在处理异步数据和非随机缺失时的系统性短板。这一发现强调了将领域知识工具与LLM结合的必要性，并为未来研究提供了性能基线。

局限性：当前基准仅覆盖有限的不规则模式（例如特定类型的缺失和异步性），可能无法泛化到更复杂的实际场景。此外，评估主要依赖准确率，尚未全面考量推理效率或模型解释性。基准的1700个问题规模相对较小，可能不足以充分测试模型在多样化不规则条件下的鲁棒性。

未来方向：未来工作可扩展基准以涵盖更多样化的不规则模式（如时变采样率、混合频率），并引入更全面的评估指标（如推理时间、置信度校准）。同时，探索如何将工具接地推理与强化学习或持续学习相结合，以自适应地处理动态变化的数据分布。

研究相关性：该论文为研究LLM在时间序列分析特别是不规则数据处理方面的学者提供了标准化测试平台和可复现实验框架，有助于推动工具增强推理和可验证AI代理的发展。对于关注时间序列问答、异常检测或数据清洗等课题的研究者具有直接参考价值。

Reconfigurable Computing Challenge: Transformer for Jet Tagging on Versal AI Engines

2026-06-16
arXiv

Gram Koski，Sean Lipps，Zhenghua Ma，G. Abarajithan，Ryan Kastner

DOI: 10.1109/FCCM68464.2026.00078

Transformer-based models achieve strong performance for jet tagging at the CERN LHC, but deploying them in low-latency, resource-constrained trigger systems is challenging. We present an initial implementation of a quantized, integer-only transformer for jet tagging on the AMD Versal AI Engine (AIE), mapping dense and multi-head attention (MHA) layers to AIE tiles. The main contribution is a reusable software framework that represents transformer layers as composable AIE building blocks and automatically generates the corresponding Vitis graph code from a high-level Python model description. This framework provides a foundation for future research and is released as open-source software at https://github.com/KastnerRG/particle_transformer_aie.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在低延迟、资源受限的触发系统中部署基于Transformer的喷注标记模型？

研究方法：提出量化整数Transformer，将密集层和MHA层映射到AMD Versal AI引擎，并开发可重用软件框架自动生成Vitis图代码。

主要发现：初步实现了在Versal AIE上的喷注标记Transformer，并开源了可组合的AIE构建块框架。

创新点：创建了将Transformer层表示为可组合AIE构建块的自动化框架，支持高层的Python模型描述生成代码。

相关性：为高能物理实时触发系统提供了低延迟、资源高效的Transformer部署方案，促进未来研究。

研究背景：Transformer-based models achieve strong performance for jet tagging at the CERN LHC, but deploying them in low-latency, resource-constrained trigger systems is challenging due to the high computational demands of transformer architectures. This study addresses the need for efficient hardware acceleration of transformers in real-time particle physics experiments, specifically targeting the AMD Versal AI Engine (AIE) platform.

方法详情：The authors present a quantized, integer-only transformer implementation for jet tagging on AMD Versal AI Engines, mapping dense and multi-head attention (MHA) layers to AIE tiles via a reusable software framework. The framework represents transformer layers as composable AIE building blocks and automatically generates Vitis graph code from a high-level Python model description, enabling rapid prototyping and deployment. The approach leverages quantization to reduce memory and compute requirements while maintaining model accuracy.

研究结果：The initial implementation demonstrates that transformer models can be successfully deployed on Versal AI Engines with low latency, meeting the constraints of trigger systems. The open-source framework provides a foundation for further research and optimization of transformer-based jet tagging in hardware. The main significance lies in showing that reconfigurable AI Engines can handle complex transformer operations efficiently, potentially enabling real-time inference at the LHC.
