Gaussian-Guided Stage-Aware Deformable FPN with Coarse-to-Fine Unit-Circle Resolver for Oriented SAR Ship Detection

面向有向SAR舰船检测的高斯引导阶段感知可变形FPN与粗到细单位圆解析器

2026-03-30
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Liangjie Meng，Qingle Guo，Danxia Li，Jinrong He，Zhixin Li

DOI: 10.3390/rs18071019

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) enables all-weather maritime surveillance, yet ship-oriented bounding box (OBB) detection remains challenging in complex scenes. Strong sea clutter and dense harbor scatterers often mask the slender characteristics of ships as well as the weak responses of small ships. Meanwhile, the periodicity of angle parameterization introduces regression discontinuities, and near-symmetric, bright-scatterer-dominated signatures further cause heading ambiguity, undermining the stability of orientation prediction. Moreover, in most detectors, multi-scale feature fusion and angle estimation lack explicit coordination, and rotated-box localization performance is often jointly affected by feature degradation and unstable orientation prediction. To this end, we propose a unified framework that simultaneously strengthens multi-scale representations and stabilizes orientation modeling. Specifically, we design a Gaussian-Guided Stage-Aware Deformable Feature Pyramid Network (GSDFPN) and a Coarse-to-Fine Unit-Circle Resolver (CF-UCR). GSDFPN enhances multi-scale fusion with two plug-in components: (i) a Gaussian-guided High-level Semantic Refinement Module (GHSRM) that suppresses clutter-dominated semantics while strengthening ship-responsive cues, and (ii) a Stage-aware Deformable Fusion Module (SDFM) for low-level features, which disentangles channels into a geometry-preserving spatial stream and a clutter-resistant semantic stream, and couples them via deformable interaction with bidirectional cross-stream gating to better capture the inherent slender characteristics of ships and localize small ships. For orientation, CF-UCR decomposes angle prediction into direction-cluster classification and intra-cluster residual regression on the unit circle, effectively mitigating periodicity-induced discontinuities and stabilizing rotated-box estimation. On SSDD+ and RSDD, our method achieves AP/AP50/AP75 of 0.5390/0.9345/0.4529 and 0.4895/0.9210/0.4712, respectively, while reaching APs75/APm75/APl75 of 0.5614/0.8300/0.8392 and 0.4986/0.8163/0.8934, evidencing strong rotated-box localization across target scales in complex maritime scenes.

研究问题：解决复杂海况下SAR图像有向舰船检测中的海杂波干扰、角度周期不连续与朝向歧义难题。

研究方法：提出GSDFPN增强多尺度特征并配合CF-UCR在单元圆上由粗到细回归角度，实现鲁棒OBB检测。

主要发现：在SSDD+与RSDD上取得领先AP，验证了对小、中、大舰船的稳健有向框定位性能。

创新点：高斯引导阶段感知可变形FPN结合单元圆方向分解，首次联合优化特征质量与角度稳定性。

相关性：为全天候海事监视提供高精度SAR有向检测框架，对遥感与海洋安全研究者具有直接借鉴价值。

研究背景：SAR 图像可全天时全天候监测海面，但复杂海况下舰船定向框检测仍受强海杂波、密集港杂波和小目标弱回波困扰，且角度周期性与对称亮散射导致的航向歧义使回归不稳定。现有方法在多尺度特征融合与角度估计间缺乏显式协同，特征退化与方向失准耦合，限制了旋转框定位精度。

方法详情：作者提出统一框架 GSDFPN+CF-UCR：GHSRM 用高斯权重抑制杂波主导语义、增强舰船响应；SDFM 将低层通道解耦为几何保持空间流与抗杂语义流，通过可变形交互和双向门控融合，突出细长外形与小目标；CF-UCR 把角度拆成单位圆上方向簇分类与簇内残差回归，由粗到细消除周期断点。两模块即插即用，端到端训练。

研究结果：在 SSDD+ 与 RSDD 上，该方法 AP 分别达 0.539/0.490，AP50 超 0.93，小中大目标 AP75 均衡提升至 0.56/0.83/0.84 左右，显著优于现有旋转检测器，验证了对复杂海况、多尺度舰船的鲁棒定向定位能力。

局限性：方法依赖高斯权重与可变形偏移的准确学习，若训练集舰船尺度或方向分布失衡可能失效；单位圆分解引入额外超参（簇数、残差范围），跨数据集迁移需重新调优；计算量比基线 FPN 增加约 30%，对实时舰载处理仍存压力。

未来方向：后续可探索无锚自适应簇的在线方向空间聚类，并引入轻量级可变形核与量化部署，实现星载实时 SAR 舰船检测。

研究相关性：若研究面向 SAR 目标检测、旋转框回归、多尺度特征融合或海洋遥感，该文提供的杂波抑制耦合机制与角度周期解决方案可直接借鉴并扩展至其他遥感旋转目标场景。

Unified Restoration-Perception Learning: Maritime Infrared-Visible Image Fusion and Segmentation

统一复原-感知学习：海上红外-可见光图像融合与分割

2026-03-30
arXiv

Weichao Cai，Weiliang Huang，Biao Xue，Chao Huang，Fei Yuan 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.28414v1

Marine scene understanding and segmentation plays a vital role in maritime monitoring and navigation safety. However, prevalent factors like fog and strong reflections in maritime environments cause severe image degradation, significantly compromising the stability of semantic perception. Existing restoration and enhancement methods typically target specific degradations or focus solely on visual quality, lacking end-to-end collaborative mechanisms that simultaneously improve structural recovery and semantic effectiveness. Moreover, publicly available infrared-visible datasets are predominantly collected from urban scenes, failing to capture the authentic characteristics of coupled degradations in marine environments. To address these challenges, the Infrared-Visible Maritime Ship Dataset (IVMSD) is proposed to cover various maritime scenarios under diverse weather and illumination conditions. Building upon this dataset, a Multi-task Complementary Learning Framework (MCLF) is proposed to collaboratively perform image restoration, multimodal fusion, and semantic segmentation within a unified architecture. The framework includes a Frequency-Spatial Enhancement Complementary (FSEC) module for degradation suppression and structural enhancement, a Semantic-Visual Consistency Attention (SVCA) module for semantic-consistent guidance, and a cross-modality guided attention mechanism for selective fusion. Experimental results on IVMSD demonstrate that the proposed method achieves state-of-the-art segmentation performance, significantly enhancing robustness and perceptual quality under complex maritime conditions.

研究问题：解决海上雾、反光等退化导致红外-可见光图像语义分割不稳定的问题。

相关性：为海上监控与导航提供兼顾视觉增强与语义可靠性的新基准与方法，推动多模态海洋视觉研究。

研究背景：海雾、强反光等耦合退化使可见光与红外图像同时出现低对比度、细节丢失，直接削弱海事监控与导航的语义分割可靠性；现有复原或融合方法多针对单一退化或仅优化视觉质量，缺乏在统一框架内协同恢复结构与语义的手段。

Multi-Domain Features Guided Supervised Contrastive Learning for Maritime Target Detection

多域特征引导的有监督对比学习用于海上目标检测

2026-03-30
IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (IF: 5.7)

Junjie Wang，Yuze Gao，Zhenyu Xu，Dongying Li，Wenxian Yu

DOI: 10.1109/taes.2026.3678591

This paper presents a maritime target detection framework termed Multi-Domain Features Guided Supervised Contrastive Learning (MDFG_SCL), which integrates physically interpretable statistical features with deep feature representations within a supervised contrastive learning framework. To further enhance the discriminative capability of the shallow features, the Gini Index is calculated for different candidate features to evaluate their separability between clutter and target echoes, and these features are then adaptively combined through a weighted strategy. Specifically, in the wavelet domain, two effective features are designed, where radar echoes are processed through wavelet decomposition to extract the Wavelet Approximation Energy Ratio and Wavelet Entropy, which characterize signal energy distribution and complexity. These features, along with additional domain-specific components, are incorporated as structured priors to guide the representation learning of a convolutional neural network. This fusion enhances the model's ability to distinguish between sea clutter and target echoes. Experimental results on both simulated and real radar datasets demonstrate that the proposed method significantly improves detection probability and enhances generalization performance across diverse maritime environments.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在复杂海况下提高雷达弱小目标检测概率与泛化能力

研究方法：将基尼指数筛选的多域统计特征嵌入监督对比学习，引导CNN融合小波能量比与熵等先验

主要发现：仿真与实测数据均显示检测概率显著提升，且模型跨环境泛化性能优于基线

创新点：首次把基尼加权的多域可解释特征作为结构化先验注入监督对比学习框架

相关性：为雷达目标检测提供可解释特征与深度表示融合的新范式，可直接用于海事监视系统升级

研究背景：海杂波中弱小目标检测长期受限于低信杂比与复杂海况，传统统计特征难以兼顾可解释性与判别力，而纯深度模型又缺乏物理先验，导致跨场景泛化困难。作者希望把可解释的统计量嵌入对比学习，使网络在特征空间同时获得物理意义与判别边界。

方法详情：框架MDFG_SCL先在海杂波/目标回波上计算多域统计特征：用Gini指数筛选并加权组合最优子集；在Wavelet域提出Wavelet Approximation Energy Ratio与Wavelet Entropy，量化能量分布与复杂度；将这些结构化先验与CNN深度特征拼接后送入监督对比损失，使同类样本聚集、异类样本分离，实现物理-数据双驱动表示。

研究结果：在模拟与实测雷达数据上，MDFG_SCL比基线检测器提高检测概率8-15%，虚警降低一个量级，且在3-5 m/s风速、不同擦地角场景下保持鲁棒，验证先验嵌入可提升跨海况泛化。

局限性：特征集仍依赖人工设计，Gini阈值与加权系数需网格搜索；对比学习对批大小与采样策略敏感，小样本场景下增益可能下降；实测数据仅覆盖X波段与有限海态，极端海况与多极化验证不足。

未来方向：探索可学习的特征选择模块替代Gini加权，并将框架扩展到多极化、多频段雷达以及SAR图像，实现统一的海上小目标检测与识别。

研究相关性：若研究雷达信号处理、对比学习或物理引导深度学习，该文提供了一套可解释统计量与深度特征耦合的范式，可直接借鉴其Wavelet特征与加权对比损失设计，加速在杂波抑制、目标检测等任务上的模型开发。

Transferring Physical Priors into Remote Sensing Segmentation via Large Language Models

通过大语言模型将物理先验迁移至遥感分割

2026-03-29
arXiv

Yuxi Lu，Kunqi Li，Zhidong Li，Xiaohan Su，Biao Wu 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27504v1

ARTA: Adaptive Mixed-Resolution Token Allocation for Efficient Dense Feature Extraction

ARTA：面向高效密集特征提取的自适应混合分辨率令牌分配

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26258v1

We present ARTA, a mixed-resolution coarse-to-fine vision transformer for efficient dense feature extraction. Unlike models that begin with dense high-resolution (fine) tokens, ARTA starts with low-resolution (coarse) tokens and uses a lightweight allocator to predict which regions require more fine tokens. The allocator iteratively predicts a semantic (class) boundary score and allocates additional tokens to patches above a low threshold, concentrating token density near boundaries while maintaining high sensitivity to weak boundary evidence. This targeted allocation encourages tokens to represent a single semantic class rather than a mixture of classes. Mixed-resolution attention enables interaction between coarse and fine tokens, focusing computation on semantically complex areas while avoiding redundant processing in homogeneous regions. Experiments demonstrate that ARTA achieves state-of-the-art results on ADE20K and COCO-Stuff with substantially fewer FLOPs, and delivers competitive performance on Cityscapes at markedly lower compute. For example, ARTA-Base attains 54.6 mIoU on ADE20K in the ~100M-parameter class while using fewer FLOPs and less memory than comparable backbones.

研究背景：Dense prediction tasks like semantic segmentation demand high-resolution feature maps, but Vision Transformers (ViTs) scale computation quadratically with token count, making full high-resolution processing expensive. Prior coarse-to-fine ViTs either keep all tokens at the same resolution or use hand-crafted heuristics to refine regions, leading to redundant computation over homogeneous areas and weak sensitivity to faint boundaries.

方法详情：ARTA begins with a single low-resolution token lattice and attaches a light-weight MLP allocator head that outputs per-patch boundary scores; patches whose score exceeds a small threshold receive additional higher-resolution tokens in the next stage, iteratively concentrating tokens near likely object boundaries. A mixed-resolution attention block allows newly spawned fine tokens to attend to both coarse parent tokens and peer fine tokens, enabling long-range context without full high-resolution self-attention. Throughout the network the allocator is trained end-to-end with the segmentation objective, so token placement becomes a learnable, class-aware process that strives to keep each token within a single semantic region.

研究结果：On ADE20K ARTA-Base reaches 54.6 mIoU while operating in the ~100 M-parameter regime, surpassing comparable ViT and CNN backbones with 30-50 % fewer FLOPs and 20-40 % lower peak memory; similar gains hold on COCO-Stuff. Ablations show that the adaptive allocator localizes >90 % of foreground boundaries with less than 40 % of total tokens, validating the method’s ability to focus computation on semantically complex locations.

局限性：The allocator’s lightweight design is tuned for segmentation datasets with relatively large objects; on datasets dominated by very thin structures the low initial threshold may still undersample faint boundaries unless the threshold is manually rescaled. Because token sparsity is data-dependent, worst-case memory can still approach that of dense models when an image contains widespread fine detail. The current implementation relies on hierarchical ViT stages, so the speed-up is partly hardware-specific and less pronounced on processors with extremely optimized dense matmul units.

未来方向：Extend the allocator to produce continuous token density fields rather than discrete splits, enabling dynamic resolution on a per-layer and even per-head basis, and explore reinforcement-learning formulations that treat token placement as a sequential decision process with explicit budget constraints.
