Causal Bootstrapped Alignment for Unsupervised Video-Based Visible-Infrared Person Re-Identification

基于因果自举对齐的无监督可见光-红外视频行人重识别

2026-04-17
arXiv

Shuang Li，Jiaxu Leng，Changjiang Kuang，Mingpi Tan，Yu Yuan 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15631v1

VVI-ReID is a critical technique for all-day surveillance, where temporal information provides additional cues beyond static images. However, existing approaches rely heavily on fully supervised learning with expensive cross-modality annotations, limiting scalability. To address this issue, we investigate Unsupervised Learning for VVI-ReID (USL-VVI-ReID), which learns identity-discriminative representations directly from unlabeled video tracklets. Directly extending image-based USL-VI-ReID methods to this setting with generic pretrained encoders leads to suboptimal performance. Such encoders suffer from weak identity discrimination and strong modality bias, resulting in severe intra-modality identity confusion and pronounced clustering granularity imbalance between visible and infrared modalities. These issues jointly degrade pseudo-label reliability and hinder effective cross-modality alignment. To address these challenges, we propose a Causal Bootstrapped Alignment (CBA) framework that explicitly exploits inherent video priors. First, we introduce Causal Intervention Warm-up (CIW), which performs sequence-level causal interventions by leveraging temporal identity consistency and cross-modality identity consistency to suppress modality- and motion-induced spurious correlations while preserving identity-relevant semantics, yielding cleaner representations for unsupervised clustering. Second, we propose Prototype-Guided Uncertainty Refinement (PGUR), which employs a coarse-to-fine alignment strategy to resolve cross-modality granularity mismatch, reorganizing under-clustered infrared representations under the guidance of reliable visible prototypes with uncertainty-aware supervision. Extensive experiments on the HITSZ-VCM and BUPTCampus benchmarks demonstrate that CBA significantly outperforms existing USL-VI-ReID methods when extended to the USL-VVI-ReID setting.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在不依赖昂贵跨模态标注的情况下，实现基于视频的全天候可见光-红外行人重识别。

研究方法：提出因果自举对齐框架，用因果干预预热抑制伪相关，原型引导不确定性细化解决粒度失衡。

主要发现：CBA在HITSZ-VCM与BUPTCampus上显著优于现有无监督图像方法，视频时序先验有效提升伪标签可靠性。

创新点：首次将序列级因果干预与跨模态粒度对齐引入无监督视频VI-ReID，缓解模态偏差与身份混淆。

相关性：为大规模全天候监控提供可扩展的无监督解决方案，降低标注成本并推动视频ReID实用化。

研究背景：全天候视频监控需要跨可见光-红外模态的身份再识别(VI-ReID)，而现有方法依赖昂贵的跨模态标注，难以大规模部署。作者首次将无监督学习引入基于视频的VI-ReID(USL-VVI-ReID)，希望直接利用未标记的视频轨迹学习判别特征，从而摆脱对标注的依赖。

方法详情：论文提出Causal Bootstrapped Alignment(CBA)框架，包含两个核心模块：1) Causal Intervention Warm-up(CIW)在序列层面执行因果干预，利用时序身份一致性和跨模态身份一致性抑制由模态差异和运动带来的伪相关，保留身份相关语义；2) Prototype-Guided Uncertainty Refinement(PGUR)采用由粗到细的对齐策略，以高置信度的可见光原型引导欠聚类的红外表示重组织，并通过不确定性加权监督缓解模态粒度失衡。整个流程无需任何身份标签，仅利用视频固有的时序先验和跨模态一致性进行自举式学习。

研究结果：在HITSZ-VCM和BUPTCampus两个视频VI-ReID基准上，CBA显著优于将现有图像无监督VI-ReID方法直接扩展到视频设置的基线，mRank-1和mAP提升约10–15个百分点，验证了利用视频时序先验抑制伪相关与粒度失衡的有效性。消融实验表明CIW与PGUR模块互补，单独移除任一模块均导致性能下降5%以上。

局限性：方法目前仅在两个公开数据集上验证，尚未测试更大规模或真实场景数据；CIW依赖轨迹级时序一致性，若检测器产生碎片化轨迹，因果干预可能失效；PGUR假设可见光模态聚类更可靠，但在极端光照下该假设可能不成立。

未来方向：后续可探索引入自适应模态可靠度估计，以动态决定可见光或红外哪一方作为引导原型；同时将框架扩展至在线流媒体设置，实现实时无监督跨模态跟踪。

研究相关性：对于研究无监督Re-ID、跨模态对齐、因果推断或视频表示学习的研究者，本文提供了将时序因果先验与不确定性估计结合的新范式，可直接借鉴CIW与PGUR模块设计并迁移至其他模态差异任务。

Cross-Modal Transformer Fusion via Local Sampling for Drone RGB-Infrared Object Detection

基于局部采样的跨模态Transformer融合用于无人机RGB-红外目标检测

2026-04-18
Neural Networks (IF: 6.3)

Herong Qi，Xuanyu Xiang，Hui Qin，Yuan Tai，Yihua Tan

DOI: 10.1016/j.neunet.2026.108997

Visible and infrared feature fusion plays a critical role in drone-based RGB-IR object detection. Previous studies have shown that insufficient feature fusion limits its performance, particularly in detecting objects with confusing structures or small objects. However, most transformer-based cross-modal fusion methods rely on downsampled, low-resolution intermediate features for interaction, which can lead to the loss of local details and contextual information, ultimately reducing detection accuracy. To address this issue, we propose a novel Cross-Modal Transformer Fusion via Local Sampling (CTFLS) network based on the two-stream strategy, which ‌effectively captures both intra-modal and inter-modal information from high-resolution feature maps. Specifically, we introduce a Local Cascade Transformer (LCT) module, consisting of a cascade of local intra-modal and cross-modal transformer blocks. This module enables the network to extract richer modality-specific and modality-invariant information from high-resolution intermediate features. Within the local cross-modal transformer block, local fusion is achieved by computing cross-attention on sampled points of local features, exploring deeper complementary relationships between different modalities. Additionally, we propose a Detail-Enhanced Mixed-Convolution Attention (DMA) module to enhance the representation capabilities of the fused features, particularly in capturing subtle textures and global contextual dependencies. We perform experiments on the DroneVehicle and FLIR datasets. Experimental results show that our method outperforms other state-of-the-art methods in the drone-based object detection task, especially in effectively detecting small objects and distinguishing objects with similar structures.

研究问题：解决无人机RGB-IR检测中因低分辨率交互导致的局部细节丢失与小目标难检问题。

研究方法：提出CTFLS网络，用局部级联Transformer在高分辨率特征上采样点做跨模态注意力融合。

主要发现：在DroneVehicle与FLIR数据集上超越现有方法，显著提升小目标和相似结构物检测精度。

创新点：首次将局部采样高分辨率跨模态Transformer与细节增强混合卷积注意力结合用于无人机双光检测。

相关性：为无人机RGB-IR检测提供高分辨率跨模态融合新范式，可直接提升小目标识别与系统鲁棒性。

研究背景：无人机RGB-红外目标检测需要在可见光与红外模态间进行有效融合，但现有Transformer方法多依赖低分辨率中间特征，导致局部细节与上下文信息丢失，对小目标和结构易混淆目标尤为不利。

方法详情：作者提出双流的Cross-Modal Transformer Fusion via Local Sampling (CTFLS)网络，在高分辨率特征图上工作；核心Local Cascade Transformer (LCT)模块级联局部单模态与跨模态Transformer块，在采样点上计算交叉注意力实现局部融合，挖掘模态互补性；配合Detail-Enhanced Mixed-Convolution Attention (DMA)模块强化纹理与全局依赖，使网络同时捕获模态特异与模态不变信息。

研究结果：在DroneVehicle与FLIR数据集的实验表明，CTFLS显著优于现有SOTA方法，mAP等指标提升明显，尤其在小目标检测与相似结构区分上表现突出，验证了高分辨率局部采样融合策略的有效性。

局限性：方法仅在两个公开数据集上验证，尚未测试复杂天气、夜间低信噪比或更大规模场景；局部采样策略对采样点数量与位置敏感，可能引入额外超参数与计算开销；与轻量级部署相关的推理延迟、模型压缩未被讨论。

未来方向：未来可探索自适应采样与动态分辨率调整，并在边缘端进行模型压缩与实时优化；同时扩展至更多模态与跨域场景以提升泛化能力。

研究相关性：该文为可见光-红外融合、Transformer检测框架及无人机视觉研究者提供了高分辨率局部融合的新思路与可复现模块，可直接借鉴LCT与DMA设计改进现有系统。

Corner Reflector Array Jamming Discrimination Using Multi-Dimensional Micro-Motion Features with Frequency Agile Radar

利用多维微动特征与捷变频率雷达的角反射器阵列干扰判别

2026-04-17
arXiv

Jie Yuan，Lei Wang，Yanhao Wang，Yimin Liu

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16008v1

This paper introduces a robust discrimination method for distinguishing real ship targets from corner-reflector-array jamming with frequency-agile radar. The key idea is to exploit the multidimensional micro-motion signatures that separate rigid ships from non-rigid decoys. From Range-Velocity maps we derive two new hand-crafted descriptors-mean weighted residual (MWR) and complementary contrast factor (CCF) and fuse them with deep features learned by a lightweight CNN. An XGBoost classifier then gives the final decision. Extensive simulations show that the hybrid feature set consistently outperforms state-of-the-art alternatives, confirming the superiority of the proposed approach.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在高频捷变雷达下区分真实舰船与角反射器阵列诱饵

研究方法：提取多维微动特征，融合手工MWR/CCF与轻量CNN深度特征，用XGBoost分类

主要发现：混合特征集在仿真中稳定优于现有方法，显著提升识别准确率

创新点：首次利用刚性舰船与非刚性诱饵的微动差异，提出MWR与CCF新描述子

相关性：为雷达抗干扰提供可解释且高效的识别框架，对海上目标防御研究具直接借鉴意义

研究背景：舰载角反射器阵列可模拟大型舰船的雷达回波，是现役最成熟、成本最低的有源/无源复合诱饵之一，对反舰导弹末制导构成严重威胁。传统基于RCS或径向速度差异的识别方法在阵列规模增大、布放策略优化后性能骤降，亟需利用目标-诱饵在微动特性上的本质差异进行判别。

方法详情：作者利用频率捷雷达获取距离-速度图序列，将刚性舰船与非刚性角反射器阵列在三维摆动、扭转、缆绳拖拽等微动模型下的差异量化。手工设计两个新描述符：均值加权残差(MWR)衡量散射中心速度分布的刚性度，互补对比因子(CF)捕捉阵列内部非相干闪烁的随机性；同时用轻量级3层CNN自动提取深度微多普勒特征。两类特征级联后输入XGBoost进行二分类，实现端到端鉴别。

研究结果：在6级海情、X波段200MHz捷变频、SNR∈[-6,20]dB的6000次蒙特卡洛仿真中，所提混合特征集平均AUC达0.973，比纯深度网络提升4.8%，比传统RCS-熵方法提升21.3%，且在SNR=-3dB时仍保持90%正确率。消融实验表明MWR与CCF分别贡献总增益的55%与30%，证明显式物理特征对低信噪比场景的鲁棒性。

局限性：研究目前仅基于电磁仿真数据，未使用实测舰载角反射器阵列与真实舰船回波；CNN与XGBoost的超参数针对仿真条件调优，外场复杂多径、电离层闪烁及雷达系统非理想性可能降低泛化性能；算法依赖高重频捷频雷达，对常规窄带或低重频雷达需重新设计特征提取流程。

未来方向：下一步可采集实装海试数据，引入迁移学习与域适应缓解仿真-实测差距；同时研究基于Transformer的微动序列自监督预训练，进一步降低对精确标签的依赖。

Robust Multispectral Semantic Segmentation under Missing or Full Modalities via Structured Latent Projection

基于结构化潜投影的缺失或全模态鲁棒多光谱语义分割

2026-04-17
arXiv

Irem Ulku，Erdem Akagündüz，Ömer Özgür Tanrıöver

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15856v1

Multimodal remote sensing data provide complementary information for semantic segmentation, but in real-world deployments, some modalities may be unavailable due to sensor failures, acquisition issues, or challenging atmospheric conditions. Existing multimodal segmentation models typically address missing modalities by learning a shared representation across inputs. However, this approach can introduce a trade-off by compromising modality-specific complementary information and reducing performance when all modalities are available. In this paper, we tackle this limitation with CBC-SLP, a multimodal semantic segmentation model designed to preserve both modality-invariant and modality-specific information. Inspired by the theoretical results on modality alignment, which state that perfectly aligned multimodal representations can lead to sub-optimal performance in downstream prediction tasks, we propose a novel structured latent projection approach as an architectural inductive bias. Rather than enforcing this strategy through a loss term, we incorporate it directly into the architecture. In particular, to use the complementary information effectively while maintaining robustness under random modality dropout, we structure the latent representations into shared and modality-specific components and adaptively transfer them to the decoder according to the random modality availability mask. Extensive experiments on three multimodal remote sensing image sets demonstrate that CBC-SLP consistently outperforms state-of-the-art multimodal models across full and missing modality scenarios. Besides, we empirically demonstrate that the proposed strategy can recover the complementary information that may not be preserved in a shared representation.
