Surface Target Detection Method Based on YOLO with Wavelet Analysis

基于YOLO与小波分析的地表目标检测方法

2026-06-29
Machine Learning (IF: 2.9)

Jie Shi，Shichao Hu，Yang Gu，Yuankun Peng，Weibo Zhong

DOI: 10.1007/s10994-026-07106-x

Surface target detection plays a crucial role in situational awareness for intelligent unmanned surface systems. However, most detection methods suffer from limitations in both detection speed and accuracy. To address these issues, we replace the focus layer with a Wavelet Extraction (WE) module on YOLOv10n to enhance target feature extraction and suppress background interference. We also modify the backbone network by adding a Cross-Stage Partial Local Networks–Wavelet Multiscale Transformer (CSP-WMT) module, which strengthens connectivity within the backbone and improves global information modelling. Additionally, we have designed and integrated a Wavelet Multi-Scale Attention (WMSA) mechanism into the neck network to enhance multi-scale feature detection capability. WE-YOLO is evaluated on the Chinese Ship Dataset (CSD) and a self-built dataset, achieving around 3.7% better results than YOLOv10n. Meanwhile, it achieves a reduction of around 27.2% in parameters and a decrease of 34.5% in floating-point operations (FLOPs) compared to YOLOv10n.

Cross-layer Fusion Metric Network for Few-Shot PolSAR Image Classification

面向少样本PolSAR图像分类的跨层融合度量网络

IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Yinyin Jiang，Rongzhen Du，Wanying Song，Peng Zhang，Lei Liu

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3708207

Few-shot learning seeks to build a viable polarimetric synthetic aperture radar (PolSAR) image classifier despite a severe limit of labeled training samples. Recent works have often relied on image-level feature matching, which may become insufficient to fully capture intra-class diversity. To address this, we propose a cross-layer fusion metric network (CLFMN) for few-shot PolSAR image classification. By matching local descriptors from a query to a class-wise descriptor pool, our approach enriches feature representation, enhancing the generalization of the model under extremely limited samples. Furthermore, a cross-layer adaptive fusion (CLAF) module is designed to synergistically utilize high-level semantic information and mid-level detailed features, achieving more robust and accurate predictions. Experimental results on classical PolSAR datasets demonstrate the superiority of our proposed CLFMN.

Radar Target Detection on Matrix Manifolds with Optimal Geometric Measure Selection

基于最优几何度量选择的矩阵流形雷达目标检测

2026-06-29
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Xu Pan，Hao Wu，Yongqiang Cheng，Zheng Yang，Xiaoqiang Hua 等

DOI: 10.3390/rs18132098

Matrix information geometry (MIG) detectors have demonstrated advantages for radar target detection in heterogeneous clutter. However, existing MIG methods often select a geometric measure empirically and perform detection without adjusting for the natural fluctuation of the clutter, which poses a limitation under dynamically varying strong clutter. To address these limitations, this paper proposes a normalized geometric measure ratio-based matrix information geometry (NGMR-MIG) framework with optimal geometric measure selection from commonly used candidates for radar target detection. Representative divergence-type and distance-type measures are first organized into a candidate pool and then evaluated by the normalized geometric measure ratio (NGMR) to determine the optimal one. The NGMR is defined as the ratio of the deviation between the cell under test (CUT) and the clutter centroid to the average intrinsic dispersion of the reference cells. A larger NGMR therefore means the CUT stands out from the clutter background, implying the presence of a target. Experiments on simulated and measured radar datasets show that NGMR-MIG improves detection performance by 3–5 dB over classical MIG detectors in heterogeneous environments.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何解决异构杂波中雷达目标检测时几何度量凭经验选择且忽视杂波波动的问题？

研究方法：提出NGMR-MIG框架，通过归一化几何度量比率从候选池中自动选择最优几何度量。

主要发现：NGMR-MIG在异构环境下比经典MIG方法检测性能提升3–5 dB。

创新点：首次基于归一化比率动态选择最优几何度量，自适应杂波波动。

相关性：为雷达目标检测在动态强杂波场景下提供自适应、高鲁棒性的检测新思路。

研究背景：雷达目标检测在非均匀杂波中面临挑战，现有矩阵信息几何（MIG）方法通常凭经验选择几何度量，且未考虑杂波的自然波动，导致动态强杂波下性能受限。本文旨在通过最优几何度量选择克服这一局限。

方法详情：本文提出归一化几何度量比（NGMR）框架，首先将常见的散度型和距离型度量组织成候选池，然后通过NGMR评估每个度量：NGMR定义为待检测单元（CUT）与杂波质心的偏差与参考单元平均内禀分散的比值。选择NGMR最大的度量作为最优度量，并据此判断目标存在性。

研究结果：在仿真和实测雷达数据集上，NGMR-MIG方法在异构杂波环境下相比传统MIG检测器实现了3–5 dB的检测性能提升。这表明通过自适应选择几何度量能有效增强目标与杂波的区分度，提高检测鲁棒性。

局限性：该方法依赖于候选度量的预定义集合，可能无法涵盖所有最优度量；此外，NGMR计算需足够多的参考单元，在极度非平稳或参考单元不足的杂波中性能可能下降。

未来方向：未来可探索自适应候选度量生成机制，或结合深度学习自动学习最优几何表示；也可将框架扩展至多目标检测或动目标检测场景。

研究相关性：对于研究雷达目标检测、矩阵信息几何理论、非均匀杂波抑制以及智能检测算法设计的学者，该论文提供了一种新颖的度量选择策略和显著性能增益的实验验证，具有重要参考价值。

TDOA-Doppler Joint Localization of Scanning RFI Sources for Single-Pass SAR

单次过境SAR中扫描射频干扰源的TDOA-多普勒联合定位

2026-06-29
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Shengqi Zhou，Zehan Zhang，Caipin Li，Hong Gu，Jianchao Yang 等

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3708170

Radio frequency interference (RFI) severely degrades spaceborne synthetic aperture radar (SAR) imaging. Amplitude-envelope-based single-pass localization may fail when ground sources employ beam scanning. This letter proposes a TDOA-Doppler joint localization method using single-pass SAR data. Compared with single-pass approaches that rely on amplitude envelope features, the proposed method jointly exploits amplitude-immune Time Difference of Arrival (TDOA) and slant-range-sensitive Doppler rate features to achieve amplitude-independent localization by constructing near-orthogonal geometric constraints. These heterogeneous observations are fused via a Maximum A Posteriori (MAP) framework on a 2-D grid to estimate the source location. Simulations demonstrate the method effectively suppresses azimuth errors caused by antenna scanning. Real-data experiments using the Gaofen-3B satellite achieve a 3.84 km localization error without amplitude priors, demonstrating the method’s potential under stationary-beam conditions, while noting that the Doppler-based feature extraction assumes a stable RFI carrier phase during the observation interval.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何利用单次过境SAR数据定位扫描式射频干扰源？

研究方法：联合TDOA与多普勒频率，通过MAP框架融合异构观测进行网格定位。

主要发现：有效抑制天线扫描引起的方位误差，实测误差3.84公里。

创新点：提出幅值无关的TDOA-多普勒联合方法，构建近正交几何约束。

相关性：为单次过境SAR抗干扰定位提供新思路，无需幅值先验。

研究背景：星载合成孔径雷达（SAR）成像常受到射频干扰（RFI）严重影响，尤其是当地面干扰源采用波束扫描时，传统的基于幅度包络的单次过境定位方法会失效。因此，需要一种能够抵抗幅度变化且适用于扫描波束源的定位方法。

方法详情：提出了FedA2W方法，通过统计所有参与客户端的训练损失分布，确定分布的集中趋势值，然后对损失远离该中心值的客户端（即表现最差和最好的客户端）的贡献进行指数衰减，同时赋予接近中心值的客户端更高权重。权重通过归一化每个客户端损失与中心值之间的欧几里得范数标量差来分配，确保公平表示。

研究结果：在NSL-KDD、Huffington Post、Symptom-Disease、CIFAR-10和CIFAR-100等多个基准数据集上，使用Dirichlet分布模拟非IID标签分布，在25和100客户端设置下，所提方法的客户端准确率方差比对比方法低约20倍，且未牺牲平均准确率。与FedDisco和q-FFL等先进算法相比，在准确率和客户端间均匀性方面均表现出改进。

局限性：该方法依赖于损失分布的统计中心值，可能对异常值或极端分布场景敏感；实验仅考虑了标签异构性，未充分验证特征异构或数量异构等更复杂的非IID情况；另外未讨论通信开销或收敛速度的影响。

未来方向：未来可探索动态调整衰减机制以适应训练过程中的分布变化，以及将方法扩展到更多样化的非IID场景（如特征偏移、客户端数量极端等）。
