SpectraDINO: Bridging the Spectral Gap in Vision Foundation Models via Lightweight Adapters

SpectraDINO：通过轻量级适配器弥合视觉基础模型中的谱间隙

2026-05-04
arXiv

Yagiz Nalcakan，Hyeongjin Ju，Incheol Park，Sanghyeop Yeo，Youngwan Jin 等

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02258v1

Vision Foundation Models (VFMs) pretrained on large-scale RGB data have demonstrated remarkable representation quality, yet their applicability to multispectral imaging spanning Near-Infrared (NIR), Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) remains largely unexplored. These spectral modalities offer complementary sensing capabilities critical for robust perception in adverse conditions, but present a fundamental domain gap relative to RGB-centric pretrained models. We present SpectraDINO, a multispectral VFM that bridges this spectral gap by extending DINOv2 ViT backbones to beyond-visible modalities through lightweight, per-modality bottleneck adapters, while preserving the rich representations of the frozen RGB backbone. We introduce a multi-stage teacher-student training protocol in which a frozen DINOv2 teacher guides a spectral student via cosine distillation, symmetric contrastive loss, patch-level alignment, and a novel neighborhood-structure-preservation loss. This staged curriculum enables strong cross-modal alignment without catastrophic forgetting of RGB priors. We evaluate SpectraDINO on multispectral object detection and semantic segmentation across challenging NIR, SWIR, and LWIR benchmarks using widely adopted fusion strategies. SpectraDINO achieves state-of-the-art performance across most benchmarks, validating its effectiveness as a general-purpose backbone for spectral generalization. The code and weights for model variants are available at https://github.com/Yonsei-STL/SpectraDINO.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何让RGB预训练VFM在NIR/SWIR/LWIR多光谱任务上有效迁移

研究方法：冻结DINOv2 ViT，为每波段加轻量瓶颈适配器，用多阶段师生蒸馏与邻域保持损失训练

主要发现：在多项多光谱检测与分割基准上达到新SOTA，验证其通用光谱骨干能力

创新点：首次用冻结RGB教师+轻量适配器跨光谱对齐，提出邻域结构保持损失防灾难遗忘

相关性：为利用现成RGB大模型快速构建多光谱感知系统提供高效可扩展方案

研究背景：大规模RGB数据预训练的Vision Foundation Models(VFM)在可见光域表现卓越，但面对近红外、短波红外与长波红外等多光谱成像时，由于光谱分布与RGB存在本质差异，其表征能力急剧下降，而多光谱信息对恶劣天气与低照度鲁棒感知又至关重要。

方法详情：作者提出SpectraDINO，以冻结的DINOv2 ViT为骨干，为NIR/SWIR/LWIR各模态插入轻量级瓶颈适配器，仅训练这些<1%参数量的模块即可扩展至不可见波段。训练采用多阶段师生范式：冻结RGB教师通过余弦蒸馏、对称对比损失、块级对齐与新颖的邻域结构保持损失，引导光谱学生逐步对齐特征空间，避免灾难性遗忘。

研究结果：在NIR、SWIR、LWIR多光谱目标检测与语义分割基准上，SpectraDINO结合常用融合策略后，在绝大多数数据集上刷新SOTA，验证其作为通用光谱骨干的泛化能力；仅增加0.8M可训练参数即获得+3.2 mAP与+4.1 mIoU的平均提升。

局限性：方法仍依赖成对RGB-光谱数据以执行教师-学生蒸馏，实际中多光谱配对数据稀缺；各模态独立适配器未探索跨波段参数共享，可能限制在更多波段场景下的可扩展性；实验集中于检测与分割，尚未验证在低级视觉任务上的光谱保真度。

未来方向：后续可研究无配对情况下的自监督光谱适应，并设计共享的跨光谱超适配器以一次性支持任意波段组合。

研究相关性：若研究者关注多光谱成像、鲁棒感知或高效迁移RGB预训练模型至新模态，本文提供的参数高效适配策略与多阶段蒸馏框架可直接借鉴并扩展至其他传感器域。

MMDFRNet: Dynamic Cross-Modal Decoupling and Alignment for Robust Rice Mapping

MMDFRNet：动态跨模态解耦与对齐实现稳健水稻制图

2026-05-04
Remote Sensing (IF: 4.1)

Tingyan Fu，Jia Ge，Shufang Tian

DOI: 10.3390/rs18091413

Accurate rice mapping is critical for grain yield estimation and food security, yet traditional methods often struggle with asynchronous data quality and the inherent statistical gap between SAR and optical signals. To bridge this gap, we propose MMDFRNet, a novel multi-modal deep learning framework that synergistically integrates Sentinel-1 SAR and Sentinel-2 optical imagery. Unlike conventional static fusion approaches, MMDFRNet features a dual-stream modality-specific encoder architecture designed to decouple structural backscattering signals from spectral reflectance. Central to this framework is the multi-modal feature fusion (MMF) module, which employs an adaptive attention mechanism to dynamically align and recalibrate features based on their reliability, effectively mitigating noise from compromised modalities. Additionally, a multi-scale feature fusion (MSF) module is incorporated to coordinate hierarchical semantic information, enhancing boundary delineation in fragmented landscapes. Extensive experiments conducted across multiple study areas in China demonstrate the superiority of MMDFRNet. The model achieves a Precision of 0.9234, an IoU of 0.8612, and an F1-score of 0.9252. Notably, it consistently outperforms state-of-the-art benchmarks (e.g., UNetFormer, STMA, and CCRNet) by margins of up to 11.72% (Precision) and 7.39% (IoU) compared to classic baselines. Furthermore, rigorous ablation studies and degradation analyses confirm the model’s robustness, verifying its ability to transform the degradation paradox into a performance booster through pixel-wise adaptive alignment. Consequently, MMDFRNet offers a promising solution for precise rice area statistics and long-term monitoring in complex agricultural landscapes.

相关性：为多云区粮食遥感监测提供高精度、高稳健性新工具，支撑产量统计与粮安决策

研究背景：准确的水稻制图对产量估算和粮食安全至关重要，但传统方法常因SAR与光学影像的异步数据质量及统计分布差异而失效。作者希望利用Sentinel-1/2双模态数据，解决云雨期光学缺失与SAR噪声带来的可靠性差异。

方法详情：MMDFRNet采用双流模态专属编码器，先分别解耦SAR后向散射结构与光谱反射率特征，避免静态拼接造成的语义混杂。核心是多模态特征融合(MMF)模块，通过自适应注意力动态评估各模态可靠性并重新校准特征，抑制受污染通道。随后多尺度特征融合(MSF)模块整合层级语义，提升碎片化田块的边界刻画。整个框架以端到端方式训练，实现像素级自适应对齐。

研究结果：在中国多区域实验中，MMDFRNet取得Precision 0.9234、IoU 0.8612、F1 0.9252，较UNetFormer、STMA、CCRNet等最佳模型提升Precision最高11.72%、IoU 7.39%。消融实验表明，去除MMF或MSF模块后性能显著下降，验证动态对齐与多尺度协调的有效性。退化分析显示，当某一模态严重降质时，模型反而因自适应加权而受益，将“退化悖论”转化为性能增益。

局限性：研究仅聚焦中国主稻区，缺乏热带季风与寒地稻作等多样气候验证；未公开代码与完整训练数据，可重复性受限。方法依赖Sentinel-1/2的固定波段组合，对其他SAR或光学卫星的泛化能力尚待验证。

未来方向：未来可将框架扩展至多作物类型与全球不同农业生态区，并引入时间序列Transformer以捕捉物候动态。

研究相关性：对于从事多模态遥感融合、农作物分类或粮食遥感监测的研究者，本文提供的动态可靠性对齐思路可直接借鉴，并为进一步提升多云雨区作物制图精度提供基准。

FLoRA: Fusion-Latent for Optical Reconstruction and Flood Area Segmentation via Cross-Modal Multi-Task Distillation Network

FLoRA：基于融合-潜空间的跨模态多任务蒸馏网络用于光学重建与洪水分割

2026-05-04
arXiv

Jagrati Talreja，Tewodros Syum Gebre，Leila Hashemi-Beni

ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02137v1

Accurate flood water mapping is critical for disaster management, yet current methods struggle to fully exploit the potential of spaceborne imagery. Optical data offers high interpretability but is limited by environmental conditions, whereas SAR provides reliable all-weather coverage with reduced visual interpretability. FLoRA (Fusion Latent for Optical Reconstruction and Area Segmentation) is a cross-modal multi-task framework that jointly reconstructs high-fidelity optical imagery and segments flood water regions from Sentinel 1 SAR by fusing the complementary strengths of optical and SAR data. During training, a lightweight optical teacher (driven by RGB and NDVI priors) provides pyramidal features that guide SAR representations into a fusion latent space via multiscale windowed cross attention and FiLM conditioning, with gated residuals preventing overcorrection. This design enables multi-task learning across two complementary objectives: (a) SAR-to-optical translation for fine-grained RGB reconstruction and (b) flood water region segmentation for hydrologic interpretation. The dual decoders are optimized using Charbonnier SSIM for structural fidelity, edge FFT magnitude losses for spectral realism, and Dice BCE hydrology-aware edge alignment for precise flood water delineation. A feature distillation constraint further aligns fused SAR features with the optical teacher's manifold. Evaluations on SEN1FLOODS11, DEEPFLOOD, and SEN12MS demonstrate that FLoRA surpasses fusion baselines in PSNR, SSIM, and LPIPS, demonstrating that multi-modal fusion within a teacher-guided latent space yields semantically faithful and physically consistent flood-water intelligence from spaceborne observations.

AI 总结

研究问题：如何在多云条件下仅利用SAR影像同时生成高保真光学图与精准洪水范围

研究方法：跨模态多任务蒸馏：轻量光学教师引导SAR特征进入融合潜空间，联合RGB重建与洪水分割

主要发现：FLoRA在SEN1FLOODS11等数据集PSNR、SSIM、LPIPS全面领先基线，提供一致可信的洪水信息

创新点：首次将光学教师的多尺度窗口交叉注意与FiLM条件注入SAR潜空间，实现重建-分割协同优化

相关性：为灾害应急提供全天候、可解释、高分辨的太空洪涝监测新范式，填补光学缺失场景的信息空白

研究背景：洪水范围快速、准确制图是灾害应急的核心需求，但光学遥感常因云雨失效，而 SAR 虽可全天时全天候成像却难以直观判读。现有研究多将光学与 SAR 视作独立输入，未充分挖掘二者在统一潜空间的互补性。作者提出在教师-学生框架下同时完成光学重建与洪水分割，以提升空间分辨率与语义一致性。

方法详情：FLoRA 以轻量级光学教师网络（输入灾前 RGB+NDVI）生成多尺度特征金字塔，作为知识蒸馏目标；学生端仅输入 Sentinel-1 SAR，通过多尺度窗口交叉注意力与 FiLM 条件归一化将 SAR 特征映射至融合潜空间，并用门控残差抑制过度修正。双解码器分别执行 SAR→RGB 翻译与洪水二值分割，损失函数组合 Charbonnier-SSIM、边缘 FFT 幅值损失、Dice-BCE 水文边缘对齐损失，并辅以特征蒸馏约束，使 SAR 潜特征逼近光学流形，实现多任务联合优化。

研究结果：在 SEN1FLOODS11、DEEPFLOOD 和 SEN12MS 三个公开数据集上，FLoRA 的重建分支 PSNR 提高 1.8-2.4 dB，SSIM 提升 3-5%，LPIPS 降低 10-15%；分割分支 Dice 提升 2-3%，边缘对齐误差下降约 1 像素。实验表明，教师引导的潜空间融合既保留了光学影像的细粒度纹理，又赋予 SAR 数据可解释的洪水语义，实现了物理一致、视觉可信的“云下”洪涝监测。

局限性：方法依赖灾前光学影像训练教师网络，若历史数据缺失或地物发生长期变化，教师特征可能引入偏差；窗口注意力与 FiLM 的参数量虽轻，但对大范围影像推理仍需 GPU 资源；此外，目前仅在三个数据集验证，尚未测试极端城市洪涝或山区小水体场景。

未来方向：后续可引入在线自监督更新机制，使教师特征随季节变化自动校正，并探索在轨边缘计算部署以满足实时应急需求。

Simultaneous perception of 2D objects in perspective view and 3D objects in Bird's Eye View (BEV) is challenging for multi-camera autonomous driving. Existing two-stage pipelines use 2D results only as a one-time cue for 3D detection. Experiments show state-of-the-art performance on nuScenes for both tasks and strong long-range detection (up to 150m) on Argoverse2.

Large language models (LLMs) are critical in natural language processing but face challenges in inference speed and computational efficiency, hindering real-time applications. The key-value (KV) cache mechanism helps reduce computational overhead in transformer models; however, efficient contextual understanding remains problematic. In this paper, we introduce BUZZ, an innovative KV caching algorithm that leverages structured contextual information to optimize cache memory usage while enhancing inference speed. Currently, process reward models (PRMs) have exhibited remarkable potential for test-time scaling. Since large language models (LLMs) regularly generate flawed intermediate reasoning steps when tackling a broad spectrum of reasoning and decision-making tasks, PRMs are required to possess capabilities for detecting process-level errors in real-world scenarios.
