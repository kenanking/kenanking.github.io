Towards generalized spaceborne SAR ship detection via Fourier-based perturbation augmentation and quality-aware invariance learning

基于傅里叶扰动增强和质量感知不变性学习的通用星载SAR舰船检测方法

2026-05-25
ISPRS Journal of Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (IF: 12.2)

Shuang Liu，Dong Li，Mi He，Haibo Song，Caizhi Fan 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.isprsjprs.2026.05.020

Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) ship detection is essential for maritime situational awareness. As spaceborne SAR platforms continue to expand, there is an urgent demand for detectors that can generalize to new or unseen platforms. However, existing methods are typically tailored to specific platforms and suffer significant performance degradation on unseen data due to inherent distribution shifts and limited generalization ability. To alleviate this, we propose a generalized SAR ship detection framework through F ourier-based perturbation A ugmentation and Q uality- A ware invariance learning, termed FAQA. FAQA aims to integrate multiple platform SAR data to learn generalized ship representations, boosting detection on previously unseen data. Specifically, to improve the model’s adaptability to unknown data, a Fourier-based Perturbation Augmentation (FPA) strategy is proposed. FPA simulates potential unknown domains by applying controlled random perturbations to the low-frequency amplitude regions, while preserving semantic integrity. Then, a Quality-Aware Invariance Learning method (QAIL) is developed. QAIL jointly evaluates the location-classification quality of each proposal as guidance, prompting the model to focus on invariance learning of semantically reliable ship regions while suppressing SAR-specific background interference. Lastly, an Adaptive Parameter Search (APS) mechanism is designed. APS leverages gradient alignment between original and augmented images as the supervisory signal, adaptively steering optimization toward more generalized solutions. Experimental results show that FAQA consistently outperforms existing DG methods across diverse settings. Under multi-DG, it achieves an average AP gain of 2.24% across four target domains. Under single-DG, it achieves gains of 2.35%, 0.59%, and 3.37% across three cases, demonstrating superior generalization and robustness to domain shifts.

研究问题：解决星载SAR舰船检测在未见平台上的泛化能力不足问题。

研究方法：提出FAQA框架，含傅里叶扰动增强、质量感知不变性学习和自适应参数搜索。

主要发现：多域泛化平均AP提升2.24%，单域泛化最高提升3.37%。

创新点：首次结合傅里叶域扰动与质量感知不变性学习实现跨平台泛化。

相关性：为多平台SAR舰船检测提供通用框架，增强实际应用鲁棒性。

研究背景：星载SAR舰船检测对海上态势感知至关重要，但现有方法通常针对特定平台设计，在未见过的数据上性能显著下降，面临分布偏移和泛化能力不足的问题。为此，本文提出一种泛化框架FAQA，旨在整合多平台SAR数据学习通用舰船表示。

方法详情：FAQA包含三个核心组件：首先，基于傅里叶的扰动增强（FPA）通过对低频振幅区域施加受控随机扰动模拟未知域，同时保留语义完整性；其次，质量感知不变性学习（QAIL）联合评估提议的位置-分类质量，引导模型聚焦于语义可靠的舰船区域并抑制SAR背景干扰；最后，自适应参数搜索（APS）利用原始与增强图像之间的梯度对齐作为监督信号，自适应地引导优化朝向更泛化的解。

研究结果：在多域泛化（multi-DG）设置下，FAQA在四个目标域上平均AP增益达2.24%；在单域泛化（single-DG）设置下，三个案例分别提升2.35%、0.59%和3.37%，一致优于现有DG方法，展示了出色的泛化能力和对域偏移的鲁棒性。

局限性：该方法依赖低频频谱扰动模拟未知域，可能无法覆盖所有真实分布偏移类型（如高分辨异质噪声）；此外，质量感知学习需额外计算提议质量，可能增加模型复杂度，且对极端不平衡场景的适应性有待验证。

未来方向：未来可探索更灵活的域扰动生成策略（如结合相位信息），或引入无监督域自适应机制以减少对标注多域数据的依赖，进一步扩展至实时或嵌入式SAR系统。

研究相关性：该研究面向多平台SAR数据泛化检测的迫切需求，提出的傅里叶域增强与不变性学习框架为跨域迁移学习提供了新范式，对从事遥感目标检测、域适应及SAR图像分析的研究者具有直接参考价值。

CDRL-Det: Causal-Debiased Representation Learning for Robust Rotated SAR Ship Detection

CDRL-Det：用于鲁棒旋转SAR舰船检测的因果去偏表示学习

2026-05-25
IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (IF: 4.4)

Shanshan Sun，Xiaogang Dong，Xiwen Qin

DOI: 10.1109/lgrs.2026.3696277

Synthetic aperture radar ship detection faces unique challenges where coherent speckle noise and dynamic sea clutter often mimic ship scattering characteristics, leading to severe confounding bias. To address this, we propose a causal de-biased representation learning framework. Grounded in a structural causal model, we introduce a causal feature de-biasing module. By deriving a first-order Taylor approximation of backdoor adjustment, this module disentangles target semantics from environmental confounders. Furthermore, a contextual angle rectification module is designed to correct orientation biases via spatial disentanglement. Experiments on the SSDD+ and RSDD datasets demonstrate state-of-the-art performance. Notably, the proposed method boosts the average precision to 88.7% on the complex RSDD dataset and achieves a 7.9% improvement in high-sea-state scenarios, validating its superior robustness in learning de-biased ship representations.

Robust Fine-Grained Oriented Ship Detection for Remote Sensing imagery via Controllable Generative Pretraining

基于可控生成式预训练的遥感图像鲁棒细粒度定向舰船检测

2026-05-25
IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (IF: 13.7)

Da He，Haoran Liu，Zeyu Li，Yikai Zhao，Xikun Hu 等

DOI: 10.1109/tip.2026.3694663

Fine-grained ship recognition in remote sensing imagery is essential for maritime applications. However, its development is hindered by two challenges: (1) the limited granularity of existing ship detection datasets, and (2) the disturbance of complex maritime conditions as well as the arbitrary ship orientations and distributions. To address the first issue, we annotated a large-scale fine-grained ship instance detection dataset (LAFI), comprising 48,717 ship instances worldwide with 49 categories. To tackle the challenges of marine disturbance and diverse ship status, we proposed a controllable generative knowledge-driven ship detection framework (COSD). It employs a controllable generative model guided by ship-marine knowledge to generate millions of synthetic images that not only preserve ship structures but also cover diverse sea and weather conditions for robust pretraining. Furthermore, a heterogeneous feature alignment decoder is designed to align multi-modal metrics of orientation and distribution features in the latent space, allowing for accurate representation of diverse ship status. Extensive experiments on two benchmark datasets showed that our method respectively increased 0.093 and 0.129 mean average precision (mAP) over SOTA methods, particularly in scenarios involving small, densely packed and arbitrary oriented ships.

ISAR Technique for Inland Waterway Surveillance Using Spaceborne Single-Channel SAR Data

利用星载单通道SAR数据的内河航道监视ISAR技术

2026-05-25
IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (IF: 5.5)

Ilaria Nasso，Fabrizio Santi，Debora Pastina

DOI: 10.1109/jstars.2026.3696855

This paper proposes an inverse synthetic aperture radar (ISAR) technique specifically tailored for inland waterway surveillance using single-channel spaceborne SAR data. The approach delivers well-focused vessel images on a homogeneous plane, enabling reliable size estimation and supporting automatic target recognition. In addition, it jointly estimates vessel kinematic parameters, including speed and heading, and corrects azimuthal displacement to recover the actual vessel position. A key novelty lies in exploiting the elongated structural characteristics of ships for their positioning without relying on along-track interferometry, multi-platform acquisitions, or wake detection. Comprehensive theoretical and numerical performance analyses are presented under different use cases. The proposed method is further validated using real SAR data acquired over different waterways and vessel types, demonstrating robust performance and practical applicability across a wide range of operational scenarios relevant to inland traffic.

主要发现：在多个数据集上零样本和微调分类达到最优，F1分数最高提升6.36%。

创新点：结合LLM简化文本、知识注入、无标注病灶采样和不对称损失，提升对齐和泛化。

相关性：为医学视觉语言模型提供新框架，提升诊断可靠性和对新类别适应能力。

研究背景：Medical vision-language alignment aims to leverage images and reports for analysis, but current methods often fail to precisely align semantics, leading to inaccurate diagnoses. This study addresses the challenge by proposing a lesion-aware model that improves alignment through contrastive learning, using rich information from both modalities.

方法详情：MedLAMA introduces a component extractor using an LLM to simplify disease details from reports, reducing complexity while preserving information. It employs a knowledge injector to augment reports with external disease-specific imaging details, enabling unseen class prediction. A lesion-aware attention sampling focuses on suspicious regions without extra annotations, and an asymmetric term loss ensures high similarity for matched pairs and same-disease pairs, minimizing false negatives.

研究结果：The model achieves top performance in zero-shot and fine-tuned classification across RSNA Pneumonia, ChestX-ray14, SIIM-ACR Pneumothorax, COVIDx, and CheXpert datasets, with a maximum F1 score improvement of 6.36%. The results demonstrate significant gains in alignment accuracy and generalization to new categories.

局限性：The reliance on LLMs and external knowledge bases may introduce computational overhead and potential biases from those sources. Additionally, the lesion-aware attention sampling might not generalize well to diseases with highly varied or subtle imaging features not captured by the training data.

未来方向：Future work could explore integrating temporal or multi-view information to further enhance lesion-aware alignment, and investigate the model's applicability to other medical imaging modalities beyond chest X-rays.

研究相关性：This paper is highly relevant for researchers in medical vision-language pretraining, contrastive learning, and zero-shot classification, as it provides a novel framework for fine-grained multimodal alignment with explicit lesion focus and improved generalization.
